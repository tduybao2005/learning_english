### Giải thích đáp án Reading

**Passage 1 — The Psychology of Motivation**

- **Q1 TRUE:** "Research consistently demonstrates that intrinsically motivated individuals tend to display greater creativity, persistence, and overall well-being than those who are primarily extrinsically motivated."
- **Q2 TRUE:** "first articulated in his 1943 paper 'A Theory of Human Motivation'."
- **Q3 FALSE:** SDT's ba nhu cầu là competence (năng lực), autonomy (tự chủ) và relatedness (kết nối). Không có nhu cầu về financial security.
- **Q4 FALSE:** "based on thousands of interviews across multiple cultures" — không phải chỉ với vận động viên chuyên nghiệp.
- **Q5 TRUE:** "producing intense concentration, a distorted sense of time, and deep personal satisfaction."
- **Q6 FALSE:** "Carol Dweck through decades of research with children and adults" — bao gồm cả người lớn, không chỉ trẻ em.
- **Q7 intrinsic:** "Intrinsic motivation describes the drive to engage in an activity because it is inherently interesting..."
- **Q8 pyramid:** "Maslow proposed that human needs are arranged in a five-level pyramid."
- **Q9 self-actualisation:** "at the apex, self-actualisation: the realisation of one's fullest potential."
- **Q10 flow:** "The concept of 'flow', introduced by Hungarian-American psychologist Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi."
- **Q11 competence:** "competence (the need to feel effective in one's interactions with the environment)."
- **Q12 overjustification effect:** "a phenomenon known as the 'overjustification effect'."
- **Q13 immediate feedback:** "activities with clear goals and immediate feedback."

**Passage 2 — The Roman Empire**

- **Q14 ii (Para B):** Đoạn B nói về Julius Caesar — thiên tài quân sự và sự kết thúc của nền cộng hòa: "Military genius and the end of republican government."
- **Q15 v (Para C):** Đoạn C nói về Augustus, Pax Romana và cải cách hành chính: "An era of peace and administrative reform under the first emperor."
- **Q16 vii (Para D):** Đoạn D nói về lãnh thổ rộng nhất dưới Trajan và hệ thống đường xá: "The greatest territorial extent and the infrastructure that sustained it."
- **Q17 iii (Para E):** Đoạn E nói về bê tông, cầu dẫn nước và luật pháp La Mã: "Engineering achievements and enduring legal frameworks."
- **Q18 i (Para F):** Đoạn F nói về lao động nô lệ và mạng lưới thương mại: "The expansion of trade and the role of forced labour in the Roman economy."
- **Q19 TRUE:** "For nearly five centuries, Rome was governed as a republic."
- **Q20 FALSE:** "the conflict he had unleashed continued until his adopted heir Octavian defeated his rivals at the Battle of Actium in 31 BC" — xung đột tiếp tục sau khi Caesar bị ám sát.
- **Q21 TRUE:** "At its peak in 117 AD, the empire encompassed approximately five million square kilometres."
- **Q22 FALSE:** "performing tasks ranging from manual agricultural labour to skilled craftsmanship, teaching, and financial administration" — nô lệ làm nhiều loại công việc, không chỉ nông nghiệp.
- **Q23 NOT GIVEN:** Bài đọc không đề cập tổng dân số đế chế vượt 70 triệu người.
- **Q24 B:** "he had found Rome a city of brick and left it a city of marble."
- **Q25 C:** "stronger and more durable than anything previously known, enabling the construction of structures such as the Pantheon and the Colosseum that have survived largely intact."
- **Q26 C:** "the Germanic chieftain Odoacer deposed the last Western Roman emperor, Romulus Augustulus, in an event that historians conventionally mark as the fall of the Western Roman Empire."

**Passage 3 — AI Ethics**

- **Q27 B:** "AI systems learn their behaviours from data and reward signals... it is technically difficult to guarantee that a powerful optimising system will not find unintended ways to achieve the goals it has been given."
- **Q28 C:** "error rates of up to 34.7 per cent for darker-skinned women."
- **Q29 B:** "established a right to explanation for individuals subject to automated decisions with significant effects on their lives."
- **Q30 C:** "superintelligence is a speculative extrapolation from current capabilities and that the more immediate and tractable AI risks are those relating to bias, misuse, and inadequate governance."
- **Q31 TRUE:** "the models will tend to reproduce and often amplify those inequalities."
- **Q32 FALSE:** "No international treaty governing autonomous weapons currently exists."
- **Q33 TRUE:** ProPublica "found that the algorithm incorrectly flagged Black defendants as future criminals at nearly twice the rate of white defendants" — cho thấy sự bất bình đẳng chủng tộc rõ ràng.
- **Q34 NOT GIVEN:** GDPR không đề cập tỷ lệ chính xác tối thiểu 90%.
- **Q35 TRUE:** "Systems classified as 'high risk', including AI used in education, employment, critical infrastructure, and law enforcement."
- **Q36 FALSE:** "This position is contested by prominent AI researchers such as Yann LeCun" — LeCun không đồng ý với Bostrom về rủi ro sinh tồn.
- **Q37 alignment:** "the alignment problem — ensuring AI systems pursue genuinely beneficial goals."
- **Q38 inequalities:** "if that data reflects existing social inequalities."
- **Q39 disparities:** Bài đọc ghi nhận sự chênh lệch chủng tộc (racial disparities) trong kết quả của thuật toán COMPAS.
- **Q40 risk:** "The EU AI Act adopts a risk-based approach."
