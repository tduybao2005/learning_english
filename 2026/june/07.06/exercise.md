## BÀI TẬP 3: /v/ vs /b/ — Luyện đọc cặp tối thiểu — 15 phút

Đọc to 5 lần mỗi cặp, chú ý vị trí miệng:

| /v/ | /b/ | Nghĩa |
|---|---|---|
| very | berry | rất / quả mọng |
| van | ban | xe tải nhỏ / cấm |
| vale | bail | thung lũng / bảo lãnh |
| vest | best | áo gile / tốt nhất |
| vet | bet | bác sĩ thú y / cược |

**Quy tắc nhớ:**
- /b/ = hai môi khép lại, bật ra
- /v/ = răng trên nhẹ nhàng chạm môi dưới, hơi thổi ra liên tục

## BÀI TẬP 5: Shadowing & Dictation — 20 phút

**Phần A: Shadowing (10 phút)**
Đọc to đoạn văn sau, chú ý phát âm các âm /θ/, /ð/, /v/:

> "The theory of autonomous robotics involves three fundamental principles. First, the robot must think and make decisions independently. Second, it needs to navigate through its environment without human help. Third, it must have the ability to recognize and avoid obstacles. These methods require a vast amount of data and sophisticated algorithms. Although the technology is complex, researchers believe that the value it provides to society is worth the effort."

*Chú ý các từ cần đặc biệt luyện:*
- /θ/: theory, three, think, through, third, worth
- /ð/: the, they, this, these, although
- /v/: involves, vast, value, provide

**Phần B: Dictation (10 phút)**
1. They think the theory needs three more tests.
2. The method involves both design and development.
3. This technology has great value for medicine.
4. Although it is difficult, they believe it is worth the effort.
5. The robot navigates through the corridor using the algorithm.
6. Listen carefully and write down what you hear.
7. Knowledge is the most valuable thing we can gain.
8. The design team climbed to the roof to test the signal.
