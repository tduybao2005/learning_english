## BÀI TẬP 1: Đặt câu hỏi Wh- phù hợp — 20 phút

Nhìn vào câu trả lời và đặt câu hỏi đúng:

1. ______________________________? — I study Electrical Engineering. (What)
2. ______________________________? — The lab is on the third floor of Building B. (Where)
3. ______________________________? — Classes start at 7:30 AM. (What time / When)
4. ______________________________? — Because the sensor was broken. (Why)
5. ______________________________? — Professor Nguyen designed this robot. (Who)
6. ______________________________? — It costs about 5 million VND. (How much)
7. ______________________________? — We use the DWA algorithm. (Which / What)
8. ______________________________? — It takes about 30 seconds to navigate the corridor. (How long)
9. ______________________________? — I study English three times a week. (How often)
10. ______________________________? — This laptop belongs to Nam. (Whose)
11. ______________________________? — The robot can reach 0.8 m/s. (How fast)
12. ______________________________? — By using LIDAR and path planning algorithms. (How)

**Đáp án:**
1. What do you study?
2. Where is the lab?
3. What time / When do classes start?
4. Why did the robot stop? / Why is the sensor broken?
5. Who designed this robot?
6. How much does it cost?
7. Which / What algorithm do you use?
8. How long does it take to navigate the corridor?
9. How often do you study English?
10. Whose laptop is this?
11. How fast can the robot move?
12. How does the robot navigate?

## BÀI TẬP 2: Câu hỏi gián tiếp — 20 phút

Chuyển từ câu hỏi trực tiếp → gián tiếp:

1. Where is the manual? → Could you tell me _________________?
2. What does she do after class? → I wonder _________________?
3. Why did the project fail? → Do you know _________________?
4. How long will it take? → Could you tell me _________________?
5. How many bugs are there? → Can you tell me _________________?
6. When is the deadline? → I'd like to know _________________?
7. Who wrote this algorithm? → Do you have any idea _________________?
8. Which version should I download? → Could you advise me on _________________?

**Đáp án:**
1. where the manual is
2. what she does after class
3. why the project failed
4. how long it will take
5. how many bugs there are
6. when the deadline is
7. who wrote this algorithm
8. which version I should download

## BÀI TẬP 3: Diễn đạt lý do — 20 phút

Dùng because / because of / due to / since / the reason is that để điền vào câu:

1. The demo was cancelled _______ a technical failure.
2. I was late _______ I missed the bus.
3. She passed the exam _______ her consistent practice.
4. We stopped the test _______ the battery was at 5%.
5. _______ I've been studying daily, my English has improved a lot.
6. _______ the project was delayed _______ budget constraints.
7. The robot crashed _______ a bug in the path-planning code.
8. I can't submit today _______ I haven't finished writing it yet.

**Đáp án:**
1. due to / because of | 2. because | 3. because of / due to | 4. because | 5. Since | 6. The reason is that / due to | 7. because of / due to | 8. because

## BÀI TẬP 4: Đọc & Trả lời câu hỏi — 20 phút

> "The HospitGo robot was designed with a specific purpose in mind: to assist hospital staff by delivering medicine, documents, and equipment between floors. The team chose to use ROS 2 as the main framework because it offers real-time support and better security than its predecessor.
>
> In order to navigate safely, the robot uses a combination of LIDAR, ultrasonic sensors, and cameras. The reason it uses multiple sensors is that each one has different strengths — LIDAR is accurate but expensive, while ultrasonic sensors are cheap but limited in range.
>
> How fast can the robot move? Under normal conditions, it moves at 0.5 m/s. How long does a typical delivery take? From floor 1 to floor 3, it usually takes about 90 seconds, including elevator waiting time.
>
> The team faced many challenges. Why did the first version fail? Due to a design flaw in the navigation algorithm, the robot would stop randomly in crowded environments. This was fixed through extensive testing and parameter tuning."

**Câu hỏi:**
1. What was HospitGo designed to do?
2. Why did the team choose ROS 2?
3. What sensors does the robot use?
4. Why does it use multiple sensors?
5. How fast does the robot move?
6. How long does a delivery from floor 1 to floor 3 take?
7. Why did the first version fail?
8. How was the problem fixed?

**Đáp án:**
1. To assist hospital staff by delivering medicine, documents, and equipment.
2. Because it offers real-time support and better security.
3. LIDAR, ultrasonic sensors, and cameras.
4. Because each sensor has different strengths.
5. 0.5 m/s under normal conditions.
6. About 90 seconds.
7. Due to a design flaw in the navigation algorithm.
8. Through extensive testing and parameter tuning.
