## BÀI TẬP 1: Điền tính từ đúng vào chỗ trống — 15 phút

Dùng từ trong ngoặc đặt đúng vị trí:

1. The _______ robot (autonomous) can navigate without human control.
2. She is _______ with the new software. (familiar)
3. The results were _______. (inaccurate)
4. He is _______ about the upcoming presentation. (nervous)
5. This is a very _______ problem. (complex)
6. I am _______ for testing the hardware. (responsible)
7. The sensor gave very _______ readings. (accurate)
8. We need a more _______ approach. (practical)

**Đáp án:**
1. autonomous | 2. familiar (familiar with) | 3. inaccurate | 4. nervous | 5. complex | 6. responsible (responsible for) | 7. accurate | 8. practical

## BÀI TẬP 2: Sắp xếp thứ tự tính từ — 20 phút

3. a (Japanese / new / excellent / compact) processor
4. two (autonomous / hospital / modern / white) robots

**Đáp án:**

3. an excellent (opinion) compact (size) new (age) Japanese (origin) processor
4. two excellent (opinion) modern (age) white (color) autonomous hospital robots

## BÀI TẬP 3: Adjective + Preposition — 20 phút

Điền giới từ đúng:

1. I'm very interested _______ machine learning.
2. She's really good _______ writing academic essays.
3. Are you familiar _______ the new testing framework?
4. We're excited _______ the conference next month.
5. He's worried _______ the upcoming exam.
6. Who is responsible _______ maintaining the server?
7. This robot is different _______ the ones we built before.
8. I'm satisfied _______ my progress this month.
9. She is proud _______ her team's achievement.
10. Are you capable _______ solving this without help?

**Đáp án:**
1. in | 2. at | 3. with | 4. about | 5. about | 6. for | 7. from | 8. with | 9. of | 10. of

## BÀI TẬP 5: Đọc hiểu — 15 phút

> "In the modern robotics lab, the equipment is both impressive and expensive. The new robotic arm is a large, precise, Japanese industrial machine. It is very different from the old, small, plastic prototype we built last year.
>
> The team working on the project is motivated and skilled. Linh is particularly good at mechanical design — she is responsible for the structural components. Nam is familiar with the software side; he is excellent at writing clean, efficient code. Bao is the team leader, and he is determined to make the project successful.
>
> There are challenges, of course. The most critical issue right now is the inaccurate sensor data. The team is worried about this problem, but they are confident they can find a reliable solution."

**Câu hỏi:**
1. Describe the new robotic arm using adjectives from the text.
2. What is Linh responsible for?
3. What is Nam good at?
4. What is the most critical issue?
5. How does the team feel about the problem?
6. Find 5 "Adjective + Preposition" combinations in the text.

**Đáp án:**
1. Large, precise, Japanese, industrial.
2. She is responsible for the structural components.
3. He is good at writing clean, efficient code.
4. The most critical issue is the inaccurate sensor data.
5. They are worried but confident.
6. different from / good at / familiar with / responsible for / worried about / confident (about) [any 5]
