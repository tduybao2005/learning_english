## BÀI TẬP 1: Chọn liên từ đúng (FANBOYS) — 20 phút

Điền and / but / or / so / yet / for:

1. I studied hard, _______ I still didn't pass the speaking section.
2. The sensor was broken, _______ we had to order a new one.
3. You can use Python, _______ you can use C++ for this project.
4. She coded the algorithm, _______ he wrote the documentation.
5. The demo went well, _______ we were proud of what we achieved.
6. He hadn't slept in 24 hours, _______ he was still alert and focused.
7. We couldn't finish the project, _______ the deadline was too tight.
8. The result wasn't perfect, _______ it was a significant improvement.
9. Fix the bug before 5 PM, _______ the professor will see the errors.
10. She majors in AI, _______ he specializes in mechanical engineering.

**Đáp án:**
1. but | 2. so | 3. or | 4. and | 5. and/for | 6. yet | 7. for | 8. but/yet | 9. or | 10. and/but

## BÀI TẬP 2: Nối hai câu thành câu ghép — 20 phút

Nối hai câu đơn thành câu ghép dùng FANBOYS phù hợp:

1. I wanted to study English. I didn't have enough time.
2. The code is efficient. The memory usage is too high.
3. We can redesign the algorithm. We can optimize the current one.
4. The robot failed the navigation test. We restarted it and tried again.
5. She works very long hours. She never complains about it.
6. He finished the assignment. He submitted it before midnight.
7. I didn't understand the problem. I asked my professor for help.
8. You should study today. You will regret it during the exam.

**Đáp án (có thể có nhiều đáp án):**
1. I wanted to study English, but I didn't have enough time.
2. The code is efficient, but the memory usage is too high.
3. We can redesign the algorithm, or we can optimize the current one.
4. The robot failed the navigation test, so we restarted it and tried again.
5. She works very long hours, yet she never complains about it.
6. He finished the assignment, and he submitted it before midnight.
7. I didn't understand the problem, so I asked my professor for help.
8. You should study today, or you will regret it during the exam.

## BÀI TẬP 3: Liên từ phụ thuộc — 20 phút

Điền liên từ phù hợp: although / because / when / while / if / before / after / until / so that / as soon as

1. _______ I was debugging, my teammate was writing the documentation.
2. _______ it was raining, we continued the outdoor robot test.
3. _______ the robot reached the destination, it sent a notification.
4. I studied hard _______ I could understand every concept clearly.
5. _______ you fix this bug, the system won't work correctly.
6. _______ I fix the issue, I'll let you know immediately.
7. Calibrate the sensors _______ starting the navigation test.
8. We worked on the project _______ the problem was completely resolved.
9. _______ you study consistently, your English will improve rapidly.
10. _______ he was exhausted, he pushed through and finished the report.

**Đáp án:**
1. While | 2. Although | 3. When/As soon as | 4. so that | 5. Unless/Until | 6. As soon as | 7. before | 8. until | 9. If | 10. Although/Even though

## BÀI TẬP 4: Dịch Việt → Anh — 20 phút

1. Tôi đã làm việc chăm chỉ, nhưng tôi không kịp deadline.
2. Cảm biến bị hỏng, vì vậy chúng tôi phải đặt mua cái mới.
3. Mặc dù code phức tạp, kết quả rất ấn tượng.
4. Bạn có thể dùng Python hoặc C++ cho dự án này.
5. Tôi đã học rất nhiều kể từ khi bắt đầu ôn thi IELTS.
6. Sau khi sửa lỗi, robot hoạt động hoàn hảo.
7. Trừ khi bạn luyện tập mỗi ngày, bạn sẽ không cải thiện được.
8. Hệ thống bị crash do đó chúng tôi phải khởi động lại server.

**Đáp án:**
1. I worked hard, but I didn't meet the deadline.
2. The sensor broke, so we had to order a new one.
3. Although the code is complex, the results are very impressive.
4. You can use Python or C++ for this project.
5. I have learned a lot since I started preparing for IELTS.
6. After fixing the bug, the robot worked perfectly.
7. Unless you practice every day, you won't improve.
8. The system crashed; consequently, we had to restart the server.
