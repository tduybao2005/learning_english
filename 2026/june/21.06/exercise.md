## BÀI TẬP 1: Will hay Be going to? — 20 phút

1. Look at the schedule — we _______ (present) the project next Friday. *(be going to)*
2. The battery is almost empty. The robot _______ (stop) soon. *(be going to)*
3. Wait, I _______ (carry) those heavy components for you. *(will — instant offer)*
4. I think future robots _______ (work) alongside humans in hospitals. *(will — prediction)*
5. She has already packed her bags — she _______ (fly) to Canada tomorrow. *(be going to)*
6. I promise I _______ (not, miss) the deadline this time. *(will — promise)*
7. The weather looks bad — it _______ (rain) during the outdoor test. *(be going to)*
8. A: I need help debugging. B: Don't worry, I _______ (help) you. *(will — instant decision)*
9. He _______ (study) for his IELTS exam this weekend — he told me about it. *(be going to)*
10. I don't know what the result will be, but I'm optimistic — I think we _______ (succeed). *(will)*

**Đáp án:**
1. are going to present | 2. is going to stop | 3. will carry | 4. will work | 5. is going to fly | 6. won't miss | 7. is going to rain | 8. will help | 9. is going to study | 10. will succeed

## BÀI TẬP 2: Kết hợp 4 thì — 20 phút

Chọn thì đúng:

1. By the time I graduate, I _______ (study) English for three years. *(future perfect: will have studied)*
2. I _______ (just, finish) my report when you called. *(past perfect: had just finished)*
3. She _______ (work) on this project since the beginning of the semester. *(present perfect: has been working)*
4. _______ you _______ (ever, visit) Canada? *(present perfect: Have you ever visited)*
5. We _______ (not, complete) the full system yet, but we're close. *(present perfect: haven't completed)*
6. I _______ (call) you as soon as I _______ (arrive) at the lab. *(will call / arrive)*
7. He _______ (be) an engineer for five years before he _______ (decide) to go back to school. *(had been / decided)*
8. Unless the team _______ (fix) the bug, the demo _______ (fail) tomorrow. *(fixes / will fail)*

**Đáp án:**
1. will have studied | 2. had just finished | 3. has been working | 4. Have you ever visited | 5. haven't completed | 6. will call / arrive | 7. had been / decided | 8. fixes / will fail

## BÀI TẬP 4: Nói về tương lai của bạn — 15 phút

Viết 8-10 câu trả lời cho câu hỏi IELTS Speaking sau:

**"What are your plans for the next five years?"**

*Dùng ít nhất:*
- 2 câu với "be going to" (kế hoạch cụ thể)
- 2 câu với "will" (dự đoán/hy vọng)
- 1 câu Present Perfect (điều đã hoàn thành)
- 1 câu "plan/intend/hope to"

**Ví dụ mẫu:**
> "Over the next five years, I have several goals that I am working towards. First of all, I am going to complete my degree in Electrical Engineering at Industrial University by 2027. I am also going to take the IELTS exam by the end of this year and aim for a score of 7.0.
>
> In terms of my career, I plan to work in the robotics and AI field, specifically in healthcare robotics. I have already gained valuable experience through the HospitGo project, which I believe will open many doors for me. I think autonomous systems will play an increasingly important role in hospitals, and I hope to contribute to that development.
>
> Additionally, I intend to pursue a master's degree in the future — possibly at a university in Canada, where my brother currently lives. I believe this experience will significantly broaden my perspective and enhance my technical skills."
