## BÀI TẬP 1: Nhận biết loại từ — 15 phút

Xác định loại từ (N/V/Adj/Adv) của từ được gạch chân:

1. The robot **navigates** the hospital corridors **autonomously**.
2. The **accuracy** of the sensor system is **extremely** high.
3. An **automated** delivery system can work **continuously** without breaks.
4. We need to **analyze** the data **carefully** before drawing conclusions.
5. The **development** of AI has been **rapid** in recent years.
6. She **efficiently** completed the **complex** task in record time.
7. The **implementation** of the new algorithm **significantly** improved performance.

**Đáp án:**
1. navigates (V), autonomously (Adv) | 2. accuracy (N), extremely (Adv) | 3. automated (Adj), continuously (Adv) | 4. analyze (V), carefully (Adv) | 5. development (N), rapid (Adj) | 6. efficiently (Adv), complex (Adj) | 7. implementation (N), significantly (Adv)

## BÀI TẬP 2: Điền đúng dạng từ — 20 phút

Dùng dạng đúng của từ trong ngoặc:

1. The robot moved _______ through the crowded corridor. (efficiency)
2. We need to _______ the current system before adding new features. (optimization)
3. The new design is far more _______ than the previous version. (reliability)
4. She _______ the LIDAR data to map the environment. (analysis)
5. The algorithm operates _______ without any human input. (autonomous)
6. There is a growing _______ for healthcare robotics engineers. (demand)
7. The system should be able to _______ to different environments. (adaptation)
8. The navigation module works with _______ precision. (remarkable)
9. He is an _______ programmer — he can code in five different languages. (experience)
10. The sensors _______ the surroundings in real-time. (monitor)

**Đáp án:**
1. efficiently | 2. optimize | 3. reliable | 4. analyzed | 5. autonomously | 6. demand | 7. adapt | 8. remarkable | 9. experienced | 10. monitor
