### 3. Tần suất (Frequency Expressions)
- **every day**: mỗi ngày
- **every morning/evening**: mỗi buổi sáng/tối
- **three times a week**: 3 lần một tuần
- **once a month**: một lần mỗi tháng
- **on weekdays**: vào các ngày trong tuần
- **at the weekend**: vào cuối tuần
- **from time to time**: thỉnh thoảng
- **without fail**: không bao giờ bỏ lỡ

### 4. Cấu trúc mô tả thói quen

| Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|---|---|
| **I usually + V** | I usually wake up at 6 AM. |
| **I make it a habit to + V** | I make it a habit to review vocabulary every night. |
| **I try to + V** | I try to exercise at least three times a week. |
| **I tend to + V** | I tend to stay up late when I'm coding. |
| **I have a routine of + V-ing** | I have a routine of studying English in the evening. |
| **I've gotten into the habit of + V-ing** | I've gotten into the habit of planning my day the night before. |

### 5. Hội thoại thực tế — IELTS Speaking Part 1
> **Q:** Can you describe a typical day in your life?
> **A:** Sure! I usually wake up around 6 AM. I brush my teeth, have a quick breakfast — usually some bread and coffee — and then commute to university. Classes typically run from 7:30 to 11:30 in the morning. After lunch, I either attend an afternoon lab session or go to the library to study independently.
>
> In the evenings, I make it a point to review English vocabulary for at least 30 minutes. I've gotten into the habit of doing dictation exercises before bed. I usually wind down around 10 PM with some light reading and then try to be in bed by 11 PM. On weekends, I allow myself to sleep in until 7 or 7:30.

### 6. Healthy vs Unhealthy Habits (cho Writing Task 2)
**Healthy:** regular exercise, balanced diet, consistent sleep schedule, daily study
**Unhealthy:** staying up too late, skipping meals, too much screen time, sedentary lifestyle
