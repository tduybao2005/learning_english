## 2. PHRASES CHO THESIS STATEMENT

| Loại bài | Thesis phrase |
|---|---|
| Agree | "I firmly believe that...", "I strongly agree that...", "In my view, the benefits clearly outweigh..." |
| Disagree | "I completely disagree with this view...", "In my opinion, the disadvantages far outweigh..." |
| Both sides | "While there are valid arguments on both sides, I believe..." |
| Problem-Solution | "This essay will examine the main causes of... and propose practical solutions." |

## 3. TỪ CHO BODY PARAGRAPHS

### Giới thiệu ý chính:
- "One of the main advantages/disadvantages is that..."
- "Perhaps the most significant reason is..."
- "A key argument in favor of/against this is..."
- "First and foremost, it is important to consider..."

### Giải thích & Mở rộng:
- "This is because..." / "The reason for this is that..."
- "To put it another way..." / "In other words..."
- "This means that..." / "As a result..."

### Đưa ra ví dụ:
- "For example, in Vietnam, robots are being used to..."
- "A clear illustration of this is the case of HospitGo, which..."
- "Research has shown that..." / "Studies indicate that..."

### Kết thúc đoạn:
- "Therefore, it is clear that..." / "Consequently..."
- "This demonstrates that..."
