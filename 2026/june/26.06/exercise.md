## BÀI TẬP 1: Ôn tập tổng hợp ngữ pháp — 30 phút

Điền dạng đúng của từ/chia động từ (tổng hợp tất cả kiến thức):

1. If the sensors _______ (calibrate) correctly, the robot _______ (navigate) much better.
2. The prototype _______ (design) by our team and _______ (test) over a three-month period.
3. She _______ (study) English _______ (for/since) she was in high school and her skills _______ (improve) greatly.
4. I _______ (not, understand) why the algorithm _______ (fail) during the test last night.
5. Unless you _______ (submit) the application by Friday, you _______ (not, consider) for the scholarship.
6. Look! The robot _______ (just, stop) — the battery _______ (die).
7. There _______ (be) several improvements _______ (make) to the system since the last demonstration.
8. Although the project _______ (be) very challenging, we never _______ (consider) giving up.
9. By the time the professor arrives, we _______ (finish) setting up the demonstration.
10. The data _______ (collect) automatically by the sensors every hour.

**Đáp án:**
1. are calibrated / will navigate | 2. was designed / was tested | 3. has been studying / since / has improved | 4. don't understand / failed | 5. submit / won't be considered | 6. has just stopped / is dying/has died | 7. have been / made | 8. was / considered | 9. will have finished | 10. are collected / is collected

## BÀI TẬP 2: Paraphrase đề bài IELTS — 15 phút

Viết lại các câu sau bằng từ khác nhau, giữ nguyên ý nghĩa:

1. "Many people think that robots will replace human workers in the future."
2. "Technology is becoming increasingly important in education."
3. "The government should do more to protect the environment."
4. "More and more young people are choosing to study abroad."

**Ví dụ mẫu:**
1. → "A growing number of individuals believe that automated machines and AI will take over jobs currently performed by humans in the coming decades."
2. → "Technological advancement is playing an increasingly significant role in modern educational settings."
3. → "Authorities and policymakers need to implement stronger measures to preserve and protect the natural environment."
4. → "An increasing proportion of young adults are opting to pursue their studies at overseas institutions."

## BÀI TẬP 3: Viết đoạn Body IELTS Task 2 — 40 phút

**Đề bài:** *"Some people believe that the use of robots in manufacturing will lead to widespread unemployment. Others think it will create new types of jobs. Discuss both views and give your opinion."*

**Nhiệm vụ:** Viết **2 đoạn body** (mỗi đoạn 5-6 câu) bàn về:
- Body 1: Robots gây thất nghiệp hàng loạt
- Body 2: Robots tạo ra việc làm mới

**Dùng:**
- Ít nhất 3 từ nối học thuật mỗi đoạn
- Câu bị động ít nhất 1 lần mỗi đoạn
- Ví dụ cụ thể

**Mẫu Body 1:**
> "Those who argue that robotics leads to unemployment point to the displacement of workers in traditional industries. As manufacturing processes are increasingly automated, many low-skilled workers find themselves unable to compete with machines that can perform the same tasks faster and more cost-effectively. For example, in countries like China, millions of factory workers have been replaced by robotic assembly lines over the past decade. As a result, unemployment rates in certain sectors have risen significantly, particularly among those without higher education or technical skills. Consequently, entire communities that relied on manufacturing have been economically devastated."

**Gợi ý Body 2 — Robots tạo việc làm mới:**
- Topic sentence: On the other hand, the rise of robotics has also generated entirely new categories of employment.
- Explanation: industries need engineers, programmers, maintenance technicians
- Example: robot maintenance engineers, AI trainers, data analysts
- Result: demand for these roles is growing rapidly

## BÀI TẬP 4: Viết Introduction & Conclusion — 20 phút

**Đề:** *"In many countries, the government provides funding for the arts. Some people believe this money should be spent on more important things. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"*

**Viết Introduction và Conclusion cho bài này.**

**Mẫu Introduction:**
> "In numerous nations around the world, governments allocate a portion of public funding to support artistic endeavors such as music, theatre, and fine arts. While some argue that this expenditure is wasteful and that resources should be directed toward more pressing social needs, others maintain that the arts play a vital role in cultural development. I strongly believe that, while basic needs must always take priority, a reasonable level of arts funding is both justified and beneficial to society."

**Mẫu Conclusion:**
> "In conclusion, while it is understandable that some people prioritize practical expenditure over arts funding, I maintain that the two are not necessarily incompatible. A well-functioning society requires both material welfare and cultural enrichment. Therefore, governments should continue to support the arts, albeit in a measured and targeted manner, ensuring that essential services receive adequate resources first."

## BÀI TẬP 5: Phân tích bài viết — 20 phút

Đọc đoạn sau và đánh giá theo 4 tiêu chí IELTS:

> "Nowadays robot is use in hospital. They help doctor and nurse to do work. For example, deliver medicine. This is good because save time. However, some people think robot cannot replace human because patient need human. I agree that robot is helpful but human also important. In conclusion robot and human should work together."

**Câu hỏi phân tích:**
1. Task Achievement: Bài có trả lời đề bài không? Có đủ ý không?
2. Coherence: Các câu có liên kết logic không? Thiếu gì?
3. Lexical Resource: Từ vựng có phong phú không? Liệt kê 5 từ nên nâng cấp.
4. Grammar: Tìm và sửa 5 lỗi ngữ pháp.

**Đáp án phân tích:**
1. TA: Có nhắc đến ý kiến nhưng quá ngắn, thiếu phân tích sâu và ví dụ cụ thể
2. CC: Thiếu từ nối học thuật, chuyển đoạn đột ngột, không rõ cấu trúc
3. LR: "robot" → autonomous robot / robotic system; "good" → beneficial; "help" → assist; "work" → tasks; "important" → essential
4. Lỗi ngữ pháp:
   - "robot **is** use" → "robots **are** used" (bị động, số nhiều)
   - "deliver medicine" → "**such as** delivering medicine" (dùng ví dụ đúng cú pháp)
   - "save time" → "**it** save**s** time" (chủ ngữ + chia động từ)
   - "robot cannot replace" → "robots cannot replace"
   - "robot is helpful" → "robots **are** helpful"
