## BÀI TẬP 1: Phân loại đuôi -ed — 25 phút

Phân loại các từ sau vào đúng cột /t/, /d/, hoặc /ɪd/:

*worked, needed, called, started, checked, used, tested, finished, learned, wanted, fixed, saved, visited, helped, opened, detected, studied, crashed, edited, configured*

| /t/ | /d/ | /ɪd/ |
|---|---|---|
| | | |

**Đáp án:**
- **/t/**: worked, checked, finished, fixed, helped, crashed
- **/d/**: called, used, learned, saved, opened, studied, configured
- **/ɪd/**: needed, started, tested, wanted, visited, detected, edited

## BÀI TẬP 3: Điền vào câu + đọc to — 20 phút

4. He _______ (fix) the bug and _______ (save) his work.
5. They _______ (start) the project in February and _______ (end) it in May.
6. We _______ (learn) a lot from this experience.
7. She _______ (want) to apply for an internship abroad.

**Đáp án (với phát âm đuôi):**
1. worked /t/ | 2. detected /ɪd/ | 3. tested /ɪd/ | 4. fixed /t/ / saved /d/ | 5. started /ɪd/ / ended /ɪd/ | 6. learned /d/ | 7. wanted /ɪd/ | 8. processed /t/ / generated /ɪd/

## BÀI TẬP 4: Tính từ quá khứ phân từ — 15 phút

Điền tính từ phù hợp:

3. I'm _______ in learning about natural language processing. (excited / interested)
4. After 10 hours of coding, I was completely _______. (excited / exhausted)

8. We need an _______ developer for this project. (experienced / new)

1. excited | 2. frustrated | 3. interested | 4. exhausted | 5. surprised | 6. dedicated | 7. advanced | 8. experienced

## BÀI TẬP 5: Shadowing & Dictation — 10 phút

Đọc to các câu sau, chú ý phát âm đuôi -ed:

1. She worked all night and finished the code by morning.
2. The team tested the robot and detected three problems.
3. He wanted to update the system but decided to wait.
4. They studied the results and learned from their mistakes.
5. The algorithm loaded the data and started processing immediately.
6. I fixed the bug, saved my work, and pushed the commit.
7. She was excited when the robot navigated successfully.
8. The engineer visited the lab and checked the equipment.

*Chú ý khi đọc:*
- worked /t/, finished /t/ | tested /ɪd/, detected /ɪd/, wanted /ɪd/, decided /ɪd/ | studied /d/, learned /d/, saved /d/, fixed /t/
