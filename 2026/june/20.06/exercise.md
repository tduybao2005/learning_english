## BÀI TẬP 1: Chia động từ — Simple Past hay Present Perfect? — 25 phút

1. I _______ (fix) the bug at midnight last night.
2. She _______ (never, fail) an exam since she entered university.
3. We _______ (complete) the first prototype last semester.
4. _______ you _______ (ever, use) ROS 2 before?
5. He _______ (not, finish) the report yet.
6. When _______ you _______ (start) learning Python?
7. They _______ (make) significant improvements since the last version.
8. The robot _______ (stop) working three times yesterday.
9. I _______ (just, send) you the updated file.
10. How long _______ she _______ (study) at Industrial University?
11. We _______ (present) our project at the tech fair two weeks ago.
12. I _______ (not, see) him since the semester began.

**Đáp án:**
1. fixed | 2. has never failed | 3. completed | 4. Have you ever used | 5. hasn't finished | 6. did you start | 7. have made | 8. stopped | 9. have just sent | 10. has she studied | 11. presented | 12. haven't seen

## BÀI TẬP 2: Since hay For? — 10 phút

1. I have been studying English _______ three months.
2. She has lived in HCMC _______ she was born.
3. We have been working on this robot _______ last September.
4. He hasn't slept _______ more than 24 hours.
5. They have been friends _______ they first met in 2019.

**Đáp án:** 1. for | 2. since | 3. since | 4. for | 5. since

## BÀI TẬP 3: Sửa lỗi — 15 phút

1. I have seen him yesterday at the lab.
2. Did you ever visit a hospital robot demonstration?
3. She has graduated last year.
4. Have you finished the report two hours ago?
5. He worked here since 2023.
6. We've already submitted the project last Monday.
7. I never used nvim before — this is my first time.

**Đáp án:**
1. I **saw** him yesterday. (yesterday → Simple Past)
2. **Have** you **ever visited** a hospital robot demo? (ever → Present Perfect)
3. She **graduated** last year. (last year → Simple Past)
4. **Did** you finish the report two hours ago? (two hours ago → Simple Past)
5. He **has worked** here since 2023. (since → Present Perfect)
6. We submitted the project last Monday. (last Monday → Simple Past) OR We've already submitted the project. (remove "last Monday")
7. I have **never used** nvim before. (never with Present Perfect)

## BÀI TẬP 4: Dịch Việt → Anh — 20 phút

1. Tôi đã sửa lỗi đó vào tối qua lúc 11 giờ đêm.
2. Cô ấy chưa bao giờ thấy một robot bệnh viện hoạt động ngoài thực tế.
3. Chúng tôi đã bắt đầu dự án này từ tháng 9 năm ngoái.
4. Bạn đã hoàn thành bài tập chưa? — Rồi, tôi vừa nộp xong.
5. Anh ấy đã trình bày dự án ở hội chợ khoa học tuần trước.
6. Kể từ khi chúng tôi cập nhật thuật toán, robot hoạt động tốt hơn nhiều.
7. Tôi đã học lập trình được 3 năm rồi và vẫn đang tiếp tục học.
8. Nhóm đã gặp nhiều vấn đề, nhưng họ đã giải quyết tất cả.

**Đáp án:**
1. I fixed that bug at 11 PM last night.
2. She has never seen a hospital robot operating in real life.
3. We started this project in September last year.
4. Have you finished the assignment yet? — Yes, I have just submitted it.
5. He presented the project at the science fair last week.
6. Since we updated the algorithm, the robot has been working much better.
7. I have been studying programming for 3 years and am still learning.
8. The team encountered many problems, but they resolved all of them.

## BÀI TẬP 5: Đọc — Điền đúng thì — 10 phút

> "When I (1. be) _______ a first-year student, I (2. not know) _______ anything about robotics. I (3. start) _______ learning Python in my second semester, and since then, everything (4. change) _______ dramatically.
>
> Last year, our team (5. build) _______ HospitGo from scratch. We (6. face) _______ many challenges along the way, but we (7. never give up) _______. We (8. present) _______ the robot at three exhibitions so far, and the feedback (9. be) _______ very positive.
>
> I (10. learn) _______ a lot from this project. I now believe that with enough effort, any engineering challenge can be overcome."

**Đáp án:**
1. was | 2. didn't know | 3. started | 4. has changed | 5. built | 6. faced | 7. never gave up | 8. have presented | 9. has been | 10. have learned

## PHẦN DICTATION — 10 phút

1. I fixed the critical bug at midnight, and the robot has been working perfectly ever since.
2. Have you ever presented your project to a real audience outside of the university?
3. She has already submitted the report, but she hasn't received feedback yet.
4. We started this project six months ago and have made significant progress since then.
5. How long have you been studying English? — I've been studying for about two months.
