## BÀI TẬP 3: during / for / while — 15 phút

1. _______ the power outage, the robot stopped completely. (during/for/while)
2. I slept only 3 hours _______ the exam period. (during/for)
3. She was studying _______ the others were having dinner. (for/while)
4. They worked on the project _______ the entire semester. (during/for)
5. _______ I was debugging the code, Nam fixed the hardware. (During/While)
6. The robot navigated the corridor _______ 30 minutes without stopping. (during/for)

**Đáp án:**
1. During (noun follows) | 2. during (period without specific number) / for | 3. while (clause follows) | 4. for (specific duration) / during (period) | 5. While (clause follows) | 6. for (specific duration)

## BÀI TẬP 4: Sửa lỗi sai — 15 phút

1. I'll call you on the morning.
2. She was born in 2005, at May.
3. The test starts in 9 AM.
4. I haven't slept since 48 hours.
5. During three hours, we tested the robot.
6. We're going to meet at next Friday.
7. In the weekend, I usually relax.
8. He's been coding from 2 hours.

**Đáp án:**
1. ~~on~~ → **in** the morning
2. ~~at~~ → **in** May
3. ~~in~~ → **at** 9 AM
4. ~~since~~ → **for** 48 hours (since = mốc thời gian cụ thể)
5. ~~During~~ → **For** three hours (for + số lượng)
6. Bỏ "at" → ~~at~~ next Friday
7. ~~In~~ → **At** the weekend (BrE) / **On** the weekend (AmE)
8. ~~from~~ → **for** 2 hours

## BÀI TẬP 5: Đọc hiểu & Dịch — 20 phút

**Phần A: Đọc (10 phút)**
> "Our team has been working on the HospitGo project since February. During the first month, we focused entirely on the mechanical design. In March, we began coding. For two weeks in April, we tested the robot in the lab corridor. The demonstration is scheduled for next Monday at 10 AM in the main auditorium. By tomorrow, we need to submit a technical report. The professor asked us to submit it by 5 PM on Friday.
>
> After the demonstration, we plan to write a paper and submit it to a journal within three months. Until then, we will continue improving the robot's navigation in complex environments."

**Câu hỏi:**
1. Since when have they been working on the project?
2. What did they focus on during the first month?
3. How long did they test in April?
4. When and where is the demonstration?
5. What is the deadline for the technical report?
6. What will they do after the demonstration?

**Đáp án:**
1. Since February.
2. They focused on mechanical design.
3. For two weeks.
4. Next Monday at 10 AM in the main auditorium.
5. By 5 PM on Friday.
6. They plan to write a paper and submit it within three months.

**Phần B: Dịch (10 phút)**
1. Tôi thường học tiếng Anh vào buổi tối, từ 8 giờ tối đến 10 giờ tối.
2. Trong buổi thuyết trình, anh ấy không dùng điện thoại.
3. Robot đã hoạt động liên tục trong 3 tiếng mà không dừng lại.
4. Hãy nộp báo cáo trước 5 giờ chiều thứ Sáu.

**Đáp án:**
1. I usually study English in the evening, from 8 PM to 10 PM.
2. During the presentation, he didn't use his phone.
3. The robot operated continuously for 3 hours without stopping.
4. Submit the report by 5 PM on Friday.
