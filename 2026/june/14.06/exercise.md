## BÀI TẬP 1: Điền modal verb phù hợp — 20 phút

Chọn: should / shouldn't / must / mustn't / have to / don't have to / may / might

1. You _______ wear eye protection when working with circuits. (bắt buộc — quy định lab)
2. You _______ touch the high-voltage wires. It's extremely dangerous! (cấm)
3. I _______ submit the assignment today — the deadline was extended. (không cần)
4. She _______ be late. She called me and said she was on her way. (có thể ~40%)
5. You _______ back up your code regularly — otherwise you'll lose everything. (lời khuyên)
6. I _______ present my project next Monday. (bắt buộc từ lịch học)
7. He _______ be exhausted after working 14 hours straight. (suy luận chắc chắn)
8. It _______ rain this afternoon, but I'm not sure. (có thể ~50%)
9. Students _______ attend all lab sessions — it's a university rule. (bắt buộc)
10. You _______ use a dictionary during the exam. It's allowed. (có thể — được phép)

**Đáp án:**
1. must | 2. mustn't | 3. don't have to | 4. might | 5. should | 6. have to | 7. must | 8. may | 9. must/have to | 10. may/can

## BÀI TẬP 2: must vs have to vs don't have to vs mustn't — 20 phút

Giải thích sự khác biệt và dùng đúng:

1. "I _______ study more English." (Tôi tự nhắc nhở bản thân → must/should)
2. "I _______ submit the form by Monday." (Hạn chót từ văn phòng nhà trường → have to)
3. "You _______ wear formal clothes to the lab." (Không yêu cầu → don't have to)
4. "You _______ share your account password with anyone." (Cấm tuyệt đối → mustn't)
5. "She _______ pay for the software — it's free." (Không cần thiết → doesn't have to)
6. "Visitors _______ sign in at the reception." (Quy định → must/have to)

**Đáp án:**
1. must/should | 2. have to | 3. don't have to | 4. mustn't | 5. doesn't have to | 6. must/have to

## BÀI TẬP 3: Dịch Việt → Anh — 20 phút

1. Bạn nên học tiếng Anh mỗi ngày nếu bạn muốn đạt IELTS 7.0.
2. Tất cả sinh viên phải nộp báo cáo cuối kỳ trước ngày 30 tháng 6.
3. Bạn không cần phải tham gia buổi học bổ sung — nó không bắt buộc.
4. Bạn không được sử dụng điện thoại trong phòng thi.
5. Anh ấy chắc chắn rất mệt — anh ấy đã thức thâu đêm để gỡ lỗi.
6. Có thể trời sẽ mưa vào buổi chiều, vì vậy hãy mang theo ô.
7. Tôi không chắc, nhưng cô ấy có thể đã nộp báo cáo rồi.
8. Bạn nên sao lưu dữ liệu thường xuyên để tránh mất mát.

**Đáp án:**
1. You should study English every day if you want to achieve IELTS 7.0.
2. All students must submit the final report before June 30.
3. You don't have to attend the extra session — it's not compulsory.
4. You mustn't use your phone in the exam room.
5. He must be very tired — he stayed up all night debugging.
6. It may rain in the afternoon, so bring an umbrella.
7. I'm not sure, but she might have already submitted the report.
8. You should back up your data regularly to avoid losing it.

## BÀI TẬP 4: Đọc hiểu — 20 phút

> "Working in a robotics laboratory requires both technical knowledge and a strict adherence to safety regulations. Before entering the lab, all personnel must sign in and wear their ID badge. Students must not enter the lab without prior authorization from a supervisor.
>
> When working with electronic components, students should always check connections before applying power. They must not leave components unattended on the floor, as this may cause accidents. If an emergency occurs, everyone must evacuate immediately — no one should try to rescue equipment.
>
> Students don't have to attend lab sessions if they are unwell — in this case, they should notify their supervisor in advance. However, they must complete all missed sessions at an alternative time, as lab attendance is compulsory.
>
> The lab might be busy at peak times, so students may need to book equipment in advance. It's advisable to plan your schedule and arrive on time."

**Câu hỏi:**
1. What must all personnel do before entering the lab?
2. What are students not allowed to do without authorization?
3. What should students check before applying power?
4. What must everyone do in an emergency?
5. Do students have to attend if they are sick?
6. What must they do if they miss a session?
7. Find 5 different modal verbs in the text and state what each expresses.

**Đáp án:**
1. They must sign in and wear their ID badge.
2. They must not enter the lab.
3. They should check connections.
4. They must evacuate immediately.
5. No, they don't have to attend.
6. They must complete all missed sessions at an alternative time.
7. (Examples): must (obligation/compulsory rule) / must not (prohibition) / should (advice) / may (possibility) / don't have to (no obligation) / might (possibility)

## PHẦN DICTATION — 10 phút

1. You must wear safety equipment before entering the laboratory.
2. You mustn't share your password with anyone — it's against the rules.
3. I don't have to submit it today — the deadline was extended.
4. She might be late because of the traffic.
5. You should back up your code every day to avoid losing your work.
6. He must be exhausted after working all night without a break.
7. It may rain this afternoon, so you should bring an umbrella.
8. Students have to complete all assignments to pass the semester.
