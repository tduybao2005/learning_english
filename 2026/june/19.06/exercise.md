## BÀI TẬP 1: Chia động từ đúng thì — 20 phút

Dùng Simple Present hoặc Present Continuous:

1. Look! The robot _______ (navigate) through the corridor right now.
2. I _______ (study) at Industrial University in Ho Chi Minh City.
3. She usually _______ (check) the sensors before running a test.
4. Listen! Someone _______ (knock) on the lab door.
5. He _______ (not, understand) the new navigation algorithm yet.
6. The lab _______ (open) at 7 AM every morning.
7. We _______ (test) a new version of HospitGo this week.
8. My English _______ (improve) gradually thanks to daily practice.
9. I _______ (think) that ROS 2 is better for real-time applications.
10. They _______ (work) on a new project that _______ (involve) computer vision.
11. How many sensors _______ (the robot / have) in total?
12. The technology _______ (change) rapidly these days.

**Đáp án:**
1. is navigating | 2. study | 3. checks | 4. is knocking | 5. doesn't understand | 6. opens | 7. are testing | 8. is improving | 9. think | 10. are working / involves | 11. does the robot have | 12. is changing

## BÀI TẬP 2: Chọn đúng và giải thích lý do — 15 phút

1. I (study / am studying) English right now, so please don't disturb me.
2. She (believes / is believing) that hard work always pays off.
3. The train (leaves / is leaving) at 8:30 every morning.
4. What (do you do / are you doing) tonight? — I have plans.
5. He (thinks / is thinking) about switching from Python to C++ for this task.
6. AI (becomes / is becoming) increasingly important in healthcare.
7. My laptop (belongs / is belonging) to the university — I borrowed it.
8. I (smell / am smelling) something burning in the server room!

**Đáp án + Giải thích:**
1. am studying → đang xảy ra ngay lúc nói
2. believes → state verb (opinion) — không dùng -ing
3. leaves → lịch trình cố định
4. are you doing → hỏi về kế hoạch tương lai gần
5. is thinking → đang suy nghĩ (action, không phải opinion)
6. is becoming → xu hướng đang thay đổi
7. belongs → state verb (possession)
8. smell → cảm giác ngẫu nhiên (state) / am smelling → cố ý ngửi

## BÀI TẬP 3: Đặt câu với tần suất — 20 phút

Đặt câu hoàn chỉnh bằng cách sắp xếp đúng vị trí của từ tần suất:

1. (I / often / review / my notes / before an exam)
2. (She / never / forget / to back up / her work)
3. (He / usually / is / early / at the lab)
4. (sometimes / I / make mistakes / when I'm tired)
5. (rarely / the professor / cancel / classes)

**Đáp án:**
1. I often review my notes before an exam.
2. She never forgets to back up her work.
3. He is usually early at the lab. (Vị trí: after "is")
4. Sometimes I make mistakes when I'm tired. OR I sometimes make mistakes when I'm tired.
5. The professor rarely cancels classes.

## BÀI TẬP 4: Dịch Việt → Anh — 20 phút

1. Tôi đang viết code cho dự án robot ngay lúc này.
2. Chúng tôi thường kiểm tra cảm biến mỗi sáng trước khi bắt đầu thử nghiệm.
3. Nhìn kìa! Robot đang điều hướng qua hành lang mà không cần trợ giúp.
4. Tôi tin rằng AI sẽ thay đổi ngành y tế trong tương lai.
5. Dạo này tôi đang học tiếng Anh hàng ngày để chuẩn bị cho kỳ thi IELTS.
6. Cô ấy không hiểu tại sao thuật toán lại thất bại.
7. Anh ấy nghĩ gì về phiên bản mới của phần mềm? (đang suy nghĩ)
8. Công nghệ đang thay đổi rất nhanh trong thập kỷ này.

**Đáp án:**
1. I am writing code for the robot project right now.
2. We usually check the sensors every morning before starting the tests.
3. Look! The robot is navigating through the corridor without any assistance.
4. I believe that AI will transform the healthcare industry in the future.
5. These days I am studying English every day to prepare for the IELTS exam.
6. She doesn't understand why the algorithm failed.
7. What is he thinking about the new version of the software?
8. Technology is changing very rapidly this decade.

## BÀI TẬP 5: Sửa lỗi sai — 10 phút

1. She is knowing the answer to the question.
2. I am never eat breakfast before 7 AM.
3. Look! It rains outside — bring an umbrella.
4. We are having a meeting every Monday at 9 AM.
5. He don't understand the instructions for the robot.

**Đáp án:**
1. She **knows** the answer. (state verb)
2. I **never eat** breakfast before 7 AM. (never before main verb)
3. Look! It **is raining** outside. (happening right now)
4. We **have** a meeting every Monday. (routine, not continuous)
5. He **doesn't** understand the instructions. (3rd person singular)

## PHẦN DICTATION — 5 phút

1. I usually study English for two hours every evening before going to bed.

3. She is currently working on her thesis about autonomous navigation.
4. Technology is changing so rapidly that it's hard to keep up with everything.
5. The lab opens at 7 AM, but students rarely arrive that early.
