## BÀI TẬP 5: Dịch Việt → Anh + Đọc hiểu — 25 phút

**Phần A: Dịch (10 phút)**
1. Cô ấy giải thích thuật toán rất rõ ràng.
2. Robot di chuyển chậm và cẩn thận trong hành lang.
3. Tôi hầu như không bao giờ mắc lỗi ngữ pháp ngày nay.
4. Gần đây, tôi đã học được rất nhiều về mạng nơ-ron.
5. Chúng tôi luôn luôn kiểm tra code trước khi deploy.

**Đáp án:**
1. She explained the algorithm very clearly.
2. The robot moved slowly and carefully in the corridor.
3. I hardly ever make grammar mistakes nowadays.
4. Lately, I have learned a lot about neural networks.
5. We always check the code before deploying.

**Phần B: Đọc hiểu (15 phút)**
> "My IELTS study routine has changed significantly recently. I used to study randomly — sometimes I focused on grammar, sometimes vocabulary, and sometimes I just watched English videos. I rarely had a clear plan.
>
> Now, I systematically follow the study schedule I created. I always start with grammar for 30 minutes, then I carefully build my vocabulary. I usually finish with a reading or writing exercise. I hardly ever skip a study session anymore.
>
> My English has improved considerably. I can now read technical papers quite easily. I still speak rather slowly, but I'm improving consistently. I'm extremely motivated and I will never give up on this goal."

**Câu hỏi:**
1. How did Bao study before? Describe it using adverbs.
2. What does he do systematically now?
3. What does he always start with?
4. Does he often skip study sessions now?
5. Find 5 manner/degree/frequency adverbs in the text.

**Đáp án:**
1. He studied randomly — sometimes grammar, sometimes vocabulary, rarely with a plan.
2. He systematically follows his study schedule.
3. He always starts with grammar for 30 minutes.
4. No, he hardly ever skips a study session.
5. (Any 5): significantly, randomly, rarely, systematically, always, carefully, usually, hardly ever, considerably, quite, still, rather, consistently, extremely (student picks 5)
