## PHẦN LÝ THUYẾT (30 phút)

### 1. Tại sao Linking Words quan trọng với IELTS?
Trong IELTS Writing Task 2, tiêu chí **Coherence and Cohesion** (Tính mạch lạc) chiếm 25% số điểm. Dùng từ nối đúng sẽ giúp bài viết mạch lạc, chuyên nghiệp.

### 2. Bảng từ nối theo chức năng

**THÊM Ý (Addition):**
- and, also, in addition, additionally, furthermore, moreover, not only...but also

**TƯƠNG PHẢN (Contrast):**
- but, however, although/even though, despite/in spite of, nevertheless, on the other hand, in contrast, while/whereas

**NGUYÊN NHÂN (Cause):**
- because, because of, due to, owing to, since, as

**KẾT QUẢ (Result/Effect):**
- so, therefore, consequently, as a result, thus, hence

**MỤC ĐÍCH (Purpose):**
- to, in order to, so that, with the aim of

**VÍ DỤ (Example):**
- for example, for instance, such as, including, namely

**KẾT LUẬN (Conclusion):**
- in conclusion, to conclude, to sum up, overall, in summary

**ĐỐI CHIẾU (Comparison):**
- similarly, likewise, in the same way, compared to, both...and

### 3. Phân biệt although / despite / however

| Từ nối | Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **although** | although + S + V | **Although** it is expensive, it works well. |
| **even though** | even though + S + V | **Even though** we tried, we failed. |
| **despite** | despite + N/V-ing | **Despite** the difficulties, we succeeded. |
| **in spite of** | in spite of + N/V-ing | **In spite of** working hard, he failed. |
| **however** | Câu 1. **However**, câu 2 | It failed. **However**, we learned a lot. |

### 4. Lỗi thường gặp

**Sai:** *Although the result was good, but we can improve it.* ← KHÔNG dùng "but" cùng "although"
**Đúng:** *Although the result was good, we can improve it.* OR *The result was good, but we can improve it.*

**Sai:** *Despite it was raining, we continued.* ← "despite" theo sau danh từ/V-ing
**Đúng:** *Despite the rain, we continued.* OR *Despite it **being** rainy, ...*

### 5. IELTS Writing — Cấu trúc đoạn văn tiêu chuẩn
```
Topic sentence (ý chính của đoạn)
↓
Explaining sentence (giải thích)
↓
Evidence / Example (bằng chứng / ví dụ — for example, for instance)
↓
Result / Conclusion (kết quả — consequently, as a result, therefore)
```

*Ví dụ đoạn văn tốt:*
> "AI has transformed healthcare significantly. **In particular**, it has enabled doctors to diagnose diseases more accurately and quickly. **For example**, AI systems can now detect early-stage cancer in medical images with greater precision than human radiologists. **As a result**, treatment can begin sooner, **thereby** improving patient survival rates. **Furthermore**, AI reduces the workload on medical staff, **which** allows them to focus on more complex tasks."
