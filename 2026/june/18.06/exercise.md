## BÀI TẬP 1: Điền từ nối phù hợp — 20 phút

Chọn từ: however / therefore / moreover / although / despite / for example / in addition / as a result / in contrast / consequently

1. The project was successful. _______, there are still areas for improvement.
2. _______ its high cost, the LIDAR sensor provides the most accurate data.
3. The robot failed to navigate. _______, the team decided to recalibrate the sensors.
4. AI has many applications, _______ speech recognition, image processing, and translation.
5. _______ she is new to the team, she has already made significant contributions.
6. The battery discharged faster than expected; _______, the test was cut short.
7. The new algorithm is more accurate. _______, it runs faster than the previous one.
8. ROS 1 is well-established. _______, ROS 2 offers better real-time support and security.
9. _______ working all night, he couldn't solve the problem.
10. I studied hard; _______, I passed the IELTS exam with a 7.0 score.

**Đáp án:**
1. However | 2. Despite | 3. Therefore/Consequently | 4. for example/such as | 5. Although | 6. consequently/as a result | 7. Moreover/Furthermore/In addition | 8. In contrast/However | 9. Despite | 10. therefore/as a result

## BÀI TẬP 2: Sửa lỗi dùng từ nối — 20 phút

1. Although the result was good, but we can still improve it.
2. Despite it was raining, we continued the outdoor test.
3. The robot failed. However we restarted it.
4. She studied hard. Nevertheless she passed the exam.
5. For example robots can be used in hospitals and clinics.

**Đáp án:**
1. ~~but~~ → *Although the result was good, we can still improve it.*
2. ~~it was raining~~ → *Despite the rain / Despite it **being** rainy, ...*
3. Missing comma → *The robot failed. **However,** we restarted it.*
4. Missing punctuation → *She studied hard. **Nevertheless,** she passed.* (She passed, so "nevertheless" meaning "despite difficulty she succeeded" — actually this makes perfect sense as written if the comma is added after "Nevertheless")
5. Missing comma → *For example, robots can be used...*

## BÀI TẬP 3: Viết đoạn văn với từ nối — 30 phút

**Chủ đề:** "Robots will replace human workers in many industries."

Viết một đoạn thân (body paragraph) cho bài IELTS Task 2 này, thể hiện **lập luận ĐỒNG Ý** với chủ đề. Đoạn văn 5-7 câu. Dùng ít nhất 4 từ nối khác nhau.

**Gợi ý cấu trúc:**
1. Topic sentence: Robots are increasingly replacing human workers...
2. Explain: due to advancements in AI...
3. Example: For example, in manufacturing...
4. Effect: As a result, productivity has increased...
5. Additional point: Furthermore, robots can work 24/7...

**Ví dụ mẫu:**
> "There is compelling evidence to suggest that robots are increasingly replacing human workers in various industries. This is largely due to significant advancements in artificial intelligence and automation technology. For example, in manufacturing plants, robotic arms can perform assembly tasks with greater speed and precision than human workers. As a result, production costs have decreased substantially, while output quality has improved. Furthermore, unlike humans, robots can operate continuously without breaks, thereby maximizing efficiency. Consequently, many large corporations have invested heavily in robotics to gain a competitive advantage."

## BÀI TẬP 4: Đọc & Phân tích từ nối — 10 phút

> "AI-powered robots offer numerous advantages in healthcare settings. First and foremost, they can perform repetitive tasks such as delivering medicine and transporting equipment with great efficiency. Furthermore, they reduce the risk of human error in tasks that require precision, such as dispensing medication. As a result, patient safety is significantly improved.
>
> However, there are also notable concerns. Despite their capabilities, robots cannot replace the human connection that is central to effective healthcare. For instance, a patient who is scared or in pain needs empathy and emotional support — qualities that, at present, machines cannot provide. In addition, the initial cost of implementing robotic systems is extremely high, which may limit access in developing countries.
>
> In conclusion, while robots have great potential to transform healthcare positively, they should be seen as a complement to human professionals, rather than a replacement."

**Nhiệm vụ:** Gạch chân và ghi chú chức năng của tất cả từ nối trong đoạn văn.

**Đáp án:**
- **First and foremost** (introduction of first point)
- **such as** (example)
- **Furthermore** (addition)
- **such as** (example)
- **As a result** (result)
- **However** (contrast)
- **Despite** (contrast)
- **For instance** (example)
- **In addition** (addition)
- **In conclusion** (conclusion)
- **while** (contrast)
- **rather than** (contrast/clarification)

## PHẦN DICTATION — 10 phút

1. The project was successful. However, there are still areas for improvement.
2. Despite the difficulties, the team completed the project on time.
3. AI has many applications, such as speech recognition and image processing.
4. The sensor failed; consequently, the entire test had to be restarted.
5. Although robots are very useful, they cannot replace human empathy.
6. Furthermore, robots can work continuously without taking breaks.
7. In conclusion, the benefits of automation outweigh the disadvantages.
8. As a result of consistent practice, my English has improved significantly.
