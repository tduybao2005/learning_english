# TỪ VỰNG TIẾNG ANH - NGÀY 20 (10/06/2026)
*Chủ đề: Gia đình — Mở rộng & Collocation*

---

## 1. ĐỘNG TỪ VỀ GIA ĐÌNH & CÁC MỐI QUAN HỆ

| Cụm từ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **get along with** | hòa thuận với | I get along well with my brother. |
| **look up to** | kính trọng, ngưỡng mộ | I look up to my father. |
| **take after** | giống (cha/mẹ) | She takes after her mother. |
| **bring up / raise** | nuôi dưỡng, dạy dỗ | He was brought up in a small village. |
| **pass down** | truyền lại | My grandfather passed down his love of reading. |
| **grow up** | lớn lên | I grew up in Da Nang. |
| **settle down** | ổn định cuộc sống | They plan to settle down after graduation. |
| **start a family** | lập gia đình, có con | They want to start a family soon. |
| **stay in touch** | giữ liên lạc | We stay in touch through video calls. |
| **look after / take care of** | chăm sóc | She looks after her elderly grandmother. |

## 2. COLLOCATION VỀ GIA ĐÌNH

| Collocation | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **close-knit family** | gia đình gắn bó | We are a very close-knit family. |
| **immediate family** | gia đình trực tiếp (bố mẹ, anh/em) | Only immediate family attended. |
| **extended family** | đại gia đình | My extended family is very large. |
| **family bond** | mối quan hệ gia đình | Strong family bonds are important. |
| **family gathering** | buổi họp mặt gia đình | We have a family gathering every Tet. |
| **family background** | nền tảng gia đình | He comes from a humble family background. |
| **family tradition** | truyền thống gia đình | It's a family tradition to eat together every Sunday. |
| **family member** | thành viên gia đình | All family members attended. |
| **nuclear family** | gia đình hạt nhân | The nuclear family consists of parents and children. |

## 3. BIỂU ĐẠT VỊ TRÍ TRONG GIA ĐÌNH
- **the oldest / eldest**: lớn nhất, con cả
- **the youngest**: nhỏ nhất, con út
- **the middle child**: con giữa
- **only child**: con một
- **five years older/younger than**: hơn/kém 5 tuổi
  *My brother is five years older than me.*
- **the same age as**: cùng tuổi
  *We are the same age.*

## 4. MÔ TẢ NGHỀ NGHIỆP GIA ĐÌNH (Ứng dụng Speaking)
- **My father is a/an [job]**: Bố tôi là...
- **My mother works as a [job]**: Mẹ tôi làm...
- **She is retired**: Bà ấy đã về hưu.
- **He runs his own business**: Anh ấy tự kinh doanh.
- **She is a stay-at-home mother**: Cô ấy là bà nội trợ.
- **He works in the [field] sector**: Anh ấy làm trong lĩnh vực...

## 5. TALKING ABOUT FAMILY — IELTS EXPRESSIONS
- "We are very close to each other."
- "Family means everything to me."
- "My parents have been a great source of support."
- "I have a strong connection with my grandparents."
- "Growing up, my family instilled in me the value of hard work."
- "My siblings and I are quite different in personality."
