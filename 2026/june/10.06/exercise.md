## BÀI TẬP 1: Điền từ gia đình đúng — 20 phút

1. My mother's mother is my _______.
2. My brother's daughter is my _______.
3. My father's brother is my _______.
4. My aunt's son is my _______.
5. My sister and I are _______s. (dùng từ chung cho anh chị em)
6. My mother's parents are my _______ on my mother's side.
7. My wife's mother is my _______.
8. I have no brothers or sisters. I am an _______ child.

**Đáp án:**
1. grandmother | 2. niece | 3. uncle | 4. cousin | 5. sibling | 6. maternal grandparents | 7. mother-in-law | 8. only

## BÀI TẬP 2: Điền cụm động từ — 20 phút

Chọn từ phù hợp: get along with / look up to / take after / bring up / grow up / stay in touch / look after / pass down

1. I _______ my father the most. He is my role model.
2. My grandmother _______ traditional Vietnamese cooking skills to us.
3. She _______ her mother — they have the same eyes and personality.
4. Do you _______ your siblings well? — Yes, we rarely argue.
5. I _______ in a small town in Quang Nam province.

## BÀI TẬP 3: Đọc & Trả lời — 20 phút

> "My name is Bao. I come from a close-knit family of four. My father, Mr. Nguyen, is a retired engineer. He was born in Hue and moved to Da Nang after graduating. My mother, Mrs. Le, is a high school English teacher. I take after my mother in many ways — I love languages and I'm quite patient like her.
>
> I have one older brother named Tuan, who is five years older than me. He is a software engineer living in Ho Chi Minh City. Although we live in different cities, we stay in touch every week through video calls and gaming sessions online.
>
> My grandparents on my father's side are still alive. My grandmother is 75 and my grandfather is 78. They live with us. My grandmother looks after the house while my grandfather enjoys reading and watching the news. I look up to both of them — they have worked so hard all their lives to provide for the family."

**Câu hỏi:**
1. How many members are in Bao's immediate family?
2. What does Bao's father do? What did he do before?
3. Who is Bao's mother and what does she do?
4. In what ways does Bao take after his mother?
5. How old is Tuan? How do he and Bao stay in touch?
6. Where do the paternal grandparents live?
7. Who looks after the house?
8. Why does Bao look up to his grandparents?

**Đáp án:**
1. Four members.
2. He is retired. He was an engineer.
3. Mrs. Le. She is a high school English teacher.
4. He loves languages and is patient.
5. He is 5 years older than Bao / ~25-26. They stay in touch through video calls and gaming.
6. They live with Bao's family.
7. His grandmother.
8. Because they worked very hard to provide for the family.

## BÀI TẬP 4: IELTS Speaking Part 1 — Viết câu trả lời — 20 phút

Viết câu trả lời đầy đủ cho các câu hỏi Speaking Part 1 sau (mỗi câu 3-5 câu):

**Q1: Do you have a large or small family?**
**Q2: Are you close to your family members?**
**Q3: What do you usually do together as a family?**
**Q4: Who are you most similar to in your family, and why?**

**Ví dụ mẫu cho Q1:**
> "I would say I have a moderately-sized family. My immediate family consists of my father, mother, and one older brother. On both sides, I have several aunts, uncles, and cousins, so at family gatherings there can be quite a lot of people. But day-to-day, I live with just my parents and my grandparents."
