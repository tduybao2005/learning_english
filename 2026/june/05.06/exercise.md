## BÀI TẬP 1: Chọn động từ đúng — 20 phút

1. The team (is / are) working on the new prototype.
2. Python and ROS 2 (is / are) essential tools for robotics.
3. Each sensor (need / needs) to be calibrated before the test.
4. The data (show / shows) a clear trend.
5. Neither the battery nor the motors (is / are) damaged.
6. Everyone in the lab (understand / understands) the safety rules.
7. 30% of the code (has / have) been reviewed.
8. Three hours (is / are) not enough to finish this task.
9. Most of the team members (has / have) submitted their reports.
10. Nobody (know / knows) who wrote this comment in the code.
11. The committee (has / have) made a final decision.
12. Either Linh or Nam (is / are) going to present the findings.
13. All of the information (is / are) stored on the cloud server.
14. Half of the robots (is / are) fully operational.
15. "Introduction to Robotics" (is / are) a great textbook.

**Đáp án:**
1. is | 2. are | 3. needs | 4. shows | 5. are | 6. understands | 7. has | 8. is | 9. have | 10. knows | 11. has | 12. is | 13. is | 14. are | 15. is

## BÀI TẬP 2: few / a few / little / a little — 15 phút

1. I have _______ time before the deadline. Let's hurry! (negative — not enough)
2. There are _______ things I want to discuss with you. (positive — 3-4 things)
3. We have _______ data left in the dataset. (negative — almost empty)
4. Could you give me _______ advice on my essay? (positive — some is enough)
5. _______ students failed the exam — only 2 out of 30. (negative implication)
6. Fortunately, _______ students failed — most of them passed. (wait, this is positive: only a few failed)
7. She has _______ experience with machine learning. She just started. (negative)
8. He speaks _______ English — just enough to greet people. (positive — some)

**Đáp án:**
1. little | 2. a few | 3. little | 4. a little | 5. few | 6. a few | 7. little | 8. a little

## BÀI TẬP 3: Sửa lỗi sai — 20 phút

1. The team are going to present tomorrow.
2. The data are very accurate.
3. Everyone have finished the assignment.
4. Neither the camera nor the LIDAR are working.
5. Few of the information was useful.
6. A little students attended the lecture.
7. Python and Java is both object-oriented languages.
8. Research show that exercise improves memory.

**Đáp án:**
1. ~~are~~ → **is** (team = collective noun, số ít trong formal writing)
2. ~~are~~ → **is** (data = uncountable in formal contexts)
3. ~~have~~ → **has** (everyone = singular)
4. ~~are~~ → **is** (neither...nor → theo chủ ngữ gần hơn: LIDAR → số ít)
5. ~~Few~~ → **Little** (information = uncountable → little, không phải few)
6. ~~A little~~ → **A few** (students = countable → a few)
7. ~~is~~ → **are** (Python and Java = plural)
8. ~~show~~ → **shows** (Research = uncountable → số ít)

## BÀI TẬP 4: Dịch Việt → Anh — 20 phút

1. Đội của chúng tôi đang làm việc chăm chỉ để hoàn thành prototype trước hạn chót.
2. Dữ liệu thu thập được từ cảm biến rất chính xác.
3. Hầu hết code đã được xem xét và không có lỗi nào.
4. Mỗi thành viên trong nhóm đã nộp báo cáo của họ.
5. Không ai trong phòng lab biết ai đã xóa code đó.
6. 50% các robot đang hoạt động bình thường.
7. Cần ít nhất một vài người để kiểm tra hệ thống.
8. Ít dữ liệu quá — chúng ta cần thu thập thêm trước khi huấn luyện mô hình.

**Đáp án:**
1. Our team is working hard to finish the prototype before the deadline.
2. The data collected from the sensors is very accurate.
3. Most of the code has been reviewed and there are no errors.
4. Each team member has submitted their report.
5. Nobody in the lab knows who deleted that code.
6. 50% of the robots are operating normally.
7. At least a few people are needed to test the system.
8. There is too little data — we need to collect more before training the model.

## BÀI TẬP 5: Đọc hiểu — 15 phút

> "The robotics department at our university has many talented students. Everyone here is passionate about technology and innovation. The faculty encourages collaboration — each student is expected to contribute to at least one group project per semester.
>
> Currently, three different teams are competing for the best project award. Team A has 5 members and is working on an autonomous delivery drone. Team B consists of only 4 students, but they have produced impressive results. Neither Team A nor Team B has a clear advantage yet.
>
> Most of the testing has been completed. A few technical issues remain, but they are minor. The committee will announce the winner next Friday at 2 PM. Everyone is nervous, but all the teams have done excellent work."

**Câu hỏi:**
1. Is the faculty individual teachers or a collective group?
2. What is each student expected to do?
3. Are Team A and B equally matched? Quote the text.
4. Has most of the testing been completed?
5. Identify 5 cases of subject-verb agreement in the text and explain why each verb is correct.

**Đáp án:**
1. A collective group (faculty = collective noun, số ít → "encourages").
2. Each student is expected to contribute to at least one group project.
3. Yes — "Neither Team A nor Team B has a clear advantage yet."
4. Yes — "Most of the testing has been completed."
5. (Student selects 5): Everyone is / faculty encourages / each student is / neither...has / most...has been / committee will announce / everyone is / all the teams have

## PHẦN DICTATION — 10 phút

1. The team is ready to present their prototype tomorrow.
2. Everyone in the lab understands the safety protocols.
3. Neither the sensor nor the camera is working correctly.
4. Most of the data has been analyzed and stored.
5. Each component needs to be tested before final assembly.
6. Three hours is not enough time to complete this calibration.
7. A few students stayed late to finish the programming exercise.
8. The committee has made its final decision on the project winner.
