## BÀI TẬP 1: Điền can / can't / could / couldn't — 20 phút

1. I _______ code in Python, but I _______ code in Java yet. (khả năng hiện tại)
2. _______ you help me debug this function? (yêu cầu thông thường)
3. When I was 12, I _______ solve a Rubik's cube in under 3 minutes.
4. She _______ understand the error message at first, but now she _______.
5. _______ you please explain this algorithm more slowly? (yêu cầu lịch sự)
6. The robot _______ navigate in complete darkness — it needs light sensors.
7. He _______ speak Vietnamese when he was a child, but he's forgotten most of it.
8. _______ I borrow your charger for a moment?
9. I tried all night but I _______ find the bug. I finally found it this morning.
10. Robots of the future _______ perform complex surgeries autonomously.

**Đáp án:**
1. can / can't | 2. Can | 3. could | 4. couldn't / can | 5. Could | 6. can't | 7. could | 8. Can/Could | 9. couldn't | 10. could/will be able to

## BÀI TẬP 2: Chọn đúng và giải thích — 20 phút

1. "(Can / Could) you pass me that screwdriver, please?" — Which is more polite?
2. "When I was young, I (can / could) run very fast." — Past or present ability?
3. "You (can / could) leave early today if you finish the work." — Permission.
4. "I (can / could) try a different approach — what do you think?" — Suggestion.
5. "The sensor (can't / couldn't) detect obstacles last week, but now it (can / could)." — Past vs. present ability.

**Đáp án + Giải thích:**
1. **Could** — lịch sự hơn "can"
2. **could** — khả năng trong quá khứ
3. **can** — cho phép (informal)
4. **could** — gợi ý
5. **couldn't** (quá khứ) / **can** (hiện tại)

## BÀI TẬP 3: Dịch Việt → Anh — 20 phút

1. Tôi có thể viết code bằng Python nhưng tôi chưa thể viết bằng C++.
2. Bạn có thể giúp tôi với bài tập này không?
3. Khi còn nhỏ, anh ấy có thể giải toán rất nhanh.
4. Robot không thể điều hướng trong bóng tối mà không có cảm biến hồng ngoại.
5. Bạn có thể vui lòng gửi cho tôi báo cáo trước 5 giờ chiều không?
6. Tôi đã không thể tìm thấy lỗi tối qua, nhưng bây giờ tôi đã sửa được rồi.
7. Cô ấy có thể giải quyết các vấn đề phức tạp rất hiệu quả.
8. Chúng ta có thể thử một thuật toán khác không?

**Đáp án:**
1. I can code in Python but I can't code in C++ yet.
2. Can you help me with this exercise?
3. When he was young, he could solve math problems very quickly.
4. The robot can't navigate in the dark without infrared sensors.
5. Could you please send me the report before 5 PM?
6. I couldn't find the bug last night, but now I've fixed it.
7. She can solve complex problems very efficiently.
8. Could we try a different algorithm?

## BÀI TẬP 4: Đọc hiểu — 20 phút

> "Artificial intelligence systems today can perform tasks that were once considered exclusively human. They can recognize faces, translate languages, write poetry, and even diagnose diseases. However, there are still many things AI cannot do — or at least cannot do well.
>
> AI systems cannot truly understand emotion. They can simulate emotional responses, but they can't feel empathy the way humans can. They also cannot apply common sense in unpredictable situations. A robot could navigate a clean, organized environment, but it might struggle in a messy, dynamic real-world setting.
>
> Ten years ago, AI couldn't reliably recognize speech. Now, it can understand natural language with impressive accuracy. Could we imagine, fifty years ago, that machines could write coherent articles? Probably not. The question is: what could AI be capable of in another fifty years?"

**Câu hỏi:**
1. List 4 things AI can do today.
2. List 2 things AI cannot do well.
3. What could AI NOT do ten years ago that it can now?
4. What question does the author leave for the reader?
5. Find 3 uses of "can" and identify whether each shows ability, permission, or possibility.

**Đáp án:**
1. Recognize faces, translate languages, write poetry, diagnose diseases.
2. Understand emotion (feel empathy) / Apply common sense in unpredictable situations.
3. Reliably recognize speech.
4. What could AI be capable of in another fifty years?
5. (ability examples): *can recognize faces* (ability) / *can simulate emotional responses* (ability) / *can understand natural language* (ability)
