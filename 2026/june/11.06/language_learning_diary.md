### 4. Hội thoại thực tế — IELTS Speaking Part 1
> **Q:** What do you study?
> **A:** I'm currently in my second year at Industrial University in Ho Chi Minh City. I'm majoring in Electrical Engineering, with a focus on robotics and artificial intelligence.
>
> **Q:** What do you enjoy most about your studies?
> **A:** I particularly enjoy the lab sessions because they give me the opportunity to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world problems. I also love the collaborative atmosphere — working with my teammates on projects is both challenging and rewarding.
>
> **Q:** What is the most challenging aspect of university life?
> **A:** I think the biggest challenge is managing my time effectively. Between attending lectures, completing assignments, and working on my research project, it can be quite overwhelming. However, I've learned to prioritize tasks and work systematically.
