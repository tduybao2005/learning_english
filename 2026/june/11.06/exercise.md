## BÀI TẬP 1: Điền từ đúng — 20 phút

Chọn từ thích hợp:

1. I need to _______ my assignment before midnight. (submit/enroll)
2. She _______ in computer science at Industrial University. (majors/graduates)
3. He _______ the IELTS exam twice before passing. (attended/took)
4. The professor asked us to _______ the data and explain the results. (analyze/pass)
5. She _______ a scholarship to study abroad. (earned/enrolled)
6. I need to _______ for the final exam — there's a lot of material. (revise/submit)
7. He _______ from university with a distinction. (graduated/enrolled)
8. We need to _______ this theory in our experiment. (demonstrate/graduate)

**Đáp án:**
1. submit | 2. majors | 3. took | 4. analyze | 5. earned | 6. revise | 7. graduated | 8. demonstrate

## BÀI TẬP 3: AWL — Điền từ vào câu — 20 phút

Dùng từ trong ngoặc ở dạng đúng:

1. The professor will _______ our projects based on three criteria. (assess)
2. The research _______ that most students struggle with time management. (indicate)
3. We need to _______ a more efficient approach to data collection. (implement)
4. The results were very _______. They exceeded our expectations. (significance)
5. Can you _______ the main problem with this algorithm? (identify)
6. The team _______ excellent results after months of hard work. (obtain)
7. This is a key _______ in machine learning that everyone should understand. (concept)
8. The paper _______ a clear _______ for the experiment. (demonstrate / structure)

**Đáp án:**
1. assess | 2. indicates | 3. implement | 4. significant | 5. identify | 6. obtained | 7. concept | 8. demonstrates / structure

## BÀI TẬP 4: Đọc hiểu — 20 phút

> "The university education system in Vietnam has been undergoing significant changes in recent years. Universities are now requiring students to demonstrate not only theoretical knowledge but also practical skills.
>
> At Industrial University, the curriculum combines academic lectures with hands-on lab sessions. Students must complete both compulsory and elective subjects. The assessment is based on attendance, assignments, midterm exams, and a final exam. Students who obtain a GPA above 3.6 graduate with distinction.
>
> The university also offers scholarship opportunities for high-achieving students. To apply, students must submit a research proposal and a letter of motivation. The scholarship covers tuition fees and provides a monthly stipend.
>
> Many students pursue internships during their studies to gain work experience. This is particularly important in technical fields like robotics and artificial intelligence, where employers require candidates to demonstrate practical experience."

**Câu hỏi:**
1. What does the new education system require students to demonstrate?
2. What does the curriculum combine?
3. What are the 4 types of assessment?
4. What GPA is required to graduate with distinction?
5. What must students submit to apply for a scholarship?
6. Why are internships particularly important in technical fields?
7. Tìm 5 Academic Words (AWL) trong đoạn văn.

**Đáp án:**
1. Both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.
2. Academic lectures with hands-on lab sessions.
3. Attendance, assignments, midterm exams, and final exam.
4. Above 3.6.
5. A research proposal and a letter of motivation.
6. Because employers require candidates to demonstrate practical experience.
7. (Any 5): significant, demonstrate, practical, assessment, obtain, academic, require, technical

## BÀI TẬP 5: IELTS Speaking — 10 phút

Trả lời 3 câu hỏi Speaking Part 1 sau bằng tiếng Anh (nói to hoặc viết):

1. "What subject do you find most interesting at university?"
2. "How do you prepare for exams?"
3. "What are your plans after you graduate?"
