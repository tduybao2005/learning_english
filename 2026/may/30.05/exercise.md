## BÀI TẬP 1: Simple Past hay Present Perfect? — 20 phút

Chia động từ đúng thì:

1. I _______ (fix) the bug **yesterday**.
2. I _______ (just / fix) the bug. Now the robot can move.
3. She _______ (go) to Japan **in 2023**.
4. She _______ (be) to Japan three times.
5. **Did** you **eat** lunch **at noon**? / **Have** you **eaten** lunch **yet**?
   → Câu nào đúng nếu bây giờ là 1 PM và bạn hỏi bạn của bạn?
6. He _______ (send) the email **at 9 AM**.
7. _______ (you / ever / use) a Raspberry Pi?
8. When _______ (you / start) learning English?
9. I _______ (study) English **for** two months.
10. We _______ (build) this robot **last semester**.

**Đáp án:**
1. fixed (yesterday = specific past time)
2. have just fixed (just = PP signal + result affects now)
3. went (in 2023 = specific year)
4. has been (three times = experience)
5. **Have you eaten lunch yet?** (asking about result now — 1 PM, the action may or may not have happened)
6. sent (at 9 AM = specific time)
7. Have you ever used (ever = PP signal)
8. did you start (when = asking specific time point)
9. have studied (for + duration, still ongoing)
10. built (last semester = specific completed period)

## BÀI TẬP 2: Sửa lỗi sai — 15 phút

Mỗi câu dưới đây có một lỗi. Tìm và sửa:

1. I have fixed the bug yesterday.
2. She went to the lab three times this week.
3. Have you started this project when?
4. He has never went abroad.
5. Did you finish the report yet?
6. I have studied in 2024.
7. When have you met the professor?
8. We have built the robot last month.

**Đáp án:**
1. I **fixed** the bug yesterday. (PP không dùng với "yesterday")
2. She **has gone** to the lab three times this week. (PP vì "this week" chưa kết thúc)
3. **When did** you start this project? (Simple Past với "when")
4. He has never **gone** abroad. (V3 của "go" là "gone", không phải "went")
5. **Have** you finished the report yet? (PP vì "yet")
6. I **studied** in 2024. (SP vì năm cụ thể đã qua)
7. **When did** you meet the professor? (SP với "when")
8. We **built** the robot last month. (SP vì "last month")

## BÀI TẬP 3: Hoàn thành đoạn hội thoại — 20 phút

Điền vào chỗ trống: Simple Past hoặc Present Perfect.

**At a Job Interview:**
> Interviewer: _______ you ever _______ (work) with embedded systems?
> Bao: Yes, I _______. I _______ (work) with STM32 and ESP32 for my university projects.
> Interviewer: What _______ you _______ (build) most recently?
> Bao: I _______ (build) a hospital navigation robot called HospitGo. We _______ (complete) it last semester.
> Interviewer: _______ the robot _______ (work) well in testing?
> Bao: It _______ (have) some problems at first, but we _______ (solve) them. The final demo _______ (be) successful.
> Interviewer: _______ you _______ (publish) any papers?
> Bao: Not yet, but I _______ (write) a report that I _______ (plan) to submit to a journal.

**Đáp án:**
Have you ever worked / I have / worked / have you built / built / completed / Did the robot work / had / solved / was / Have you published / haven't published yet / have written / plan (am planning)

## BÀI TẬP 4: Đọc hiểu — 20 phút

> "My name is Bao. I am a second-year student at Industrial University. I have been interested in robotics since I was in high school. I have built three robots so far.
>
> My first robot was very simple. I made it in 2024 using an Arduino. It could only move in a straight line. I was not satisfied, so I studied more.
>
> Last year, I built a more complex robot. I used ROS 2 and Python. This robot could navigate autonomously using a camera and LIDAR sensor. I learned so much from that project.
>
> This year, I have developed HospitGo — a robot for hospital environments. It has been the most challenging project I've ever worked on. We have tested it over 50 times, and we've fixed more than 30 bugs. I haven't slept much, but I've learned more than I've ever learned in a single year."

**Câu hỏi:**
1. How long has Bao been interested in robotics?
2. How many robots has he built so far?
3. When did he make his first robot and what did he use?
4. What could the first robot do?
5. What did he use for the second robot?
6. What is HospitGo?
7. How many times have they tested it?
8. How many bugs have they fixed?
9. Find THREE examples of Simple Past in the text.
10. Find THREE examples of Present Perfect in the text.

**Đáp án:**
1. Since high school.
2. Three robots.
3. In 2024, using an Arduino.
4. It could only move in a straight line.
5. ROS 2 and Python.
6. A robot for hospital environments.
7. Over 50 times.
8. More than 30 bugs.
9. (Any 3): made / was / could move / was / studied / built / used / learned
10. (Any 3): have been / have built / have developed / has been / have tested / have fixed / haven't slept / have learned

## PHẦN DICTATION — 10 phút

1. Have you ever worked with machine learning before?
2. Yes, I have. I worked on a classification project last year.
3. She has never failed an exam in her entire university career.
4. When did you start learning Python?
5. I've studied Python for three years and I love it.
6. We built the prototype last month and we've tested it ten times since then.
7. Have you submitted the assignment yet?
8. Not yet. I finished the code but I haven't written the report.
