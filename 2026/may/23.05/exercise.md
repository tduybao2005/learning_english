## BÀI TẬP 1: Chia động từ đúng dạng (Present Continuous) — 15 phút

Chia động từ trong ngoặc ở thì Hiện tại Tiếp diễn.

1. He _____________ (sit) at his desk right now.
2. They _____________ (debug) the robot code at the moment.
3. I _____________ (listen) to an English podcast now.
4. She _____________ (not / work) today. She _____________ (rest).
5. _____________ (you / use) nvim right now?
6. The robot _____________ (not / move). Something is wrong.
7. We _____________ (build) a new ROS 2 package this week.
8. Look! The sensor _____________ (detect) movement.
9. What _____________ (you / study) at the moment?
10. Nam _____________ (configure) the server. He _____________ (not / eat) lunch yet.
11. I _____________ (write) a report for the lab. It _____________ (take) a long time.
12. _____________ (the system / upload) data right now?

**Đáp án:**
1. is sitting
2. are debugging
3. am listening
4. is not working / is resting
5. Are you using
6. is not moving
7. are building
8. is detecting
9. are you studying
10. is configuring / is not eating
11. am writing / is taking
12. Is the system uploading

## BÀI TẬP 2: Chọn đúng — Hiện tại Đơn hay Tiếp diễn? — 20 phút

Khoanh tròn đáp án đúng.

1. I *(code / am coding)* in nvim **every day**.
2. Look! The robot *(moves / is moving)* by itself!
3. She usually *(works / is working)* at the lab, but today she *(stays / is staying)* at home.
4. He *(knows / is knowing)* how to use ROS 2 very well.
5. I *(learn / am learning)* English **this month**.
6. They *(test / are testing)* the sensors **right now**.
7. She *(likes / is liking)* to code in Python.
8. **Currently**, the system *(processes / is processing)* the data.
9. He *(uploads / is uploading)* the model **at the moment**.
10. The robot always *(needs / is needing)* to be calibrated before use.

**Đáp án:**
1. code (every day → Simple)
2. is moving (Look! → Continuous)
3. works (usually → Simple) / is staying (today → Continuous)
4. knows (state verb → Simple)
5. am learning (this month → Continuous)
6. are testing (right now → Continuous)
7. likes (state verb → Simple)
8. is processing (currently → Continuous)
9. is uploading (at the moment → Continuous)
10. needs (state verb → Simple)

## BÀI TẬP 4: Đọc hiểu — 20 phút

Đọc đoạn văn sau và trả lời câu hỏi.

> "It is 9 PM on a Thursday night. Bao is not relaxing. He is working very hard. He is sitting at his desk and typing on his keyboard. He is debugging a ROS 2 node for his university project. The robot is not working correctly right now — it is moving in the wrong direction.
>
> In the next room, his roommate Minh is not studying. He is watching a movie and eating instant noodles. He is not paying attention to his homework at the moment.
>
> Meanwhile, at the university lab, the professor is checking the students' progress reports. She is reading Bao's report right now. She is smiling because the report is looking very good."

**Câu hỏi:**
1. What is Bao doing right now?
2. Is Bao relaxing tonight?
3. What problem is the robot having?
4. What is Minh doing? (2 things)
5. Where is the professor?
6. Why is the professor smiling?
7. Is Minh doing his homework? Write a full negative sentence.

**Đáp án:**
1. He is sitting at his desk, typing on his keyboard, and debugging a ROS 2 node.
2. No, he is not. He is working very hard.
3. The robot is moving in the wrong direction.
4. He is watching a movie and eating instant noodles.
5. She is at the university lab.
6. She is smiling because the report is looking very good.
7. No, Minh is not doing his homework at the moment.

## BÀI TẬP 5: Viết đoạn văn — 15 phút

Viết một đoạn văn ngắn (5-7 câu) bằng tiếng Anh, mô tả những gì bạn đang làm **ngay lúc này** hoặc **tối nay**.

**Gợi ý:** Sử dụng các động từ: study, write, use, sit, listen, think, learn, read.

**Ví dụ mẫu:**
> Right now, I am sitting at my desk and studying English. I am writing sentences in my notebook. I am using this textbook to learn new grammar. The fan is running because it is hot tonight. I am not watching TV because I am focusing on my English. I am thinking about the IELTS exam and I am working hard for my goal.
