## BÀI TẬP 2: "will" hay "be going to"? — 20 phút

Chọn đáp án đúng và giải thích lý do:

1. A: The robot is not moving. B: I _______ check the power cable. (will/am going to)
2. A: What are your plans for this weekend? B: I _______ work on my project. (will/am going to)
3. A: Look at those dark clouds! B: Yes, it _______ rain. (will/is going to)
4. I think AI _______ be everywhere in 10 years. (will/is going to)
5. A: I can't open this file. B: I _______ help you right now. (will/am going to)
6. She has booked the flight. She _______ visit Singapore next month. (will/is going to)
7. The battery is at 1%. The laptop _______ die. (will/is going to)
8. I _______ definitely pass IELTS. I study every day. (will/am going to)

**Đáp án + Giải thích:**
1. **will check** — Quyết định tức thì ngay lúc đó.
2. **am going to work** — Kế hoạch đã có từ trước.
3. **is going to rain** — Bằng chứng hiện tại (thấy mây đen).
4. **will be** — Dự đoán từ ý kiến cá nhân.
5. **will help** — Quyết định tức thì.
6. **is going to visit** — Kế hoạch đã sắp xếp (đã đặt vé).
7. **is going to die** — Bằng chứng hiện tại (thấy pin 1%).
8. **will** — Dự đoán/lời hứa từ ý chí cá nhân (cả hai đều chấp nhận được).

5. Chúng tôi đang có kế hoạch phát hành phiên bản mới vào tháng tới.
6. Anh ấy không có kế hoạch chuyển ngành. Anh ấy yêu thích robotics.
7. Điện thoại của bạn sắp hết pin. Bạn có muốn dùng bộ sạc của tôi không?

## BÀI TẬP 4: Đọc hiểu — 20 phút

> "It is Sunday morning and Bao is making plans for the week. He is going to start his IELTS preparation seriously. He has created a study schedule. He is going to study English for 2 hours every evening. On Monday, he is going to focus on grammar. On Tuesday, he is going to build vocabulary. He has already bought two IELTS books.
>
> His roommate Minh looks at the schedule and laughs. 'That's a lot of studying!' he says. Bao replies, 'I know, but I'm going to stick to this plan. I will not give up this time.'
>
> Minh says, 'Look at the weather forecast — it says it's going to rain heavily this week. You won't be able to go out much anyway.' 
>
> Bao smiles. 'Perfect,' he says. 'Then I'll have no excuse not to study!'"

**Câu hỏi:**
1. What is Bao going to do this week?
2. How long is he going to study English each day?
3. What is he going to focus on Monday?
4. What has he already bought?
5. What does Minh think about the schedule?
6. What is Bao going to do differently "this time"?
7. What does the weather forecast say?
8. Why is Bao happy about the rain?

**Đáp án:**
1. He is going to start his IELTS preparation seriously.
2. He is going to study for 2 hours every evening.
3. He is going to focus on grammar.
4. He has already bought two IELTS books.
5. He thinks it is a lot of studying (he laughed at the schedule).
6. He is not going to give up / he is going to stick to the plan.
7. It is going to rain heavily.
8. Because he won't be able to go out and will have no excuse not to study.
