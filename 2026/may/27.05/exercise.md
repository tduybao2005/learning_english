## BÀI TẬP 1: Điền will/won't — 15 phút

1. I _______ submit the report tomorrow. (promise)
2. She _______ be late. She is already on her way. (negative prediction)
3. _______ you help me debug this code? (request)
4. I think AI _______ transform the healthcare industry.
5. The robot _______ work if you don't configure the sensors first. (negative)
6. Don't worry. I _______ send you the files tonight. (promise)
7. _______ the professor be at the lab tomorrow?
8. We _______ probably finish the project by next Friday.
9. I _______ definitely study English every day from now on.
10. He _______ come to the meeting because he has another class. (negative)

**Đáp án:**
1. will | 2. won't | 3. Will | 4. will | 5. won't | 6. will | 7. Will | 8. will | 9. will | 10. won't

## BÀI TẬP 3: Dịch Việt → Anh — 20 phút

1. Tôi sẽ hoàn thành báo cáo vào tối mai.
2. Cô ấy sẽ không đến phòng lab ngày mai vì cô ấy bị bệnh.
3. Bạn có nghĩ rằng trí tuệ nhân tạo sẽ thay thế lập trình viên không?
4. Tôi chắc chắn rằng bạn sẽ vượt qua kỳ thi IELTS.
5. Robot sẽ trở nên phổ biến hơn trong tương lai.
6. Đội của chúng tôi sẽ trình bày kết quả vào tuần tới.
7. Bạn có thể giúp tôi không? Tôi sẽ gặp khó khăn với phần này.
8. Hệ thống sẽ cải thiện khi chúng tôi thu thập thêm dữ liệu.

1. I will finish the report tomorrow evening.
2. She won't come to the lab tomorrow because she is sick.
3. Do you think AI will replace programmers?
4. I'm sure you will pass the IELTS exam.
5. Robots will become more common in the future.
6. Our team will present the results next week.
7. Will you help me? I will have trouble with this part.
8. The system will improve as we collect more data.

## BÀI TẬP 4: Đọc hiểu — 20 phút

> "The robotics conference will take place next month in Ho Chi Minh City. Our team will participate and present our HospitGo project. I'm excited but also nervous.
>
> Linh will prepare the technical presentation. She will explain how the robot navigates autonomously. Nam will handle the live demo — he will show the robot moving through the hospital corridor simulation. I will answer questions from the audience.
>
> I think the conference will be a great opportunity. We will probably meet researchers from other universities. Maybe we will find a potential sponsor for our next project. I believe our work will impress the judges. I'm certain we won't regret going.
>
> After the conference, we will write a paper and possibly submit it to an international journal. This will definitely be a big step for our team."

**Câu hỏi:**
1. When and where will the conference take place?
2. What will Linh do at the conference?
3. What will Nam's role be?
4. What will Bao do?
5. What does Bao think they will find at the conference?
6. What will they do after the conference?
7. Find and write 3 examples of "will" used for predictions in the text.

**Đáp án:**
1. It will take place next month in Ho Chi Minh City.
2. She will prepare and give the technical presentation.
3. He will handle the live demo.
4. He will answer questions from the audience.
5. They will probably meet researchers and maybe find a potential sponsor.
6. They will write a paper and possibly submit it to a journal.
7. *I think the conference will be a great opportunity. / I believe our work will impress the judges. / This will definitely be a big step for our team.* (any 3)

## BÀI TẬP 5: Viết đoạn văn — 15 phút

Viết 5-8 câu về **kế hoạch của bạn trong tuần tới** hoặc **dự đoán của bạn về tương lai của công nghệ/AI**.

**Gợi ý:** will, won't, I think, I believe, I'm sure, probably, definitely, next week, in the future.

**Ví dụ mẫu:**
> Next week will be very busy for me. I will have two exams and a project deadline. I will study every day after class. I probably won't have much free time, but I will try to learn 10 new English words each day.
>
> I believe AI will become the most important technology of the 21st century. Robots will work alongside humans in hospitals and factories. I think Python will remain the dominant language for AI development. In the future, I will build intelligent systems that help people in Vietnam.
