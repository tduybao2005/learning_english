## BÀI TẬP 4: Đọc hiểu — 20 phút

Đọc đoạn văn và trả lời câu hỏi:

> "Last Saturday was a big day for our robotics team. We were all at the university lab from 7 AM. The lab was very crowded — there were students from three different teams. Our robot, HospitGo, wasn't working at first. The wheels were slow and the sensors were not accurate. Professor Nguyen was there. She was very patient with us.
>
> At noon, I was very hungry, but I wasn't able to leave. There wasn't enough time. My teammate Linh was the same — she was tired but she was still focused. Finally, at 5 PM, HospitGo was ready. The demo was a success. We were so happy and relieved. That evening, we were at a restaurant celebrating. It was a perfect ending to a very long day."

**Câu hỏi:**
1. When was the big day?
2. What time were the team at the lab?
3. Was the lab empty? Why / Why not?
4. What were the problems with HospitGo at first?
5. Was Professor Nguyen patient or impatient?
6. Why wasn't Bao able to leave at noon?
7. How did the team feel when HospitGo was ready?
8. Where were they that evening?

**Đáp án:**
1. Last Saturday.
2. They were there from 7 AM.
3. No, it wasn't. There were students from three different teams.
4. The wheels were slow and the sensors were not accurate.
5. She was patient.
6. Because there wasn't enough time.
7. They were happy and relieved.
8. They were at a restaurant.

## BÀI TẬP 5: Viết đoạn văn — 15 phút

Viết một đoạn văn 5-8 câu về **ngày hôm qua của bạn** (where you were, how you felt, who was with you).

**Gợi ý từ vựng:** was/were, at home/at university/at the lab, tired/happy/busy, there was/there were, last night/yesterday.

**Ví dụ mẫu:**
> Yesterday was a busy day for me. I was at the university from 8 AM. There were two important classes in the morning. In the afternoon, I was at the robotics lab. My teammates were there too. We were working on a new feature for HospitGo. I was very tired in the evening, but I was satisfied with the progress. There were many challenges, but the results were worth it.
