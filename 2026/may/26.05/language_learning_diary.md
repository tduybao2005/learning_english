## PHẦN LÝ THUYẾT (30 phút)

### 1. Bảng cấu trúc đầy đủ — Simple Past (Động từ thường)

| Loại câu | Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| Khẳng định (+) | S + **V2** | I **studied** last night. |
| Phủ định (-) | S + **didn't** + **V1** | I **didn't study** last night. |
| Nghi vấn (?) | **Did** + S + **V1**? | **Did** you **study** last night? |
| Câu hỏi Wh- | **Wh-** + **did** + S + **V1**? | What **did** you **study**? |
| Câu hỏi chủ ngữ | **Who/What** + **V2**? | **Who wrote** this code? |

### 2. Nguyên tắc VÀNG: Trợ động từ "did" nhận toàn bộ quá khứ

Khi dùng **did** (trợ động từ), động từ chính luôn ở **dạng nguyên mẫu (V1)**:
- ✅ Did you **go** to the lab? (go, không phải went)
- ✅ I didn't **find** the bug. (find, không phải found)
- ❌ Did you **went** to the lab? (SAI)
- ❌ I didn't **found** the bug. (SAI)

### 3. Cách trả lời Yes/No

| Câu hỏi | Trả lời khẳng định | Trả lời phủ định |
|---|---|---|
| Did you code yesterday? | Yes, I **did**. | No, I **didn't**. |
| Did she write the report? | Yes, she **did**. | No, she **didn't**. |
| Did they build a robot? | Yes, they **did**. | No, they **didn't**. |

### 4. Câu hỏi Wh- trong quá khứ

| Từ để hỏi | Câu hỏi | Trả lời |
|---|---|---|
| What | What did you do yesterday? | I wrote code. |
| Where | Where did she go last night? | She went to the library. |
| When | When did they start the project? | They started it in February. |
| Who | Who did you meet at the lab? | I met Professor Nguyen. |
| Why | Why did he leave early? | Because he was tired. |
| How long | How long did it take? | It took three hours. |
| How much | How much did it cost? | It cost 200,000 VND. |

### 5. Câu hỏi về Chủ ngữ (Subject Questions)
Khi hỏi về CHỦ NGỮ (ai làm việc đó?), KHÔNG dùng "did":
- **Who** + V2 (không dùng did): *Who **wrote** this code?* (Ai đã viết code này?)
- **What** + V2 (hỏi về vật): *What **happened** last night?* (Chuyện gì đã xảy ra tối qua?)

So sánh:
- *Who did you **call**?* (Bạn đã gọi cho ai? — "you" là chủ ngữ, hỏi tân ngữ)
- *Who **called** you?* (Ai đã gọi cho bạn? — hỏi về chủ ngữ)

### 6. Hội thoại thực tế
> **A:** Did you finish the code last night?
> **B:** No, I didn't. I worked on it for 4 hours but I didn't complete it.
> **A:** What went wrong?
> **B:** The sensor data didn't match the expected values. I didn't understand why at first.
> **A:** Did you ask anyone for help?
> **B:** I messaged Nam, but he didn't reply. So I searched online and found the solution myself.
> **A:** When did you finally fix it?
> **B:** I fixed it at 2 AM. I didn't sleep much last night.
> **A:** How long did the whole thing take?
> **B:** It took about 5 hours total. But I learned a lot!
