# Bài Tập Thực Hành - Ngày 5 (26/05/2026)
*Kiến thức: Simple Past — Phủ định (didn't) & Câu hỏi (Did / Wh- + did)*
*Thời gian dự kiến: 90 phút*

---

## BÀI TẬP 1: Chuyển sang phủ định (didn't) — 15 phút

1. I studied last night.
2. She wrote a report yesterday.
3. They built the robot correctly.
4. He found the solution immediately.
5. We went to the lab this morning.
6. She understood the error message.
7. I made a backup before editing.
8. He spent all day debugging.

**Đáp án:**
1. I didn't study last night.
2. She didn't write a report yesterday.
3. They didn't build the robot correctly.
4. He didn't find the solution immediately.
5. We didn't go to the lab this morning.
6. She didn't understand the error message.
7. I didn't make a backup before editing.
8. He didn't spend all day debugging.

## BÀI TẬP 3: Đặt câu hỏi Wh- — 20 phút

Nhìn vào câu trả lời, đặt câu hỏi Wh- thích hợp:

1. ________________________? — I studied Python and ROS 2. (What)
2. ________________________? — She went to the electronics market. (Where)
3. ________________________? — He fixed it at 2 AM. (When)
4. ________________________? — Because the sensor was broken. (Why)
5. ________________________? — It took about 5 hours. (How long)
6. ________________________? — It cost 250,000 VND. (How much)
7. ________________________? — Professor Nguyen helped me. (Who — subject)
8. ________________________? — I called Nam. (Who — object: who did...?)
9. ________________________? — I used the ROS 2 navigation package. (Which method)
10. ________________________? — Something crashed last night. (What happened)

**Đáp án:**
1. What did you study?
2. Where did she go?
3. When did he fix it?
4. Why didn't it work? / Why did you stop?
5. How long did it take?
6. How much did it cost?
7. Who helped you?
8. Who did you call?
9. Which method did you use?
10. What happened last night?

## BÀI TẬP 4: Dịch Việt → Anh — 20 phút

1. Anh ấy không đến phòng lab hôm qua vì anh ấy bị ốm.
2. Bạn có tìm thấy lỗi không? — Có, tôi đã tìm thấy nó cuối cùng.
3. Họ đã không hiểu tại sao robot dừng lại.
4. Bạn đã làm gì tối qua? — Tôi đã gỡ lỗi code trong 4 tiếng đồng hồ.
5. Tại sao bạn không gửi báo cáo? — Vì tôi chưa hoàn thành nó.
6. Ai đã viết thuật toán này? — Linh đã viết nó.
7. Mất bao lâu để sửa lỗi đó? — Mất khoảng 2 ngày.
8. Chúng tôi đã không quản lý được để hoàn thành trước deadline.

**Đáp án:**
1. He didn't come to the lab yesterday because he was sick.
2. Did you find the bug? — Yes, I finally found it.
3. They didn't understand why the robot stopped.
4. What did you do last night? — I debugged the code for 4 hours.
5. Why didn't you send the report? — Because I didn't finish it yet.
6. Who wrote this algorithm? — Linh wrote it.
7. How long did it take to fix that bug? — It took about 2 days.
8. We didn't manage to finish before the deadline.

## BÀI TẬP 5: Đọc hiểu — 15 phút

> "Last week, our team had a difficult experience. We didn't meet the project deadline. Why didn't we finish on time? There were several reasons.
>
> First, the hardware didn't arrive on time. We ordered sensors online, but they didn't come until Thursday. We lost three days waiting. Then, when we finally started testing, the robot didn't respond to the commands correctly. We didn't understand why at first.
>
> Nam discovered the problem. He found that the communication protocol didn't match between the two microcontrollers. He spent two days rewriting the code. The professor didn't punish us, but she asked us to submit a detailed explanation. We wrote a full report and sent it to her on Friday."

**Câu hỏi:**
1. Did the team meet their deadline?
2. Why didn't they finish on time? (Give two reasons)
3. When did the sensors arrive?
4. What was wrong with the robot?
5. Who discovered the problem?
6. What was the communication problem?
7. Did the professor punish them?
8. What did they do on Friday?

**Đáp án:**
1. No, they didn't.
2. The sensors didn't arrive on time / The robot didn't respond correctly.
3. They arrived on Thursday.
4. It didn't respond to the commands correctly.
5. Nam discovered the problem.
6. The communication protocol didn't match between the two microcontrollers.
7. No, she didn't.
8. They wrote a full report and sent it to the professor.
