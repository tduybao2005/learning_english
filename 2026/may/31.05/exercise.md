## BÀI TẬP 3: Sửa lỗi sai — 20 phút

Mỗi câu có 1-2 lỗi. Tìm và sửa:

1. Can you give me an advice about my project?
2. I need more informations about the library.
3. She has a lot of knowledges about machine learning.
4. We bought new equipments for the lab.
5. A research shows that robots improve hospital efficiency.
6. The softwares we use are all open-source.
7. I made a progress with my English this month.
8. He is an university student.
9. These are the childrens from the coding class.
10. I have a feedback for you about your code.

**Đáp án:**
1. ~~an advice~~ → **some advice** hoặc **a piece of advice**
2. ~~informations~~ → **information** (không có s)
3. ~~knowledges~~ → **knowledge** (không có s)
4. ~~equipments~~ → **equipment** (không có s)
5. ~~A research~~ → **Research** (không dùng mạo từ với uncountable)
6. ~~softwares~~ → **software** (không có s)
7. ~~a progress~~ → **progress** hoặc **some progress**
8. ~~an university~~ → **a university** (âm /j/ — phụ âm)
9. ~~childrens~~ → **children** (bất quy tắc, không thêm s)
10. ~~a feedback~~ → **some feedback** hoặc **a piece of feedback**

## BÀI TẬP 5: Đọc hiểu — 15 phút

> "Building a robot requires a lot of knowledge and skill. First, you need to understand the hardware — the sensors, processors, and mechanical components. Then, you need software skills: you need to write code and work with frameworks like ROS 2.
>
> Data is crucial in robotics. The robot collects data from sensors and makes decisions based on that information. Without accurate data, the robot cannot navigate correctly.
>
> Research in robotics is advancing rapidly. New algorithms are being developed every year. The progress in AI has made robots much smarter. For a student interested in robotics, there is an enormous amount of knowledge to gain — but each piece of information learned brings you one step closer to building something amazing."

**Câu hỏi:**
1. Is "knowledge" countable or uncountable? Find it in the text.
2. Find 3 uncountable nouns in the text.
3. Find 3 countable nouns in the text and write their plural forms.
4. Why does the author write "data is crucial" not "data are crucial"? (Hint: uncountable)
5. Translate: "Dữ liệu rất quan trọng trong robotics. Không có thông tin chính xác, robot không thể điều hướng đúng cách."

**Đáp án:**
1. Uncountable — "a lot of **knowledge**"
2. knowledge, software, data, information, research, progress (any 3)
3. sensors/sensor, processors/processor, frameworks/framework, algorithms/algorithm, students/student (any 3)
4. Because "data" is treated as uncountable, so we use singular verb "is."
5. Data is crucial in robotics. Without accurate information, the robot cannot navigate correctly.
