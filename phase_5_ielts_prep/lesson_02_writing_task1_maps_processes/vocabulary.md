## LUYỆN TẬP TỪ VỰNG

### Exercise 1: Complete the Map Description Sentences

Điền vào chỗ trống bằng cụm từ vị trí thích hợp.

1. The library is _____________ (ở phía bắc của) the town hall.
2. A new supermarket was built _____________ (liền kề) the railway station.
3. The old hospital was demolished, and an office block was built _____________ (trên khu đất đó / at that site).
4. A cycle path was constructed _____________ (dọc theo) the river bank.
5. Two new apartment buildings now stand _____________ (đối diện) the park.
6. The bus terminal is located _____________ (ở ngoại ô) of the town.
7. A roundabout was installed _____________ (tại ngã tư) of Park Road and High Street.
8. The sports hall is _____________ (ở phía tây nam) of the main campus.
9. Several restaurants opened _____________ (ở vùng lân cận) of the new museum.
10. The car park _____________ (vẫn không thay đổi), despite the redevelopment of the area.

**Đáp án gợi ý:** 1. to the north of | 2. adjacent to | 3. on that site | 4. alongside | 5. opposite | 6. on the outskirts | 7. at the intersection | 8. in the southwest | 9. in the vicinity | 10. remained unchanged

### Exercise 2: Process Sequence — Reorder and Fill in Sequence Words

Các bước dưới đây mô tả quá trình làm bánh mì, nhưng bị xáo trộn. Sắp xếp lại đúng thứ tự (A→F) và điền từ chỉ trình tự phù hợp.

**Các bước (chưa theo thứ tự):**
- ( ) The bread is removed from the oven and allowed to cool.
- ( ) Water and yeast are added to the flour and the mixture is kneaded into dough.
- ( ) _____________, the dough is left to rise for approximately one hour.
- ( ) _____________, wheat is ground into flour at a mill.
- ( ) The dough is shaped into loaves and placed in baking tins.
- ( ) _____________, the loaves are baked in a heated oven for 30-40 minutes.

Viết lại đoạn văn hoàn chỉnh sử dụng các cụm từ: *Initially / Following this / Once / After that / Subsequently / Finally*

### Exercise 3: Match the Map Change Description

Nối mô tả (1–8) với điều đã xảy ra trên bản đồ (A–H).

| # | Mô tả | | Điều xảy ra |
|---|-------|-|-------------|
| 1 | "A car park was built where the school once stood." | A | The park became a residential area. |
| 2 | "The road was extended to the ring road." | B | The school was replaced by a car park. |
| 3 | "Apartments were constructed on the site of the former park." | C | A new road link was created. |
| 4 | "The factory was converted into a shopping centre." | D | The factory changed its purpose. |
| 5 | "The hospital remained unchanged throughout." | E | The hospital stayed the same. |
| 6 | "The river was redirected to the east." | F | The river's course shifted. |
| 7 | "A new bridge was introduced across the main river." | G | A river crossing was built. |
| 8 | "The school was enlarged and a new wing was added." | H | The school became bigger. |

**Đáp án:** 1-B | 2-C | 3-A | 4-D | 5-E | 6-F | 7-G | 8-H
