# BÀI 2: IELTS WRITING TASK 1 — BẢN ĐỒ & SƠ ĐỒ QUY TRÌNH

**Giai đoạn:** Phase 5 — IELTS Preparation  
**Thời gian học:** ~3 giờ  
**Thời gian làm bài thi:** 20 phút  
**Số từ tối thiểu:** 150 từ

---

## TỔNG QUAN

Bài học này bao gồm hai dạng Task 1 đặc biệt mà nhiều học viên thấy khó nhất:
1. **MAP TASK (Bản đồ):** So sánh địa điểm ở hai thời điểm khác nhau
2. **PROCESS DIAGRAM (Sơ đồ quy trình):** Mô tả các bước trong một quy trình

Điểm chung: Cả hai đều **KHÔNG có số liệu thống kê** để mô tả — đây là điểm khác biệt lớn so với charts/graphs.

## PHẦN 1: MAP TASK (BẢN ĐỒ)

### 1.1 Hai loại bài Map phổ biến:

**Loại 1: Map change over time (Thay đổi theo thời gian)**
- Ví dụ: "The maps below show a town in 1980 and the same town in 2020."
- Yêu cầu: Mô tả những gì ĐÃ THAY ĐỔI và những gì còn NGUYÊN VẸN

**Loại 2: Plan comparison (So sánh hai thiết kế)**
- Ví dụ: "The maps below show a proposed design for a new library (Design A and Design B)."
- Yêu cầu: So sánh hai bản thiết kế khác nhau

> **Lưu ý quan trọng:** Nhiều học viên mắc lỗi **chỉ mô tả những gì có** trong bản đồ mà không nêu bật **sự thay đổi**. Tập trung vào CHANGE là chìa khóa để đạt Band 7+.

### 1.2 Cấu trúc bài viết Map:

**Đoạn 1 — Introduction:**
Paraphrase: bản đồ thể hiện điều gì, ở đâu, khi nào

**Đoạn 2 — Overview:**
Nêu sự thay đổi TỔNG QUAN NHẤT — ví dụ: "The area underwent significant development" hoặc "The town expanded considerably" hoặc "The layout was redesigned to prioritize X"

**Đoạn 3 & 4 — Body Paragraphs:**
Mô tả chi tiết theo VỊ TRÍ địa lý (ví dụ: phần phía Bắc trong body 1, phía Nam trong body 2) hoặc theo LOẠI THAY ĐỔI (ví dụ: thêm mới trong body 1, phá bỏ/xây lại trong body 2)

---

### 1.3 Ngôn ngữ mô tả THAY ĐỔI:

#### Xây mới / Thêm vào:
| Cụm từ | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| was built / was constructed | A new shopping centre was built. |
| was added | A car park was added to the north. |
| was introduced | A bus station was introduced. |
| appeared / emerged | A residential area appeared on the eastern side. |
| a new X was developed | A new park was developed near the river. |
| X was established | A hospital was established. |

#### Phá bỏ / Xóa đi:
| Cụm từ | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| was demolished | The old factory was demolished. |
| was knocked down | The farmhouse was knocked down. |
| was torn down | The original buildings were torn down. |
| was cleared | The woodland area was cleared. |
| disappeared | The small shops disappeared. |
| was removed | The railway line was removed. |

#### Chuyển đổi / Thay thế:
| Cụm từ | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| was replaced by | The forest was replaced by housing. |
| was converted into | The factory was converted into apartments. |
| was transformed into | The wasteland was transformed into a park. |
| was turned into | The school was turned into offices. |
| became | The farmland became a retail park. |
| gave way to | Open fields gave way to a road network. |

#### Mở rộng / Thu hẹp:
| Cụm từ | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| was extended / expanded | The road network was extended. |
| an extension was added | An extension was added to the hospital. |
| was enlarged | The parking area was enlarged. |
| was reduced in size | The garden area was reduced. |
| contracted | The industrial zone contracted. |

#### Không thay đổi:
| Cụm từ | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| remained unchanged | The church remained unchanged. |
| stayed the same | The river's course stayed the same. |
| no changes occurred to | No changes occurred to the main road. |
| was retained | The original library was retained. |
| continued to exist | The park continued to exist. |

---

### 1.4 Ngôn ngữ mô tả VỊ TRÍ:

#### Hướng địa lý:
- in the north/south/east/west of the area
- in the northeastern/southwestern corner
- in the centre / in the central area / centrally located
- to the north/south/east/west of [building/road]
- on the northern/southern side

#### Vị trí tương đối:
- adjacent to / next to / beside / alongside
- opposite (to) / across from
- near / close to / in the vicinity of / in the proximity of
- between [X] and [Y]
- to the left/right of
- at the top/bottom of the map
- surrounding / around

#### Di chuyển:
- moved to / was relocated to / was shifted to
- was moved from [location] to [location]

### 1.5 BÀI MẪU MAP TASK

**Đề bài:** *The maps below show an island before and after the construction of some tourist facilities.*

**Mô tả bản đồ trước (1985):**
- Forest in the north
- Beach on the south coast
- Small reception area in the center
- 1 footpath crossing the island east-west
- No buildings except reception

**Mô tả bản đồ sau (2015):**
- Some forest retained in the north, but accommodation (10 huts) built in the northwest
- Beach on south coast (unchanged)
- Reception area expanded and new restaurant added beside it
- Footpath now a road, vehicle track added
- Pier added to the west coast
- Swimming and snorkeling area marked in the sea

---

**BAND 7-8 MODEL ANSWER:**

The two maps compare the layout of a small island in its natural state in 1985 and following the development of tourist infrastructure by 2015.

Overall, the island underwent substantial development over the thirty-year period, with the addition of a range of tourist facilities transforming it from an almost entirely undeveloped landscape into a managed resort destination. Despite these changes, the beach and a portion of the forest were retained.

In 1985, the island was largely undeveloped, featuring only a small reception area in the centre and a single footpath running from east to west. The majority of the northern area was covered by forest, while the beach remained natural along the entire southern coastline.

By 2015, significant changes had taken place. The original footpath had been upgraded to a road, and a vehicle track was added to facilitate movement around the island. In the north-western corner, ten accommodation huts had been built within the partially cleared forest, while the remaining trees were preserved. The reception area was enlarged, and a new restaurant was constructed adjacent to it. A pier was added on the western coast to allow boats to dock, and designated swimming and snorkelling zones were established in the sea to the south.

*(Word count: ~190 words | Band: 7-8)*

## PHẦN 2: PROCESS DIAGRAM (SƠ ĐỒ QUY TRÌNH)

### 2.1 Hai loại Process phổ biến:

**Loại 1: Natural processes (Quy trình tự nhiên)**
- Ví dụ: The water cycle, rock formation, life cycle of a butterfly
- Dùng **ACTIVE VOICE** (chủ ngữ thực hiện hành động)
- Ví dụ: "Water evaporates from the ocean surface."

**Loại 2: Man-made processes (Quy trình nhân tạo)**
- Ví dụ: Manufacturing, recycling, food production
- Dùng **PASSIVE VOICE** (tập trung vào quy trình, không phải người làm)
- Ví dụ: "The raw materials are sorted and cleaned."

> **Quy tắc chung:** Nếu có con người hoặc máy móc thực hiện → Passive. Nếu là hiện tượng tự nhiên → Active.

### 2.2 Cấu trúc bài viết Process:

**Đoạn 1 — Introduction:**
Paraphrase: sơ đồ mô tả quy trình gì

**Đoạn 2 — Overview:**
- Tổng số bước (stage / step) là bao nhiêu?
- Đầu vào (input) và đầu ra (output) là gì?
- Quy trình tuyến tính (linear) hay vòng tròn (cyclical)?

**Đoạn 3 & 4 — Body Paragraphs:**
- Mô tả lần lượt theo thứ tự từ đầu đến cuối
- Body 1: các bước đầu (nửa đầu quy trình)
- Body 2: các bước tiếp theo (nửa sau quy trình)

### 2.3 Ngôn ngữ mô tả THỨ TỰ TRÌNH TỰ:

#### Mở đầu:
- First / Initially / The process begins with / At the first stage
- The first step involves / The initial stage is

#### Các bước tiếp theo:
- Next / Then / Following this / After that
- Subsequently / At the next stage / The following step
- Once this is complete / When X has been done
- At this point / At this stage

#### Song song:
- At the same time / Simultaneously / Meanwhile / Concurrently
- While X is happening, Y occurs

#### Kết thúc:
- Finally / Lastly / At the final stage / The process concludes with
- The end product is / The output is / The result is

### 2.4 Ngôn ngữ mô tả QUÁ TRÌNH (động từ hữu ích):

**Chuyển đổi trạng thái:**
- is converted into / is transformed into / is turned into
- becomes / changes into

**Di chuyển:**
- flows into / is fed into / passes through / enters / exits
- travels to / is transported to / is moved to / is transferred to
- returns to / is recycled back into / is sent back to

**Xử lý:**
- is processed / is treated / is filtered / is sorted
- is heated / is cooled / is dried / is compressed
- is collected / is harvested / is extracted / is separated
- is mixed with / is combined with / is added to

**Mô tả số lượng:**
- a small amount of / the majority of / some of the / all of the
- a proportion of / a fraction of / most of / part of

### 2.5 BÀI MẪU PROCESS DIAGRAM — Man-made (Passive)

**Đề bài:** *The diagram below shows how bricks are manufactured for the building industry.*

**Mô tả quy trình (7 bước):**
1. Clay is dug from the ground
2. Clay is placed in a metal grid (milling machine) to break it down
3. Sand and water are added to the clay; it is mixed
4. The mixture is placed in a wire-cutting machine or mould to form bricks
5. Bricks are dried in a drying oven for 24-48 hours
6. Bricks are fired in a kiln at 200°C–1300°C
7. Bricks are cooled in a cooling chamber; packaged and delivered

---

**BAND 7-8 MODEL ANSWER:**

The diagram illustrates the process by which bricks are produced for use in the construction industry.

Overall, the manufacturing process involves seven distinct stages, beginning with the extraction of raw materials from the ground and concluding with the packaging and delivery of the finished product. The process is largely industrial, relying on machinery at several key stages.

In the first stage of production, clay is excavated from the ground using a digger. The clay is then placed in a metal grid, known as a milling machine, where it is broken down into smaller pieces. Sand and water are subsequently added to the clay and the mixture is thoroughly blended together to achieve the correct consistency.

Once the clay mixture has been prepared, it is passed through a wire cutter or pressed into moulds to create individual bricks of uniform shape and size. These newly formed bricks are then placed in a drying oven, where they remain for a period of one to two days. Following drying, the bricks are fired in a kiln at temperatures ranging from 200°C to 1,300°C, depending on the type of brick being produced. Finally, the fired bricks are moved into a cooling chamber, before being packaged and delivered to customers.

*(Word count: ~185 words | Band: 7-8)*

---

### 2.6 BÀI MẪU PROCESS DIAGRAM — Natural (Active)

**Đề bài:** *The diagram below shows the water cycle.*

**Mô tả quy trình:**
1. Sun heats water in oceans/lakes → evaporation
2. Water vapor rises into atmosphere
3. Water vapor cools and condenses → clouds form (condensation)
4. Rain/snow falls (precipitation)
5. Water flows into rivers (surface run-off) or soaks into ground (groundwater)
6. Rivers flow back to the ocean; groundwater feeds springs

---

**BAND 7-8 MODEL ANSWER:**

The diagram illustrates the continuous cycle through which water moves through the natural environment, from the oceans to the atmosphere and back to the earth's surface.

Overall, the water cycle is a continuous and self-sustaining process involving four main stages: evaporation, condensation, precipitation, and collection. It is driven primarily by solar energy and gravity.

The cycle begins when heat from the sun causes water to evaporate from the surfaces of oceans, lakes, and rivers, transforming it into water vapour that rises into the atmosphere. As this vapour ascends to higher altitudes, it cools and condenses, forming clouds and other types of precipitation. When sufficient moisture has accumulated, water falls back to the earth's surface in the form of rain, snow, or hail.

Once it reaches the ground, water follows one of two paths. Some flows across the land surface as surface run-off, eventually making its way into rivers and streams that carry it back to the ocean. The remainder soaks into the soil and becomes groundwater, which may re-emerge as springs or be absorbed by plant roots before being released back into the atmosphere through transpiration. In this way, the cycle repeats continuously.

*(Word count: ~190 words | Band: 7-8)*

## PHẦN 3: CÁC LỖI PHỔ BIẾN

### Lỗi Map Task:

**Lỗi 1: Mô tả những gì có trong bản đồ, không mô tả sự thay đổi ❌**
> "In 1985, there was a forest. There was a beach. There was a footpath."
> → **Sửa:** Focus on what CHANGED: "The forest in the north was partially cleared to make way for accommodation."

**Lỗi 2: Chỉ mô tả một thời điểm ❌**
> Viết cả bài về bản đồ 1985 rồi không so sánh với 2015
> → **Sửa:** Cần liên tục so sánh "before" và "after"

**Lỗi 3: Sai từ vựng vị trí ❌**
> "in the north-east direction" → "in the north-east" (không cần "direction")
> "at north" → "in the north" (dùng "in", không dùng "at")

### Lỗi Process Diagram:

**Lỗi 1: Sai thì động từ ❌**
> "First, clay digs from the ground" → "First, clay is dug from the ground" (Passive!)
> "Water evaporated" (past tense) → "Water evaporates" (present simple — natural process)

**Lỗi 2: Mô tả không theo thứ tự ❌**
> Nhảy từ bước 1 sang bước 5 rồi mới quay về bước 3
> → **Sửa:** Luôn theo thứ tự linear, dùng sequencing language

**Lỗi 3: Overview quá đơn giản ❌**
> "Overall, there are 7 steps."
> → **Sửa:** "Overall, the process comprises seven distinct stages, beginning with X and culminating in Y, with the finished product being used for Z."

## TIPS PHÂN BIỆT ACTIVE vs PASSIVE TRONG PROCESS

| Câu hỏi | Câu trả lời → |
|---------|----------------|
| Có người hoặc máy móc thực hiện không? | Có → **PASSIVE** |
| Đây có phải hiện tượng tự nhiên không? | Có → **ACTIVE** |
| Có "worker", "machine", "operator" không? | Có → **PASSIVE** |
| Đây là vòng đời động vật/thực vật? | Vòng đời → **ACTIVE** |

**Ví dụ:**
- "The bricks **are cooled** in a chamber." → Passive (máy móc làm)
- "Water **evaporates** from the surface." → Active (hiện tượng tự nhiên)
- "The fruit **is harvested** and **transported** to the factory." → Passive (người làm)
- "The larva **develops** into a pupa." → Active (vòng đời tự nhiên)

## TÓM TẮT NHANH

- **Map task — tập trung vào thay đổi:** Cốt lõi là mô tả điều gì ĐÃ THAY ĐỔI (was built, was demolished, was replaced by, was converted into) và điều gì CÒN NGUYÊN VẸN (remained unchanged, was retained, continued to exist). Đừng chỉ liệt kê những gì có.
- **Vị trí địa lý chuẩn:** Dùng "in the north/south/east/west", "in the northeastern corner", "adjacent to [X]", "opposite to [Y]", "in the centre of the area". Tuyệt đối tránh "at north" hoặc "in north direction".
- **Active vs Passive trong Process — quy tắc cứng:** Quy trình tự nhiên (water cycle, butterfly life cycle) → Active voice. Quy trình nhân tạo (brick manufacturing, recycling) → Passive voice. Không được nhầm hai loại này.
- **Overview cho Process phải có:** (1) Tổng số bước; (2) Điểm bắt đầu (input/raw material); (3) Điểm kết thúc (output/finished product); (4) Quy trình tuyến tính (linear) hay vòng tròn (cyclical).
- **Sequencing language bắt buộc:** "First / Initially / The process begins with..." → "Next / Then / Subsequently..." → "At this stage / Once X has been completed..." → "Finally / Lastly / The process concludes with...". Không được mô tả process mà thiếu từ nối thứ tự.
- **Từ vựng Map task theo nhóm:** Xây mới (was built/was constructed/appeared), phá bỏ (was demolished/was torn down/disappeared), chuyển đổi (was converted into/was replaced by/became), mở rộng (was extended/was enlarged), không thay đổi (remained unchanged/was retained).
