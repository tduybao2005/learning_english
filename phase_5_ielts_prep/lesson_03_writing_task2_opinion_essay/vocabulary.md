## LUYỆN TẬP TỪ VỰNG

### Exercise 1: Identify the Function (15 items)

Đọc mỗi câu và xác định chức năng của cụm từ in đậm:
**(A) Stating position / (B) Main argument / (C) Explaining / (D) Example / (E) Concession / (F) Conclusion**

1. "**I firmly believe that** governments have a duty to fund public healthcare."
2. "**For instance**, Finland has demonstrated that free university education leads to higher graduation rates."
3. "**Admittedly, critics might argue that** such policies are too costly to implement."
4. "**The reason for this is that** children who grow up in poverty are less likely to access quality nutrition."
5. "**In conclusion, I maintain that** urban green spaces are essential to community wellbeing."
6. "**A compelling argument for this view is** the demonstrable link between physical activity and mental health."
7. "**This can be seen in the case of** Denmark, where flexible working hours have increased productivity."
8. "**While it is true that** technology creates some risks, it also offers immense opportunities."
9. "**What this means in practice is that** schools must adapt their curricula to reflect modern skills."
10. "**Research conducted in** several Scandinavian countries **has shown that** early childhood education yields lifelong benefits."
11. "**It is my contention that** the benefits of globalisation outweigh the drawbacks."
12. "**To summarise, this essay has argued that** renewable energy is the only viable long-term solution."
13. "**This stems from the fact that** urbanisation has increased demand for housing beyond current supply."
14. "**Countries such as Germany and Japan have demonstrated that** strong vocational training systems reduce youth unemployment."
15. "**Ultimately, the evidence strongly suggests that** individual behaviour change alone is insufficient to address climate change."

**Đáp án:** 1-A | 2-D | 3-E | 4-C | 5-F | 6-B | 7-D | 8-E | 9-C | 10-D | 11-A | 12-F | 13-C | 14-D | 15-F

### Exercise 2: Replace Weak Phrases with Strong Ones (10 items)

Thay thế cụm từ yếu (in đậm) bằng cụm từ học thuật mạnh hơn.

1. "**I think** that education is more important than money."
   → ____________________________________________________________

2. "**Many people say** that social media is bad for young people."
   → ____________________________________________________________

3. "**For example,** **in some countries** they have free healthcare."
   → ____________________________________________________________

4. "**Because** technology is everywhere, people depend on it too much."
   → ____________________________________________________________

5. "**At the end,** I believe that the government should take action."
   → ____________________________________________________________

6. "**Some say** this is a good idea, **but others don't agree.**"
   → ____________________________________________________________

7. "**Technology is a big topic** these days."
   → ____________________________________________________________

8. "**Studies show** that exercise is good for health."
   → ____________________________________________________________

9. "**Also,** another reason is that young people need guidance."
   → ____________________________________________________________

10. "**To finish,** I want to say that my opinion is that the environment should be protected."
    → ____________________________________________________________

**Đáp án gợi ý:**
1. I am firmly of the view that education holds greater value than financial success.
2. It is widely acknowledged that social media poses significant risks to adolescent wellbeing.
3. This can be seen in the case of countries such as Canada, where universal healthcare has been implemented effectively.
4. As a direct consequence of technology's pervasive role in modern life, dependency has grown considerably.
5. In conclusion, I maintain that governments bear a primary responsibility to address this issue.
6. Admittedly, there are those who support this approach; however, a compelling case can be made against it.
7. The issue of technology's role in contemporary society has attracted considerable debate.
8. Research conducted in the field of sports science has consistently demonstrated that regular physical activity enhances both physical and mental health.
9. A further compelling reason to support this position is the need for structured guidance during adolescence.
10. In summary, it is my firm belief that environmental protection should be regarded as a non-negotiable priority.

### Exercise 3: Paraphrase 5 IELTS Question Prompts (5 items)

Paraphrase phần chủ đề trong đề bài. KHÔNG được dùng từ gốc từ đề. Mục tiêu: giữ ý nghĩa, thay đổi từ ngữ và cấu trúc.

1. **Original:** "Technology has made the world a better place to live."
   → ____________________________________________________________

2. **Original:** "The government should provide free university education for all students."
   → ____________________________________________________________

3. **Original:** "Children spend too much time watching television."
   → ____________________________________________________________

4. **Original:** "People are increasingly living and working in cities."
   → ____________________________________________________________

5. **Original:** "International tourism causes more harm than good."
   → ____________________________________________________________

**Đáp án gợi ý:**
1. Technological advancement has significantly improved the quality of human life. / The rapid development of technology has transformed living standards for the better.
2. Tertiary education should be funded by the state and made accessible at no cost to students. / Governments ought to bear the financial burden of higher education for all citizens.
3. Young people are devoting excessive amounts of time to screen-based entertainment. / There is growing concern about the number of hours minors spend in front of the television.
4. Urban migration and the concentration of employment opportunities in metropolitan areas are accelerating trends in many nations. / An increasing proportion of the population is relocating to cities in search of work and improved living conditions.
5. The global travel and tourism industry inflicts greater damage — socially, culturally, and environmentally — than the benefits it brings. / Cross-border tourism may create more problems than it resolves.
