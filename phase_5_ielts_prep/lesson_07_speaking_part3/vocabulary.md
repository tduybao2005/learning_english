### Nhóm 1: Hedging & Uncertainty (15 phrases)

*Đây là đặc điểm ngôn ngữ QUAN TRỌNG NHẤT để đạt Band 7+. Thay vì nói chắc chắn (sai về mặt học thuật và không tự nhiên), bạn thể hiện tư duy phân tích bằng cách "dè dặt" ý kiến.*

| Cụm từ | Nghĩa tiếng Việt | Ví dụ trong Part 3 |
|---|---|---|
| It's difficult to say with certainty, but... | Khó nói chắc chắn, nhưng... | "It's difficult to say with certainty, but I feel that governments bear the greatest responsibility." |
| I would venture to suggest that... | Tôi mạo muội cho rằng... | "I would venture to suggest that this trend will only accelerate over the coming decade." |
| I'm somewhat sceptical of this view... | Tôi hơi hoài nghi về quan điểm này... | "I'm somewhat sceptical of the claim that technology always leads to progress." |
| I'd be inclined to say that... | Tôi có xu hướng cho rằng... | "I'd be inclined to say that urbanisation has weakened community bonds." |
| One could argue either way, but on balance... | Có thể lập luận theo cả hai hướng, nhưng nhìn chung... | "One could argue either way, but on balance, I think the benefits outweigh the drawbacks." |
| My overall sense is that... | Cảm nhận chung của tôi là... | "My overall sense is that the younger generation is more environmentally aware." |
| Whether this is entirely accurate is debatable... | Điều này có chính xác hoàn toàn hay không là vấn đề tranh luận... | "Whether this is entirely accurate is debatable, but the trend seems clear." |
| I may be wrong about this, but... | Tôi có thể sai, nhưng... | "I may be wrong about this, but I suspect the situation is more complex than it appears." |
| I have mixed feelings about this, to be honest. | Thành thật mà nói, tôi có cảm giác lẫn lộn về điều này. | Dùng khi câu hỏi có hai mặt và bạn thực sự không chắc. |
| The picture is more nuanced than it might appear... | Bức tranh phức tạp hơn vẻ ngoài... | "The picture is more nuanced than it might initially appear — there are real costs on both sides." |
| I'm not entirely convinced that... | Tôi không hoàn toàn tin rằng... | "I'm not entirely convinced that economic growth alone can solve inequality." |
| It depends to a large extent on... | Điều đó phụ thuộc phần lớn vào... | "It depends to a large extent on the cultural and economic context." |
| Broadly speaking, I think... though I acknowledge... | Nhìn rộng ra, tôi nghĩ... dù tôi thừa nhận... | "Broadly speaking, I think regulation is necessary, though I acknowledge its limitations." |
| My instinct is that... but I acknowledge the counterargument. | Linh cảm của tôi là... nhưng tôi thừa nhận lập luận ngược lại. | Combines opinion + concession in one sentence. |
| This is contentious, but I'd argue that... | Đây là vấn đề gây tranh cãi, nhưng tôi sẽ lập luận rằng... | "This is contentious, but I'd argue that some degree of censorship is necessary." |

### Nhóm 2: Speculation (10 phrases)

*Dùng cho câu hỏi về tương lai hoặc tình huống giả định*

| Cụm từ | Nghĩa tiếng Việt | Ví dụ trong Part 3 |
|---|---|---|
| I imagine that in the coming decades... | Tôi hình dung rằng trong những thập kỷ tới... | "I imagine that in the coming decades, remote work will become the norm in many industries." |
| Were this trend to continue, we might expect... | Nếu xu hướng này tiếp tục, chúng ta có thể kỳ vọng... | "Were this trend to continue, we might expect significant disruption to traditional retail." |
| There's a real possibility that... | Có khả năng thực sự rằng... | "There's a real possibility that AI will replace many white-collar jobs within twenty years." |
| It seems entirely plausible that... | Có vẻ hoàn toàn hợp lý rằng... | "It seems entirely plausible that renewable energy will dominate by 2050." |
| One scenario is that... though another is equally possible. | Một kịch bản là... dù một kịch bản khác cũng có thể xảy ra như vậy. | Shows you can think in multiple scenarios — a Band 7+ quality. |
| It strikes me that in the long term... | Tôi nhận thấy rằng về lâu dài... | "It strikes me that in the long term, the costs of inaction will far exceed those of acting now." |
| I suspect that future generations will... | Tôi nghi ngờ rằng các thế hệ tương lai sẽ... | "I suspect that future generations will look back on our use of fossil fuels with disbelief." |
| Hypothetically speaking, if [X] were to happen... | Giả thuyết mà nói, nếu [X] xảy ra... | "Hypothetically speaking, if all nations committed to net zero by 2040, the transition costs would be enormous." |
| Should this become widespread, the consequences could be... | Nếu điều này trở nên phổ biến, hậu quả có thể là... | "Should this technology become widespread, the consequences for privacy could be severe." |
| If nothing changes, I would predict that... | Nếu không có gì thay đổi, tôi sẽ dự đoán rằng... | "If nothing changes, I would predict that inequality will continue to widen." |

### Nhóm 3: Evaluating Complexity (10 phrases)

*Thể hiện bạn suy nghĩ nhiều chiều — cực kỳ quan trọng để đạt Band 7–8*

| Cụm từ | Nghĩa tiếng Việt | Ví dụ trong Part 3 |
|---|---|---|
| It would be an oversimplification to suggest that... | Sẽ là đơn giản hóa quá mức nếu gợi ý rằng... | "It would be an oversimplification to suggest that poverty is solely a result of individual choices." |
| It's worth distinguishing between [X] and [Y]... | Đáng để phân biệt giữa [X] và [Y]... | "It's worth distinguishing between short-term economic growth and long-term sustainable development." |
| What makes this particularly complex is... | Điều làm cho điều này phức tạp đặc biệt là... | "What makes this particularly complex is the interplay between cultural, economic, and political factors." |
| This is fundamentally a question of [X]... | Về cơ bản đây là vấn đề về [X]... | "This is fundamentally a question of values — what kind of society do we want to live in?" |
| The issue is genuinely multifaceted... | Vấn đề thực sự đa diện... | "The issue is genuinely multifaceted and cannot be resolved with a single policy intervention." |
| At its core, this debate is really about... | Cốt lõi của cuộc tranh luận này thực sự là về... | "At its core, this debate is really about the tension between individual freedom and collective responsibility." |
| There are genuinely competing perspectives here... | Có những quan điểm thực sự cạnh tranh ở đây... | "There are genuinely competing perspectives here that deserve to be taken seriously." |
| The answer varies significantly depending on... | Câu trả lời thay đổi đáng kể tùy thuộc vào... | "The answer varies significantly depending on whether you're looking at developed or developing economies." |
| I find it helpful to separate two distinct questions... | Tôi thấy hữu ích khi tách biệt hai câu hỏi riêng biệt... | "I find it helpful to separate two distinct questions: what *is* happening, and what *should* happen." |
| The key tension here is between [X] and [Y]... | Sự căng thẳng chính ở đây là giữa [X] và [Y]... | "The key tension here is between economic growth and environmental sustainability." |

## Exercise 3: Common Part 3 Mistakes — Identify and Fix (5 items)

**Hướng dẫn:** Identify the problem with each Part 3 response. Then write a corrected version.

---

**1.** *"Yes. I think technology is good."*

Problem: _______________________________________________

Corrected version: _______________________________________________
_______________________________________________

*Answer:* Problem: Far too short — one sentence is never sufficient for Part 3. No reasons, no development, no examples. Corrected: "I think technology, broadly speaking, has been a positive force — it has improved healthcare, democratised access to information, and enabled levels of global communication that were unimaginable a generation ago. That said, I'd be cautious about characterising it as uniformly good — the same tools that connect us can also polarise and isolate."

---

**2.** *"On the one hand, technology has many benefits. On the other hand, technology has many disadvantages. I don't know which is better."*

Problem: _______________________________________________

Corrected version: _______________________________________________
_______________________________________________

*Answer:* Problem: No conclusion or personal position — ends with "I don't know" which signals lack of confidence and fails to demonstrate the analytical ability examiners are looking for. Corrected: Add a concluding position — "On balance, however, I think the benefits outweigh the drawbacks, provided we develop appropriate regulatory frameworks."

---

**3.** *"Everyone knows that education is the most important thing in life."*

Problem: _______________________________________________

Corrected version: _______________________________________________
_______________________________________________

*Answer:* Problem: Overstatement ("everyone knows") and no hedging — this is not how academic or thoughtful speakers express themselves. Overly absolute. Corrected: "I would argue that education is arguably one of the most powerful determinants of life outcomes, both for individuals and for societies as a whole — though I recognise that other factors, such as health, family support, and economic opportunity, are equally significant."

---

**4.** *"This is because technology, technology is really very important and technology helps us a lot with technology..."*

Problem: _______________________________________________

Corrected version: _______________________________________________
_______________________________________________

*Answer:* Problem: Extreme lexical repetition of "technology" — a key criterion for Band 7+ is vocabulary range and avoidance of repetition. Corrected: Replace repeated "technology" with synonyms/related terms: "innovation," "digital tools," "these platforms," "such systems," "these advancements."

---

**5.** *"I completely disagree. Social media is absolutely terrible for everyone and has no positive effects whatsoever."*

Problem: _______________________________________________

Corrected version: _______________________________________________
_______________________________________________

*Answer:* Problem: No nuance, no hedging, no acknowledgement of complexity. "Absolutely terrible for everyone" is an overstatement that a Band 7+ speaker would never make. Corrected: "I have serious reservations about the overall impact of social media, particularly on adolescent mental health. Evidence suggests that certain patterns of use — particularly passive consumption and social comparison — are genuinely harmful. However, I'd resist the claim that social media is *entirely* negative — its effects depend significantly on how, and by whom, it is used."
