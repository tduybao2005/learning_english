## BỘ TỪ VỰNG THEO CHỦ ĐỀ IELTS TASK 1 THƯỜNG GẶP

### Chủ đề: DÂN SỐ & XÃ HỘI
- population growth / population decline / aging population
- birth rate / death rate / fertility rate / life expectancy
- urbanization / rural-to-urban migration / suburban sprawl
- household size / household income
- literacy rate / employment rate / unemployment rate

### Chủ đề: KINH TẾ & THƯƠNG MẠI
- GDP (Gross Domestic Product) / economic output
- exports / imports / trade balance / trade deficit
- consumer spending / household expenditure
- inflation rate / interest rate / exchange rate
- revenue / profit / turnover / market share

### Chủ đề: NĂNG LƯỢNG & MÔI TRƯỜNG
- renewable energy / fossil fuels / carbon emissions
- electricity generation / energy consumption / energy mix
- coal / oil / natural gas / nuclear power / solar / wind
- greenhouse gas emissions / carbon footprint
- recycling rate / waste production

### Chủ đề: GIÁO DỤC & CÔNG NGHỆ
- enrollment rate / graduation rate / dropout rate
- higher education / tertiary education / vocational training
- internet access / broadband connectivity / digital literacy
- smartphone ownership / social media usage
- research and development (R&D) spending

### Chủ đề: GIAO THÔNG & DU LỊCH
- passenger numbers / freight volumes / transport mode
- car ownership / public transport usage
- international arrivals / tourist spending
- aviation / rail / road / maritime transport

## BÀI TẬP 1: ĐIỀN VÀO CHỖ TRỐNG

**Hướng dẫn:** Chọn từ phù hợp từ hộp từ bên dưới để điền vào chỗ trống.

*(peaked / whereas / roughly / declined / illustrates / approximately / account for / gradually)*

1. The pie chart ________ the proportion of energy from different sources in 2020.
2. Coal and oil together ________ just over half of total electricity production.
3. ________ 3.5 million tourists visited the country in 2019.
4. The number of visitors rose ________ throughout the 1990s before leveling off.
5. Car usage ________ by 15% between 2010 and 2020.
6. Tourism figures ________ in 2015 at just over 5 million arrivals.
7. ________ half of those surveyed used public transport daily.
8. The UK saw strong growth, ________ France's figures remained relatively static.

**Đáp án:** 1. illustrates 2. account for 3. Approximately 4. gradually 5. declined 6. peaked 7. Roughly 8. whereas

## BÀI TẬP 2: SẮP XẾP THEO MỨC ĐỘ

**Hướng dẫn:** Sắp xếp các từ dưới đây theo mức độ từ NHẸ → MẠNH

**Nhóm từ chỉ TĂNG:**
- grew / surged / rose / shot up / climbed / soared

*(nhẹ nhất → mạnh nhất)*

**Nhóm từ chỉ GIẢM:**
- declined / plummeted / dipped / fell / dropped / collapsed

*(nhẹ nhất → mạnh nhất)*

**Đáp án gợi ý:**
- TĂNG: grew → rose → climbed → surged → soared → shot up
- GIẢM: dipped → declined → fell → dropped → collapsed → plummeted

## BÀI TẬP 3: VIẾT LẠI CÂU

**Hướng dẫn:** Viết lại các câu sau sử dụng từ/cụm từ trong ngoặc mà không thay đổi nghĩa.

1. 50% of the budget went on housing. *(account for)*
2. The rate went up quickly after 2010. *(surge)*
3. Germany had more cars than France. *(in comparison to)*
4. About 70% of students graduated. *(approximately)*
5. The figure went down to 25% by 2015. *(decline to)*
6. Both countries saw similar increases. *(likewise)*
7. The highest point was in 2008. *(reach a peak)*
8. The difference between the two groups was large. *(considerably)*

**Đáp án gợi ý:**
1. Housing accounted for 50% of the budget.
2. The rate surged after 2010.
3. Germany had more cars in comparison to / compared with France.
4. Approximately 70% of students graduated.
5. The figure declined to 25% by 2015.
6. Likewise, both countries saw similar increases. / France likewise saw similar increases.
7. The figure reached a peak in 2008.
8. The gap between the two groups was considerable.
