## BÀI TẬP TỪ VỰNG (VOCABULARY EXERCISES)

### Bài tập 1: Chọn is hoặc are cho mỗi chủ ngữ (Choose is or are)

*Điền "is" hoặc "are" vào chỗ trống dựa trên chủ ngữ.*

1. The committee _______________ meeting tomorrow.
2. The police _______________ looking for the suspect.
3. Mathematics _______________ a required subject.
4. The data _______________ very interesting.
5. Everyone _______________ invited to the party.
6. The scissors _______________ in the drawer.
7. The news _______________ shocking this morning.
8. Neither of the students _______________ ready.
9. The staff _______________ working overtime.
10. Bacteria _______________ found everywhere.
11. The criteria _______________ clearly stated.
12. Each of the books _______________ on the shelf.
13. The media _______________ often biased.
14. Economics _______________ not easy to understand.
15. A number of problems _______________ still unresolved.

**Đáp án:** 1. is 2. are 3. is 4. are 5. is 6. are 7. is 8. is 9. is/are 10. are 11. are 12. is 13. are 14. is 15. have

### Bài tập 2: Danh từ tập thể — Số ít hay số nhiều? (Collective Noun Agreement)

*Chọn động từ đúng. Đôi khi cả hai đều chấp nhận được — hãy giải thích.*

1. The jury (has / have) reached a unanimous decision.
2. The team (is / are) arguing about the strategy. *(British English)*
3. My family (live / lives) in Ho Chi Minh City.
4. The crowd (was / were) pushing towards the exit.
5. The government (has / have) announced new tax policies.
6. The board of directors (meets / meet) every quarter.
7. The audience (was / were) on their feet applauding.
8. Our class (is / are) going on a field trip.
9. The couple (was / were) married last June.
10. The public (is / are) not aware of the risks.

**Đáp án:** 1. has 2. are (British) / is (American) 3. lives 4. was 5. has 6. meets 7. were (each individually) 8. is 9. were 10. is

### Bài tập 3: Điền động từ đúng cho các chủ ngữ khó (Tricky subjects)

*Chia động từ trong ngoặc sao cho đúng.*

1. The number of applications _______________ (increase) every year.
2. A number of candidates _______________ (be) shortlisted.
3. Not only the teacher but also the students _______________ (need) support.
4. Either the manager or his assistants _______________ (be) responsible.
5. The rich often _______________ (not/understand) the struggles of the poor.
6. Five kilometres _______________ (be) too far to walk every day.
7. Ten dollars _______________ (be) not enough to buy that book.
8. Every student and every teacher _______________ (be) required to attend.
9. News about the floods _______________ (be) broadcast yesterday.
10. The series of lectures _______________ (have) been very informative.

**Đáp án:** 1. increases 2. have been 3. need 4. are 5. do not understand 6. is 7. is 8. is 9. was 10. has
