## PHẦN A — NGỮ PHÁP (50 điểm — 2đ/câu)

**Câu 1-5: Chọn đáp án đúng (Past Continuous & Past Perfect)**
1. When she arrived, I _______ dinner.
   A) cooked  B) was cooking  C) had cooked  D) cook

2. By the time the film started, we _______ our seats.
   A) find  B) found  C) had found  D) were finding

3. She _______ for two hours when the phone rang.
   A) was studying  B) studied  C) had been studying  D) had studied

4. While he _______, the lights went out.
   A) was reading  B) read  C) had read  D) reads

5. They _______ the project before the deadline.
   A) complete  B) completed  C) had completed  D) were completing

**Câu 6-10: Điền vào chỗ trống (Future Tenses)**
6. By 2030, scientists _______ (discover) a cure for this disease.
7. This time next week, I _______ (lie) on a beach in Vietnam.
8. She _______ (go) to the cinema tonight — she's already bought her ticket.
9. I think it _______ (rain) tomorrow.
10. By the time you arrive, I _______ (finish) cooking dinner.

**Câu 11-15: Subject-Verb Agreement — Chọn đúng**
11. The news (is/are) very shocking today.
12. Neither the teacher nor the students (was/were) late.
13. Everyone in the class (has/have) submitted their homework.
14. The committee (is/are — British English) meeting next week.
15. Mathematics (is/are) my favourite subject.

**Câu 16-20: Articles — Điền a/an/the/-**
16. She is _____ engineer. (nghề nghiệp)
17. _____ sun rises in the east.
18. He plays _____ football every weekend.
19. _____ book you recommended was excellent.
20. She is studying at _____ University of Hanoi.

**Câu 21-25: Modals — Chọn đáp án đúng**
21. You _______ smoke in this hospital. (absolute prohibition)
   A) mustn't  B) don't have to  C) shouldn't  D) needn't

22. She _______ bring a jacket — it's very warm today. (not necessary)
   A) mustn't  B) doesn't have to  C) can't  D) won't

23. He _______ be at home — his car is outside. (logical deduction)
   A) should  B) must  C) can  D) will

24. _______ I use your phone? (polite request)
   A) Will  B) Shall  C) May  D) Must

25. You _______ exercise more — the doctor recommended it. (advice)
   A) should  B) must  C) shall  D) will

## PHẦN C — ĐỌC HIỂU (15 điểm)

**Đọc đoạn văn và trả lời câu hỏi:**

Remote working, once considered a privilege for a select few, has become mainstream following recent global changes. Many companies now offer flexible work arrangements, allowing employees to work from home either full-time or on a hybrid basis. Proponents argue that remote working increases productivity, reduces commuting time, and improves work-life balance. Research by Stanford University found that remote workers were 13% more productive than their office-based counterparts. However, critics point out that working from home can lead to social isolation, difficulties in separating work from personal life, and challenges in collaboration. Some employees, particularly younger workers who live in shared accommodation, struggle to find a quiet, dedicated workspace. Despite these challenges, surveys show that the majority of workers who have experienced remote working would prefer to continue doing so, at least part of the time.

**Câu 1-3: True / False / Not Given**
1. Remote working was previously common for most workers. (FALSE)
2. Stanford University conducted research on remote working productivity. (TRUE)
3. Remote workers are always more productive than office workers. (NOT GIVEN)

**Câu 4-5: Chọn đáp án đúng**
4. According to the passage, what percentage productivity increase was found for remote workers?
   A) 3%  B) 13%  C) 30%  D) Not stated

5. Who particularly struggles with remote working, according to the passage?
   A) Older workers  B) Office managers  C) Younger workers in shared housing  D) Part-time employees

**Câu 6-7: Short answer (không quá 3 từ)**
6. What did surveys show that most remote workers prefer?
7. Name TWO disadvantages of remote working mentioned in the passage.
