### Exercise 3: Contextual Usage — Về tương lai bạn

Viết 5 câu về tương lai của BẠN, sử dụng Future Continuous và Future Perfect.

**Hướng dẫn:**
- Viết 3 câu Future Continuous (sử dụng: at this time next year / in 5 years / this time tomorrow)
- Viết 3 câu Future Perfect (sử dụng: by the end of this year / by 2030 / by the time I'm 30)

**Mẫu:**
- (FC) This time next year, I will be studying at university.
- (FP) By the end of 2026, I will have completed all phases of this English course.

Viết câu của bạn:
1. (FC) At this time tomorrow, I will be _____________________________________________
2. (FC) This time next year, I will be _____________________________________________
3. (FC) In five years, I will be _____________________________________________
4. (FP) By the end of this year, I will have _____________________________________________
5. (FP) By 2030, I will have _____________________________________________
6. (FP) By the time I finish university, I will have _____________________________________________
