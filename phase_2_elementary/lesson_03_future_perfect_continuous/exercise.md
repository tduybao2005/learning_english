## Phần D:
29. Lỗi: **will arrive** → Sửa: By the time you **arrive** (dùng Present Simple sau "by the time")
30. Lỗi: **will have fly** → Sửa: I will **be flying** (Future Continuous = will be + V-ing)
31. Lỗi: **will replaced** → Sửa: many jobs **will have been replaced** (Future Perfect Passive) / Hoặc: robots **will have replaced** many jobs
32. Lỗi: **will be knowing** → Sửa: She **will know** the result (know = stative verb)
33. Lỗi: **will finished** → Sửa: the team **will have finished** (Future Perfect = will have + V3)
