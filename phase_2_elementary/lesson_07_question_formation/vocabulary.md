### Bài tập 2: Chuyển câu hỏi trực tiếp sang gián tiếp (10 items)

*Viết lại câu hỏi trực tiếp thành câu hỏi gián tiếp lịch sự.*

1. "Where is the post office?" → Could you tell me _______________?
2. "What time does the bank close?" → Do you know _______________?
3. "Is this seat taken?" → Could you tell me _______________?
4. "How much does this jacket cost?" → Do you know _______________?
5. "Did she pass the interview?" → I wonder _______________?
6. "Why are they arguing?" → Can you tell me _______________?
7. "Will the bus be on time?" → Do you have any idea _______________?
8. "How long will the journey take?" → Could you tell me _______________?
9. "Where did he go after the meeting?" → I wonder _______________?
10. "Is the manager available?" → Could you tell me _______________?

**Đáp án:**
1. where the post office is
2. what time the bank closes
3. whether/if this seat is taken
4. how much this jacket costs
5. whether/if she passed the interview
6. why they are arguing
7. whether/if the bus will be on time
8. how long the journey will take
9. where he went after the meeting
10. whether/if the manager is available
