4. inherit

### Exercise 2: Gap-Fill — Điền từ thích hợp

Chọn từ trong hộp và chia đúng dạng Past Perfect.

**Từ trong hộp:** confirm | publish | graduate | invest | submit | recover | launch | reject | revise | overcome

1. By the time the meeting started, all participants _________________ their registration.
2. She _________________ her application a week before the deadline.
3. He _________________ in three tech companies before the market crashed.
4. By the age of 28, she _________________ her first novel.
5. The athlete _________________ from her injury before the championship.
6. Before he _________________, he _________________ this essay five times. *(Use 2 words)*
7. The company _________________ two new products before declaring bankruptcy.
8. They _________________ his proposal three times before finally accepting it.
9. She _________________ her greatest fear by the time she became a doctor.
10. By the time I called the hotel, they _________________ our reservation.

**Đáp án:**
1. had confirmed
2. had submitted
3. had invested
4. had published
5. had recovered
6. graduated / had revised
7. had launched
8. had rejected
9. had overcome

### Exercise 3: Timeline Writing — Viết câu theo trật tự thời gian

Nhìn vào chuỗi sự kiện và viết một câu hoàn chỉnh sử dụng Past Perfect.

**Mẫu:** [Sarah / learn to drive] → [She / buy a car]
→ Sarah **had learned** to drive before she bought her first car.

1. [He / sign the contract] → [The lawyers / review it]
→ _______________________________________________________________

2. [The team / develop the software] → [The client / request it]
→ _______________________________________________________________

3. [She / never / visit Europe] → [She / go on an exchange program]
→ _______________________________________________________________

4. [The company / already / close] → [We / arrive to apply for jobs]
→ _______________________________________________________________

5. [By 2022 / she / publish / 3 books]
→ _______________________________________________________________

6. [The party / finish] → [I / arrive]
→ _______________________________________________________________

**Đáp án gợi ý:**
1. He had already signed the contract before the lawyers reviewed it.
2. The team had developed the software before the client even requested it.
3. She had never visited Europe before she went on the exchange program.
4. The company had already closed by the time we arrived to apply for jobs.
5. By 2022, she had published 3 books.
6. The party had already finished when I arrived.
