## PHẦN A: ĐIỀN VÀO CHỖ TRỐNG (FILL IN THE BLANK) — 14 câu

*Chia động từ trong ngoặc đúng thì: Past Perfect hoặc Past Simple.*

1. By the time we _________________ (arrive) at the cinema, the film _________________ (already/start).

2. She _________________ (not/eat) anything all day, so she _________________ (order) a large meal.

3. When he _________________ (open) the fridge, he _________________ (realize) someone _________________ (eat) his lunch.

4. They _________________ (be) married for ten years before they _________________ (have) their first child.

5. _________________ you _________________ (ever/visit) Ha Long Bay before you _________________ (move) to Quang Ninh?

6. After she _________________ (read) the letter, she _________________ (burst) into tears.

7. I _________________ (not/hear) the news yet when she _________________ (call) me to congratulate me.

8. He _________________ (miss) the bus because he _________________ (wake up) late.

9. By the age of 40, the scientist _________________ (publish) more than 50 research papers.

10. The teacher _________________ (already/explain) the grammar rule before the textbook _________________ (arrive).

11. When I _________________ (get) to the party, most of the guests _________________ (already/leave).

12. She _________________ (say) she _________________ (never/see) such beautiful mountains in her life.

13. _________________ the doctor _________________ (examine) him before the nurse _________________ (give) the injection?

14. By the time the rescue team _________________ (reach) them, the hikers _________________ (survive) three nights in the mountains.

# ĐÁP ÁN (ANSWER KEY)

## Phần A:
1. arrived / had already started
2. hadn't eaten / ordered
3. opened / realized / had eaten
4. had been / had
5. Had you ever visited / moved
6. had read / burst
7. hadn't heard / called
8. missed / had woken up
9. had published
10. had already explained / arrived
11. got / had already left
12. said / had never seen
13. Had the doctor examined / gave
14. reached / had survived

## Phần B:
15. C) had lost
16. B) had already collapsed
17. C) had never eaten
18. C) Both A and B are correct (after = thứ tự rõ ràng → có thể dùng cả hai)
19. B) turned / had learned
20. B) hadn't stayed
21. C) Had / seen
22. C) hadn't eaten
23. C) Before or at the moment he graduated
24. C) — Sai vì không có sự kiện tham chiếu; nên dùng "went" và "bought"

## Phần C:
25. By the time his parents arrived, he had already eaten dinner.
26. She had studied French for five years before she moved to Paris.
27. After he had lost his job, he applied for a new one.
28. When we arrived, the concert had already started.
29. She made mistakes because she hadn't read the instructions.
30. I was surprised because nobody had told me about the change (already).
31. When I first saw the bridge, they had already built it (two years earlier) / it had already been built in 2018.

## Phần D:
32. Lỗi: **have** → Sửa: the guests **had** already eaten. (QKĐ yêu cầu QKHT, không phải HTHT)
33. Lỗi: **spend** → Sửa: they **had spent** over a million dollars.
34. Lỗi: **saw** → Sửa: I had **seen** that film twice. (V3 = seen, không phải V2 = saw)
35. Lỗi: **has** → Sửa: he **had** visited Rome the previous year. (reported speech → lùi thì)
36. Lỗi: **hadn't never** (double negative) → Sửa: She **had never** been to Japan. (bỏ "hadn't")
37. Lỗi: **had ate** → Sửa: After they **had eaten** all the food. (V3 = eaten)
38. Lỗi: **had come** → Sửa: when your mother **came** home. (mệnh đề điều kiện/thời gian → Past Simple)

## Phần E:
39. When I arrived at the station, the last train had already left.
40. She felt tired because she hadn't slept well the night before.
41. He told me that he had once lived in Japan before moving to Vietnam.
42. By the age of 25, she had graduated from university and started her career.
43. Had you ever eaten pho before you came to Hanoi?
44. By the time we arrived at the restaurant, they had already closed.
45. After he had studied English for three years, he was confident enough to apply for a job at a foreign company.
