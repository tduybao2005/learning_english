# EXERCISE — BÀI 1: THÌ QUÁ KHỨ TIẾP DIỄN (PAST CONTINUOUS TENSE)

**Tổng số bài tập: 40**

---

## PHẦN A: ĐIỀN VÀO CHỖ TRỐNG (FILL IN THE BLANK) — 14 câu

*Chia động từ trong ngoặc theo thì Quá Khứ Tiếp Diễn hoặc Quá Khứ Đơn khi cần.*

1. I _________________ (watch) TV when my mother _________________ (call) me for dinner.

2. While the students _________________ (take) the exam, a fire alarm _________________ (go off).

3. She _________________ (not/pay) attention during the lesson, so she _________________ (miss) the explanation.

4. What _________________ you _________________ (do) at 7 o'clock yesterday morning?

5. _________________ it _________________ (rain) when you _________________ (leave) the house?

6. He _________________ (drive) to work when he _________________ (hear) the news on the radio.

7. The sun _________________ (shine) and birds _________________ (sing) when we _________________ (wake up) this morning. *(Chú ý: "this morning" ở đây là sáng ngày hôm trước trong câu chuyện)*

8. While I _________________ (cook) dinner, my sister _________________ (set) the table.

9. They _________________ (argue) about money when their child _________________ (walk) into the room.

10. The teacher _________________ (explain) the lesson when the bell _________________ (ring).

11. _________________ she _________________ (wear) her new dress when you _________________ (see) her?

12. The baby _________________ (sleep) peacefully when the doorbell _________________ (wake) it up.

13. We _________________ (live) in Da Nang when the typhoon _________________ (hit).

14. He _________________ (not/expect) to see me there because I _________________ (walk) in the opposite direction.

## PHẦN B: TRẮC NGHIỆM (MULTIPLE CHOICE) — 10 câu

- A) played
- B) were playing
- C) have played
- D) play

**16.** I _______ a shower when someone knocked at the door.
- A) took

**17.** While she _______ to music, her phone fell off the table.
- A) listened

- C) was listening
- D) had listened

**18.** What _______ at 10 PM last night?
- A) were you doing
- B) did you do
- C) have you done
- D) you were doing

**19.** He _______ dinner when I called him. He answered immediately.
- A) was having
- B) wasn't having
- C) had

- A) Was / crying
- B) Did / cry

- A) watched
- B) were watching
- C) watch
- D) have watched

**22.** "I _______ the answer" is INCORRECT because 'know' is a stative verb. The correct form is:
- A) was knowing
- B) knew
- C) had known
- D) am knowing

**23.** The manager _______ a report _______ the fire alarm went off.
- A) wrote / while
- B) was writing / when

- A) set / began

## Phần D:
32. Lỗi: **were** → Sửa: She **was** cooking dinner when the guests arrived.
33. Lỗi: **was hearing** → Sửa: While he was driving, he **heard** a strange noise. ('hear' là stative verb)
34. Lỗi: **was** → Sửa: The children **were** playing in the park.
35. Lỗi: **was knowing** → Sửa: I **knew** all the answers. ('know' là stative verb)
36. Lỗi: **eatting** → Sửa: We were **eating** dinner. (eat → eating, không nhân đôi 't')
37. Lỗi: Trật tự câu hỏi → Sửa: **What was she doing** when you called her?
38. Lỗi: **played** → Sửa: While they **were playing** chess, I was studying.
