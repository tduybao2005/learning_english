### Exercise 2: Gap-Fill — Điền từ thích hợp vào chỗ trống

Chọn từ trong hộp để điền vào chỗ trống. Chia động từ đúng dạng nếu cần.

**Từ trong hộp:** rush | sneak | rehearse | investigate | gaze | panic | settle | protest | unfold | tremble

1. Police were _____________ the suspicious activity in the neighborhood.
2. The actress was _____________ her lines backstage before the show.
3. As the story _____________, we realized it was more complex than we thought.
4. She was _____________ out of the house so she wouldn't wake her parents.
5. Students were _____________ outside the university about the new fees.
6. He was _____________ to finish his report before the deadline.
7. Dust was _____________ on every surface in the old abandoned house.
8. People were _____________ and running in all directions after the alarm.
9. Her voice was _____________ as she delivered the difficult news.
10. He was _____________ at the ocean while thinking about his future.

**Đáp án:**
1. investigating
2. rehearsing
3. unfolded
4. sneaking
5. protesting
6. rushing
7. settling
8. panicking

10. gazing

### Exercise 3: Contextual Usage — Viết câu hoàn chỉnh

Dùng từ gợi ý để viết câu sử dụng Past Continuous Tense. Thêm chi tiết để câu có nghĩa rõ ràng.

**Mẫu:** (wander / market) → She was wandering through the busy market when she found a beautiful bracelet.

1. (sprint / park) → _____________________________________________
