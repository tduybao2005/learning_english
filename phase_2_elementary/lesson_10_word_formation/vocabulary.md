## Exercise 2: Choose the Correct Word Form
Choose the correct form of the word:

1. The (educate/education/educational) system needs reform.
2. She is very (create/creative/creativity) — she designs amazing clothes.
3. The (develop/development/developing) of AI has been rapid.
4. He behaved very (responsible/irresponsible/irresponsibly) at the party.
5. We need to (organize/organization/organized) this event carefully.
6. Pollution is (danger/dangerous/dangerously) to marine life.
7. The government has (success/succeed/successfully) reduced unemployment.
8. His (manage/management/manageable) skills are excellent.
9. She works very (hard/hardly/hardness) every day.
10. The project is (produce/production/productive) — we're making progress.

**Answers:** 1.educational 2.creative 3.development 4.irresponsibly 5.organize 6.dangerous 7.successfully 8.management 9.hard 10.productive
