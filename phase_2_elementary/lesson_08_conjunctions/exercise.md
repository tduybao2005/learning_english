## PHẦN F: VIẾT ĐOẠN VĂN — 1 bài

*Viết một đoạn văn (80–100 từ) về một thách thức bạn đã từng đối mặt (ví dụ: thi cử, học bài, tập thể thao, hoặc công việc). Sử dụng ít nhất 5 liên từ KHÁC NHAU từ các nhóm sau và gạch chân chúng:*

- Coordinating (but / so / yet)
- Cause (because / since / due to)
- Contrast (although / despite / however)
- Condition (unless / if / provided that)
- Purpose (so that / in order to)

> **Gợi ý mở đầu:**
> *"When I was preparing for my university entrance exam, I faced many challenges. Although I studied every evening, I often felt overwhelmed. However, I knew that unless I stayed focused, I would not achieve my goals. I created a daily schedule so that I could manage my time effectively. Despite feeling stressed, I..."*

### Phần C:

27. Even though he didn't have the required qualifications, he applied for the scholarship.
28. You must confirm your attendance, or your seat will be given away.

30. As soon as the project ended, we began a new one.
31. Despite exercising every day, he was out of shape.

33. Although he is a very hard worker, he lacks confidence. / Even though he works very hard, he lacks confidence.

### Phần D:
34. Remove "but": "Although she trained hard every day, she still didn't make it to the team."
35. Change "due to it was raining" → "because it was raining" / "due to the heavy rain"
36. Remove "don't": "Unless you study harder, you will pass the exam." *(hoặc: "If you don't study harder, you will fail.")*
37. Remove "to" after "so that": "She whispered so as to avoid waking the baby." / "She whispered so that she wouldn't wake the baby."
38. Change "Despite" → "Although": "Although he was tired, he continued working until midnight."
39. Add auxiliary: "Not only **does** she sing beautifully, but she also plays three instruments."

### Phần E (gợi ý):

43. Because of the noise, she couldn't concentrate on her studies. / She couldn't concentrate because of the noise.
44. She studied hard so that she could / in order to get a full scholarship.
45. The flight was delayed; therefore / consequently, they had to stay at the airport overnight.
