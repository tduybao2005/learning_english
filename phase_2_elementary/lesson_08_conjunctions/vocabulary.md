## BÀI TẬP TỪ VỰNG (VOCABULARY EXERCISES)

### Bài tập 1: Chọn liên từ đúng (15 items)

*Chọn liên từ phù hợp nhất để điền vào chỗ trống.*

*(although / because / so / but / however / therefore / unless / despite / whereas / since / while / as long as / in case / due to / moreover)*

1. _______________ she is very young, she is extremely mature for her age.
2. I didn't eat breakfast _______________ I was in a hurry this morning.
3. He is extroverted, _______________ his twin sister is quite introverted.
4. The project was delayed _______________ unexpected technical problems.
5. She loves swimming; _______________, she hates getting up early for practice.
6. You can borrow my notes _______________ you return them by Monday.
7. _______________ you study tonight, you'll fail the test tomorrow.
8. Take some extra money _______________ the ATM doesn't work.
9. She studied very hard, _______________ she failed the exam.
10. It was hot, _______________ we decided to go to the beach.
11. I was cooking _______________ my roommate was doing the laundry.
12. He has lost weight _______________ starting his new exercise routine.
13. This phone is excellent; _______________, it's much more affordable than similar models.
14. _______________ the heavy rain, the farmers continued working in the fields.
15. _______________ the traffic was terrible, she arrived on time.

**Đáp án:**
1. Although  2. because  3. whereas  4. due to  5. however  6. as long as  7. Unless  8. in case  9. but  10. so  11. while  12. since  13. moreover  14. Despite  15. Although

### Bài tập 2: Nối hai câu dùng liên từ cho sẵn (10 items)

*Nối hai câu thành một câu hoàn chỉnh sử dụng liên từ đã cho trong ngoặc.*

1. She practised every day. She won the competition. **[so]**
→ _______________________________________________________________

2. The weather was terrible. They enjoyed the festival. **[despite]**
→ _______________________________________________________________

3. He didn't have a car. He couldn't attend the event. **[because]**
→ _______________________________________________________________

4. She is good at science. She is also talented in arts. **[not only...but also]**
→ _______________________________________________________________

5. Turn off the lights. Leave the room. **[before]**
→ _______________________________________________________________

6. I don't like crowded places. I still went to the concert. **[although]**
→ _______________________________________________________________

7. He works hard. He doesn't earn much money. **[yet]**
→ _______________________________________________________________

8. She practised every day. She would pass the driving test. **[so that]**
→ _______________________________________________________________

9. The show will begin. All guests have arrived. **[as soon as]**
→ _______________________________________________________________

10. He is quiet in class. He is very confident on stage. **[whereas]**
→ _______________________________________________________________

**Đáp án (gợi ý):**
1. She practised every day, so she won the competition.
2. Despite the terrible weather, they enjoyed the festival.
3. He couldn't attend the event because he didn't have a car.
4. She is not only good at science but also talented in arts.
5. Turn off the lights before you leave the room.
6. Although I don't like crowded places, I still went to the concert.
7. He works hard, yet he doesn't earn much money.
8. She practised every day so that she would pass the driving test.
9. The show will begin as soon as all guests have arrived.
10. He is quiet in class, whereas he is very confident on stage.

### Bài tập 3: Viết lại không đổi nghĩa (8 items)

*Viết lại câu dùng từ/cụm từ đã cho, sao cho ý nghĩa không thay đổi.*

1. She was tired. She still finished the report. → **Despite** _______________
2. He failed because he didn't practise enough. → He failed **due to** _______________
3. Although it was cold, they went swimming. → **Despite** _______________
4. She didn't call. She sent a message instead. → She didn't call; **instead,** _______________
5. If you don't leave now, you'll miss the bus. → **Unless** _______________
6. Both the food and the service were excellent. → **Not only** _______________
7. The exam was difficult, but she passed. → The exam was difficult; **however,** _______________
8. He studied hard so that he could get a scholarship. → He studied hard **in order to** _______________

**Đáp án (gợi ý):**
1. Despite being tired, she still finished the report.
2. He failed due to insufficient practice / not practising enough.
3. Despite the cold, they went swimming. / Despite the cold weather, they went swimming.
4. She didn't call; instead, she sent a message.
5. Unless you leave now, you'll miss the bus.
6. Not only was the food excellent, but the service was also excellent.
7. The exam was difficult; however, she passed.
8. He studied hard in order to get a scholarship.
