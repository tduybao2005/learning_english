### Bài tập 2: Đọc đoạn văn và thêm mạo từ đúng vào 10 chỗ trống

*Điền a / an / the / — vào mỗi số trong ngoặc.*

"(1)___ life in (2)___ big city can be exciting but also stressful. Last week, I had (3)___ opportunity to visit (4)___ National Museum of Vietnam, which is located near (5)___ Hoan Kiem Lake in Hanoi. (6)___ museum was full of (7)___ tourists from (8)___ Europe and (9)___ Asia. I spent (10)___ hour exploring the ancient exhibits."

**Đáp án:** 1. — / Life   2. a   3. an   4. the   5. —   6. The   7. —   8. —   9. —   10. an

### Bài tập 3: Sửa lỗi mạo từ trong 10 câu sau

*Mỗi câu có một lỗi về mạo từ. Tìm và sửa lỗi.*

1. She is a best student in my class.
2. I love the music and the art.
3. He went to the school because he is a student.
4. The life is too short to waste.
5. Mount Everest is the highest mountain in a world.
6. She has a MBA from London Business School.
7. I usually eat a breakfast at 7am.
8. They travelled to Netherlands last summer.
9. He is an honest and an hardworking man.
10. The English is the most widely spoken language.

**Đáp án:**
1. a → **the** best student
2. the music and the art → **music and art** (sở thích chung)
3. the school → **school** (đi học)
4. The life → **Life** (khái niệm chung)
5. a world → **the world** (duy nhất)
6. a MBA → **an** MBA (âm /em/)
7. a breakfast → **breakfast** (bữa ăn)
8. Netherlands → **the Netherlands** (quốc gia dạng số nhiều)
9. an honest and an hardworking → **an honest and hardworking** (chỉ cần một "an")
10. The English → **English** (ngôn ngữ)
