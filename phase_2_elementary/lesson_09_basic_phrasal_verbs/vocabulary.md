## Exercise 1: Match the Phrasal Verb to its Definition
Match each phrasal verb (1-15) with its meaning (A-O):

1. give up          A. to find information by searching
2. find out         B. to stop doing something permanently
3. put off          C. to postpone, delay
4. break down       D. to disappoint someone
5. look after       E. to discover or learn something
6. let down         F. to stop working (machine)
7. work out         G. to care for someone
8. carry on         H. to exercise
9. turn down        I. to refuse
10. end up          J. to continue doing something
11. run out of      K. to have no more of something
12. call off        L. to finally be in a place/situation
13. get along with  M. to cancel
14. put up with     N. to have a good relationship with
15. look up         O. to tolerate something unpleasant
