# EXERCISE — Bài 9: Phrasal Verbs Cơ Bản

## SECTION A: Fill in the blank with the correct phrasal verb (15 items)
Choose from: [give up / find out / break down / get up / turn on / put off / carry out / look after / work out / turn down / set up / run out of / come back / clean up / end up]

1. The government plans to _____________ a new environmental policy next year.
2. You should never _____________ your dreams, no matter how difficult things get.
3. My computer _____________ in the middle of an important presentation.
4. Can someone _____________ the heating? It's freezing in here.
5. She _____________ at 5am every morning to go jogging.
6. Please _____________ after the meeting — there's paper everywhere.
7. How did you _____________ that information about the new project?
8. He _____________ the job offer because the salary was too low.
9. The babysitter will _____________ the children while their parents are away.
10. They've _____________ milk — can you go to the supermarket?
11. Don't _____________ your homework until the night before!
12. She _____________ at the gym every Monday and Thursday.
13. When will your brother _____________ from his trip to Japan?
14. The research team will _____________ a series of experiments this month.
15. We got lost and _____________ at a completely different restaurant.

## SECTION B: Separable or Inseparable? (10 items)
Choose the correct sentence (A or B):

1. A) She turned down the offer.  B) She turned the offer down.
*(Which is/are correct? Both? Just A? Just B?)*

2. A) He looked after the children.  B) He looked the children after.

3. A) She put off the meeting.  B) She put the meeting off.  C) She put it off.  D) All are correct.

4. A) Can you look into it?  B) Can you look it into?

5. A) I'll pay back you.  B) I'll pay you back.  C) I'll pay back the money.  D) Both B and C.

6. A) He gave up smoking.  B) He gave smoking up.  C) He gave it up.  D) All are correct.

7. A) She ran out of time.  B) She ran time out of.

8. A) They called the match off.  B) They called off the match.  C) They called it off.  D) All are correct.

9. A) He get along his boss with.  B) He gets along with his boss.

10. A) She put up noise with.  B) She put up with the noise.
