## BÀI TẬP TỪ VỰNG (VOCABULARY EXERCISES)

### Bài tập 1: Ghép modal với chức năng (Match modal to function)

*Nối từng modal verb (1–10) với chức năng phù hợp (a–j).*

1. must (affirmative)       a. Lời khuyên có cảnh báo
2. don't have to            b. Xin phép trang trọng
3. could (past)             c. Bắt buộc nội tâm / suy luận chắc
4. might                    d. Thói quen trong quá khứ (không còn nữa)
5. should                   e. Tuyệt đối cấm
6. may (formal request)     f. Khả năng thấp (~30%)
7. had better               g. Không bắt buộc, tùy ý
8. mustn't                  h. Lời khuyên thông thường
9. used to                  i. Khả năng trong quá khứ
10. would (polite)          j. Yêu cầu lịch sự

**Đáp án:** 1-c, 2-g, 3-i, 4-f, 5-h, 6-b, 7-a, 8-e, 9-d, 10-j

### Bài tập 2: Chọn modal đúng theo ngữ cảnh (15 items)

*Chọn modal phù hợp nhất trong ngoặc.*

1. The doctor said I (should / must) take this medicine three times a day — it's a prescription.
2. You (don't have to / mustn't) shout — I can hear you perfectly well.
3. (Could / Would) you like another cup of tea? I just made a fresh pot.
4. I (used to / would) hate spicy food, but now I love it.
5. She (must / might) be at home — I'm not sure, she didn't reply to my message.
6. You (had better / should) hand in your report now, or the teacher will deduct marks.
7. In 1990, he (could / was able to) run the 100m in under 11 seconds.
8. (May / Can) I borrow your dictionary for a moment? *(trang trọng)*
9. Students (must not / don't have to) use their phones during the exam. *(CẤM)*
10. She (must / can't) have left already — her coat is still here.
11. You (ought to / had better) apologise to him — it was your fault.
12. (Shall / Will) I open the window? It's quite warm in here.
13. He (might / must) have forgotten the meeting — he never misses it otherwise.
14. I (would / used to) visit my grandparents every Sunday when I was a child.
15. They (could / would) not find a solution even after three hours of discussion.

**Đáp án:** 1. must  2. don't have to  3. Would  4. used to  5. might  6. had better  7. was able to  8. May  9. must not  10. can't  11. ought to  12. Shall  13. must  14. would  15. could
