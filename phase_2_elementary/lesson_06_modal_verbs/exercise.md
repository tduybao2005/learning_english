## PHẦN B: MUST / HAVE TO / MUSTN'T / DON'T HAVE TO — 10 câu

*Điền must / have to / mustn't / don't have to vào chỗ trống. Đôi khi có thể có hơn một đáp án đúng.*

**16.** You _______ return your library books today — they're due back.
> (bắt buộc vì quy định thư viện)

**17.** Passengers _______ smoke anywhere on the aircraft.
> (cấm tuyệt đối)

**18.** Children under 12 _______ pay — admission is free for them.
> (không cần thiết)

**19.** I really feel I _______ apologise to her after what I said.
> (cảm thấy nội tâm muốn làm)

**20.** You _______ submit your assignment online — you can also hand it in at the office.
> (không bắt buộc một cách duy nhất)

**21.** Visitors to this museum _______ touch the exhibits under any circumstances.
> (cấm tuyệt đối)

**22.** I _______ get up early tomorrow — it's a public holiday.
> (không cần thiết)

**23.** All new employees _______ attend an orientation session during their first week.
> (bắt buộc — quy định công ty)

**24.** We _______ use all the budget — we can carry some over to next year.
> (không bắt buộc)

**25.** She _______ be very tired — she worked a double shift at the hospital.
> (suy luận chắc chắn)
