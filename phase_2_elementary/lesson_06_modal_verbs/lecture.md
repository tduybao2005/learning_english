# BÀI 6: ĐỘNG TỪ TÌNH THÁI (MODAL VERBS)

---

## GIỚI THIỆU

Động từ tình thái (Modal Verbs) là các động từ đặc biệt dùng để diễn đạt:
- **Khả năng** (ability): can, could, be able to
- **Xin phép / cho phép** (permission): can, may, could
- **Nghĩa vụ / bắt buộc** (obligation): must, have to, should
- **Sự cho phép, lời đề nghị** (offers): will, shall, would
- **Suy đoán** (deduction): must, can't, might, could
- **Lời khuyên** (advice): should, ought to, had better

### Đặc điểm chung của Modal Verbs:
1. Theo sau là **động từ nguyên mẫu không có "to"**: She **can swim**. (NOT: She can to swim.)
2. **Không thêm -s/-es** ở ngôi 3 số ít: He **can** go. (NOT: He cans go.)
3. Tạo câu phủ định bằng cách thêm **not**: cannot, could not, will not...
4. Tạo câu hỏi bằng cách **đảo modal lên trước chủ ngữ**: **Can** you help?

---

## 1. CAN / COULD / BE ABLE TO

### 1.1 Can — Khả năng hiện tại
- She **can** speak three languages fluently.
- **Can** you swim? — Yes, I can. / No, I can't.
- I **can't** understand this instruction.

### 1.2 Can — Xin phép / Cho phép (không trang trọng)
- **Can** I use your phone? (informal)
- You **can** leave early today.

### 1.3 Could — Khả năng trong quá khứ
- When I was a child, I **could** run very fast.
- She **could** read at the age of four.
- We **couldn't** go out because of the storm.

### 1.4 Could — Lịch sự / Điều kiện
- **Could** you help me with this? *(lịch sự hơn "can")*
- **Could** you pass the salt, please?
- If I had more time, I **could** help you.

### 1.5 Be able to — Thay thế can ở tất cả các thì
- I **was able to** finish the project on time. (không dùng "could" trong tình huống cụ thể)
- She **has been able to** speak English since she was ten.
- They will **be able to** attend the conference next month.
- I would like **to be able to** travel the world.

> **Lưu ý:** "Could" (khả năng quá khứ chung chung) nhưng "was/were able to" (thực hiện thành công một lần):
> - He **could** run fast when he was young. ✓ (khả năng chung)
> - He **was able to** escape from the building. ✓ (thành công cụ thể)
> - He **could** escape from the building. ⚠️ (không tự nhiên cho tình huống cụ thể)

---

## 2. MAY / MIGHT

### 2.1 May — Xin phép trang trọng
- **May** I come in? *(trang trọng hơn "can")*
- **May** I ask you a question?

### 2.2 May — Khả năng xảy ra (~50%)
- She **may** be late today — she had an early meeting.
- It **may** rain this afternoon. Bring a jacket.
- He **may not** be able to attend the meeting.

### 2.3 Might — Khả năng thấp hơn (~30%)
- I **might** go to the party, but I'm not sure.
- She **might** have missed the train.
- You **might** want to call ahead before visiting.
- He **might not** agree with our proposal.

> **So sánh:**
> - "She **may** come." → 50% có thể đến
> - "She **might** come." → 30% có thể đến, ít chắc hơn

---

## 3. WILL / WOULD

### 3.1 Will — Quyết định tức thời
- The phone is ringing — I **will** answer it!
- I **won't** eat any more — I'm full.

### 3.2 Will — Lời hứa và lời đề nghị
- I **will** help you move the furniture this weekend.
- **Will** you marry me?
- I **will** always be there for you.

### 3.3 Will — Dự đoán chắc chắn (có bằng chứng)
- Look at those clouds — it **will** rain soon.
- She **will** pass the exam — she studied very hard.

### 3.4 Would — Lịch sự
- **Would** you like some tea?
- **Would** you mind closing the window?
- I **would** like to make a reservation.

### 3.5 Would — Điều kiện (câu điều kiện loại 2)
- If I were rich, I **would** travel the world.
- If she studied harder, she **would** get better grades.

### 3.6 Would — Thói quen trong quá khứ (= "used to")
- When I was young, I **would** play by the river every day.
- Every summer, we **would** visit our grandparents.

---

## 4. SHALL / SHOULD

### 4.1 Shall — Đề nghị trang trọng (với I/We)
- **Shall** I help you carry those bags?
- **Shall** we begin the meeting now?
- What **shall** we do this evening?

### 4.2 Should — Lời khuyên
- You **should** exercise more regularly.
- She **should** see a doctor about that cough.
- You **shouldn't** eat so much junk food.

### 4.3 Should — Kỳ vọng (expectation)
- The package **should** arrive by Thursday.
- It **should** be warm in July — it always is.
- She **should** know the answer — she's an expert.

### 4.4 Should — Phê bình nhẹ về quá khứ (với have + past participle)
- You **should have** called me earlier!
- She **should have** checked her work before submitting.
- They **shouldn't have** left without saying goodbye.

---

## 5. MUST / HAVE TO

### 5.1 Must — Bắt buộc nội tâm (cảm thấy cần thiết)
- I **must** study harder — I want to pass this exam.
- You **must** try this restaurant — the food is incredible!
- He felt he **must** apologise for his behaviour.

### 5.2 Have to — Bắt buộc ngoại cảnh (quy định, luật lệ, người khác yêu cầu)
- Students **have to** wear uniforms at this school. *(quy định)*
- I **have to** finish this report by 5pm. *(sếp yêu cầu)*
- Do you **have to** work on Saturdays?

### 5.3 PHÂN BIỆT QUAN TRỌNG: Must not vs Don't have to

| Must not (mustn't) | Don't have to |
|-------------------|---------------|
| Tuyệt đối **KHÔNG ĐƯỢC** làm | Không **BẮT BUỘC** phải làm |
| Cấm cản | Tự do lựa chọn |
| You **mustn't** smoke here. (CẤM) | You **don't have to** wear a tie. (không bắt buộc) |
| You **mustn't** tell anyone. (bí mật) | You **don't have to** come if you're busy. (tùy ý) |

> **Đây là lỗi cực kỳ phổ biến của người Việt:**
> - ❌ "You must not come if you don't want to." (SAI — nghe như CẤM đến)
> - ✓ "You **don't have to** come if you don't want to." (ĐÚNG — không bắt buộc)

### 5.4 Must — Suy luận chắc chắn (logical deduction)
- She's been working all night — she **must** be exhausted.
- He knows everything about football — he **must** love the sport.
- There are no keys in the lock — someone **must** have taken them.

### 5.5 Can't — Suy luận phủ định (nearly certain it's NOT true)
- She **can't** be 40 — she looks so young!
- He **can't** have eaten all that food by himself.
- That **can't** be right — the figures don't add up.

---

## 6. OUGHT TO / HAD BETTER

### 6.1 Ought to — Lời khuyên trang trọng (giống should)
- You **ought to** apologise for what you said.
- He **ought to** see a specialist about his back pain.
- They **ought not to** waste so much food.

### 6.2 Had better — Lời khuyên CÓ CẢNH BÁO (nên làm không thì...)
- You **had better** leave now or you'll miss the train.
- She **had better** study tonight — the exam is tomorrow.
- We **had better not** be late — the boss hates waiting.

> **Chú ý:** "had better" luôn rút gọn thành "'d better" trong văn nói:
> - You'**d** better hurry up.

---

## 7. USED TO

### 7.1 Diễn đạt thói quen trong quá khứ (không còn nữa)
- I **used to** play computer games every day. *(bây giờ không còn nữa)*
- She **used to** live in Da Nang before moving to Hanoi.
- We **used to** walk to school — now we take the bus.

### 7.2 Phủ định và câu hỏi
- I **didn't use to** like coffee, but now I love it.
- **Did** you **use to** have long hair when you were young?
- He **used not to** be so serious. *(formal)*

> **Phân biệt:**
> - **Used to + V** (thói quen quá khứ): I used to smoke. *(đã từng, nay không còn)*
> - **Be used to + V-ing** (quen với): I am used to waking up early. *(quen rồi)*
> - **Get used to + V-ing** (đang dần quen với): I'm getting used to living alone.

---

## 8. MODAL PERFECTS (ĐỘNG TỪ TÌNH THÁI + HAVE + V3)

Dùng để nói về **tình huống trong quá khứ** mà chúng ta suy đoán hoặc tiếc nuối.

| Modal Perfect | Ý nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|--------------|---------|-------|
| must have done | chắc chắn đã xảy ra | She **must have left** early — her bag is gone. |
| can't have done | chắc chắn KHÔNG xảy ra | He **can't have stolen** it — he's so honest. |
| could have done | có thể đã xảy ra, hoặc có thể đã làm nhưng không | I **could have helped** but I didn't know. |
| might have done | có lẽ đã xảy ra (~30%) | She **might have forgotten** the appointment. |
| should have done | đáng lẽ đã làm (nhưng không làm) | You **should have told** me sooner! |
| shouldn't have done | đáng lẽ không nên làm | He **shouldn't have driven** after drinking. |
| needn't have done | không cần làm (nhưng đã làm) | You **needn't have worried** — everything was fine. |
| would have done | điều kiện loại 3 (câu điều kiện) | I **would have come** if I had known. |

---

## 9. MODAL + PASSIVE (ĐỘNG TỪ TÌNH THÁI + BỊ ĐỘNG)

```
Modal + be + past participle
```

- The report **should be submitted** by Friday.
- All phones **must be switched off** in the exam room.
- The project **can be completed** in two weeks.
- Mistakes **might be made** when working under pressure.
- The building **will be renovated** next year.
- This problem **could be solved** with more resources.

---

## 10. BẢNG TỔNG HỢP MODAL VERBS

| Modal | Chức năng chính | Ví dụ |
|-------|----------------|-------|
| can | khả năng hiện tại, xin phép (informal) | Can you drive? |
| could | khả năng quá khứ, lịch sự, điều kiện | Could you help me? |
| be able to | khả năng (mọi thì) | I was able to finish on time. |
| may | xin phép (formal), khả năng 50% | May I leave? / It may rain. |
| might | khả năng thấp (~30%) | She might be late. |
| will | quyết định tức thời, lời hứa, dự đoán chắc | I'll help you. |
| would | lịch sự, điều kiện, thói quen quá khứ | Would you like tea? |
| shall | đề nghị/gợi ý (I/We) | Shall we start? |
| should | lời khuyên, kỳ vọng | You should rest. |
| must | bắt buộc nội tâm, suy luận chắc | I must study. / He must be tired. |
| have to | bắt buộc ngoại cảnh | I have to go to work. |
| mustn't | tuyệt đối cấm | You mustn't smoke here. |
| don't have to | không bắt buộc | You don't have to come. |
| ought to | lời khuyên (trang trọng) | You ought to apologise. |
| had better | lời khuyên có cảnh báo | You'd better hurry. |
| used to | thói quen quá khứ (không còn nữa) | I used to play football. |

## LỖI THƯỜNG GẶP

| Sai | Đúng | Giải thích |
|---|---|---|
| She can to swim very fast. | She can swim very fast. | Sau modal verb KHÔNG dùng "to" (dùng bare infinitive) |
| He musts leave now. | He must leave now. | Modal verbs không chia theo ngôi, không thêm -s |
| You don't must smoke here. | You mustn't smoke here. | "Mustn't" = cấm tuyệt đối; không nói "don't must" |
| You don't have to go there — it's forbidden. | You mustn't go there — it's forbidden. | "Don't have to" = không cần thiết; "mustn't" = bị cấm |
| I could finished the work yesterday. | I could have finished the work yesterday. | Modal + quá khứ = modal + have + V3 |
| May I to borrow your pen? | May I borrow your pen? | Sau "may" không dùng "to" |
| She should to rest more. | She should rest more. | Sau "should" không dùng "to" |
| He might to come later. | He might come later. | Sau "might" không dùng "to" |

---

## TÓM TẮT NHANH

- **Không chia theo ngôi và thì:** Modal verbs (can, could, may, might, will, would, shall, should, must, ought to) không thêm -s/-ed.
- **Sau modal: bare infinitive:** She can swim / He must leave / You should rest (không có "to").
- **Must vs Have to:** "must" = bắt buộc từ nội tâm người nói; "have to" = bắt buộc do quy tắc bên ngoài.
- **Mustn't vs Don't have to:** "mustn't" = tuyệt đối cấm; "don't have to" = không bắt buộc (nhưng có thể làm).
- **Modal + have + V3 cho quá khứ:** "She could have won" (đáng lẽ đã thắng được) / "He must have left" (chắc đã đi rồi).
- **Khả năng:** can (chắc chắn) > may (50%) > might (30%) > could (giả thuyết).
