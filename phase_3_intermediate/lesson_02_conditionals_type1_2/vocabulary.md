## BÀI TẬP TỪ VỰNG

### Bài tập 1: Điền từ thích hợp

Chọn từ trong danh sách: *deforestation / inequality / legislation / alleviate / catastrophe / collaboration / incentives / sustainable / emissions / accountability*

1. If __________________ were introduced to ban harmful chemicals, pollution would be reduced.
2. If carbon __________________ are not cut by 50% by 2030, global warming will accelerate.
3. If wealth __________________ continues to grow, social unrest will become inevitable.
4. If adequate __________________ were offered, more farmers would adopt __________________ practices.
5. If __________________ is not stopped, biodiversity in tropical regions will collapse.
6. International __________________ is essential if we are to address global challenges effectively.
7. Aid programs can __________________ suffering in the short term, but long-term solutions are needed.
8. If leaders were held to greater __________________, corruption would be harder to conceal.
9. Without immediate action, the situation could turn into an environmental __________________.
