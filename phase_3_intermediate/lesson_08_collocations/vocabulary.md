## EXERCISES (BÀI TẬP)

### Exercise 1: Choose the Correct Collocation

Chọn từ đúng để tạo collocation tự nhiên.

1. The government needs to _______ action on climate change. (make / take / do)
2. She _______ considerable progress in her second year of study. (did / took / made)
3. The factory fire did serious _______ to the surrounding neighbourhood. (harm / damage / injury) — *Note: both harm and damage work; choose the more appropriate one.*
4. There is _______ evidence that diet affects mental health. (compelling / strong / big)
5. The new policy will come into _______ next January. (power / effect / force)
6. Rising temperatures give _______ to increased hurricane activity. (birth / rise / way)
7. Deforestation has a _______ impact on local water cycles. (powerful / profound / strong) — *all possible; choose the most academic*
8. Scientists are deeply _______ about the rate of Arctic ice loss. (worried / concerned / troubled)

*(Answers: 1-take, 2-made, 3-damage, 4-compelling, 5-effect, 6-rise, 7-profound, 8-concerned)*

### Exercise 2: Correct the Collocation Errors

Tìm và sửa lỗi collocation trong mỗi câu.

1. The new traffic laws have done a great difference to road safety.
2. She made extensive research into the effects of social media on teenagers.
3. The government needs to do strong action to combat poverty.
4. There is a high concern about the levels of youth unemployment.
5. The factory makes serious damage to the nearby river ecosystem.
6. Policymakers must take priority to investing in renewable energy.
7. The company says an ambitious plan to reduce emissions by 50%.
8. Strong rain and flooding caused widespread disruption to transport services.

*(Corrections: 1-made→a great difference; 2-made→did; 3-do→take; 4-high→deep/widespread; 5-makes→does/causes; 6-take priority→give priority; 7-says→announced/put forward; 8-Strong→Heavy)*
