## SECTION A: CONVERT TO CORRECT NOUN FORM (15 điểm — mỗi câu 1 điểm)

Viết dạng danh từ hóa đúng của từ được gạch chân trong mỗi câu. Viết vào chỗ trống.

1. We need to **invest** more in infrastructure. → The need for greater _______ in infrastructure is clear.

2. Scientists are trying to **discover** a vaccine. → The **_______** of a vaccine remains the top priority.

3. The city has **grown** dramatically since 2000. → The dramatic **_______** of the city since 2000 is evident.

4. Many workers have **lost** their jobs. → Mass **_______** of employment has affected thousands.

5. The government **decided** to reduce spending. → The government's **_______** to reduce spending proved controversial.

6. Education systems must **improve** continuously. → Continuous **_______** of education systems is essential.

7. The factory **produces** 5,000 units daily. → Daily **_______** at the factory reaches 5,000 units.

8. Companies must **manage** resources wisely. → Wise **_______** of resources ensures long-term sustainability.

9. Nations need to **cooperate** on climate issues. → International **_______** on climate issues is indispensable.

10. Children should be **educated** in a safe environment. → **_______** in a safe environment is a child's basic right.

11. Researchers need to **analyze** the findings carefully. → A careful **_______** of the findings is needed.

12. The new law **establishes** environmental standards. → The **_______** of environmental standards is a major step.

13. The economy has **recovered** slowly. → The economy's slow **_______** has disappointed analysts.

14. People should be more **responsible** for their actions. → Greater personal **_______** is expected of citizens.

15. Scientists **discovered** that the compound is toxic. → The **_______** that this compound is toxic prompted a recall.

### Section B (đáp án gợi ý — chấp nhận các cách diễn đạt tương đương):
1. *Industrial pollution of rivers is an urgent environmental concern.*
2. *The protection of endangered species requires governmental commitment.*
3. *Electricity consumption has reached an all-time high.*
4. *The rise in temperatures is a direct consequence of fossil fuel combustion.*
5. *The company's failure to meet its carbon reduction targets has raised concerns.*
6. *Teacher motivation is a key factor in the enhancement of student learning.*
7. *Conflict over natural resources is escalating between nations.* (hoặc: *Competition for natural resources has intensified.*)
8. *The internet has transformed the nature of human communication.*
9. *The migration of rural populations to urban areas is accelerating.*
10. *The researchers' suggestion was that increased investment in renewable energy is necessary.*

### Section C (đáp án mẫu):
> *There is widespread argument among researchers that social media is detrimental to young people's mental health. The decline in face-to-face communication among teenagers has resulted in a deterioration of social skills. This situation has worsened since the widespread availability of smartphones. The failure of governments to effectively regulate social media platforms remains a significant concern. Therefore, the resolution of this issue demands immediate action before it develops into a full-scale crisis.*

Những nominalization đã sử dụng: argument, decline, communication, deterioration, availability, failure, resolution

### Section D:
1. Lỗi: "decision of the government" — cần "of" chỉ hành động thuộc về ai → không sai hoàn toàn, nhưng tự nhiên hơn khi dùng: *The government's decision to raise taxes was met with public opposition.* (possessive thường tự nhiên hơn "of + agent")
2. Lỗi: "increase of" → đúng: "increase IN" → *There has been a significant increase **in** road accidents this year.*
3. Lỗi: "exploration to" → đúng: "exploration of" → *The exploration **of** Mars represents a major achievement for humanity.*
4. Lỗi: "made the development" → "made" không phải collocate của "development"; đúng: "achieved the development" hoặc hay hơn: *Scientists **developed** a new drug...* / *Scientists achieved a breakthrough in developing a new drug that treats diabetes.*
5. Lỗi: "populace" (dân chúng) — dùng sai; đúng: "population" → *The growth in urban **population**...*
6. Lỗi: "decline of" → đúng: "decline IN" → *There was a major decline **in** foreign investment following the political crisis.*

### Section E (đáp án gợi ý):
1. *The rapid **growth** of major cities has led to the **loss** of significant green space.*
2. *A lack of **investment** in education is one of the primary causes of poverty.*
3. *The growing public **awareness** of climate change has placed increasing pressure on governments.*
4. *The government's **decision** to legalize renewable energy has attracted considerable foreign **investment**.*
5. *The **decline** in biodiversity is an urgent issue that demands an immediate **solution**.*

### Section F (đáp án mẫu — Band 7):
> *The rapid **urbanization** (1) of the world's major cities poses significant challenges for modern governance. The **concentration** (2) of population in urban centres has strained public services and infrastructure, while simultaneously generating environmental pressures. This essay will examine the primary problems associated with urban **growth** (3) and propose practical measures to address them, including improvements in urban **planning** (4) and investment in sustainable transportation.*

Điểm cho Section F:
- Sử dụng đúng ≥4 nominalization: 12 điểm
- Độ chính xác ngữ pháp: 5 điểm
- Câu thesis rõ ràng: 3 điểm
- Từ vựng tự nhiên, học thuật: 3 điểm
