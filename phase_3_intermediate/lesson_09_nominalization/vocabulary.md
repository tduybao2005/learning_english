## GIỚI TỪ SAU NOMINALIZATION — BẢNG TÓM TẮT

| Giới từ | Nominalization đi kèm | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **in** | increase, decrease, rise, fall, decline, change, improvement, reduction | *a rise **in** temperatures* |
| **of** | development, establishment, loss, failure, discovery, cause | *the discovery **of** a cure* |
| **on** | effect, impact, influence, effect | *the impact **on** health* |
| **to** | solution, response, reaction, alternative, answer, access | *a solution **to** the problem* |
| **for** | need, reason, demand, justification | *the need **for** reform* |
| **from** | recovery, protection, escape | *protection **from** disease* |
| **between** | relationship, difference, distinction, comparison | *the difference **between** them* |

### Bài tập 3: Điền danh từ hóa đúng vào chỗ trống

Chọn từ trong ngoặc và điền vào chỗ trống (dùng dạng danh từ hóa):

1. The _______ of new technology has transformed daily life. (introduce / apply)
2. There has been a significant _______ in the number of university graduates. (increase / develop)
3. The _______ of fossil fuels contributes to climate change. (consume / burn)
4. _______ of information is easier than ever before in the digital age. (access / communicate)
5. The _______ between rural and urban income remains a serious issue. (differ / contrast)
6. A _______ to the housing crisis requires multi-sector cooperation. (solve / respond)
7. The _______ of democracy in developing nations has been inconsistent. (establish / govern)
8. Public _______ of the health risks of smoking has increased. (aware / know)
9. The _______ of wildlife habitats is largely due to agricultural expansion. (lose / destroy)
10. Greater _______ in working hours has improved employee wellbeing. (flex / free)

**Đáp án:**
1. introduction 2. increase 3. consumption 4. Access 5. difference 6. solution 7. establishment 8. awareness 9. loss 10. flexibility
