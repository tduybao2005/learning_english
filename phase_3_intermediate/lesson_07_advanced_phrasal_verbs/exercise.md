## Section D: Error Correction

32. **Error:** "gave out" **Correction:** "gave in" *(gave out = phân phát; gave in = nhượng bộ)*
33. **Assessment:** This sentence is grammatically and semantically acceptable. "Stood up against" and "spoke out against" both work here. If error required: "stood up against" → "spoke out against" is slightly more natural for public protest.
34. **Error:** "drawing on" **Correction:** "drawing up" *(drawing on = vận dụng kiến thức có sẵn; drawing up = soạn thảo kế hoạch/tài liệu)*
35. **Error:** "looked across" **Correction:** "came across" *(tình cờ phát hiện ra)*
36. **Assessment:** "Carrying on" (= tiếp tục) is grammatically correct here but "carrying out" (= tiến hành) is more precise for research. **Error:** "carrying on" **Better:** "carrying out"
37. **Error:** "broke up" **Correction:** "broke down" *(broke up = chia tay/tan vỡ nhóm; broke down = đổ vỡ trong đàm phán)*
38. **Error:** "turn over" **Correction:** "turn around" *(turn around = xoay chuyển tình huống; turn over = lật qua, hoặc doanh thu)*
39. **Error:** "keep out with" **Correction:** "keep up with" *(keep up with = theo kịp)*
