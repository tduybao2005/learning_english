### Exercise 2: Complete with the Correct Phrasal Verb

Chọn phrasal verb phù hợp từ danh sách để hoàn thành câu. Chia đúng thì động từ.

**Phrasal verbs:** *break through / carry out / account for / give in / hold out / speak out against / draw on / phase in / look back on / catch up on*

1. Tourism _____________ approximately 15% of the country's GDP.
2. After years of research, scientists finally _____________ and developed a working prototype.
3. The new safety regulations will be _____________ over the next two years to minimise disruption.
4. Workers refused to _____________ to management's unreasonable demands.
5. The author _____________ decades of personal experience to write this insightful book.
6. Environmental groups _____________ the government's decision to approve the pipeline.
7. The investigation was _____________ by an independent team of experts.
8. After the holidays, students needed a week to _____________ missed coursework.
9. When we _____________ this period, we may recognise it as a turning point in history.
10. Protesters _____________ for weeks before authorities agreed to negotiate.

*(Answers: 1-accounts for, 2-broke through, 3-phased in, 4-give in, 5-drew on, 6-spoke out against, 7-carried out, 8-catch up on, 9-look back on, 10-held out)*

### Exercise 3: Phrasal Verb to Formal Transformation (IELTS Writing Practice)

Viết lại câu bằng từ học thuật trang trọng hơn để phù hợp với IELTS Writing Task 2.

1. "The new government came up with several economic reforms."
   → "The new government _____________ several economic reforms."

2. "Many industries are slowly phasing out coal-based production."
   → "Many industries are gradually _____________ coal-based production."

3. "The accident set off a chain of events that led to widespread reform."
   → "The accident _____________ a chain of events that led to widespread reform."

4. "Scientists have sorted out the main causes of antibiotic resistance."
   → "Scientists have _____________ the main causes of antibiotic resistance."

5. "The report points out that urban areas face greater challenges."
   → "The report _____________ that urban areas face greater challenges."

6. "Automation has brought about major changes in manufacturing."
   → "Automation has _____________ major changes in manufacturing."

*(Sample Answers: 1-devised/formulated, 2-eliminating/phasing out is acceptable, 3-triggered/sparked/initiated, 4-identified/determined/resolved, 5-highlights/indicates/demonstrates, 6-caused/generated/produced)*
