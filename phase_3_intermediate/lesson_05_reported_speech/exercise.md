# EXERCISE — Bài 5: Câu Tường Thuật

## SECTION A: Change direct speech to reported speech (15 items)

1. "I am studying for my exam," she said.
   → She said that _______________

2. "We will visit you next week," they told me.
   → They told me that _______________

3. "I have never been to Japan," he said.
   → He said that _______________

4. "She can speak three languages," the teacher told us.
   → The teacher told us that _______________

5. "I was working when you called," he explained.
   → He explained that _______________

6. "Where do you live?" she asked me.
   → She asked me where _______________

7. "Have you finished the report?" my boss asked.
   → My boss asked me whether/if _______________

8. "Don't touch the machine!" the engineer warned us.
   → The engineer warned us _______________

9. "Please submit your assignment by Friday," the professor said.
   → The professor asked us _______________

10. "I didn't steal the money," the suspect said.
    → The suspect denied _______________

11. "You should see a doctor," she advised me.
    → She advised me _______________

12. "Let's go to the cinema tonight," he suggested.
    → He suggested _______________

13. "I'm sorry I was late," she apologised.
    → She apologised for _______________

14. "I'll give you the report by Monday," he promised.
    → He promised _______________

15. "The Earth orbits the Sun," the scientist said.
    → The scientist said that _______________

## SECTION B: Change reported speech back to direct speech (6 items)

   → She said: "_______________"

   → He asked: "_______________"

   → They said: "_______________"

   → She said: "_______________"

5. He claimed that he had never seen that man before.
   → He said: "_______________"

6. The manager announced that the company would be hiring new staff.
   → The manager announced: "_______________"

## SECTION C: Choose the correct reporting verb (8 items)

1. "I didn't break the window!" Tom said.
   → Tom _______ breaking the window.
   A) admitted  B) denied  C) claimed  D) suggested

2. "You should eat more vegetables," the doctor said.
   → The doctor _______ eating more vegetables.
   A) warned  B) denied  C) advised  D) promised

3. "I will definitely finish it on time," she said.
   → She _______ to finish it on time.
   A) offered  B) refused  C) promised  D) warned

4. "Okay, I was wrong," he said.
   → He _______ that he had been wrong.
   A) denied  B) admitted  C) promised  D) claimed

5. "Why don't we try a different approach?" she said.
   → She _______ trying a different approach.
   A) warned  B) refused  C) suggested  D) admitted

6. "I'll help you carry those boxes," he said.
   → He _______ to help carry the boxes.
   A) refused  B) offered  C) warned  D) claimed

7. "Don't go near that dog," she said.
   → She _______ us not to go near the dog.
   A) advised/warned  B) suggested  C) offered  D) admitted

8. "I won't do your homework for you," she said.
   → She _______ to do the homework.
   A) offered  B) refused  C) claimed  D) suggested

## SECTION F: Writing task
Write a paragraph (100-120 words) reporting a conversation you had with a friend recently. Use at least 5 different reporting verbs. Use correct backshift and pronoun changes.
