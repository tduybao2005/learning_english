# BÀI 5: CÂU TƯỜNG THUẬT (REPORTED SPEECH)

---

## MỤC TIÊU BÀI HỌC

Sau bài này, bạn sẽ:
- Chuyển câu trực tiếp (direct speech) sang câu gián tiếp (reported speech) với đầy đủ backshift
- Biết cách đổi đại từ, thời gian, nơi chốn trong câu tường thuật
- Tường thuật câu hỏi (Wh-questions & Yes/No questions) đúng cú pháp
- Tường thuật mệnh lệnh, lời đề nghị, lời khuyên với động từ tường thuật phong phú
- Biết khi nào KHÔNG cần backshift (general truths, immediate reporting)
- Dùng hơn 20 reporting verbs khác nhau cho IELTS Writing & Speaking

---

## PHẦN 1: CÂU TƯỜNG THUẬT CÂU PHÁT BIỂU (STATEMENTS)

### 1.1 Cấu trúc cơ bản

```
Direct:   He said, "I am tired."
Reported: He said (that) he was tired.

Direct:   She told me, "I have finished the report."
Reported: She told me (that) she had finished the report.
```

**Phân biệt SAY vs TELL:**
- **say** (nói chung): He **said** that... / She **said** that... *(KHÔNG có tân ngữ người)*
- **tell** (nói với ai đó): He **told me** that... / She **told the class** that... *(CÓ tân ngữ người)*
- ✗ He told that he was tired. *(WRONG — tell phải có tân ngữ người)*
- ✗ She said me that she was busy. *(WRONG — say không có tân ngữ người)*

---

### 1.2 Bảng Backshift (Lùi thì) — ĐẦY ĐỦ

| Direct Speech | Reported Speech | Ví dụ |
|---------------|-----------------|-------|
| **Simple Present** | Simple Past | "I *work* here." → She said she *worked* there. |
| **Present Continuous** | Past Continuous | "I *am studying*." → He said he *was studying*. |
| **Simple Past** | Past Perfect | "She *left* early." → He said she *had left* early. |
| **Past Continuous** | Past Perfect Continuous | "We *were driving*." → She said they *had been driving*. |
| **Present Perfect** | Past Perfect | "I *have seen* it." → She said she *had seen* it. |
| **Present Perfect Continuous** | Past Perfect Continuous | "He *has been working*." → She said he *had been working*. |
| **Past Perfect** | Past Perfect *(không đổi)* | "She *had finished*." → He said she *had finished*. |
| **will** | would | "I *will* help." → She said she *would* help. |
| **can** | could | "I *can* do it." → He said he *could* do it. |
| **may** | might | "It *may* rain." → She said it *might* rain. |
| **must** | had to | "You *must* leave." → He said I *had to* leave. |
| **shall** | should / would | "We *shall* return." → They said they *would* return. |
| **might** | might *(không đổi)* | "She *might* come." → He said she *might* come. |
| **could** | could *(không đổi)* | "I *could* swim." → She said she *could* swim. |
| **should** | should *(không đổi)* | "You *should* rest." → He said I *should* rest. |
| **would** | would *(không đổi)* | "I *would* try." → She said she *would* try. |

## PHẦN 2: ĐỔI ĐẠI TỪ VÀ CÁC TỪ CHỈ THỜI GIAN / NƠI CHỐN

### 2.1 Thay đổi đại từ

| Direct Speech | Reported Speech | Chú ý |
|---------------|-----------------|-------|
| I | he / she | Theo chủ ngữ câu gốc |
| me | him / her | |
| my | his / her | |
| mine | his / hers | |
| we | they | |
| us | them | |
| our | their | |
| ours | theirs | |
| you | he / she / they / I | Phụ thuộc vào ngữ cảnh — người nói đang nói với ai? |
| your | his / her / their / my | |

**Ví dụ với "you":**
> "I will call you tomorrow," he told me.
→ He told me he would call **me** the following day.

> "Can you help us?" they asked the teacher.
→ They asked the teacher if **she** could help **them**.

---

### 2.2 Thay đổi trạng từ thời gian và nơi chốn

| Direct Speech | Reported Speech |
|---------------|-----------------|
| **now** | then / at that time |
| **today** | that day |
| **tonight** | that night |
| **yesterday** | the day before / the previous day |
| **tomorrow** | the next day / the following day |
| **last week** | the previous week / the week before |
| **next week** | the following week / the week after |
| **this week** | that week |
| **this year** | that year |
| **ago** | before / previously |
| **here** | there |
| **this** | that |
| **these** | those |
| **come** | go |

**Ví dụ:**
> "I came **here** **yesterday** to pick up **this** file," he said.
→ He said he had gone **there** **the day before** to pick up **that** file.

> "I will finish the report **tomorrow**," she told me.
→ She told me she would finish the report **the following day**.

## PHẦN 3: TƯỜNG THUẬT CÂU HỎI (REPORTED QUESTIONS)

### 3.1 Câu hỏi WH (Who / What / Where / When / Why / How)

**Quy tắc:** Reporting verb + **WH-word + S + V** (KHÔNG đảo ngữ — NO inversion!)

> Direct:   "**Where** does she live?" he asked.
> Reported: He asked **where she lived**. ✓
> ✗ He asked where did she live. *(WRONG — no inversion)*

> Direct:   "**Why** have you been crying?" she asked me.
> Reported: She asked me **why I had been crying**. ✓

> Direct:   "**How long** have you been learning English?" the teacher asked.
> Reported: The teacher asked **how long I had been learning English**. ✓

> Direct:   "**What** are you doing?" he asked her.
> Reported: He asked her **what she was doing**. ✓

> Direct:   "**Who** told you that?" she asked.
> Reported: She asked **who had told me that**. ✓

---

### 3.2 Câu hỏi Yes/No

**Quy tắc:** Reporting verb + **if / whether + S + V**

> Direct:   "Are you coming to the party?" she asked him.
> Reported: She asked him **if/whether he was coming** to the party. ✓

> Direct:   "Do you speak Japanese?" the interviewer asked.
> Reported: The interviewer asked **if/whether I spoke Japanese**. ✓

> Direct:   "Have you seen my keys?" he asked her.
> Reported: He asked her **if/whether she had seen his keys**. ✓

**Phân biệt if vs whether:**
- Cả hai đều được dùng sau ask, want to know, wonder
- **whether** thường trang trọng hơn và thường dùng với "or not": She asked **whether or not** he intended to stay. / He asked **whether** I was coming or going home.
- **if** thường phổ biến hơn trong văn nói.

## PHẦN 4: TƯỜNG THUẬT MỆNH LỆNH VÀ LỜI ĐỀ NGHỊ

### 4.1 Commands, Instructions, Requests

**Cấu trúc:** Reporting verb + object + **to + infinitive** / **not to + infinitive**

> "Open the window," he told her.
→ He told her **to open** the window.

> "Please don't make so much noise," she asked them.
→ She asked them **not to make** so much noise.

> "You must leave immediately," the officer ordered the crowd.
→ The officer ordered the crowd **to leave** immediately.

> "Don't touch anything in this room," the detective warned us.
→ The detective warned us **not to touch** anything in that room.

> "Would you mind lending me your pen?" she asked me.
→ She asked me **to lend** her my pen.

---

### 4.2 Reporting verbs cho Commands/Requests/Advice

| Verb | Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|------|----------|-------|
| **tell** | tell + obj + to do | She **told** me to be quiet. |
| **ask** | ask + obj + to do | He **asked** her to wait outside. |
| **order** | order + obj + to do | The judge **ordered** him to pay a fine. |
| **command** | command + obj + to do | The general **commanded** the troops to advance. |
| **warn** | warn + obj + not to do | She **warned** us not to walk alone at night. |
| **advise** | advise + obj + to do | The doctor **advised** him to rest for a week. |
| **remind** | remind + obj + to do | She **reminded** me to bring my passport. |
| **encourage** | encourage + obj + to do | He **encouraged** her to apply for the job. |
| **invite** | invite + obj + to do | They **invited** us to attend the ceremony. |
| **beg** | beg + obj + to do | She **begged** him not to leave. |
| **urge** | urge + obj + to do | Doctors **urge** people to exercise regularly. |
| **forbid** | forbid + obj + to do | They **forbade** employees to use their phones. |

## PHẦN 5: REPORTING VERBS PHONG PHÚ (KHÔNG CHỈ "SAID")

### 5.1 Verbs + that + clause

| Verb | Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|------|----------|-------|-------|
| **claim** | claim that | tuyên bố, khẳng định (có thể không tin) | She **claimed** that she had never seen the document. |
| **argue** | argue that | lập luận rằng | The lawyer **argued** that his client was innocent. |
| **explain** | explain that | giải thích | He **explained** that the delay was due to bad weather. |
| **admit** | admit that | thừa nhận | She **admitted** that she had made a serious error. |
| **deny** | deny that | phủ nhận | He **denied** that he had been involved in the fraud. |
| **insist** | insist that | khăng khăng cho rằng | She **insisted** that she had paid the full amount. |
| **confirm** | confirm that | xác nhận | The doctor **confirmed** that the test results were negative. |
| **announce** | announce that | thông báo | The president **announced** that elections would be held in March. |
| **promise** | promise that | hứa | He **promised** that he would return the money. |
| **warn** | warn that | cảnh báo (fact) | Scientists **warned** that sea levels were rising rapidly. |
| **suggest** | suggest that (+should) | đề xuất | She **suggested** that we should meet again next week. |
| **complain** | complain that | phàn nàn | He **complained** that the service was appallingly slow. |
| **doubt** | doubt that | nghi ngờ | I **doubted** that he would actually keep his word. |
| **reveal** | reveal that | tiết lộ | The investigation **revealed** that funds had been misused. |

---

### 5.2 Verbs + to infinitive (no object needed)

| Verb | Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|------|----------|-------|
| **offer** | offer to do | đề nghị giúp | She **offered** to drive him to the airport. |
| **refuse** | refuse to do | từ chối | He **refused** to answer any further questions. |
| **promise** | promise to do | hứa sẽ | She **promised** to call as soon as she arrived. |
| **threaten** | threaten to do | đe dọa sẽ | He **threatened** to take legal action. |
| **agree** | agree to do | đồng ý sẽ | She **agreed** to postpone the meeting until Thursday. |
| **decide** | decide to do | quyết định | They **decided** to relocate the headquarters. |
| **manage** | manage to do | xoay xở | He **managed** to finish the report despite the difficulties. |

---

### 5.3 Suggest — cấu trúc đặc biệt

**suggest** KHÔNG theo sau bằng to-infinitive:
- suggest + **-ing**: "Why don't we go for a walk?" → She **suggested going** for a walk.
- suggest + **(that) + S + (should) + bare infinitive**: "Let's leave early." → He **suggested (that) they (should) leave** early.
- ✗ She suggested me to go. *(WRONG)*
- ✗ She suggested to go. *(WRONG in this context)*

## PHẦN 6: KHI NÀO KHÔNG CẦN BACKSHIFT

### 6.1 Sự thật phổ quát (General truths)
> "The Earth orbits the Sun," the teacher explained.
→ The teacher explained that the Earth **orbits** the Sun. *(không backshift — sự thật khoa học)*

### 6.2 Tường thuật ngay lập tức (Immediate reporting)
Khi bạn tường thuật lại điều vừa xảy ra (same conversation or shortly after):
> "I am feeling much better today," she says / she said (she is feeling much better today). *(optional — both acceptable)*

### 6.3 Vẫn còn đúng ở thời điểm tường thuật
> "I live in Ho Chi Minh City," she told me last week.
→ She told me she **lives** / **lived** in Ho Chi Minh City. *(both acceptable if still true)*

### 6.4 Past perfect cannot be backshifted further
> "She had already left when I arrived," he said.
→ He said she **had already left** when he arrived. *(past perfect stays as past perfect)*

---

## PHẦN 7: DOUBLE REPORTING VÀ CẤU TRÚC PHỨC TẠP

### Double reporting
> She said she **had told** him that she **wanted** to leave.
> He claimed that the witness **had stated** that the defendant **had not been** present.

### Reporting with passive
> "The building was designed by a famous architect," she explained.
→ She explained that the building **had been designed** by a famous architect.

### Reporting with modal would/could/might (stay the same)
> "I might come if I have time," she said.
→ She said she **might come** if she **had** time.

## PHẦN 8: CÂU TƯỜNG THUẬT TRONG IELTS

### Sử dụng trong Task 1 (Academic — describing a graph or process)
> "The report shows that..." → The report shows that... *(present simple reporting of written data — no backshift)*

### Sử dụng trong Task 2 (Essay)
> Some people argue that technology has made our lives easier, while others claim that it has created more problems than solutions.

### Sử dụng trong Speaking Part 3 (Discussion)
> "Research suggests that..." / "Experts have argued that..." / "Studies have shown that..."
> A recent survey **revealed that** over 60% of young people **preferred** online learning to traditional classroom instruction.

## BẢNG TỔNG KẾT NHANH

| Loại câu trực tiếp | Cấu trúc tường thuật | Ví dụ |
|---------------------|----------------------|-------|
| Statement | said/told + (that) + S + V(lùi) | "I am busy." → She said she **was** busy. |
| Wh-question | asked + WH + S + V (no inversion) | "Where do you live?" → He asked where I **lived**. |
| Yes/No question | asked + if/whether + S + V | "Are you ready?" → She asked if I **was** ready. |
| Command | told/ordered/asked + obj + to do | "Stop." → He told me **to stop**. |
| Negative command | warned/told + obj + not to do | "Don't go." → She told me **not to go**. |
| Offer | offered + to do | "I'll help." → She **offered to help**. |
| Refusal | refused + to do | "I won't sign." → He **refused to sign**. |
| Suggestion | suggested + -ing / that + S + should | "Let's eat." → He **suggested eating** / that we **should eat**. |
