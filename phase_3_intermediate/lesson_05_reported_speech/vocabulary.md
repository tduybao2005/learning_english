## BẢNG TỪ VỰNG — REPORTING VERBS VÀ CẤU TRÚC (70 mục)

| Verb / Expression | Cấu trúc sau động từ | Nghĩa & Sắc thái | Ví dụ tường thuật |
|-------------------|----------------------|------------------|-------------------|
| **say** | say (that) | nói chung chung | He **said** that he would be late for the meeting. |
| **tell** | tell + obj + (that) | nói với ai đó | She **told** me that the project had been approved. |
| **claim** | claim (that) | tuyên bố (người nghe có thể nghi ngờ) | He **claimed** that he had never received the email. |
| **argue** | argue (that) | lập luận, biện luận | The lawyer **argued** that the evidence was inadmissible. |
| **explain** | explain (that) | giải thích, làm rõ | She **explained** that the delay was caused by a technical fault. |
| **admit** | admit (that) | thừa nhận (điều tiêu cực/sai lầm) | He **admitted** that he had been responsible for the error. |
| **deny** | deny (that) / deny + -ing | phủ nhận (điều bị buộc tội) | She **denied** taking any money from the cash register. |
| **insist** | insist (that) | khăng khăng, quả quyết | He **insisted** that he had been working at the time of the crime. |
| **confirm** | confirm (that) | xác nhận chính thức | The spokesperson **confirmed** that the company would be restructured. |
| **announce** | announce (that) | thông báo công khai | The prime minister **announced** that a general election would be held. |
| **declare** | declare (that) | tuyên bố trang trọng | The president **declared** that a state of emergency had been imposed. |
| **suggest** | suggest + -ing / suggest (that) + should | đề xuất, gợi ý | She **suggested** taking a different approach to the problem. |
| **propose** | propose + -ing / propose (that) | đề xuất (formal) | He **proposed** that the committee should meet more frequently. |
| **recommend** | recommend + -ing / recommend (that) | khuyến nghị | The report **recommended** increasing funding for public health. |
| **promise** | promise (that) / promise to do | hứa hẹn | She **promised** that she would return the borrowed documents. |
| **refuse** | refuse to do | từ chối làm | He **refused** to answer any further questions from the press. |
| **offer** | offer to do | đề nghị giúp đỡ (tự nguyện) | She **offered** to stay late and finish the report herself. |
| **agree** | agree to do / agree (that) | đồng ý, nhất trí | He **agreed** to postpone the launch until the following month. |
| **disagree** | disagree (that) / disagree with | không đồng ý | She **disagreed** that the policy would have the intended effect. |
| **warn** | warn + obj + that / warn + obj + not to do | cảnh báo | He **warned** the investors that the market was extremely volatile. |
| **threaten** | threaten to do / threaten (that) | đe dọa | The union **threatened** to call a strike if demands were not met. |
| **urge** | urge + obj + to do | thúc giục, kêu gọi mạnh mẽ | Health officials **urged** the public to get vaccinated immediately. |
| **advise** | advise + obj + to do / advise (that) | khuyên bảo | The consultant **advised** the company to reduce its operational costs. |
| **encourage** | encourage + obj + to do | khuyến khích | Her mentor **encouraged** her to submit the article for publication. |
| **persuade** | persuade + obj + to do | thuyết phục | He eventually **persuaded** her to reconsider her resignation. |
| **beg** | beg + obj + to do | van xin, cầu xin | She **begged** him not to leave her behind. |
| **demand** | demand (that) / demand to do | yêu cầu (mạnh mẽ, quyết đoán) | The protesters **demanded** that the government resign immediately. |
| **request** | request (that) / request + obj + to do | yêu cầu (lịch sự, formal) | The chairperson **requested** that all members submit reports in advance. |
| **order** | order + obj + to do | ra lệnh | The commander **ordered** the troops to retreat across the border. |
| **command** | command + obj + to do | ra lệnh (quân sự/trang trọng) | The officer **commanded** the soldiers to hold their positions. |
| **remind** | remind + obj + to do / remind + obj + that | nhắc nhở | She **reminded** him to bring all the necessary documents. |
| **instruct** | instruct + obj + to do | hướng dẫn, chỉ thị | The manager **instructed** the team to complete the audit by Friday. |
| **forbid** | forbid + obj + to do | cấm | The regulations **forbid** employees to disclose confidential information. |
| **invite** | invite + obj + to do | mời | They **invited** the researchers to present their findings at the symposium. |
| **complain** | complain (that) / complain about | phàn nàn | He **complained** that the noise from the construction was unbearable. |
| **boast** | boast (that) / boast about | khoe khoang | She **boasted** that her team had broken the sales record for three years running. |
| **concede** | concede (that) | nhượng bộ, thừa nhận (miễn cưỡng) | He **conceded** that the opposition had raised some valid points. |
| **confess** | confess (that) / confess to + -ing | thú nhận (tội lỗi) | She **confessed** that she had been hiding the truth for months. |
| **doubt** | doubt (that) | nghi ngờ, hoài nghi | Many analysts **doubted** that the reforms would succeed in the long term. |
| **reveal** | reveal (that) | tiết lộ (bí mật, thông tin) | The investigation **revealed** that funds had been systematically misappropriated. |
| **report** | report (that) | tường thuật, báo cáo | Witnesses **reported** that a large explosion had been heard at midnight. |
| **note** | note (that) | ghi nhận, lưu ý | The researcher **noted** that sample sizes had been limited by funding constraints. |
| **observe** | observe (that) | nhận xét, quan sát | The inspector **observed** that safety standards had been consistently neglected. |
| **point out** | point out (that) | chỉ ra, nhấn mạnh | She **pointed out** that the original estimate had been unrealistically low. |
| **emphasise** | emphasise (that) | nhấn mạnh | The CEO **emphasised** that transparency would be central to the new strategy. |
| **stress** | stress (that) | nhấn mạnh mạnh hơn | The minister **stressed** that the deadline was absolutely non-negotiable. |
| **maintain** | maintain (that) | duy trì lập luận/quan điểm | Despite the evidence, he **maintained** that he had acted in good faith. |
| **assert** | assert (that) | khẳng định mạnh mẽ | The scientist **asserted** that the results were reproducible under any conditions. |
| **acknowledge** | acknowledge (that) | thừa nhận (thực tế/sự thật) | The company **acknowledged** that the product had a significant design flaw. |
| **allege** | allege (that) | cáo buộc (chưa được chứng minh) | The former employee **alleged** that safety regulations had been routinely ignored. |
| **speculate** | speculate (that) | suy đoán | Journalists **speculated** that the minister would announce his resignation soon. |
| **assume** | assume (that) | cho rằng, giả định | He **assumed** that the meeting had been cancelled since no one had called. |
| **predict** | predict (that) | dự đoán | Economists **predicted** that inflation would fall below 3% by the end of the year. |
| **estimate** | estimate (that) | ước tính | Experts **estimated** that the damage would cost over $2 billion to repair. |
| **calculate** | calculate (that) | tính toán và kết luận | Scientists **calculated** that the asteroid would pass within 50,000 km of Earth. |
| **conclude** | conclude (that) | kết luận | The committee **concluded** that the project had been fundamentally misconceived. |
| **determine** | determine (that) | xác định, kết luận (qua nghiên cứu) | Investigators **determined** that the fire had been started deliberately. |
| **find** | find (that) | phát hiện, nhận thấy (research) | The study **found** that regular exercise significantly reduced the risk of depression. |
| **show** | show (that) | cho thấy, chứng minh | The data **showed** that productivity had increased by 15% over the quarter. |
| **demonstrate** | demonstrate (that) | chứng minh rõ ràng | The experiment **demonstrated** that the compound was highly effective. |
| **indicate** | indicate (that) | cho thấy, chỉ ra | Survey results **indicated** that satisfaction levels had declined sharply. |
| **suggest** *(academic)* | suggest (that) | gợi ý (kết quả/data) | The findings **suggested** that there might be a genetic component to the condition. |
| **imply** | imply (that) | ngụ ý (không nói thẳng) | Her silence **implied** that she had known about the problem all along. |
| **reassure** | reassure + obj + that | trấn an | The doctor **reassured** the family that the operation had been a complete success. |
| **guarantee** | guarantee (that) / guarantee to do | đảm bảo, cam kết | The contractor **guaranteed** that the work would be finished on schedule. |
| **pledge** | pledge to do / pledge (that) | cam kết long trọng | The government **pledged** to reduce carbon emissions by 50% by 2030. |
| **vow** | vow to do / vow (that) | thề, lập thề | She **vowed** that she would never make the same mistake again. |
| **testify** | testify (that) | làm chứng | The witness **testified** that she had seen the defendant leave the building at 11 pm. |
| **object** | object (that) / object to + -ing | phản đối | Several board members **objected** that the decision had been made without proper consultation. |
| **protest** | protest (that) | phản đối (công khai) | The accused **protested** that he was completely innocent. |
| **lament** | lament (that) | than thở, đau lòng vì | The professor **lamented** that fewer students were choosing to study the humanities. |
| **wonder** | wonder if/whether / wonder what/how | tự hỏi | She **wondered** whether the new policy would actually improve working conditions. |

### Exercise 1: Match each reporting verb (1–15) to its meaning or connotation (A–O)

*Ghép động từ tường thuật với nghĩa/sắc thái phù hợp.*

**Verbs:**
1. allege  2. concede  3. lament  4. threaten  5. boast
6. deny  7. urge  8. reassure  9. vow  10. speculate
11. insist  12. confess  13. imply  14. pledge  15. testify

**Meanings:**
A. To make a solemn, public commitment (formal/political)
B. To suggest something indirectly without stating it outright
C. To make a guess or conjecture without certainty
D. To admit guilt or wrongdoing (often reluctantly)
E. To strongly encourage or push someone to take action
F. To make a personal, emotional oath or promise
G. To reluctantly admit that someone else has a valid point
H. To brag or speak proudly about one's own achievements
I. To state under oath in a court of law
J. To comfort someone by removing their fears
K. To express sadness or regret about a situation
L. To make a claim without proof (the claim may be false)
M. To say that something did not happen / one is not responsible
N. To warn of harmful consequences if demands are not met
O. To state repeatedly and forcefully that something is true

**→ Answers:** 1-L | 2-G | 3-K | 4-N | 5-H | 6-M | 7-E | 8-J | 9-F | 10-C | 11-O | 12-D | 13-B | 14-A | 15-I

### Exercise 2: Choose the most appropriate reporting verb based on the context (10 items)

*Chọn động từ tường thuật phù hợp nhất với ngữ cảnh. (Có thể có nhiều hơn một đáp án đúng — chọn đáp án phù hợp nhất về sắc thái.)*

**Word bank:** admitted / threatened / suggested / warned / urged / announced / refused / confirmed / complained / denied

1. "You absolutely must get vaccinated before winter arrives," the health official told the public.
→ The health official _____________ the public to get vaccinated before winter.

2. "I will take you to court if you do not pay back the money," he said.
→ He _____________ to take them to court if the money was not repaid.

3. "The noise from the road works is completely unbearable," she said.
→ She _____________ that the noise from the road works was completely unbearable.

4. "Why don't we hold the meeting online instead of in person?" he said.
→ He _____________ holding the meeting online instead of in person.

5. "Yes, I did make a mistake in the financial report," she said.
→ She _____________ that she had made a mistake in the financial report.

6. "I will not sign this agreement under any circumstances," the union leader said.
→ The union leader _____________ to sign the agreement under any circumstances.

7. "The company will be moving its headquarters to Singapore next year," the CEO said at the press conference.
→ The CEO _____________ that the company would be moving its headquarters the following year.

8. "I was not present at the meeting when those decisions were made," he said.
→ He _____________ being present at the meeting when those decisions were made.

9. "I can assure you that all your personal data is completely safe," the bank manager told the customers.
→ The bank manager _____________ customers that their personal data was completely safe.

10. "The laboratory tests have shown that the new drug is both safe and effective," the researcher said.
→ The researcher _____________ that the laboratory tests had shown the drug to be both safe and effective.

**→ Answers:** 1. urged | 2. threatened | 3. complained | 4. suggested | 5. admitted | 6. refused | 7. announced | 8. denied | 9. confirmed/reassured | 10. confirmed/announced

### Exercise 3: Complete the reported speech — fill in the correct verb form (10 items)

*Điền dạng đúng của động từ trong ngoặc. Lùi thì nếu cần thiết.*

1. "I am currently working on a new project," she said.
→ She said that she _____________ (currently / work) on a new project.

2. "We have already submitted the application," they told the officer.
→ They told the officer that they _____________ (already / submit) the application.

3. "The results will be published next month," the researcher announced.
→ The researcher announced that the results _____________ (publish) the following month.

4. "I haven't seen him since last Tuesday," she said.
→ She said that she _____________ (not / see) him since the previous Tuesday.

5. "Can you explain why the budget was exceeded?" the director asked her team.
→ The director asked her team if they _____________ (explain) why the budget _____________ (exceed).

6. "The building was constructed in 1895," the tour guide explained.
→ The tour guide explained that the building _____________ (construct) in 1895.

7. "We were negotiating when the news broke," the executive said.
→ The executive said that they _____________ (negotiate) when the news _____________ (break).

8. "Don't leave your valuables unattended," the announcement said.
→ The announcement warned passengers not _____________ (leave) their valuables unattended.

9. "I may need more time to review the documents," he said.
→ He said that he _____________ (may / need) more time to review the documents.

10. "Why has the meeting been postponed?" she asked.
→ She asked why the meeting _____________ (postpone).

**→ Answers:**
1. was currently working
2. had already submitted
3. would be published
4. hadn't seen
5. could explain / had been exceeded
6. had been constructed
7. had been negotiating / had broken
8. to leave
9. might need
10. had been postponed
