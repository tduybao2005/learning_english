## BÀI TẬP TỪ VỰNG

### Exercise 1: Match the conditional sentence to what kind of situation or regret it expresses (10 items)

*Ghép mỗi câu điều kiện (1–10) với loại tình huống/hối tiếc phù hợp (A–J).*

**Sentences:**
1. If she had studied harder, she would have passed the university entrance exam.
2. If he had been taller, he might have become a professional basketball player.
3. If I had left the house ten minutes earlier, I would have caught the last train.
4. If they hadn't built the dam, the entire valley would have flooded every year.
5. If the government had acted sooner, thousands of jobs could have been saved.
6. If the fire alarm hadn't sounded in time, many people would have lost their lives.
7. If she hadn't quit her stable job, she would never have started her own business.
8. If the scientists had discovered the vaccine earlier, millions of people might have survived.
9. If I had invested in that company in 2010, I would be very wealthy now. *(mixed)*
10. If he hadn't moved to the big city at 18, he would still be working on the farm.

**Types (A–J):**
A. A regret about a missed transport connection
B. An action that, in hindsight, led to unexpected success
C. A regret about academic underperformance
D. A physical characteristic that limited a career option
E. A policy decision that could have protected workers
F. An action that prevented a natural disaster
G. A scientific regret about a delayed discovery
H. A missed financial opportunity with a present impact
I. An emergency event that saved multiple lives
J. A life-path decision with a permanent impact on present circumstances

**→ Answers:** 1-C | 2-D | 3-A | 4-F | 5-E | 6-I | 7-B | 8-G | 9-H | 10-J

### Exercise 2: Complete the wish / if only sentences (10 items)

*Điền dạng đúng của động từ trong ngoặc vào câu.*

1. I wish I _____________ (study) harder when I was at university.
2. She wishes she _____________ (not / say) those hurtful words to her friend.
3. If only he _____________ (take) the doctor's advice before the situation got so serious.
4. I wish I _____________ (know) about this opportunity six months ago.
5. She wishes she _____________ (choose) a different career when she had the chance.
6. If only they _____________ (arrive) before the storm hit the coastline.
7. He wishes he _____________ (not / quit) his job so hastily without having a backup plan.
8. I wish I _____________ (be) more careful with my finances in my twenties.
9. If only the rescue team _____________ (have) better equipment when they started.
10. They wish they _____________ (invest) in that property ten years ago instead of spending the money.

**→ Answers:**
1. had studied
2. hadn't said
3. had taken
4. had known
5. had chosen
6. had arrived
7. hadn't quit
8. had been
9. had had
10. had invested

### Exercise 3: Transform each Type 3 conditional into its inverted form (8 items)

*Viết lại câu bằng cách đảo ngữ (Had he... / Had she not... / Had they...). Không thay đổi nghĩa câu.*

**Example:**
If he had known the answer, he would have spoken up.
→ **Had he known the answer, he would have spoken up.**

1. If she had not made that critical mistake, everything would have turned out fine.
→ _______________________________________________

2. If they had arrived on time, they could have joined the meeting from the beginning.
→ _______________________________________________

3. If the government had responded more quickly, the flood damage could have been prevented.
→ _______________________________________________

4. If we had been given more resources and support, we would have achieved far better results.
→ _______________________________________________

5. If she had not been so stubborn, the two sides might have reached a compromise.
→ _______________________________________________

6. If the early warning system had been in place, thousands of lives would have been saved.
→ _______________________________________________

7. If the scientists had discovered the cure even five years earlier, millions of people might have survived.
→ _______________________________________________

8. If the financial records had not been falsified, the fraud would have been detected much sooner.
→ _______________________________________________

**→ Answers:**

4. Had we been given more resources and support, we would have achieved far better results.

6. Had the early warning system been in place, thousands of lives would have been saved.
7. Had the scientists discovered the cure even five years earlier, millions of people might have survived.
8. Had the financial records not been falsified, the fraud would have been detected much sooner.
