## SECTION A: COMPLETE THE TYPE 3 CONDITIONAL (15 câu)

*Điền dạng đúng của động từ trong ngoặc để tạo thành câu điều kiện Loại 3.*

**A1.** If the team __________________ (prepare) more thoroughly, they __________________ (not / lose) the championship final.

**A2.** She __________________ (become) a renowned artist if she __________________ (not / give up) painting at such a young age.

**A3.** If the government __________________ (take) action sooner, thousands of jobs __________________ (be) saved in the manufacturing sector.

**A4.** He __________________ (pass) his driving test on the first attempt if he __________________ (practice) more consistently.

**A5.** If they __________________ (install) better flood defenses along the river, the damage to the town __________________ (be) far less severe.

**A6.** I __________________ (call) for help much earlier if I __________________ (know) how serious the situation actually was.

**A7.** If the scientist __________________ (not / make) that crucial breakthrough, modern medical treatment __________________ (never / advance) so rapidly.

**A8.** If the warning signs __________________ (not / ignore) by the engineers, the bridge collapse __________________ (easily / prevent).

**A9.** He __________________ (not / be) stuck in that terrible traffic if he __________________ (leave) the office just ten minutes earlier.

**A10.** If the two countries __________________ (reach) a peace agreement in 1939, the Second World War __________________ (might / never / start).

**A11.** I __________________ (invest) my savings differently if I __________________ (anticipate) the severity of the economic crisis.

**A12.** If she __________________ (attend) that industry conference, she __________________ (meet) the person who later became her business partner.

**A13.** The patient __________________ (have) a much better chance of survival if the ambulance __________________ (arrive) sooner.

**A14.** If the ancient dam __________________ (not / construct) in the 1960s, the entire valley __________________ (flood) repeatedly over the following decades.

**A15.** If the soldiers __________________ (not / fight) so bravely against overwhelming odds, the battle __________________ (certainly / be) lost.

## PHẦN H: SPEAKING / DISCUSSION PROMPT

*Trả lời miệng hoặc viết câu trả lời, dùng cấu trúc/từ vựng của bài.*

1. If you could go back in time and change one historical event, what would it be and why would this change benefit humanity?
2. Reflecting on your own past, if you had made one different decision, how might your life be different today?
3. Some historians argue that major historical events are inevitable regardless of individual actions. Do you agree or disagree?
4. Is dwelling on past regrets a harmful habit, or can it be a useful tool for self-improvement? Give reasons and examples.

**Model answer (Band 7–8):** If I could change one historical event, I would choose the early stages of industrialisation in the nineteenth century. Had governments at the time understood the long-term consequences of burning fossil fuels, they would surely have invested in cleaner alternatives from the very beginning. If that had happened, we would not be facing the climate emergency that now threatens our planet today. Of course, if the Industrial Revolution had not occurred at all, many modern advances in medicine, transport, and communication would not exist either. It remains a deeply complex question with no simple answer.

**E2.** Error: *will have collapsed* → *would have collapsed*
→ Had they not acted immediately, the building **would have collapsed** within minutes.

**E3.** Error: *had* (simple past) → *had had* (past perfect — for wish about past)
→ I wish I **had had** more time yesterday to properly finish the report before the deadline.

→ If he **had not made** that critical mistake, the entire project would have been a success.

## PHẦN H — GỢI Ý

*Câu trả lời tốt nên bao gồm:*
- Câu điều kiện Loại 3 (if + had + V3, would have + V3) để nói về quá khứ không có thật
- Câu điều kiện hỗn hợp (if + had + V3, would/might + V) để liên kết quá khứ với hiện tại
- Cấu trúc wish/if only + had + V3 để diễn đạt sự tiếc nuối về quá khứ
