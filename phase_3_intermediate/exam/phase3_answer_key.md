# ĐÁP ÁN — KIỂM TRA CUỐI GIAI ĐOẠN 3

## PHẦN A — NGỮ PHÁP

**Câu 1-5 (Passive Voice):**
1. New environmental policies are being introduced (by the government).
2. Three new hospitals have been built in the past year.
3. The accident must be reported immediately.
4. The experiment was being conducted when the power cut occurred.
5. The results will be announced next week.

**Câu 6-10 (Conditionals):**
6. B (had known — Conditional 3: if + past perfect)
7. B (rains — Conditional 1: if + present simple)
8. C (had studied — Conditional 3)
9. A (reaches — Zero conditional: scientific fact)
10. B (had left — Mixed conditional: past → present result)

**Câu 11-15 (Relative Clauses):**
11. who
12. where
13. which/that
14. whose
15. whose

**Câu 16-20 (Reported Speech):**
16. he was working on the project
17. I had finished / whether I had finished the project
18. not to touch the experiment
19. (that) they would submit the report the following week
20. she hadn't seen that film yet

## PHẦN B — TỪ VỰNG

**Câu 1-5 (Gerund/Infinitive):**
1. locking (remember + gerund = past memory)
2. smoking (stop + gerund = quit permanently)
3. to inform (regret + infinitive = formal bad news)
4. adding (try + gerund = experiment to see if it works)
5. to bring (forget + infinitive = future obligation)

**Câu 6-10 (Collocations):**
6. conduct / do (both acceptable)
7. made
8. take
9. made / gave (both acceptable)
10. made / struck (both acceptable)

**Câu 11-15 (AWL):**
11. analysis
12. consequences
13. established
14. consistent
15. indicates / evidence indicates
