## BÀI TẬP TỪ VỰNG

### Bài tập 1: Viết so sánh kép cho từng tình huống

Viết câu double comparative dựa trên thông tin:

**Ví dụ:** Technology advances quickly → People rely on it more.
→ *The more quickly technology advances, the more people rely on it.*

1. The economy grows → More jobs are available.
→ _____________________________________

2. People eat unhealthily → Their health deteriorates.
→ _____________________________________

3. A country invests in education → Its workforce is more skilled.
→ _____________________________________

4. Traffic increases → Air quality worsens.
→ _____________________________________

5. You sleep less → You become less productive.
→ _____________________________________

6. Companies cut costs → Their product quality decreases.
→ _____________________________________

7. A city expands rapidly → Infrastructure struggles to keep up.
→ _____________________________________

8. Young people have fewer social skills → They struggle more in the workplace.
→ _____________________________________

9. Deforestation increases → Biodiversity decreases.
→ _____________________________________

10. Governments invest more in renewable energy → Carbon emissions fall.
→ _____________________________________

**Đáp án:**
1. *The more the economy grows, the more jobs are available.*
2. *The more unhealthily people eat, the more their health deteriorates.*
3. *The more a country invests in education, the more skilled its workforce becomes.*
4. *The more traffic increases, the worse air quality becomes.*
5. *The less you sleep, the less productive you become.*
6. *The more companies cut costs, the lower their product quality becomes.*
7. *The more rapidly a city expands, the harder it is for infrastructure to keep up.*
8. *The fewer social skills young people have, the more they struggle in the workplace.*
9. *The more deforestation increases, the greater the loss of biodiversity.*
10. *The more governments invest in renewable energy, the faster carbon emissions fall.*

### Bài tập 2: Thêm intensifier phù hợp

Chọn intensifier đúng dựa trên ngữ cảnh (much / slightly / considerably / far / marginally / dramatically):

1. Country A's GDP was $45,000 per capita; Country B's was $44,800.
→ Country A's GDP was _______ higher than Country B's.

2. In 2010, 20% used social media. By 2023, 85% used it.
→ Social media usage has _______ increased over the past decade.

3. The average temperature last year was 22.1°C versus the 20-year average of 22.0°C.
→ Last year's average temperature was _______ above the long-term average.

4. Electric vehicles cost $40,000 on average; petrol cars cost $25,000.
→ Electric vehicles are currently _______ more expensive than petrol cars.

5. Men's salaries averaged $75,000; women's averaged $62,000.
→ Men earned _______ more than women in this sector.

6. The new drug reduced symptoms in 92% of patients; the old drug achieved only 40%.
→ The new drug proved _______ more effective than its predecessor.

7. School A's test scores were 78%; School B's were 77.5%.
→ School A performed _______ better than School B.

8. The population grew from 5 million to 50 million over 60 years.
→ The population has grown _______ over the past six decades.

**Đáp án:**
1. marginally 2. dramatically 3. marginally / fractionally 4. considerably / far / substantially 5. significantly / considerably 6. dramatically / far / substantially 7. marginally / slightly 8. dramatically / enormously

### Bài tập 3: Diễn đạt lại bằng cấu trúc so sánh khác

Diễn đạt lại mỗi câu bằng cấu trúc được gợi ý trong ngoặc:

1. *Solar energy is cheaper now than gas.* [not as...as]
→ ___________________________________

2. *Country A has 45% internet users; Country B has 32%.* [considerably more than]
→ ___________________________________

3. *She earns twice as much as he does.* [half as much]
→ ___________________________________

4. *I prefer living in cities to living in rural areas.* [would rather]
→ ___________________________________

5. *Online education is effective. Traditional education is equally effective.* [just as...as]
→ ___________________________________

6. *The more it rains, the less we can do outdoor activities.* [rephrase using consequently]
→ ___________________________________

7. *The gap between rich and poor is larger than ever.* [wider...than]
→ ___________________________________

8. *The 2020 figure was 67%; the 2010 figure was 32%.* [more than twice as high]
→ ___________________________________

**Đáp án:**
1. *Gas is not as cheap as solar energy now.*
2. *Country A has considerably more internet users than Country B (45% vs 32%).*
3. *He earns half as much as she does.*
4. *I would rather live in cities than in rural areas.*
5. *Online education is just as effective as traditional classroom instruction.*
6. *Heavy rainfall consequently limits outdoor activities.*
7. *The gap between rich and poor is wider than ever before.*
8. *The 2020 figure was more than twice as high as that recorded in 2010.*
