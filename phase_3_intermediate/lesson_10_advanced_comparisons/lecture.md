# BÀI 10: SO SÁNH NÂNG CAO (ADVANCED COMPARISONS)

---

## MỤC TIÊU BÀI HỌC

Sau bài này, bạn sẽ:
- Ôn lại và củng cố so sánh hơn và so sánh nhất cơ bản
- Nắm vững cấu trúc so sánh kép (double comparative)
- Sử dụng intensifier để diễn đạt mức độ chênh lệch
- Phân biệt và sử dụng đúng as...as, less...than, fewer...than
- Hiểu prefer/would rather/rather than
- Ứng dụng ngôn ngữ so sánh trong IELTS Task 1 (mô tả biểu đồ/bảng số liệu)

---

## PHẦN 1: ÔN LẠI SO SÁNH CƠ BẢN

### 1.1 So sánh hơn (Comparative)

**Tính từ/trạng từ ngắn (1-2 âm tiết):** thêm **-er**
- tall → taller | fast → faster | simple → simpler
- hot → hotter | big → bigger | happy → happier

**Tính từ/trạng từ dài (≥3 âm tiết):** dùng **more/less**
- important → more important | efficiently → more efficiently
- complex → more complex | carefully → more carefully

**Bất quy tắc:**
| Từ gốc | So sánh hơn | So sánh nhất |
|--------|------------|-------------|
| good/well | better | best |
| bad/badly | worse | worst |
| much/many | more | most |
| little | less | least |
| far | farther/further | farthest/furthest |
| old | older/elder | oldest/eldest |

### 1.2 So sánh nhất (Superlative)

- **Ngắn:** thêm **-est** → the tallest, the fastest
- **Dài:** dùng **the most/least** → the most significant, the least effective

> *Tokyo is **the largest** city in the world by population.*
> *This is **the most effective** method available.*
> *She is **the least experienced** member of the team.*

## PHẦN 2: DOUBLE COMPARATIVE — SO SÁNH KÉP

### 2.1 Cấu trúc và ý nghĩa

**Công thức:**
> **The + comparative + S + V, the + comparative + S + V**

Ý nghĩa: diễn đạt mối quan hệ nhân-quả hoặc tương quan — khi A tăng/giảm thì B cũng tăng/giảm tương ứng.

### 2.2 Ví dụ chi tiết

**Với tính từ:**
> *The **warmer** the climate becomes, the **higher** sea levels will rise.*
> *The **older** you get, the **wiser** you become.*
> *The **greater** the investment, the **stronger** the economy.*
> *The **worse** the pollution, the **higher** the rates of respiratory disease.*

**Với trạng từ:**
> *The **more carefully** you read, the **better** you understand.*
> *The **harder** students work, the **more** they achieve.*
> *The **faster** cities expand, the **more** pressure infrastructure faces.*

**Với much/little/many/few:**
> *The **more** you practice, the **fewer** mistakes you make.*
> *The **less** time you spend on social media, the **more** productive you become.*
> *The **more** data is collected, the **more accurate** the analysis will be.*

**Trong bài IELTS:**
> *The **more** countries cooperate on climate change, the **more effective** their collective response will be.*
> *The **greater** the income inequality, the **higher** the crime rate tends to be.*
> *The **more** quickly renewable energy is adopted, the **sooner** carbon targets will be met.*

### 2.3 Lưu ý ngữ pháp

- Sau "the + comparative", chủ ngữ và động từ phải đảo vị trí trong một số cấu trúc trang trọng:
  > *The more rapidly **the economy grows**, the greater the demand for energy.*
- Có thể rút gọn khi chủ ngữ giống nhau:
  > *The more, the merrier.* (Đông vui hơn.)
  > *The sooner, the better.* (Càng sớm càng tốt.)

## PHẦN 3: INTENSIFIERS — DIỄN ĐẠT MỨC ĐỘ CHÊNH LỆCH

### 3.1 Intensifiers cho chênh lệch LỚN

Dùng để nhấn mạnh sự khác biệt đáng kể:

| Intensifier | Mức độ | Ví dụ |
|-------------|--------|-------|
| **much** | lớn (thông dụng) | *much more expensive than* |
| **far** | lớn (trang trọng) | *far less efficient than* |
| **considerably** | đáng kể | *considerably higher than* |
| **significantly** | có ý nghĩa thống kê | *significantly lower than* |
| **substantially** | đáng kể (số lượng) | *substantially greater than* |
| **dramatically** | ngoạn mục, đột ngột | *dramatically fewer than* |
| **vastly** | rất lớn | *vastly superior to* |
| **enormously** | to lớn | *enormously more complex* |
| **overwhelmingly** | áp đảo | *overwhelmingly more popular* |

**Ví dụ trong bài:**
> *Renewable energy is now **considerably** cheaper than it was a decade ago.*
> *Women in this country earn **significantly** less than their male counterparts.*
> *Urban areas are **far** more densely populated than rural regions.*
> *The treatment proved **dramatically** more effective than the placebo.*

### 3.2 Intensifiers cho chênh lệch NHỎ

| Intensifier | Mức độ | Ví dụ |
|-------------|--------|-------|
| **slightly** | nhỏ (thông dụng) | *slightly warmer than* |
| **marginally** | rất nhỏ (trang trọng) | *marginally higher than* |
| **somewhat** | phần nào, vừa phải | *somewhat more complex* |
| **a little** | ít (không trang trọng) | *a little faster than* |
| **a bit** | ít (không trang trọng) | *a bit more difficult* |
| **fractionally** | chênh lệch rất nhỏ | *fractionally above* |
| **barely** | vừa vừa đủ | *barely higher than* |

**Ví dụ:**
> *Country A's GDP growth was **marginally** higher than Country B's.*
> *The new model is **slightly** more fuel-efficient.*
> *Temperatures last year were **fractionally** above the 20-year average.*

## PHẦN 4: AS...AS — CÁC CẤU TRÚC QUAN TRỌNG

### 4.1 Bằng nhau: as + adj/adv + as

> *Solar power is now **as cheap as** coal in many regions.*
> *This proposal is **as effective as** the previous one.*

### 4.2 Không bằng nhau: not as + adj/adv + as

> *Developing countries are **not as** technologically advanced **as** developed ones.*
> *The results were **not as** promising **as** expected.*

### 4.3 Chênh lệch lớn: not nearly as / not half as

Dùng khi muốn nhấn mạnh sự khác biệt lớn:
> *Nuclear energy is **not nearly as** dangerous **as** many people believe.*
> *The second approach was **not half as** effective **as** the first.*

### 4.4 Hoàn toàn bằng nhau: just as / every bit as

> *Online learning can be **just as** effective **as** traditional classroom instruction.*
> *She is **every bit as** qualified **as** her male colleagues.*

### 4.5 Bội số (Multipliers): twice / three times as...as

> *China produces **twice as many** electric vehicles **as** the rest of the world combined.*
> *The population of Asia is **more than four times as large as** that of Europe.*
> *Urban dwellers consume **three times as much** energy **as** rural residents.*

**Lưu ý:**
- Multiplier + as many/much (đếm được/không đếm được): *twice as many books, three times as much money*
- Multiplier + as + adj + as: *twice as expensive, three times as fast*

### 4.6 As + adj + as can be (hoàn hảo tuyệt đối)

> *The instructions are **as clear as can be** — follow them exactly.*
> *The design is **as simple as possible**.*

## PHẦN 5: LESS...THAN / FEWER...THAN

### 5.1 Quy tắc cơ bản

- **fewer** + danh từ **đếm được số nhiều**: *fewer students, fewer countries, fewer opportunities*
- **less** + danh từ **không đếm được**: *less pollution, less time, less money, less traffic*
- **less** + tính từ: *less expensive, less complex, less reliable*

### 5.2 Ví dụ đúng/sai

> ✅ *There are **fewer** cars on the road today.*
> ❌ *There is **less** cars on the road today.* (sai — cars đếm được)

> ✅ *There is **less** traffic today.*
> ✅ *We have **less** time than expected.*

> ✅ *Renewable energy produces **far less** CO2 than fossil fuels.*
> ✅ *This method requires **significantly fewer** resources.*

## PHẦN 6: PREFER / WOULD RATHER / RATHER THAN

### 6.1 Prefer

**Công thức:**
- prefer + noun/gerund + **to** + noun/gerund (so sánh chung chung)
  > *I prefer **walking to driving**.*
  > *Most people prefer **cities** to **rural areas** for their amenities.*

- prefer + to infinitive + **rather than** + bare infinitive (chọn trong tình huống cụ thể)
  > *I prefer **to walk** rather than **drive**.*
  > *Governments prefer **to invest in** education rather than **cut** social spending.*

### 6.2 Would rather

**Công thức:** would rather + bare infinitive
> *I **would rather live** in the countryside.*

**So sánh:** would rather + bare infinitive + **than** + bare infinitive
> *Many young people **would rather work** from home **than** commute daily.*
> *Politicians **would rather** make popular decisions **than** tackle difficult reforms.*

**Lưu ý:** would rather không dùng để nói về người khác khi muốn họ làm gì đó (khác cấu trúc tiếng Việt!):
- Bạn muốn người khác làm gì → *I would rather **you** + past tense*
  > *I would rather **you didn't** use your phone during the lecture.*

### 6.3 Rather than

Dùng độc lập như giới từ/liên từ:
> *Governments should invest in prevention **rather than** treatment.*
> ***Rather than** blaming individuals, we should address systemic issues.*

## PHẦN 7: LIKE VS AS

### 7.1 Like (= giống như) — trước danh từ/đại từ

> *He speaks English **like** a native speaker.*
> *The city looks **like** a war zone after the floods.*
> *Diseases **like** malaria are preventable.*

### 7.2 As (= với tư cách là / theo cách như) — trước mệnh đề

> *Do **as** you are told.*
> *The economy did not recover **as** analysts had predicted.*
> ***As** I mentioned earlier, the evidence is inconclusive.*

### 7.3 Lỗi phổ biến

> ❌ *Do it **like** I told you.* (sai — sau "like" là mệnh đề)
> ✅ *Do it **as** I told you.*

> ❌ *She works **as** a machine.* (sai — như cái máy → so sánh)
> ✅ *She works **like** a machine.*
> ✅ *She works **as** an engineer.* (với tư cách là kỹ sư — đúng)

## PHẦN 8: NGÔN NGỮ SO SÁNH TRONG IELTS TASK 1

### 8.1 Biểu đồ — Ngôn ngữ tăng/giảm có so sánh

> *The figure **rose significantly compared to** the previous year.*
> *Sales in 2020 were **considerably higher than** those recorded in 2010.*
> *The proportion of female workers was **approximately twice as large as** that of males.*

### 8.2 Bảng số liệu — So sánh giữa các đối tượng

> *Country A **accounted for a substantially larger proportion than** Country B.*
> ***In contrast to** the declining trend in Europe, Asia showed consistent growth.*
> ***Whereas** online shopping increased dramatically, high street sales fell.*

### 8.3 Chuỗi so sánh hữu ích

| Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|----------|-------|
| compared to/with | *Sales rose 20% **compared to** 2019.* |
| in comparison with | *In comparison with rural areas, cities...* |
| relative to | *Spending on education, **relative to** GDP, declined.* |
| in contrast to | ***In contrast to** the 2010 data, 2020 shows...* |
| whereas / while | ***Whereas** X fell, Y rose steadily.* |
| at ... times the rate of | *Country A grew at **twice the rate of** Country B.* |
| by a margin of | *A exceeded B **by a margin of** 15%.* |
| by far the + superlative | ***By far the most** popular choice was...* |

## TÓM TẮT BÀI HỌC

| Cấu trúc | Công thức | Ví dụ |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Double comparative | The + comp + S + V, the + comp + S + V | The more you study, the better. |
| Intensifier + comparative | much/far/considerably/significantly + comp | far more expensive |
| Small difference | slightly/marginally/somewhat + comp | slightly higher |
| Multiplier | twice/three times as...as | twice as many |
| Not nearly as | not nearly as + adj + as | not nearly as effective |
| Every bit as | every bit as + adj + as | every bit as important |
| Less vs Fewer | fewer (đếm được) / less (không đếm được) | fewer cars / less traffic |
| Prefer...to | prefer + gerund + to + gerund | prefer walking to driving |
| Rather than | rather than + bare infinitive | invest rather than cut |
