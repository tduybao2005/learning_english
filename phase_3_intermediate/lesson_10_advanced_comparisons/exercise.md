## SECTION A: COMPLETE WITH CORRECT COMPARISON STRUCTURE (15 điểm — mỗi câu 1 điểm)

Điền vào chỗ trống cấu trúc so sánh đúng. Dùng từ trong ngoặc.

1. Electric vehicles are _______ environmentally friendly _______ traditional petrol cars. (considerably / more)

2. _______ the population grows, _______ the demand for clean water becomes. (The more / the greater) — Viết lại câu hoàn chỉnh: _____

3. This city is _______ as densely populated _______ Tokyo. (not nearly / as)

4. Country A uses _______ renewable energy _______ Country B by a significant margin. (far / more)

5. Online education is _______ as effective _______ traditional learning, according to recent studies. (just / as)

6. The new factory produces _______ units per day _______ the old one did. (twice as many / as)

7. The temperature last month was _______ higher _______ the long-term seasonal average. (marginally / than)

8. I would _______ study abroad _______ stay in my home country, given the opportunity. (rather / than)

9. The _______ the investment in infrastructure, the _______ the economic development. (greater / faster)

10. Women now _______ outnumber men _______ universities in many developed nations. (significantly / at)

11. Coal power is _______ less efficient _______ modern nuclear plants. (considerably / than)

12. She is _______ as qualified _______ any other candidate for the position. (every bit / as)

13. _______ countries cooperate on climate policy, _______ the chance of success. (The more / the greater)

14. Urban residents consume _______ times as much energy _______ rural residents on average. (three / as)

15. The second proposal is _______ better _______ the first; they are almost identical. (only marginally / than)

1. "There are _______ people living below the poverty line in urban areas than in rural areas."

   A. so    B. as    C. nearly    D. almost

4. "Most governments would _______ increase spending on healthcare _______ on defense."
   A. prefer / to    B. rather / than    C. like / more    D. choose / less

5. "She performed _______ than any other student in the cohort."
   A. far better    B. much more good    C. a lot gooder    D. far more well

6. "The earthquake caused _______ less damage than initially feared."
   A. significantly    B. very    C. too    D. highly

7. "Country A's internet penetration rate (67%) is _______ Country B's (32%)."
   A. more than twice as high as    B. twice more high than    C. double high than    D. two times higher to

8. "He did it _______ he had been instructed — carefully and methodically."
   A. like    B. such as    C. as    D. similar to

### Section D:
1. Lỗi: "badly" (adverb) → đúng: "bad" (adjective): *The situation is not as **bad** as we initially thought.*
2. Lỗi: "less" → đúng: "fewer" (opportunities đếm được): *There are **fewer** opportunities for graduates today.*
3. Lỗi: "more you become confident" → đúng: "the more confident you become": *The more you practice, the **more confident** you become.*
4. Lỗi: "rather than to work" → đúng: "rather than working" hoặc "rather than work": *She prefers working in a team rather than **working** alone.* (hoặc: *...rather than **work** alone.*)
5. Lỗi: "as large than" → đúng: "as large as": *Country A's GDP is three times as large **as** Country B's.*
6. Câu này đúng về mặt ngữ pháp — "like a native speaker" (so sánh danh từ) là chính xác.

### Section E (đáp án gợi ý):
1. *Larger cities generally have **significantly higher** levels of air pollution **than** rural areas.*
2. *City A had **twice as many** internet users **as** City B, according to 2020 data.*
3. *The government **prefers** investing in education **rather than** cutting social welfare.*
4. ***The more** countries transition to renewable energy, **the cheaper** that technology becomes.*
5. *The birth rate in developed countries is **not as high as** that in developing nations.*
6. *This new approach has proven **considerably more effective than** the traditional method.*

### Section F (đáp án mẫu):
> *Overall, internet usage rates increased across all three countries between 2010 and 2020. Country C consistently recorded the highest figures, rising from 67% to a substantial 91% by 2020. **By contrast**, Country B had the lowest penetration rate in both years, reaching only 54% in 2020 — **considerably lower than** Country C. Notably, Country A's 2020 figure (78%) was **more than twice as high as** Country B's 2010 rate (32%), illustrating dramatic improvement. **Whereas** Country C was already the most connected in 2010, all three countries showed strong upward trends, with Country A recording the largest absolute increase of 33 percentage points.*
