# EXERCISE — Bài 12: Academic Word List (AWL) Giới Thiệu

## SECTION A: AWL word recognition — Underline the AWL words (10 sentences)
Each sentence contains 2-3 AWL words. Identify and write them out.

1. The researchers analyzed the data and identified several significant patterns.
2. A theoretical framework was established to assess the impact of policy changes.
3. The evidence indicates that individual factors contribute to different outcomes.
4. We need to define the concept clearly before we can interpret the results.
5. The approach they used involved conducting research in various contexts.
6. The structure of the report requires a section on methodology and assumptions.
7. Significant factors such as the environment and community resources must be considered.
8. The process involves identifying sources and evaluating their reliability.
9. Researchers concluded that the benefits of the policy outweighed the consequences.
10. The theory assumes that economic factors determine behaviour in consistent ways.

## SECTION D: Academic collocation matching — Match AWL word with its typical partner (10 items)

Match the AWL word (left) with its most common academic collocate (right):

1. conduct ___       A. a theory
2. draw ___          B. research
3. provide ___       C. a conclusion
4. support ___       D. evidence
5. test ___          E. a framework
6. establish ___     F. an approach
