# VOCABULARY — Bài 12: Academic Word List (AWL) — Giới Thiệu

## Giới thiệu AWL
AWL (Academic Word List) do Averil Coxhead tổng hợp gồm 570 họ từ xuất hiện nhiều nhất trong văn bản học thuật tiếng Anh. Học AWL giúp bạn hiểu ~10% từ trong bất kỳ văn bản học thuật nào.

## Exercise 1: Fill in the AWL word (10 items)
Use words from the box: [analyze / evidence / significant / impact / approach / outcome / identify / assume / context / define]

1. You must _______ the term before using it in an academic essay.
2. The _______ of climate change on biodiversity is well-documented.
3. Without understanding the historical _______, the text is hard to interpret.
4. The research team will _______ the collected data next month.
5. There is growing _______ that sleep deprivation affects academic performance.
6. Never _______ that your reader already knows the background — always explain.
7. The _______ of the treatment trial was a 40% improvement in patients.
8. Scientists have been able to _______ three genetic markers linked to the disease.
9. There has been a _______ increase in the use of renewable energy since 2015.
10. The traditional _______ to teaching has been replaced by more student-centred methods.

**Answers:** 1.define 2.impact 3.context 4.analyze 5.evidence 6.assume 7.outcome 8.identify 9.significant 10.approach

## Exercise 3: Choose AWL word to replace underlined informal/vague word (8 items)

1. *We need to **look at** the data more carefully.*
   → We need to _______ the data more carefully.

2. *There are **big** differences between the two groups.*
   → There are _______ differences between the two groups.

3. *Several **things** led to this outcome.*
   → Several _______ led to this outcome.

4. *The **way** they studied this was flawed.*
   → The _______ they used was flawed.

5. *We can **guess** from the data that unemployment will rise.*
   → We can _______ from the data that unemployment will rise.

6. *The study **happened** during a period of economic instability.*
   → The study _______ during a period of economic instability.

7. *This has a big **effect** on student performance.*
   → This has a significant _______ on student performance.

8. *The **end result** of the program was very positive.*
   → The _______ of the program was very positive.

**Answers:** 1.analyze 2.significant 3.factors 4.method/approach 5.assume/infer 6.occurred 7.impact 8.outcome
