# TỪ VỰNG BÀI 11: TỪ NỐI VÀ LIÊN KẾT VĂN BẢN (DISCOURSE MARKERS)

---

## BẢNG ĐẦY ĐỦ: 80 DISCOURSE MARKERS

### Hướng dẫn đọc bảng:
- **Vị trí:** Start = đầu câu | Mid = giữa câu | End = cuối câu
- **Dấu câu:** C = cần dấu phẩy theo sau | 2C = hai dấu phẩy (khi ở giữa câu)
- **Register:** F = Formal (trang trọng) | N = Neutral | I = Informal

## BÀI TẬP TỪ VỰNG

### Bài tập 1: Chọn discourse marker đúng cho chỗ trống (15 câu)

Điền discourse marker phù hợp. Chỉ có một đáp án đúng nhất.

1. Air pollution kills millions annually. _______, it disproportionately affects low-income communities.
   (Furthermore / In conclusion / Conversely)

2. The research yielded important findings. _______, the sample size was too small to draw definitive conclusions.
   (That said / Therefore / As a result)

3. The government has invested heavily in healthcare. _______, life expectancy has increased.
   (Conversely / Consequently / Although)

4. Developed nations, _______ the United States and Germany, consume the most energy per capita.
   (such as / for example / namely)

5. The report identified several weaknesses, _______, inadequate funding and poor coordination.
   (namely / for instance / that said)

6. Urban areas experience higher pollution. _______, rural areas benefit from cleaner air.
   (In contrast / Moreover / Therefore)

7. _______, the technology has some drawbacks, it represents a significant breakthrough.
   (Admittedly / However / As a result)

8. The new system reduces waste. _______, it cuts operating costs by 30%.
   (Furthermore / Nevertheless / Conversely)

9. Many countries have failed to meet emissions targets. _______, global temperatures continue to rise.
   (As a result / In contrast / Specifically)

10. The plan is excellent in theory. _______, its implementation faces serious obstacles.
    (Nevertheless / In other words / Conversely) — Wait, use this marker: (In practice, however) — think again: use **However**

11. Both solar and wind energy have advantages. _______ produces no noise; _______ generates electricity at night.
    (The former / The latter)

12. _______, economic development should not come at the expense of environmental protection.
    (Above all / In contrast / Consequently)

13. Many students fail to meet deadlines. _______, time management training should be mandatory.
    (Therefore / Nevertheless / In contrast)

14. The study found no significant difference. _______, the two approaches are equally effective.
    (In other words / Furthermore / In contrast)

15. _______ the benefits are undeniable, the risks must also be carefully considered.
    (While / However / Therefore)

**Đáp án:**
1. Furthermore 2. That said 3. Consequently 4. such as 5. namely 6. In contrast 7. Admittedly 8. Furthermore 9. As a result 10. However 11. The former / The latter 12. Above all 13. Therefore 14. In other words 15. While

### Bài tập 3: Viết lại đoạn văn dùng markers tinh tế hơn

Đoạn văn dưới sử dụng các từ nối cơ bản (but / also / because / so). Thay thế chúng bằng discourse markers học thuật hơn:

**Đoạn gốc:**
> *Social media is popular because it allows people to connect easily. But it has many dangers. It spreads false information very quickly. Also, it can cause addiction and depression, especially in young people. So, governments need to regulate it better.*

Viết lại:
_______________________________________________
_______________________________________________
_______________________________________________

**Đáp án mẫu:**
> *Social media has grown in popularity due to the ease with which it facilitates human connection. **However**, it carries significant risks. **Most notably**, it enables the rapid spread of misinformation. **Furthermore**, excessive use can lead to addiction and depression, **in particular** among younger users. **Consequently**, more robust governmental regulation of these platforms is urgently needed.*
