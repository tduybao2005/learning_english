## SECTION A: FILL IN WITH MOST APPROPRIATE DISCOURSE MARKER (15 điểm — mỗi câu 1 điểm)

1. Technological innovation has transformed the global economy. _______, it has disrupted traditional industries and displaced millions of workers.

2. The new environmental policy is effective in reducing carbon emissions. _______, its economic costs remain a source of debate.

3. Many cities have successfully implemented cycling infrastructure, _______ Amsterdam and Copenhagen.

4. The research team conducted three separate experiments. _______, all three yielded consistent results.

5. Income inequality has widened significantly over the past decade. _______, the wealthiest 1% now owns more than half of global wealth.

8. The company failed to adapt to market changes. _______, it was forced to declare bankruptcy.

9. _______, economic growth should not be pursued at the cost of environmental sustainability.

10. The study identified three main causes of poverty, _______, lack of education, poor governance, and inadequate healthcare.

11. _______ the initiative has faced significant opposition, it has produced measurable improvements in air quality.

12. The evidence clearly supports this conclusion. _______, further replication of these findings would strengthen it further.

13. Urban areas have better employment opportunities. _______, rural regions offer a lower cost of living and a stronger sense of community.

14. Solar energy production costs have fallen by 90% over the past decade. _______, it is now the cheapest source of electricity in history.

## SECTION B: CHOOSE BETWEEN TWO SIMILAR MARKERS (10 điểm — mỗi câu 1 điểm)

Chọn marker đúng hơn trong ngữ cảnh này. Giải thích ngắn gọn sự khác biệt.

1. The policy was introduced in 2020. _______, the unemployment rate began to fall.
   A. **Subsequently** (sau đó về mặt thời gian)   B. Consequently (kết quả)
   → Đây là quan hệ thời gian hay nguyên nhân-kết quả? Chọn: ___

2. The team worked tirelessly for months. _______, they failed to meet the deadline.
   A. **Nevertheless** (mặc dù vậy)   B. Therefore (do đó)
   → Chọn: ___

3. Air pollution is a major problem in large cities. _______, it causes thousands of premature deaths annually.
   A. **Furthermore** (thêm ý bổ sung)   B. However (đối lập)
   → Chọn: ___

4. The report highlights several issues, _______, poor management and insufficient funding.
   A. **namely** (liệt kê cụ thể)   B. for example (ví dụ tổng quát hơn)
   → Nếu liệt kê đầy đủ tất cả: ___

5. The government invested heavily in renewable energy. _______, private sector innovation slowed considerably.
   A. **Conversely** (ngược lại)   B. Consequently (kết quả)
   → Chọn: ___

6. The data shows a clear upward trend. _______, we must be cautious about overstating its significance.
   A. **However** (đối lập)   B. Moreover (bổ sung)
   → Chọn: ___

7. Many species are endangered. _______, the Amur leopard has fewer than 100 individuals remaining.
   A. **For instance** (ví dụ)   B. Therefore (kết quả)
   → Chọn: ___

8. The programme was expensive to implement. The results, _______, exceeded expectations.
   A. **nonetheless** (mặc dù vậy — giữa câu)   B. therefore (do đó)
   → Chọn: ___

9. The medicine reduced symptoms in 70% of patients. _______, it was approved for widespread use.
   A. **Thus** (kết quả — học thuật)   B. Similarly (tương tự)
   → Chọn: ___

10. Both coal and gas are fossil fuels. _______, coal produces significantly more carbon dioxide than gas.
    A. **However** (đối lập trong cùng nhóm)   B. Furthermore (bổ sung)
    → Chọn: ___

## PHẦN G: LISTENING-STYLE DICTATION / GAP-FILL (10 câu)

*Đọc đoạn văn và điền discourse marker phù hợp vào mỗi chỗ trống (không cần audio).*

Technology has transformed the nature of modern communication in many profound and far-reaching ways. (G1), not all of these changes have been uniformly positive for society as a whole. Social media platforms, (G2), have made it considerably easier to share information and experiences with people across the entire globe. (G3), these platforms have enabled previously marginalised communities to raise their voices and demand meaningful change. (G4), there are increasingly serious concerns about the rapid spread of misinformation and extremist content through these same channels. (G5), studies have repeatedly shown that false information travels far faster online than accurate, verified reporting. (G6), public trust in traditional journalism has declined dramatically in many countries. (G7), a number of news organisations have successfully adapted by placing renewed emphasis on investigative journalism and rigorous fact-checking. (G8), those outlets that have resisted necessary change have witnessed dramatic and sustained falls in both readership and revenue. (G9), the media landscape today is more fragmented and polarised than at any previous point in modern history. (G10), while digital technology offers remarkable opportunities for global communication, it simultaneously demands far greater media literacy from all citizens.

## PHẦN H: SPEAKING / DISCUSSION PROMPT

*Trả lời miệng hoặc viết câu trả lời, dùng cấu trúc/từ vựng của bài.*

1. "Social media has done more harm than good to society." Discuss this statement using a range of discourse markers from at least three functional categories.
2. Describe the advantages and disadvantages of online learning. Use contrast and addition markers to structure your response clearly.
3. In your opinion, what is the single most serious problem facing your country today? Structure your argument using cause-effect and concession markers.
4. Compare traditional and modern approaches to news consumption. Use discourse markers to signal your comparisons and conclusions effectively.

**Model answer (Band 7–8):** Technology has undoubtedly transformed the way people access information. Furthermore, it has given a voice to communities that were previously excluded from mainstream public debate. However, the rapid spread of misinformation is a deeply troubling consequence of this openness. In fact, research consistently shows that false stories spread far more quickly online than accurate ones. Consequently, public trust in journalism has declined significantly in many countries. That said, some media organisations have responded admirably by investing in fact-checking and investigative reporting. In conclusion, while the opportunities created by digital communication are genuinely remarkable, they demand a far more critical and discerning readership than ever before.

## ANSWER KEY — ĐÁP ÁN

### Section A (đáp án ví dụ — chấp nhận markers cùng nhóm):
1. **However** / That said / Nevertheless (Contrast)
2. **That said** / However / Nonetheless (Contrast — acknowledge then move on)
3. **such as** / for example (Exemplification) — "such as" nếu trong câu, "for example" nếu đầu câu mới
4. **Indeed** / Notably / In fact (Emphasis)
5. **Specifically** / Indeed / In fact / To illustrate (Emphasis/Exemplification)
6. **Consequently** / As a result / Therefore (Cause-Effect)
7. **However** / Nevertheless / That said (Contrast)
8. **Consequently** / As a result / Therefore (Cause-Effect)
9. **Above all** / Most importantly (Emphasis)
10. **namely** (Exemplification — liệt kê đầy đủ)
11. **Admittedly** / Although / Despite the fact that (Concession)
12. **That said** / Nevertheless / However (Contrast/Concession)
13. **On the other hand** / In contrast / Conversely (Contrast)
14. **Indeed** / As a result / Consequently (Emphasis/Cause-Effect)
15. **However** / That said / Nevertheless (Contrast)

### Section B:
1. Consequently (quan hệ nhân-quả)
2. Nevertheless (mặc dù cố gắng, vẫn thất bại)
3. Furthermore (thêm thông tin về tác hại của ô nhiễm)
4. namely (liệt kê cụ thể, đầy đủ)
5. Conversely (ngược chiều với đầu tư chính phủ)
6. However (đưa ra lưu ý đối lập)
7. For instance (ví dụ cụ thể)
8. nonetheless (mặc dù đắt, vẫn vượt mong đợi — giữa câu: "The results, nonetheless, exceeded...")
9. Thus (kết quả dẫn đến quyết định — học thuật)
10. However (đối lập giữa hai loại nhiên liệu hóa thạch)

### Section C (đáp án gợi ý):
1. *Deforestation is destroying habitats. **Furthermore**, it is accelerating climate change through increased carbon emissions.*
2. *Vaccines are proven to be safe. **Nevertheless**, vaccine hesitancy among some parents remains a public health concern.*
3. *The economy grew rapidly. **Consequently**, living standards improved significantly.*
4. *Many students prefer studying in the morning. **However**, others are more productive late at night.*
5. *Technology has profoundly transformed human communication. **To illustrate**, digital messaging has largely replaced telephone calls in everyday interaction.*
6. *The overwhelming scientific evidence **confirms** that climate change is occurring.* (hoặc: *This conclusion is supported by overwhelming scientific evidence.*)
7. *While the policy appears sound in theory, **in practice**, its implementation poses considerable difficulties.*
8. *Education is vital. **Moreover**, mental health support is equally essential to student wellbeing.*

### Section D:
1. *Social media has advantages; **however**, it also poses significant risks.* (dấu chấm phẩy + however + dấu phẩy)
2. *The experiment was, **consequently**, repeated three times to verify the results.* (dấu phẩy hai bên)
3. *The situation was, **in fact**, far more serious than initially reported.* (dấu phẩy hai bên)
4. *Both solar and wind energy have been adopted widely. **The former** is most effective in sunny regions.* (câu mới)
5. ***In conclusion**, education investment remains the most powerful driver of long-term development.* (dấu phẩy sau)
6. *The plan was, **furthermore**, lacking in detail about implementation.* (dấu phẩy hai bên)

### Section E (đáp án gợi ý):
1. ***Nevertheless**, progress in reducing poverty has been documented in many developing nations.*
2. *Air pollution causes serious health problems. **Furthermore**, it affects children and the elderly most severely.*
3. *Many developed nations, **such as** Norway and Sweden, have achieved an effective balance between economic growth and environmental protection.*
4. ***As a result**, the cost of solar energy production has fallen by more than 90% over the past decade.*
5. ***While it cannot be denied that** social media offers benefits, its detrimental effects on young people's mental health cannot be overlooked.*
6. ***In other words**, rising income inequality represents the greatest threat to social stability.*

### Section F (đáp án mẫu — Band 7):
> *There is considerable evidence to suggest that technology is eroding the quality of human social interaction. **Admittedly** [Concession], platforms such as social media enable people to stay connected across vast distances. **However** [Contrast], research increasingly demonstrates that online interaction cannot replicate the depth of face-to-face communication. **For instance** [Exemplification], a 2022 study found that heavy social media users reported significantly higher levels of loneliness compared to those who used it moderately. **Furthermore** [Addition], the addictive design of these platforms actively discourages people from spending time in offline social settings. **Consequently** [Cause-Effect], while technology connects people at a surface level, it may simultaneously be undermining the meaningful relationships that contribute to long-term wellbeing.*

Điểm cho Section F:
- Sử dụng ≥5 discourse markers từ ≥3 nhóm khác nhau: 15 điểm
- Topic sentence và concluding sentence rõ ràng: 6 điểm
- Ví dụ/bằng chứng: 6 điểm
- Độ chính xác ngữ pháp: 8 điểm

## PHẦN H — GỢI Ý

*Câu trả lời tốt nên bao gồm:*
- Sử dụng markers từ ít nhất 3 nhóm chức năng khác nhau: Contrast (however, nevertheless), Addition (furthermore, moreover), Cause-Effect (consequently, as a result)
- Đặt dấu câu đúng: dấu chấm phẩy + however + dấu phẩy; hoặc câu mới bắt đầu bằng Furthermore,
- Tránh lặp lại cùng một marker — dùng đa dạng trong một đoạn văn
