# BÀI 4: MỆNH ĐỀ QUAN HỆ (RELATIVE CLAUSES)

---

## MỤC TIÊU BÀI HỌC

Sau bài này, bạn sẽ:
- Nắm vững tất cả các đại từ quan hệ và biết khi nào dùng mỗi loại
- Phân biệt và dùng đúng mệnh đề quan hệ XÁC ĐỊNH và KHÔNG XÁC ĐỊNH
- Rút gọn mệnh đề quan hệ thành cụm phân từ (participial phrase)
- Dùng giới từ trong mệnh đề quan hệ theo phong cách trang trọng và thông thường
- Dùng quantitative relatives (most of whom / some of which...)
- Tăng điểm IELTS Writing/Speaking với mệnh đề quan hệ phức tạp

---

## PHẦN 1: CÁC ĐẠI TỪ QUAN HỆ

| Đại từ | Dùng cho | Chức năng trong mệnh đề | Ví dụ |
|--------|----------|--------------------------|-------|
| **who** | người | chủ ngữ hoặc tân ngữ | The doctor **who** treated me was very kind. |
| **whom** | người | tân ngữ (trang trọng/formal) | The candidate **whom** they selected was brilliant. |
| **which** | vật, động vật | chủ ngữ hoặc tân ngữ | The book **which** I borrowed was fascinating. |
| **that** | người hoặc vật | chủ ngữ hoặc tân ngữ (chỉ trong defining) | The house **that** I want to buy is very expensive. |
| **whose** | người hoặc vật | sở hữu | The student **whose** essay won the prize was thrilled. |
| **where** | nơi chốn | trạng ngữ chỉ nơi chốn | The city **where** I grew up has changed enormously. |
| **when** | thời gian | trạng ngữ chỉ thời gian | The year **when** the war ended was 1975. |
| **why** | lý do | trạng ngữ (chỉ sau "reason") | The reason **why** she left has never been explained. |

---

### 1.1 WHO vs WHOM

**WHO** làm **chủ ngữ** của mệnh đề quan hệ (= người đó thực hiện hành động):
> The woman **who** called you is my aunt. *(who = she called)*
> The students **who** failed the exam must retake it. *(who = they failed)*
> I know someone **who** speaks seven languages. *(who = they speak)*

**WHOM** làm **tân ngữ** của mệnh đề quan hệ (= người đó nhận hành động — formal):
> The professor **whom** I admire most teaches linguistics. *(I admire him → whom = object)*
> The candidate **whom** they selected was outstanding. *(they selected her → whom = object)*
> The journalist **whom** everyone feared retired last year. *(everyone feared him → whom = object)*

**Mẹo nhớ:** Nếu thay thế được bằng "he/she" → dùng **who**. Nếu thay thế được bằng "him/her" → dùng **whom**.
> The man **who** helped me was kind. *(He helped me. → who ✓)*
> The man **whom** I helped was grateful. *(I helped him. → whom ✓)*

**Trong văn nói và văn viết informal**, "who" thường được dùng thay "whom":
> The person **who** I spoke to was very helpful. *(informal but acceptable)*

---

### 1.2 WHICH vs THAT

**WHICH:** dùng cho vật và động vật; dùng trong cả defining và non-defining clauses.
**THAT:** dùng cho người hoặc vật; CHỈ dùng trong defining clauses; KHÔNG dùng sau dấu phẩy.

> ✓ The report **which / that** I submitted was accepted. *(defining — both OK)*
> ✓ My laptop, **which** I bought last year, is already broken. *(non-defining — only which)*
> ✗ My laptop, **that** I bought last year, is already broken. *(non-defining — that WRONG)*

---

### 1.3 WHOSE (sở hữu)

Dùng cho cả người lẫn vật, thay thế cho: his / her / its / their:
> The boy **whose** bicycle was stolen went to the police. *(his bicycle)*
> The company **whose** products are the best will win the contract. *(its products)*
> She met a scientist **whose** research had changed the world. *(his/her research)*
> We visited a village **whose** residents had no access to clean water. *(their residents)*

---

### 1.4 WHERE, WHEN, WHY

**WHERE** (thay thế "there" / "in/at that place"):
> This is the school **where** I studied for six years. *(= at which I studied)*
> The café **where** we first met has now closed down.

**WHEN** (thay thế "then" / "at/during that time"):
> I remember the summer **when** we travelled through Europe together.
> There are moments **when** patience is the only option you have.

**WHY** (chỉ dùng sau "reason"):
> The reason **why** she resigned was never made public.
> No one understands the reason **why** he made that decision.
> *(Informal: the reason she resigned / the reason he made that decision — "why" can be omitted)*

## PHẦN 2: XÁC ĐỊNH vs KHÔNG XÁC ĐỊNH

### 2.1 Defining Relative Clauses — Mệnh đề quan hệ XÁC ĐỊNH

**Đặc điểm quan trọng:**
1. **Không có dấu phẩy**
2. Xác định chính xác danh từ đang nói đến — bỏ đi sẽ mất nghĩa chính
3. Có thể dùng **that** (thay cho who/which)
4. Có thể **bỏ relative pronoun** nếu nó là **tân ngữ** của mệnh đề quan hệ

**Ví dụ cơ bản:**
> The woman **who lives next door** is a nurse. *(Nếu bỏ → "The woman is a nurse" — không biết người nào)*
> The book **that/which she recommended** was excellent. *(Xác định cuốn sách cụ thể nào)*
> Students **who study consistently** tend to achieve better results. *(Chỉ những sinh viên học đều)*
> The city **where he was born** was destroyed during the war.

**Bỏ relative pronoun (contact clause):**
Khi relative pronoun làm **tân ngữ** → có thể bỏ (thường gặp trong văn nói và văn viết informal):
> The film **(that/which)** I watched last night was brilliant. *(I watched it → pronoun = object → có thể bỏ)*
> The company **(that/which)** she works for is relocating next year.
> The person **(who/whom)** I contacted never replied to my email.

Khi relative pronoun làm **chủ ngữ** → **KHÔNG thể bỏ:**
> ✗ The woman __ called you is my aunt. *(WRONG — "who" là subject)*
> ✓ The woman **who** called you is my aunt.

---

### 2.2 Non-Defining Relative Clauses — Mệnh đề quan hệ KHÔNG XÁC ĐỊNH

**Đặc điểm quan trọng:**
1. **Có dấu phẩy cả hai đầu** (hoặc dấu phẩy + dấu chấm nếu ở cuối câu)
2. Cung cấp thông tin **bổ sung** — câu gốc vẫn đủ nghĩa nếu bỏ phần này
3. **KHÔNG được dùng "that"**
4. **KHÔNG được bỏ relative pronoun**

**Ví dụ:**
> My brother, **who lives in Da Nang**, is a software engineer.
> *(Bỏ bớt: "My brother is a software engineer." → vẫn đủ nghĩa)*

> The Eiffel Tower, **which was completed in 1889**, attracts millions of tourists every year.

> Professor Nguyen, **whom all students deeply respect**, has just published a new book.

> Viet Nam, **where I was born and raised**, has a remarkably rich cultural heritage.

> Last summer, **when I visited the UK for the first time**, the weather was surprisingly warm.

> My grandmother, **whose cooking is legendary in our family**, taught me everything I know.

---

### 2.3 So sánh trực tiếp: Defining vs Non-Defining

| Câu | Loại | Ý nghĩa |
|-----|------|---------|
| Students **who work hard** get good grades. | Defining | Chỉ những học sinh chăm chỉ (không phải tất cả) |
| The students, **who all worked very hard**, passed the exam. | Non-Defining | Tất cả học sinh trong nhóm này đều chăm chỉ |
| The book **which she wrote** won an award. | Defining | Một cuốn sách cụ thể trong số nhiều cuốn của cô ấy |
| Her latest novel, **which she wrote in six months**, won a prestigious award. | Non-Defining | Thêm thông tin về cuốn tiểu thuyết đã được xác định |
| The man **who lives next door** is a retired professor. | Defining | Người đàn ông cụ thể — người sống kế bên |
| My neighbour, **who is a retired professor**, often works in his garden. | Non-Defining | Thêm thông tin về người hàng xóm đã được biết |

**Lỗi nghĩa quan trọng — dấu phẩy thay đổi ý nghĩa:**
> My sister **who lives in Hanoi** is a doctor. *(Defining: tôi có nhiều chị/em gái — người ở Hà Nội là bác sĩ)*
> My sister, **who lives in Hanoi**, is a doctor. *(Non-defining: tôi chỉ có một chị/em gái — cô ấy ở Hà Nội)*

## PHẦN 3: RÚT GỌN MỆNH ĐỀ QUAN HỆ

Khi relative pronoun là chủ ngữ của mệnh đề quan hệ, có thể rút gọn thành **cụm phân từ (participial phrase)**:

### 3.1 Active → V-ing (Hiện tại phân từ)

*Who/Which + động từ chủ động → V-ing*

> The man **who is standing** at the entrance is my uncle.
→ The man **standing** at the entrance is my uncle.

> A scientist **who studies** climate patterns is called a climatologist.
→ A scientist **studying** climate patterns is called a climatologist.

> The students **who were waiting** outside finally got in.
→ The students **waiting** outside finally got in.

> A river **which flows** through the city has been heavily polluted.
→ A river **flowing** through the city has been heavily polluted.

---

### 3.2 Passive → V3 (Quá khứ phân từ)

*Who/Which + is/are/was/were + V3 → V3*

> The building **which was designed** by a famous architect is now a museum.
→ The building **designed** by a famous architect is now a museum.

> The victims **who were injured** in the accident were taken to the nearest hospital.
→ The victims **injured** in the accident were taken to the nearest hospital.

> Documents **that are required** for the visa application must be submitted by Friday.
→ Documents **required** for the visa application must be submitted by Friday.

> The report **which was compiled** by the research team took two years to complete.
→ The report **compiled** by the research team took two years to complete.

---

### 3.3 Rút gọn trong Non-Defining Clauses

Non-defining clauses cũng có thể rút gọn:
> My sister, **who is studying** medicine in Germany, hopes to return next year.
→ My sister, **studying** medicine in Germany, hopes to return next year.

> The new policy, **which was introduced** last month, has already attracted criticism.
→ The new policy, **introduced** last month, has already attracted criticism.

---

### 3.4 Khi KHÔNG được rút gọn

1. Khi relative pronoun là **tân ngữ**: The book (that) **I read** was amazing → không thể rút gọn thành "The book read by me"... (awkward)
2. Khi mệnh đề quan hệ có **thì khác** với mệnh đề chính và cần phân biệt thời gian: The man, who had been waiting for hours, finally left.
3. Khi rút gọn tạo ra sự **mơ hồ về chủ ngữ**.

## PHẦN 4: GIỚI TỪ TRONG MỆNH ĐỀ QUAN HỆ

### 4.1 Informal vs Formal

Trong văn viết trang trọng (IELTS Academic, báo cáo), đặt giới từ **trước** relative pronoun:

| Informal (thông dụng) | Formal (trang trọng) |
|----------------------|---------------------|
| the company (that) she works **for** | the company **for which** she works |
| the person (who) I spoke **to** | the person **to whom** I spoke |
| the house I grew up **in** | the house **in which** I grew up |
| the topic (that) we argued **about** | the topic **about which** we argued |
| the colleague (who) she disagreed **with** | the colleague **with whom** she disagreed |
| the era (that) the novel was set **in** | the era **in which** the novel was set |

---

### 4.2 Preposition + Which / Whom

Các cụm giới từ + which/whom thường dùng trong IELTS Academic:
> The method **by which** the data was collected has been widely questioned.
> The era **during which** this literature emerged was one of great political turbulence.
> The scientist **from whom** I received the original paper later won a Nobel Prize.
> The circumstances **under which** they were operating were extraordinarily difficult.
> The purpose **for which** the fund was established has never been clearly stated.
> The rate **at which** deforestation is occurring is alarming.

---

## PHẦN 5: QUANTITATIVE RELATIVES

Dùng khi muốn diễn đạt **một phần của nhóm** được đề cập trước:
**[số lượng / lượng từ] + of + whom (người) / which (vật)**

> We interviewed 30 candidates, **most of whom** were highly experienced.
> The company released ten new products last year, **none of which** performed well commercially.
> She has three older brothers, **two of whom** currently live abroad.
> I studied ten research papers on this topic, **all of which** reached similar conclusions.
> The expedition had 15 members, **several of whom** had climbed Everest before.
> The survey gathered 500 responses, **the majority of which** supported the proposal.
> He applied to six universities, **only one of which** offered him a place.

**Các lượng từ thường dùng:** all / most / many / some / several / a few / a number / both / neither / none / one / two / three / half / the majority / a minority / the rest / few / the largest proportion

---

## PHẦN 6: WHAT = THE THING THAT

**What** (không phải mệnh đề quan hệ thực sự) = "the thing that" / "the things that":
> **What** surprised everyone was the speed of the recovery.
> *(= The thing that surprised everyone was...)*
> I don't fully understand **what** he was trying to say.
> **What** matters most in life is how you treat the people around you.
> They gave her exactly **what** she had asked for.
> **What** I find most fascinating about language is its constant evolution.

**Phân biệt quan trọng:**
> This is the information **which/that** I need. *(cụ thể — specific noun before the clause)*
> This is **what** I need. *(= the thing that I need — no specific noun before)*
> Tell me **what** happened. *(= the thing that happened)*
> Tell me **which** route you took. *(specific — choosing from options)*

## PHẦN 7: MỆNH ĐỀ QUAN HỆ TRONG IELTS

### 7.1 Tăng độ phức tạp ngữ pháp (Grammatical Range)

**Band 5–6:**
> Ho Chi Minh City is big. Many people live there. It is developing fast.

**Band 7:**
> Ho Chi Minh City, **where millions of people live**, is developing at an impressive rate.

**Band 7.5–8:**
> Ho Chi Minh City, **which has a population of nearly 10 million and which serves as the country's economic hub**, is undergoing rapid transformation.

**Band 8+:**
> The technological developments **that have reshaped the global economy in recent decades**, many of **which were considered purely theoretical** just thirty years ago, represent one of the most profound shifts in human history.

---

### 7.2 Kết hợp với các cấu trúc khác trong IELTS Task 2

**With non-defining for adding statistics:**
> Renewable energy, **which currently accounts for approximately 30% of global electricity production**, is expected to dominate the energy sector by 2050.

**With whose for institutional topics:**
> Universities **whose graduates consistently secure employment** are typically ranked higher in global league tables.

**With quantitative relatives:**
> The researchers analysed data from 12 countries, **most of which** showed a positive correlation between education levels and economic productivity.

---

### 7.3 Lỗi phổ biến trong IELTS — cần tránh

| Lỗi | Sai | Đúng |
|-----|-----|------|
| Dùng "that" trong non-defining | My city, *that* is beautiful, ... | My city, **which** is beautiful, ... |
| Bỏ subject pronoun | The man __ lives here is my neighbour. | The man **who** lives here is my neighbour. |
| Thiếu dấu phẩy cho non-defining | My father who is 60 is a teacher. | My father**, who is 60,** is a teacher. |
| Lặp đại từ | The book which I read **it** was good. | The book which I read was good. |
| Dùng "who" cho vật | The car *who* is parked outside is mine. | The car **which/that** is parked outside... |
| Dùng "which" cho người trong defining | The student *which* passed was happy. | The student **who/that** passed was happy. |

| whom | ✓ (formal) | ✗ | ✓ | ✓ | Khi là object |
| which | ✗ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | Khi là object |
| that | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✗ | Khi là object |
| whose | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✗ |
| where | — | — | ✓ | ✓ | ✗ |
| when | — | — | ✓ | ✓ | ✗ |

## LỖI THƯỜNG GẶP

| Sai | Đúng | Giải thích |
|---|---|---|
| My city, that is beautiful, is developing fast. | My city, **which** is beautiful, is developing fast. | Non-defining clause KHÔNG dùng "that"; chỉ dùng "which" sau dấu phẩy |
| The man lives here is my neighbour. | The man **who** lives here is my neighbour. | Không được bỏ relative pronoun khi nó là **chủ ngữ** của mệnh đề quan hệ |
| My father who is 60 is a teacher. | My father**, who is 60,** is a teacher. | Non-defining clause cần dấu phẩy hai bên để tách thông tin bổ sung |
| The book which I read it was good. | The book which I read was good. | Không lặp đại từ — "the book" đã là chủ ngữ, không thêm "it" sau "read" |
| The car who is parked outside is mine. | The car **which/that** is parked outside is mine. | "Who" chỉ dùng cho người; "which" hoặc "that" cho vật/động vật |
| The student which passed was happy. | The student **who/that** passed was happy. | "Which" không dùng cho người; phải dùng "who" hoặc "that" (defining) |
| The man whom called you is my uncle. | The man **who** called you is my uncle. | "Whom" là tân ngữ; "who" là chủ ngữ — "called" cần chủ ngữ → dùng "who" |
| The reason why is she left never known. | The reason **why** she left was never known. | Sau "why" trong mệnh đề quan hệ, giữ nguyên trật tự S + V (không đảo ngữ) |

---

## TÓM TẮT NHANH

- **Đại từ quan hệ:** who/whom = người; which = vật/động vật; that = người hoặc vật (chỉ trong defining); whose = sở hữu
- **Defining (xác định):** không có dấu phẩy, có thể dùng "that", có thể bỏ pronoun khi nó là **tân ngữ**
- **Non-defining (không xác định):** có dấu phẩy hai bên, KHÔNG dùng "that", KHÔNG bỏ relative pronoun
- **Rút gọn chủ động:** who/which + đang làm → V-ing (*the man **standing** at the door*)
- **Rút gọn bị động:** who/which + is/was + V3 → V3 (*the report **compiled** by the team*)
- **Quantitative relatives:** most of whom / none of which / several of whom — diễn đạt một phần của nhóm
