# EXERCISE — Bài 6: Gerunds vs Infinitives

## SECTION A: Gerund or Infinitive? (20 items)

1. She enjoys _______ (read) crime novels.
2. He hopes _______ (become) an engineer.
3. They finished _______ (paint) the house last week.
4. She managed _______ (pass) all her exams.
5. I'm considering _______ (change) my job.
6. He refuses _______ (apologize) for what he said.
7. She admitted _______ (make) a mistake in the report.
8. We arranged _______ (meet) at 7pm.
9. He tends _______ (arrive) late to meetings.
10. I can't help _______ (laugh) when I see that clip.
11. She promised _______ (call) me back.
12. I really miss _______ (live) near the beach.
13. Would you mind _______ (turn) down the music?
14. They decided _______ (postpone) the event.
15. She seems _______ (be) very talented.
16. He threatened _______ (report) them to the management.
17. We recommend _______ (book) early for the best prices.
18. She agreed _______ (help) with the project.
19. He's practising _______ (speak) English every day.
20. I need _______ (rest) — I'm exhausted.

## SECTION B: Choose the correct form + explain meaning (8 items — TRICKY)

1. I stopped _______ on the way to buy some milk. (stop/to stop)
   a) stopping  b) to stop  c) stop

2. She remembered _______ him at a party years ago.
   a) meeting  b) to meet  c) met

3. Don't forget _______ your passport when you travel.
   a) bringing  b) to bring  c) bring

4. He tried _______ the car but the engine was dead.
   a) starting  b) to start  c) started

5. We regret _______ you that we cannot offer you the position.
   a) telling  b) to tell  c) told

6. What did you mean by _______ that?
   a) say  b) to say  c) saying

7. The new job means _______ much earlier every morning.
   a) get up  b) to get up  c) getting up

8. After losing his first job, he went on _______ his own company.
   a) founding  b) to found  c) found

   ✓ _______________

   ✓ _______________

   ✓ _______________

## SECTION D: Rewrite using Gerund or Infinitive (6 items)

1. It's impossible for me to afford this.
   → I can't afford _______________

2. His habit is lying in bed until noon.
   → He is used to _______________

3. The task involves you to travel frequently.
   → The task involves _______________

4. It's a good idea for you to see a doctor.
   → You should consider _______________

5. She continued to work despite being tired.
   → She kept _______________

6. Her ambition is to become a journalist.
   → She hopes _______________

## SECTION F: IELTS Writing Practice
Write a paragraph (100-120 words) about your language learning experience.
Use at least:
- 2 verbs followed by Gerund only
- 2 verbs followed by Infinitive only
- 1 instance of a "meaning-change" pair (stop/remember/try)
