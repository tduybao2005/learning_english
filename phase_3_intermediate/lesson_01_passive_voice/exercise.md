## SECTION F: SHORT WRITING TASK — 8 câu / đoạn văn

### F1. Process Description (IELTS Task 1 style)

Mô tả quy trình sản xuất cà phê hòa tan bằng 6-8 câu sử dụng thể bị động. Dùng các từ gợi ý:
*harvest / transport / roast / grind / mix / heat / spray / pack / distribute*

**Gợi ý câu mở đầu:** *The production of instant coffee involves several stages, all of which are carefully controlled to ensure quality.*

---

### F2. IELTS Task 2 Paragraph

Viết một đoạn văn 80-100 từ về chủ đề: **"Environmental protection"**. Bài viết PHẢI sử dụng ít nhất:
- 3 câu bị động ở các thì khác nhau
- 1 câu với modal passive (must/should + be + V3)
- 1 câu với "It is believed/argued that..."

### F3. Rewrite the passage using passive voice where appropriate

Đoạn sau đây quá nhiều câu chủ động. Viết lại, sử dụng bị động ít nhất 4 lần:

## SECTION D

**D1.** ❌ was write → ✓ was **written**
*(V3 của "write" là "written", không phải "write")*

**D2.** ❌ is build → ✓ is **being built**
*(Present Continuous Passive = is/are + being + V3)*

**D3.** Câu đúng về ngữ pháp. Tuy nhiên "by the postman" có thể bỏ vì đã rõ ràng:
→ "The package was delivered to me yesterday, so I signed for it."

**D4.** ❌ is being operated → ✓ is being **operated on**
*(Phrasal verb "operate on" - không được tách giới từ)*

**D5.** ❌ was given to a scholarship → ✓ was given **a scholarship** (không cần "to")
*(Khi IO làm chủ ngữ trong 2-object passive, không dùng "to")*
*(Hoặc: A scholarship was given **to** her — khi DO làm chủ ngữ thì cần "to")*

**D6.** ❌ must been arrested → ✓ must **be** arrested
*(Modal passive = modal + **be** + V3, không phải "been")*

**D7.** ❌ are manufactures → ✓ are **manufactured**
*(Cần V3 "manufactured", không phải danh từ "manufactures")*

**D8.** ❌ will being affected → ✓ will **be** affected
*(Future passive = will + **be** + V3, không có "being")*

## SECTION F (Gợi ý đáp án)

### F1. Sample answer — Coffee production

*The production of instant coffee involves several stages, all of which are carefully controlled to ensure quality. First, ripe coffee cherries are harvested by hand or by machine. They are then transported to processing facilities where the beans are extracted and cleaned. The beans are carefully roasted at high temperatures to develop their flavor. After roasting, they are finely ground into a powder. The ground coffee is then mixed with hot water to produce a concentrated liquid. This liquid is heated and sprayed as a fine mist into a drying chamber, where it solidifies into powder. Finally, the powder is packed into airtight containers and distributed to markets worldwide.*

### F2. Sample paragraph

*The environment has been severely damaged by decades of industrial activity and unsustainable consumption. It is widely believed that immediate action must be taken to reverse this damage before irreversible harm is caused. Significant reductions in carbon emissions should be achieved through the transition to renewable energy. Vast amounts of plastic are released into the ocean every year, threatening marine ecosystems. If effective policies are implemented now, the worst effects of climate change can still be avoided.*

### F3. Rewritten passage

*New evidence that climate change is accelerating faster than predicted **has been discovered** by scientists. Their findings **were published** in three major journals last month. No new policies **have yet been implemented** by the government. It **is widely believed** that this crisis must be addressed immediately.*

### F4. Sample sentences

1. I **had my eyes tested** by an optician last month. *(past simple)*
2. I usually **have my hair cut** at a salon near my house. *(present simple)*
3. I **have just had my laptop repaired** — the screen was broken. *(present perfect)*
4. I **will have my bicycle serviced** before the long trip. *(future simple)*
5. I **am currently having my apartment repainted** — the walls were too dark. *(present continuous)*
