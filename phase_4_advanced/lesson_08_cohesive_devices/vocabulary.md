## BÀI TẬP 1: THAY THẾ CONNECTOR CƠ BẢN (15 items)

**Hướng dẫn:** Thay thế connector bằng chữ đậm bằng một connector nâng cao và phù hợp hơn. Không được thay đổi ý nghĩa câu.

**1.** "Many young people cannot afford to buy homes. **However**, they can still invest in other assets."
→ Connector thay thế: _______

**2.** "The experiment produced unexpected results. **Because of** the anomaly, further testing was required."
→ Connector thay thế: _______

**3.** "Urbanisation brings economic benefits. **In addition**, it concentrates healthcare and educational services."
→ Connector thay thế: _______

**4.** "Digital banking is now widespread. **For example**, in 2023, 78% of transactions in Europe were cashless."
→ Connector thay thế: _______

**5.** "Climate change is accelerating. **So**, coastal communities must adapt urgently."
→ Connector thay thế: _______

**6.** "The report was detailed. **However**, several key recommendations were overlooked."
→ Connector thay thế: _______

**7.** "Both policy options were evaluated. Option A reduces costs **but** Option B is more sustainable."
→ Connector thay thế that begins a new sentence: _______

**8.** "The government introduced the reform. **After that**, public trust in institutions improved."
→ Connector thay thế: _______

**9.** "Social media has transformed communication. **Because of** its reach, misinformation also spreads rapidly."
→ Connector thay thế: _______

**10.** "The technology has been adopted widely. **In addition**, it has been improved significantly since its launch."
→ Connector thay thế (suggests what follows is MORE important): _______

**11.** "Poverty rates remain high. **However**, significant progress has been achieved in recent decades."
→ Connector thay thế that follows a concession: _______

**12.** "Many employers prefer experienced candidates. **However**, skills are ultimately more important than credentials."
→ Connector thay thế: _______

**13.** "Research has produced conflicting results. The theory is **therefore** still widely debated."
→ Connector thay thế: _______

**14.** "**In conclusion**, both environmental and economic factors must be addressed simultaneously."
→ Connector thay thế: _______

**15.** "The data shows a strong correlation between education levels and income. **For example**, in Finland, post-secondary completion rates are the highest in Europe."
→ Connector thay thế: _______

## BÀI TẬP 2: CHỌN CONNECTOR PHÙ HỢP NHẤT (10 items)

**Hướng dẫn:** Chọn một trong hai connector trong ngoặc. Chú ý đến chức năng và register.

**1.** "Vaccination rates have improved dramatically. _______, herd immunity has not yet been achieved in all regions."
*(Nevertheless / On the contrary)*

**2.** "The manufacturing sector employs 30% of the workforce. _______ its importance, proposed cuts to industrial subsidies have been widely condemned."
*(Owing to / In light of)*

**3.** "Urban planners face competing demands for space, resources, and funding. _______ cities like Singapore have demonstrated that innovative design can reconcile these demands."
*(Nevertheless / That said)*

**4.** "Obesity rates have tripled in three decades. _______ this trend, healthcare costs are projected to become unsustainable."
*(Given / As a consequence of)*

**5.** "Digital education has clear merits. _______, it struggles to replicate the interpersonal dynamics of traditional classroom learning."
*(However / That said)*

**6.** "Remote working reduces commuting time and office expenses. _______, it enables companies to recruit from a global talent pool."
*(What is more / On the contrary)*

**7.** "It is insufficient to address symptoms without tackling root causes. _______ — real reform requires systemic change."
*(Ultimately / Nevertheless)*

**8.** "Early intervention in mental health has demonstrably positive outcomes. _______ the UK's school counselling programme, which reduced student dropout rates by 23%."
*(A case in point is / As a result)*

**9.** "Economic growth has lifted millions from poverty. _______, it has accelerated environmental degradation at an alarming rate."
*(Conversely / Additionally)*

**10.** "Studies _______ that increased screen time in children correlates with reduced sleep quality and attention deficits."
*(suggest / prove)*

## ĐÁP ÁN

### Bài tập 1:
1. That said / Nevertheless / Notwithstanding this
2. Owing to / As a consequence of / On account of
3. Moreover / What is more / Beyond that / Equally importantly
4. To illustrate / As evidenced by / A case in point is
5. Consequently / As a result / Hence / Thus
6. That said / Notwithstanding this / Nonetheless
7. The former reduces costs; the latter is more sustainable. (Use former/latter)
8. Subsequently / In the wake of this
9. By virtue of / Owing to
10. Moreover (implies the added point is MORE important)
11. That said / Be that as it may / Even so
12. Ultimately / In the final analysis / That said
13. Hence / Thus / Consequently / It follows that
14. On balance / All things considered / In the final analysis / Ultimately
15. A case in point is / As evidenced by / To illustrate

### Bài tập 2:
1. Nevertheless (contrast — không phải full negation nên On the contrary sai)
2. In light of (follows evidence/data)
3. That said (concession then contrast)
4. As a consequence of (the trend is the cause of the healthcare cost projection)
5. That said (softer, concession-contrast)
6. What is more (addition with emphasis)
7. Ultimately (conclusion/emphasis)
8. A case in point is (exemplification)
9. Conversely (opposite result)
10. suggest (hedge — not "prove" as research rarely proves definitively)

### Bài tập 3 — Mẫu viết lại Band 7–8:

*"The rapid adoption of digital learning platforms has fundamentally reshaped educational access globally. Such technology offers considerable advantages: students can engage with course material at their own pace, and the elimination of commuting reduces both time and financial costs significantly. What is more, online provision has democratised access to world-class instruction in ways that traditional institutions cannot match. That said, these platforms are not without limitations. Arguably, the most significant challenge is the social isolation inherent in remote study — the spontaneous peer interaction and mentorship that characterise campus life are difficult to replicate digitally. Added to this, reliable high-speed internet remains inaccessible to substantial portions of the global population, meaning that online education, despite its apparent inclusivity, may inadvertently widen existing educational inequalities."*
