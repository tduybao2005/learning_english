# EXERCISE — Bài 8: Cohesive Devices

## SECTION A: Choose the correct cohesive device (15 items)
Choose the most appropriate device from the options:

1. The company has invested heavily in renewable energy. _______, its carbon footprint has reduced by 40%.
   a) However  b) Consequently  c) In contrast  d) Whereas

2. Many students perform well in exams. _______, anxiety remains a significant problem for some.
   a) Furthermore  b) Therefore  c) Nevertheless  d) Similarly

3. Exercise improves cardiovascular health. _______, it has been shown to reduce symptoms of depression.
   a) In contrast  b) Moreover  c) However  d) Consequently

4. _______ social media can connect people, it can also lead to isolation.
   a) Although  b) Therefore  c) Similarly  d) Hence

5. The economy is growing rapidly. _______, not all citizens are benefiting equally.
   a) Furthermore  b) Therefore  c) However  d) Thus

6. Japan has a highly educated workforce. _______, South Korea has made significant investments in technology.
   a) Similarly  b) Therefore  c) However  d) Consequently

7. The experiment was flawed. _______, the results cannot be considered reliable.
   a) Nevertheless  b) Moreover  c) Therefore  d) In contrast

8. _______ the high cost, many families continue to invest in private education.
   a) Despite  b) Because  c) Therefore  d) Similarly

9. The data showed a clear trend. _______, researchers concluded that further study was needed.
   a) However  b) Despite  c) Nevertheless  d) In light of this

10. The industrial revolution transformed Britain. _______, it changed the entire world.
    a) Nevertheless  b) Similarly  c) More broadly  d) However

11. The patient had been taking medication for six months. _______, their condition had not improved.
    a) Furthermore  b) Nevertheless  c) Therefore  d) Similarly

12. Traditional classroom teaching remains important. _______, online learning platforms are becoming increasingly popular.
    a) Consequently  b) That said  c) Similarly  d) Therefore

13. The government has introduced stricter environmental regulations. _______, pollution levels have continued to rise.
    a) As a result  b) Ironically, however  c) Furthermore  d) Similarly

14. Many developed countries are experiencing aging populations. _______, developing nations often have very young populations.
    a) In contrast  b) Moreover  c) Therefore  d) Similarly

15. The research was conducted over ten years. _______, the sample size was relatively small.
    a) In contrast  b) Additionally  c) That said  d) Therefore

## SECTION D: Reference devices — Identify what the underlined word refers to (6 items)

Read this paragraph:
"The Amazon rainforest is often called the 'lungs of the Earth'. **This** is because **it** contains approximately 10% of all species on our planet. However, **the region** is being destroyed at an alarming rate. **Such** destruction threatens not only local biodiversity but also global climate stability. Scientists have warned that if deforestation continues at **this rate**, we may reach a tipping point within decades."

1. "This" (sentence 2) refers to _______________
2. "it" (sentence 2) refers to _______________
3. "the region" (sentence 3) refers to _______________
4. "Such" (sentence 4) refers to _______________
5. "this rate" (sentence 5) refers to _______________

## SECTION E: IELTS Writing — Cohesive paragraph practice
Write a well-cohesive paragraph (100-120 words) on ONE of these topics. Use at least 6 different cohesive devices and underline each one.

Topics:
A) "The advantages and disadvantages of remote working"
B) "The impact of tourism on local culture and economy"
C) "Whether technology has made people more or less connected"

Checklist:
[ ] At least 2 devices showing addition (furthermore/moreover/in addition)
[ ] At least 1 device showing contrast (however/nevertheless/in contrast)
[ ] At least 1 device showing result (consequently/therefore/as a result)
[ ] Reference pronouns used correctly (this/these/it)
[ ] No cohesive device overused (max 2 times per device)
