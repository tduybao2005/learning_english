# BÀI 8: TỪ NỐI NÂNG CAO & LIÊN KẾT VĂN BẢN
## ADVANCED COHESIVE DEVICES — IELTS Band 7–8 Coherence & Cohesion

---

## TẠI SAO ĐÂY LÀ MỘT TRONG NHỮNG BÀI QUAN TRỌNG NHẤT?

Coherence & Cohesion (CC) chiếm **25%** tổng điểm IELTS Writing. Tuy nhiên, đây cũng là tiêu chí mà hầu hết học viên Việt Nam mắc lỗi nhiều nhất — không phải vì thiếu connectors, mà vì **dùng quá ít loại connectors, dùng sai chức năng, hoặc dùng ở mọi chỗ một cách máy móc.**

### Band Descriptors — Coherence & Cohesion:

| Band | Mô tả |
|------|-------|
| 5 | "makes inadequate, inaccurate or overuse of cohesive devices" |
| 6 | "uses cohesive devices effectively, but cohesion within and/or between sentences may be faulty or mechanical" |
| 7 | "uses a range of cohesive devices appropriately, although there may be some under- or over-use" |
| 8 | "sequences information and ideas logically; manages all aspects of cohesion well" |
| 9 | "uses cohesion in such a way that it attracts no attention; skillfully manages paragraphing" |

**Vấn đề cốt lõi của người học ở Band 5–6:** Họ biết "however", "furthermore", "in addition" và dùng chúng ở mỗi câu như một công thức. Examiner nhận ra điều này ngay lập tức — nó trở thành "mechanical cohesion", không phải "effective cohesion."

**Mục tiêu của bài này:** Bạn sẽ học hơn 80 cách liên kết văn bản tinh tế, đúng chức năng, đúng register — để bài viết của bạn đọc như một bài viết của người nói tiếng Anh bản ngữ học thuật.

## PHẦN 1: THAY THẾ CÁC TỪ NỐI CƠ BẢN

---

### 1.1 THAY THẾ "HOWEVER"

"However" biểu thị contrast (tương phản). Vấn đề là người học dùng nó cho **mọi loại** tương phản mà không phân biệt:

**Nhóm A — Tương phản trực tiếp (nhưng vẫn tiếp nối lập luận):**
- **Nevertheless** — vẫn vậy, dù như vậy (formal, strong)
  - *"Air pollution levels remain dangerously high. Nevertheless, the government has resisted implementing stricter vehicle emission standards."*
- **Nonetheless** — tương tự nevertheless, slightly more formal
  - *"The evidence for this theory is limited. Nonetheless, it provides a useful framework for analysis."*
- **Even so** — dù vậy (semi-formal, conversational tone)
  - *"The policy faces significant opposition. Even so, the administration remains committed to its implementation."*
- **That said** — tuy nhiên (follows a concession, conversational-formal)
  - *"Digital learning has clear advantages. That said, it cannot entirely replicate the social dimensions of classroom interaction."*
- **All the same** — dù vậy, vẫn thế (semi-formal)
  - *"The risks are considerable. All the same, the potential benefits justify further investment in the research."*

**Nhóm B — Tương phản mạnh (unexpected result):**
- **Notwithstanding this** — bất chấp điều này (very formal, academic)
  - *"Significant funding has been allocated to the programme. Notwithstanding this, outcomes remain disappointing."*
- **Be that as it may** — dù sao đi nữa (formal, slightly archaic but respected in academic writing)
  - *"Critics argue that the policy is politically motivated. Be that as it may, its environmental benefits are undeniable."*
- **Yet** — nhưng mà (can begin a sentence, less formal than Nevertheless but precise)
  - *"Technology has transformed communication. Yet many communities remain digitally excluded."*

**Nhóm C — Phủ nhận hoàn toàn (ONLY when what follows COMPLETELY contradicts what came before):**
- **On the contrary** — ngược lại hoàn toàn (DO NOT USE for simple contrast — only for full negation)
  - ❌ Wrong: "Some think social media is harmful. On the contrary, it does have some benefits." (NOT full negation)
  - ✓ Correct: "Some claim that renewable energy cannot meet current demand. On the contrary, recent data demonstrates it already exceeds fossil fuel generation in several nations."

---

### 1.2 THAY THẾ "FURTHERMORE" / "IN ADDITION"

Những từ này biểu thị addition (bổ sung). Sai lầm phổ biến: dùng chúng để bắt đầu body paragraphs hoặc trong mỗi câu.

- **Moreover** — hơn nữa (formal, signals an even stronger/more important point follows)
  - *"The policy reduces carbon emissions. Moreover, it generates significant employment in the renewable sector."*
- **What is more** — hơn thế nữa (semi-formal, emphatic)
  - *"Urbanisation creates economic opportunity. What is more, it concentrates human capital in ways that drive innovation."*
- **Over and above this** — thêm vào đó (formal, used when adding something beyond what was expected)
  - *"The system provides real-time feedback. Over and above this, it stores historical data for longitudinal analysis."*
- **Added to this** — thêm vào đó (semi-formal)
  - *"Public transport reduces traffic congestion. Added to this, it significantly lowers per-capita carbon emissions."*
- **Beyond that** — ngoài ra (formal, suggests going further)
  - *"The initiative improves literacy. Beyond that, it builds community cohesion and civic pride."*
- **By the same token** — theo logic tương tự (when the addition follows the same reasoning)
  - *"Governments that invest in public health reduce long-term healthcare costs. By the same token, investment in education reduces poverty over the long term."*
- **Equally importantly** — quan trọng không kém (signals equal weight, not just an addition)
  - *"Diet plays a critical role in longevity. Equally importantly, regular physical activity and quality sleep are essential."*

---

### 1.3 THAY THẾ "BECAUSE"

"Because" là từ đúng nhưng dùng quá nhiều làm văn viết trở nên đơn điệu.

- **Owing to the fact that** — do việc/thực tế là (very formal, academic)
  - *"Deforestation rates have accelerated, owing to the fact that agricultural land demand has tripled in four decades."*
- **In light of** — xét trong bối cảnh, do (follows evidence/data)
  - *"In light of the mounting scientific consensus, further delay in climate action is indefensible."*
- **Given that** — với điều kiện là, xét rằng (semi-formal, can begin a sentence)
  - *"Given that urban populations are projected to double by 2050, sustainable city planning is no longer optional."*
- **On account of** — vì lý do, do (formal)
  - *"Many rural hospitals have closed, on account of chronic underfunding and staffing shortages."*
- **By virtue of** — nhờ, do (formal — positive reason, ability, or position)
  - *"By virtue of its geographical position, Singapore became a natural hub for international trade."*
- **As a consequence of** — do hậu quả của (formal — used when the cause led to a result)
  - *"As a consequence of widespread deforestation, biodiversity in the region has declined dramatically."*
- **Seeing that / Seeing as** — xét vì (semi-formal, slightly conversational — for Speaking or informal writing)
  - *"Seeing that public trust in institutions is declining, transparency measures are urgently needed."*
- **Inasmuch as** — trong chừng mực mà (very formal, legal/academic — use sparingly)
  - *"The policy is flawed, inasmuch as it fails to account for regional economic disparities."*

---

### 1.4 THAY THẾ "FOR EXAMPLE"

- **To illustrate** — để minh hoạ (formal)
  - *"Access to technology is unevenly distributed. To illustrate, only 32% of rural households in developing nations have reliable internet access."*
- **A case in point is** — một ví dụ điển hình là (formal, academic)
  - *"Many cities have successfully reduced car dependency. A case in point is Amsterdam, which has invested in cycling infrastructure for decades."*
- **As evidenced by** — như được chứng minh bởi (formal, data-driven)
  - *"Consumer behaviour is shifting, as evidenced by the 40% increase in plant-based food purchases in the past three years."*
- **As exemplified by** — như được thể hiện qua ví dụ của (formal)
  - *"Educational innovation takes many forms, as exemplified by Finland's project-based learning curriculum."*
- **This is demonstrated by** — điều này được thể hiện bởi (formal, after making a claim)
  - *"Public health campaigns can shift behaviour dramatically. This is demonstrated by the success of anti-smoking campaigns in reducing smoking rates by 60% over three decades."*
- **A prime illustration of this is** — một ví dụ điển hình nhất là (formal, emphatic)
  - *"Emerging economies can leapfrog traditional development stages. A prime illustration of this is Kenya's mobile banking revolution."*

---

### 1.5 THAY THẾ "IN CONCLUSION"

- **All things considered** — xét tất cả các khía cạnh (balanced conclusion)
- **Taking everything into account** — xét tất cả mọi thứ (tương tự above)
- **On balance** — nhìn chung, xét về tổng thể (suggests weighing pros and cons)
- **Weighing up both sides** — sau khi cân nhắc cả hai phía (useful for discussion essays)
- **In the final analysis** — cuối cùng, xét cho cùng (formal, emphatic)
- **Ultimately** — cuối cùng thì (can also appear mid-essay for emphasis, not only conclusions)
- **On the whole** — nhìn chung (semi-formal, good for balanced conclusions)
- **To draw a conclusion** — để kết luận (straightforward but less elegant)

**⚠️ Tránh bắt đầu conclusion bằng:** "To sum up", "As a conclusion", "Finally I conclude that..." (all sound very weak or ESL-like)

## PHẦN 2: REFERENCE DEVICES (Thiết bị tham chiếu)

Reference devices là kỹ thuật **thay thế từ lặp lại** bằng các cách tham chiếu tinh tế hơn. Đây là yếu tố phân biệt Band 7 và Band 8 rõ rệt nhất.

### 2.1 The Former / The Latter
Dùng khi có **hai** thứ vừa được đề cập:
- *"Two approaches exist: automation and reskilling. The former reduces labour costs; the latter preserves employment."*
- ⚠️ Lỗi: Đừng dùng former/latter khi có hơn 2 thứ được đề cập.

### 2.2 Such + Noun (thay thế cho "this kind of" hoặc lặp lại noun)
- *"Governments have implemented aggressive austerity measures. Such policies have drawn criticism from economists and social advocates alike."*
- *"Children exposed to excessive screen time show reduced attention spans. Such findings have prompted calls for stricter guidelines."*

### 2.3 This/These/That/Those + Summary Noun
Thay vì lặp lại noun hoặc viết "This is problematic" (vague), dùng a noun to capture what "this" refers to:

| Thay vì... | Dùng... |
|---|---|
| "This is a problem" | "This **disparity** is problematic" |
| "This happened because..." | "This **phenomenon** arose because..." |
| "These cause damage" | "These **practices** cause significant damage" |
| "This needs to change" | "This **trend** requires urgent reversal" |
| "This is good" | "This **development** represents significant progress" |

**Useful summary nouns:** phenomenon, trend, disparity, shift, development, approach, strategy, challenge, concern, notion, principle, dynamic, practice, mechanism, implication, consequence

### 2.4 The + Comparative Structure (cohesive and sophisticated)
- *"The more governments invest in preventive healthcare, the less they spend on acute treatment."*
- *"The greater the urban density, the more urgent the need for efficient public transport."*

### 2.5 Pronoun Reference — avoid vague pronoun use
- ❌ "Politicians claim they support education. They say it will improve outcomes. This is debatable."
- ✓ "Politicians frequently advocate for educational investment, claiming that such reforms yield long-term social and economic dividends. This assertion, however, is not consistently supported by evidence."

## PHẦN 3: HEDGING LANGUAGE (Ngôn ngữ thận trọng)

Trong IELTS Writing Task 2, Band 7+ không nói chắc 100% về những gì không thể chắc chắn. Overgeneralisation là lỗi phổ biến ở Band 5-6.

### 3.1 Modal Hedges
- **May / Might / Could** — suggest possibility, not certainty
  - ❌ "Technology destroys social relationships." → ✓ "Technology **may** erode certain dimensions of face-to-face social interaction."
  - ❌ "AI will replace doctors." → ✓ "AI **could** eventually supplant routine diagnostic functions performed by physicians."
- **Would** — conditional/theoretical
  - *"A universal basic income **would** theoretically address income inequality, though implementation challenges are significant."*

### 3.2 Adverbial Hedges
- **Arguably** — có thể lập luận rằng (introduces a debatable claim)
  - *"Climate change is **arguably** the most consequential challenge humanity has ever faced."*
- **Apparently** — theo như có vẻ (based on evidence but not fully verified)
- **Presumably** — có thể suy đoán là (logical but not confirmed)
- **Conceivably** — có thể tưởng tượng được (within the realm of possibility)
- **Potentially** — có khả năng (future possibility)
- **In all likelihood** — rất có thể (high probability hedge)

### 3.3 Verb Hedges
- **Suggest** / **Indicate** — evidence points to but doesn't prove
  - *"The data **suggest** a correlation between urban density and increased public transport usage."*
- **Appear to / Seem to** — observable but not confirmed
  - *"Younger generations **appear to** prioritise work-life balance over salary in job selection."*
- **Tend to** — general pattern, not universal
  - *"Students who engage in collaborative projects **tend to** develop stronger communication skills."*
- **Be likely to** — probability
  - *"Coastal cities **are likely to** experience more frequent flooding events as sea levels rise."*

### 3.4 Phrase Hedges
- **It could be argued that** — introduces a potentially controversial claim
- **There is evidence to suggest that** — data-based but not conclusive
- **To some extent** — partially true (acknowledging complexity)
- **In many cases** — frequent but not universal
- **Under certain circumstances** — conditional truth

## PHẦN 4: PARALLEL STRUCTURE (Cấu trúc song song)

Parallel structure là kỹ thuật đảm bảo các phần trong một danh sách hoặc cấu trúc so sánh có cùng dạng ngữ pháp. Đây là yếu tố quan trọng trong **Grammatical Range & Accuracy** cũng như CC.

### Quy tắc cơ bản:
Khi liệt kê 3 thứ trở lên, tất cả phải cùng dạng:

| Sai | Đúng |
|---|---|
| "She enjoys reading, to swim, and to go hiking." | "She enjoys **reading, swimming, and hiking**." |
| "The benefits are economic, they help society, and helping the environment." | "The benefits are **economic, social, and environmental**." |
| "The plan will reduce costs, efficiency will improve, and more jobs are created." | "The plan will **reduce costs, improve efficiency, and create** more jobs." |

### Parallel clauses in IELTS Writing:
- *"Investment in education **reduces** poverty, **increases** productivity, and **strengthens** social cohesion."*
- *"The policy has been criticised for **being** too expensive, **failing to** address root causes, and **ignoring** regional disparities."*
- *"Governments must **address** income inequality, **invest** in renewable infrastructure, and **reform** tax systems."*

## PHẦN 5: NOUN PHRASES AS COHESIVE REFERENCE

Instead of repeating a key term across multiple sentences, use varied noun phrases that reference the same concept:

**Topic: Climate Change**
- First mention: "climate change"
- Second: "this environmental crisis"
- Third: "this accelerating phenomenon"
- Fourth: "the challenge"
- Fifth: "this pressing global concern"

**Topic: Social media**
- "social media platforms"
- "these digital spaces"
- "such online environments"
- "these networks"
- "this medium"

## PHẦN 6: STANCE MARKERS (Dấu hiệu lập trường)

Stance markers cho biết ai đang nói và thái độ của người viết là gì. Chúng rất quan trọng trong discussion essays.

- **According to [source]** — theo [nguồn] (citing external authority)
- **In the view of** — theo quan điểm của
- **From this perspective** — từ góc nhìn này
- **As [experts/critics/proponents] argue** — theo như [chuyên gia/nhà phê bình/những người ủng hộ] lập luận
- **Critics of this view maintain that** — những người phê phán quan điểm này cho rằng
- **Proponents of this position argue that** — những người ủng hộ lập trường này lập luận rằng
- **Sceptics contend that** — những người hoài nghi cho rằng
- **Advocates of [X] insist that** — những người ủng hộ [X] khẳng định rằng

## SO SÁNH BAND 6 vs BAND 7–8 COHESION

### Ví dụ 1 — Band 6 (cơ học, lặp lại, mechanical):

*"Nowadays, many people use social media. Furthermore, social media has many advantages. However, social media also has disadvantages. For example, social media can cause addiction. In addition, social media affects mental health. In conclusion, social media has both advantages and disadvantages."*

**Vấn đề:** 
- Lặp "social media" 6 lần
- Dùng "Furthermore", "However", "In addition", "In conclusion" như công thức
- Không có stance markers
- Không có hedging

### Ví dụ 2 — Band 7–8 (flexible, effective, varied):

*"The pervasive adoption of social media platforms has generated both opportunities and risks that merit careful examination. Such technologies facilitate instantaneous communication across geographical boundaries — a development that has demonstrably strengthened professional networks and civic engagement. That said, there is growing evidence to suggest that excessive use may correlate with heightened anxiety and reduced attention span among younger users. This psychological dimension arguably represents the most pressing concern surrounding these platforms. Notwithstanding their clear utility, governing bodies are increasingly recognising the need for regulatory frameworks that protect vulnerable users while preserving the communicative freedoms social media affords."*

**Phân tích kỹ thuật dùng:**
- "Such technologies" → thay thế "social media" lần 2
- "a development that" → summary noun
- "That said" → thay thế "However"  
- "there is growing evidence to suggest" → hedging phrase
- "may correlate" → modal hedge
- "arguably" → adverbial hedge
- "This psychological dimension" → summary noun phrase replacing "this"
- "Notwithstanding their clear utility" → formal replacement for "However"
- "regulatory frameworks that protect" → parallel-style embedded clause

## TÓM TẮT NHANH — CHECKLIST KHI VIẾT BAND 7–8

Trước khi nộp bài, kiểm tra:
- [ ] Không có 2 câu liên tiếp bắt đầu bằng cùng một connector
- [ ] "However" không xuất hiện quá 2 lần trong toàn bài
- [ ] Mỗi noun chính không xuất hiện quá 2 lần liên tiếp — dùng reference devices
- [ ] Có ít nhất 2 hedging expressions trong bài
- [ ] Tất cả danh sách 3 items đều song song về ngữ pháp
- [ ] Có stance markers khi trình bày quan điểm của người khác
- [ ] Không bắt đầu conclusion bằng "To sum up" hoặc "In short"
- [ ] Có ít nhất 3 loại connector khác nhau (contrast, addition, result, concession, illustration)

## LỖI THƯỜNG GẶP

| Sai | Đúng | Giải thích |
|---|---|---|
| Technology is useful. However, it has risks. However, regulation helps. However, users must be aware. | Technology is useful. However, it carries risks. Notwithstanding these concerns, regulation can mitigate harm. Users, nonetheless, must remain vigilant. | Không dùng "However" liên tiếp nhiều câu — thay bằng nevertheless, notwithstanding, even so, yet. |
| "Some say it's harmful. On the contrary, it has some benefits." | "Some say it's harmful. That said / However, it does have benefits." | "On the contrary" chỉ dùng khi phủ nhận HOÀN TOÀN ý trước — không dùng cho contrast đơn giản. |
| "Technology is useful. Furthermore, pollution is a problem." | "Technology is useful. Furthermore, it has democratised access to information." | Chỉ dùng connector khi hai ý thực sự có quan hệ logic — "furthermore" không nối hai ý không liên quan. |
| "Social media is popular. Social media has advantages. Social media can..." | "Social media is popular. Such platforms offer advantages. This medium, however, can..." | Tránh lặp noun — dùng reference devices: such + noun, this + summary noun, these networks. |
| "Governments should invest more. This is important." | "Governments should invest more. This commitment is essential." | "This" vague cần được theo sau bởi summary noun cụ thể để clarity. |
| In conclusion, to sum up, this essay has discussed... | In the final analysis, the evidence strongly supports... | "To sum up" và "this essay has discussed" là informal và yếu — tránh bắt đầu conclusion như vậy. |
| "The benefits are economic, they help society, and helping the environment." | "The benefits are economic, social, and environmental." | Parallel structure: danh sách phải cùng loại từ (adjectives, không mix noun/clause). |

---

## TÓM TẮT NHANH

- **Thay thế "however":** nevertheless, nonetheless, even so, that said, all the same, yet, notwithstanding this — chọn theo mức độ contrast.
- **Thay thế "furthermore/in addition":** moreover, what is more, over and above this, beyond that, by the same token, equally importantly.
- **Thay thế "for example":** to illustrate, a case in point is, as evidenced by, as exemplified by, a prime illustration of this is.
- **Reference devices:** the former/latter; such + noun; this/these + summary noun (phenomenon, trend, shift, challenge, disparity, mechanism).
- **Hedging:** may/might/could, arguably, apparently, seem to, tend to, in all likelihood — không tuyệt đối hóa điều không chắc chắn.
- **Parallel structure:** danh sách 3 items phải cùng dạng ngữ pháp — "reduce costs, improve efficiency, and create jobs."
