## 1. AWL LÀ GÌ VÀ TẠI SAO NÓ QUAN TRỌNG TRONG IELTS?

### 1.1 Academic Word List (AWL) — Tổng quan

**AWL** (Academic Word List) là danh sách **570 từ gốc** (word families) được nhà ngôn ngữ học Averil Coxhead biên soạn năm 2000, dựa trên phân tích hàng triệu từ trong văn bản học thuật từ nhiều lĩnh vực khác nhau.

> **Thực tế:** AWL xuất hiện trong khoảng **10% tổng số từ** trong bất kỳ văn bản học thuật nào. Điều này có nghĩa là khi bạn đọc một bài báo học thuật 1000 từ, khoảng 100 từ sẽ là AWL.

**AWL được chia thành 10 sublists, từ phổ biến nhất (Sublist 1) đến ít phổ biến hơn (Sublist 10).**

| Sublist | Số lượng từ gốc | Mức độ phổ biến |
|---|---|---|
| Sublist 1 | 60 từ | Cực kỳ phổ biến trong mọi văn bản học thuật |
| Sublist 2 | 60 từ | Rất phổ biến |
| Sublist 3 | 60 từ | Phổ biến |
| Sublist 4 | 60 từ | Khá phổ biến |
| Sublist 5 | 60 từ | Thường gặp |
| Sublist 6–10 | 270 từ | Gặp trong một số lĩnh vực cụ thể |

### 1.2 AWL và IELTS

- **IELTS 6.0–6.5:** Cần nắm vững Sublist 1–2
- **IELTS 7.0:** Cần Sublist 1–4
- **IELTS 7.5–8.0:** Cần Sublist 1–5 + collocations chính xác

> **Quan trọng:** Không chỉ học thuộc lòng — hãy học **COLLOCATIONS** (kết hợp từ) và **WORD FAMILIES** (họ từ). Dùng sai collocation còn tệ hơn không dùng AWL.

## 2. AWL NÂNG CAO — SUBLISTS 3–5 (50 TỪ QUAN TRỌNG)

### 2.1 Sublist 3 — Nhóm từ quan trọng

---

**accommodate** /əˈkɒmədeɪt/
- **Word family:** accommodation (N) | accommodate (V) | accommodating (Adj)
- **Collocations:** *accommodate a range of needs / accommodate different learning styles*
- **Example:** *The new policy was designed to accommodate a wider range of socioeconomic circumstances.*

---

**accurate** /ˈækjərɪt/
- **Word family:** accuracy (N) | accurate (Adj) | accurately (Adv) | inaccurate (Adj)
- **Collocations:** *accurate data / accurate representation / provide an accurate account*
- **Example:** *Without accurate data, policymakers risk making decisions based on flawed assumptions.*

---

**acknowledge** /əkˈnɒlɪdʒ/
- **Word family:** acknowledgement (N) | acknowledge (V) | acknowledged (Adj)
- **Collocations:** *acknowledge a problem / widely acknowledged / acknowledge the contribution of*
- **Example:** *It is widely acknowledged that climate change represents one of the most serious threats to global stability.*

---

**adequate** /ˈædɪkwɪt/
- **Word family:** adequacy (N) | adequate (Adj) | adequately (Adv) | inadequate (Adj)
- **Collocations:** *adequate funding / adequate provision / ensure adequate access*
- **Example:** *Many rural communities lack adequate access to healthcare services.*

---

**advocate** /ˈædvəkeɪt/ (V) / /ˈædvəkɪt/ (N)
- **Word family:** advocacy (N) | advocate (V/N)
- **Collocations:** *advocate for change / advocate a policy / a strong advocate of*
- **Example:** *Numerous NGOs actively advocate for stricter environmental regulations at the international level.*

---

**alter** /ˈɔːltə/
- **Word family:** alteration (N) | alter (V) | alternative (N/Adj) | alternatively (Adv)
- **Collocations:** *alter the course of / significantly alter / alter existing policies*
- **Example:** *Globalisation has fundamentally altered the landscape of international trade and labour.*

---

**annual** /ˈænjuəl/
- **Word family:** annual (Adj/N) | annually (Adv)
- **Collocations:** *annual report / annual growth rate / annual income / annual review*
- **Example:** *The organisation's annual report revealed a 12% increase in expenditure on social programmes.*

---

**apparent** /əˈpærənt/
- **Word family:** appearance (N) | apparent (Adj) | apparently (Adv)
- **Collocations:** *it is apparent that / make apparent / apparent contradiction / apparent success*
- **Example:** *Despite the apparent success of the initiative, long-term outcomes remain uncertain.*

---

**attribute** /əˈtrɪbjuːt/ (V) / /ˈætrɪbjuːt/ (N)
- **Word family:** attribution (N) | attribute (V/N) | attributable (Adj)
- **Collocations:** *attribute [effect] to [cause] / attributable to / attribute success to*
- **Example:** *The decline in crime rates can be attributed largely to increased investment in community policing programmes.*

---

**capacity** /kəˈpæsɪti/
- **Word family:** capacity (N) | capable (Adj) | capability (N) | capacitate (V)
- **Collocations:** *capacity to [do] / capacity building / industrial capacity / expand capacity*
- **Example:** *Developing nations often lack the institutional capacity required to implement complex regulatory reforms.*

---

### 2.2 Sublist 4 — Nhóm từ quan trọng

---

**communicate** /kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/
- **Word family:** communication (N) | communicate (V) | communicative (Adj)
- **Collocations:** *communicate effectively / communicate findings / failure to communicate*
- **Example:** *The ability to communicate complex ideas clearly is an essential competency in the modern workplace.*

---

**compensate** /ˈkɒmpənseɪt/
- **Word family:** compensation (N) | compensate (V) | compensatory (Adj)
- **Collocations:** *compensate for a loss / adequate compensation / receive compensation*
- **Example:** *Higher wages in dangerous occupations partially compensate for the additional physical and psychological risks involved.*

---

**considerable** /kənˈsɪdərəbl/
- **Word family:** consider (V) | consideration (N) | considerable (Adj) | considerably (Adv)
- **Collocations:** *considerable evidence / a considerable degree of / considerably higher/lower*
- **Example:** *There is considerable evidence to suggest that early childhood nutrition has lasting effects on cognitive development.*

---

**contrast** /ˈkɒntrɑːst/ (N) / /kənˈtrɑːst/ (V)
- **Word family:** contrast (N/V) | contrasting (Adj) | contrastive (Adj) | in contrast to
- **Collocations:** *in contrast to / contrast sharply with / draw a contrast between*
- **Example:** *In contrast to the rapid urbanisation of Asian cities, rural populations in sub-Saharan Africa remain substantial.*

---

**contribute** /kənˈtrɪbjuːt/
- **Word family:** contribution (N) | contribute (V) | contributor (N) | contributory (Adj)
- **Collocations:** *contribute to [goal/problem] / make a significant contribution / contributory factor*
- **Example:** *Education contributes substantially to social mobility and economic productivity at both individual and national levels.*

---

**debate** /dɪˈbeɪt/
- **Word family:** debate (N/V) | debatable (Adj) | debated (Adj)
- **Collocations:** *ongoing debate / subject of debate / debate the merits of / spark debate*
- **Example:** *The merits of standardised testing remain a subject of considerable debate among educational researchers.*

---

**decline** /dɪˈklaɪn/
- **Word family:** decline (N/V) | declining (Adj)
- **Collocations:** *a marked decline in / declining rates / decline steadily / steep decline*
- **Example:** *A marked decline in biodiversity has been documented across all major ecosystems over the past fifty years.*

---

**distinct** /dɪˈstɪŋkt/
- **Word family:** distinction (N) | distinct (Adj) | distinctly (Adv) | distinctive (Adj) | distinguish (V)
- **Collocations:** *a distinct advantage / draw a distinction between / clearly distinct from*
- **Example:** *There is a distinct advantage to early language acquisition, particularly with respect to phonological accuracy.*

---

**enhance** /ɪnˈhɑːns/
- **Word family:** enhancement (N) | enhance (V) | enhanced (Adj)
- **Collocations:** *enhance performance / significantly enhance / enhance the quality of*
- **Example:** *Investment in professional development programmes has been shown to significantly enhance employee productivity and job satisfaction.*

---

**facilitate** /fəˈsɪlɪteɪt/
- **Word family:** facilitation (N) | facilitate (V) | facilitator (N)
- **Collocations:** *facilitate access to / facilitate communication / facilitate the process of*
- **Example:** *Digital infrastructure facilitates access to educational resources for students in geographically remote communities.*

### 2.3 Sublist 5 — Nhóm từ quan trọng

---

**formulate** /ˈfɔːmjʊleɪt/
- **Word family:** formulation (N) | formulate (V)
- **Collocations:** *formulate a policy / formulate a hypothesis / carefully formulated*
- **Example:** *The committee was tasked with formulating a comprehensive response to the emerging public health crisis.*

---

**generate** /ˈdʒenəreɪt/
- **Word family:** generation (N) | generate (V) | generative (Adj)
- **Collocations:** *generate revenue / generate employment / generate debate / generate data*
- **Example:** *The renewable energy sector has the potential to generate over 24 million jobs globally by 2030.*

---

**hypothesis** /haɪˈpɒθɪsɪs/
- **Word family:** hypothesis (N, pl: hypotheses) | hypothesise (V) | hypothetical (Adj) | hypothetically (Adv)
- **Collocations:** *test a hypothesis / propose a hypothesis / support/refute a hypothesis*

---

**implement** /ˈɪmplɪment/
- **Word family:** implementation (N) | implement (V)
- **Collocations:** *implement a policy / implement reforms / successful implementation*

---

**indicate** /ˈɪndɪkeɪt/

## 3. THAY THẾ TỪ ĐƠN GIẢN BẰNG AWL

### 3.1 Bảng thay thế từ đơn giản → AWL

| Từ đơn giản | AWL thay thế | Ví dụ AWL trong câu |
|---|---|---|
| **show** | demonstrate / indicate / illustrate | *The data demonstrates a clear correlation between poverty and low educational attainment.* |
| **use** | utilise / employ / apply | *Researchers employed a mixed-methods approach to analyse the data.* |
| **think** | consider / argue / suggest | *Some scholars argue that globalisation has widened rather than narrowed economic disparities.* |
| **big / large** | significant / substantial / considerable | *The study identified a substantial increase in urban migration over the study period.* |
| **small** | minimal / marginal / negligible | *The effect of the intervention on overall outcomes was marginal at best.* |
| **need** | require / necessitate | *Effective climate policy necessitates international cooperation at an unprecedented scale.* |
| **help** | facilitate / enable / assist | *Microfinance programmes enable small business owners to access capital without traditional collateral.* |
| **change** | alter / modify / transform | *Digital technology has transformed the way in which knowledge is produced and disseminated.* |
| **find** | establish / identify / reveal | *The investigation revealed systemic failures in the regulatory framework.* |
| **important** | significant / crucial / vital / paramount | *It is paramount that all relevant stakeholders are consulted during the policy formulation process.* |
| **start** | initiate / commence / establish | *The government initiated a series of sweeping reforms to address public health inequalities.* |
| **end** | conclude / terminate / cease | *The study concluded that access to clean water remains the most critical determinant of child health outcomes.* |
| **make** | generate / produce / create / formulate | *The report generated considerable debate within both academic and policy communities.* |
| **look at** | examine / analyse / investigate | *This essay examines the relationship between income inequality and social mobility.* |

## 4. AWL COLLOCATIONS — CÁC KẾT HỢP QUAN TRỌNG NHẤT

### 4.1 Verb + Noun Academic Collocations

```
RESEARCH COLLOCATIONS:
  conduct research (into/on)       ✅    do research               ❌ (too informal)
  carry out an analysis (of)       ✅    make an analysis          ❌
  draw a conclusion (from)         ✅    make a conclusion         ❌
  test a hypothesis                ✅    prove a hypothesis        ❌ (too absolute)

FINDING COLLOCATIONS:
  establish a link (between)       ✅    find a link               ❌ (too simple)
  identify a pattern               ✅    see a pattern             ❌
  demonstrate a trend              ✅    show a trend              ❌ (too informal)
  reveal significant differences   ✅    find big differences      ❌

ARGUMENT COLLOCATIONS:
  challenge assumptions            ✅    question assumptions      ✓ (acceptable)
  address issues/concerns          ✅    deal with issues          ❌ (informal)
  highlight concerns               ✅    show concerns             ❌
  question the validity of         ✅    doubt something           ❌

POLICY COLLOCATIONS:
  implement policies               ✅    put policies              ❌
  adopt an approach                ✅    use an approach           ❌ (too simple)
  evaluate outcomes                ✅    check results             ❌
  advocate for reform              ✅    want reform               ❌
```

---

### 4.2 Các cụm học thuật cao cấp theo loại

**Verbs for introducing research:**
- *The study seeks to / aims to / endeavours to + [infinitive]*
- *This research investigates / explores / examines + [noun phrase]*
- *The data suggests / indicates / reveals + [that-clause]*

**Verbs for presenting findings:**
- *The findings demonstrate / confirm / corroborate + [that-clause]*
- *Analysis reveals a significant / positive / negative correlation between...*
- *The results are consistent with / contrary to / in line with + previous research*

**Verbs for evaluation:**
- *The approach adopted has both strengths and limitations.*
- *One notable limitation of this study is...*
- *These findings should be interpreted with caution, as...*

## 5. AWL TRONG NGỮ CẢNH — ĐỌC VÀ NHẬN DIỆN AWL

### 5.1 Đoạn học thuật có đánh dấu AWL

Đọc đoạn sau và chú ý các từ in đậm — đây đều là AWL words:

> *The **relationship** between **economic** development and **environmental** sustainability has **generated** considerable **debate** among **researchers** and policymakers alike. While **advocates** of **unrestricted** growth **argue** that **increased** productivity ultimately **benefits** all sectors of society, a **significant** body of **evidence** **indicates** that this **assumption** fails to **account** for the **cumulative** environmental costs **associated** with **industrial** expansion. **Furthermore**, the **benefits** of growth have proven **considerably** unequal in **distribution**, with **vulnerable** populations disproportionately **exposed** to pollution and resource **depletion**. It is therefore **apparent** that **sustainable** development frameworks must **acknowledge** both ecological limits and social equity concerns if **genuine** progress is to be **achieved**.*

**Bài tập — Sau khi đọc, trả lời:**
1. Đếm có bao nhiêu AWL words trong đoạn văn trên?
2. Chọn 5 từ in đậm và viết các dạng từ khác trong họ từ (word family) của chúng.
3. Dùng 3 AWL words từ đoạn trên để viết câu mới.

### 5.2 Cách đoán nghĩa AWL từ ngữ cảnh

Khi gặp từ AWL không biết, hãy theo các bước:

**Bước 1:** Xác định loại từ (noun/verb/adj/adv) từ vị trí trong câu
**Bước 2:** Đọc toàn câu/đoạn để hiểu ngữ cảnh
**Bước 3:** Tìm clues trong tiền tố/hậu tố:
- *-tion, -sion* → thường là noun
- *-ify, -ise, -ate* → thường là verb
- *-ive, -al, -ous* → thường là adjective
- *-ly* → thường là adverb

**Ví dụ:**
> *"The **proliferation** of digital devices has outpaced the development of appropriate regulatory frameworks."*

- *proliferation* kết thúc bằng *-tion* → noun
- Ngữ cảnh: "has outpaced" = đã vượt qua → *proliferation* phải là thứ đang phát triển
- Nghĩa: **sự phát triển/phổ biến nhanh chóng** *(rapid spread)*

## 6. CHIẾN LƯỢC TỪ VỰNG IELTS BAND 7+

### 6.1 Ba nguyên tắc vàng

**Nguyên tắc 1: Kết hợp AWL + Collocations + Từ chính xác**

Không chỉ dùng từ AWL — hãy dùng đúng collocation:
- ✗ *"The research made a conclusion that..."* → sai collocation
- ✓ *"The research drew the conclusion that..."* → đúng collocation

**Nguyên tắc 2: Tránh lặp từ — dùng synonyms**

Đừng dùng cùng một từ quá 2 lần trong bài:
- Lần 1: *"The data indicates..."*
- Lần 2: *"These findings demonstrate..."*
- Lần 3: *"The evidence suggests..."*

**Nguyên tắc 3: Không dùng AWL sai nghĩa**

Nguy hiểm nhất là dùng AWL sai collocation:
- ✗ *"The problem was very significant"* → "very + significant" là redundant; "significant" đã ngầm mang nghĩa "large in scale"
- ✓ *"The problem represented a significant challenge"* → "significant + challenge" là collocation tự nhiên

### 6.2 Bảng từ vựng học thuật hay nhầm lẫn

| Từ | Nghĩa đúng | Cách dùng sai thường gặp | Cách dùng đúng |
|---|---|---|---|
| *significant* | đáng kể, quan trọng (về quy mô) | *"very significant"* (redundant) | *"a significant increase / significant evidence"* |
| *considerable* | đáng kể (về lượng/mức độ) | *"a considerable problem"* | *"considerable evidence / a considerable number"* |
| *facilitate* | tạo điều kiện, hỗ trợ | *"facilitate someone to do"* | *"facilitate access / facilitate the process"* |
| *implement* | triển khai, thực hiện (chính sách) | *"implement an idea"* | *"implement a policy / implement reforms"* |
| *indicate* | chỉ ra, gợi ý (không chắc chắn 100%) | Dùng thay "prove" | *"data indicates..." (not "proves")* |

### 6.3 Cách xây dựng AWL Vocabulary Notebook cá nhân

Mỗi từ AWL mới, ghi theo format:

```
Từ: facilitate
IPA: /fəˈsɪlɪteɪt/
Loại từ: Verb
Word family: facilitation (N), facilitator (N)
Collocations: facilitate access to / facilitate communication / facilitate the process of
Synonym: enable, assist, promote
Câu ví dụ: Digital technology facilitates access to educational resources for students in remote areas.
Sử dụng lần đầu: [ngày]
```

## LỖI THƯỜNG GẶP

| Sai | Đúng | Giải thích |
|---|---|---|
| The research made a conclusion that... | The research drew the conclusion that... | "Draw a conclusion" là collocation đúng — "make a conclusion" là sai collocation phổ biến. |
| The policy facilitates people to access healthcare. | The policy facilitates access to healthcare. | "Facilitate" đi với noun phrase, không đi với "someone to do something". |
| The data proves that pollution causes illness. | The data indicates/suggests that pollution is linked to illness. | "Indicate/suggest" phù hợp hơn "prove" trong academic writing — giữ hedging chính xác. |
| The experiment was very significant. | The experiment yielded significant results. | "Very significant" là redundant — "significant" đã hàm nghĩa đáng kể. Sửa collocation: "significant results/findings". |
| They implemented the idea of using solar panels. | They implemented a policy/strategy of using solar panels. | "Implement" đi với "policy, reforms, strategy, programme" — không đi với "idea". |
| attribute /əˈtrɪbjuːt/ (khi dùng làm noun) | attribute /ˈætrɪbjuːt/ (N) vs. /əˈtrɪbjuːt/ (V) | Nhiều AWL words có hai cách phát âm khác nhau theo loại từ — cần chú ý stress placement. |
| The study done by researchers found... | The study conducted by researchers found... | "Conduct research/a study" là collocation học thuật; "do research" là informal. |
| She utilised her time very efficiently. | She utilised her time efficiently. | "Utilised + efficiently" là collocation tự nhiên; thêm "very" trước adverb không sai nhưng thường là redundant. |

---

## TÓM TẮT NHANH

- **AWL = 10% của academic text:** Nắm Sublist 1–5 là đủ cho IELTS 7.0+; học theo word families, không chỉ từ đơn lẻ.
- **Học theo collocation, không chỉ học nghĩa:** "conduct research" (không phải "do research"), "draw a conclusion" (không phải "make"), "implement a policy" (không phải "implement an idea").
- **Word families:** Mỗi AWL word có nhiều dạng — contribute → contribution → contributory → contributor — dùng đúng dạng theo chức năng trong câu.
- **Thay thế từ đơn giản bằng AWL:** show → demonstrate/indicate; big → significant/substantial; need → require/necessitate; change → alter/transform.
- **Tránh redundancy với AWL:** Không thêm "very/extremely" trước AWL đã mang nghĩa mạnh — significant, considerable, substantial đã đủ mạnh.
- **Hedging vẫn cần thiết dù dùng AWL:** "The data indicates..." (không phải "proves") — AWL nâng register nhưng không loại bỏ yêu cầu hedging.
