## SECTION C: AWL COLLOCATIONS — CHOOSE THE CORRECT VERB (20 điểm — 2 điểm/item)

**Hướng dẫn:** Khoanh tròn động từ phù hợp nhất với collocation học thuật chuẩn.

---

**1.** Scientists _______ research into the long-term effects of microplastics on marine ecosystems.
> a) **conduct** &emsp; b) make &emsp; c) do

---

**2.** The study _______ a significant correlation between income levels and access to higher education.
> a) showed &emsp; b) **demonstrated** &emsp; c) found out

---

**3.** The new legislation _______ smaller businesses to compete more effectively in international markets.
> a) allowed &emsp; b) permitted &emsp; c) **enabled**

---

**4.** The committee will _______ a conclusion once all submitted evidence has been reviewed.
> a) make &emsp; b) **draw** &emsp; c) arrive

---

**5.** Local governments must _______ the issue of inadequate public transport infrastructure urgently.
> a) deal with &emsp; b) handle &emsp; c) **address**

---

**6.** The research team aims to _______ the effectiveness of the new intervention programme.
> a) check &emsp; b) **evaluate** &emsp; c) measure up

---

**7.** Scholars have long _______ the assumption that economic growth inevitably reduces social inequality.
> a) **challenged** &emsp; b) questioned &emsp; c) doubted

---

**8.** The organisation was founded with a mission to _______ equal access to legal representation.
> a) **advocate for** &emsp; b) support &emsp; c) push for

---

**9.** The increase in urban migration has _______ considerable strain on housing supply in major cities.
> a) caused &emsp; b) created &emsp; c) **placed**

---

**10.** A growing body of evidence _______ that sleep deprivation impairs decision-making capacity.
> a) **suggests** &emsp; b) says &emsp; c) tells

### SECTION C — Đáp án:

1. **a) conduct** — *conduct research* là collocation chuẩn học thuật
2. **b) demonstrated** — *demonstrate a correlation* là collocation học thuật; *find* chấp nhận được nhưng *demonstrated* trang trọng hơn
3. **c) enabled** — *enable [someone] to* là collocation đúng
4. **b) draw** — *draw a conclusion* là collocation cố định; không dùng *make a conclusion*
5. **c) address** — *address an issue* là collocation học thuật chuẩn
6. **b) evaluate** — *evaluate the effectiveness* là AWL collocation đúng
7. **a) challenged** — *challenge an assumption* là collocation học thuật cao
8. **a) advocate for** — *advocate for equal access* là collocation chuẩn
9. **c) placed** — *place strain on* là collocation cố định; không dùng *create strain*
10. **a) suggests** — *evidence suggests* = hedging phù hợp; *says/tells* quá informal

### SECTION E — Bài mẫu Body Paragraph (Band 7–8):

> **[P]** Education represents perhaps the most **sustainable** and evidence-based mechanism for **addressing** **economic** **inequality** at both individual and societal levels.
> **[E]** A longitudinal study **conducted** by the OECD (2021) **indicates** that each additional year of secondary education is **associated** with a 9% **increase** in individual lifetime earnings, an **outcome** that **contributes** significantly to **reducing** intergenerational poverty.
> **[E — Explain]** This **considerable** **impact** can be **attributed** to the fact that education **equips** individuals with the skills **required** to **participate** in higher-value sectors of the economy, thereby expanding their **potential** for upward mobility.
> **[L]** It is therefore **apparent** that sustained **investment** in educational access is not merely a social priority but an economic **imperative**.
