## BẢNG AWL NÂNG CAO — SUBLISTS 3–5

**Cách dùng bảng:**
- **Word:** từ gốc (root form)
- **/IPA/:** phiên âm
- **Word Family:** các dạng từ quan trọng (N = Noun, V = Verb, Adj = Adjective, Adv = Adverb)
- **Key Collocation:** kết hợp từ hay gặp nhất
- **Example:** câu ví dụ học thuật

### Exercise 2: Fill in AWL Word in Context (10 items)

**Hướng dẫn:** Chọn từ đúng từ hộp và điền vào chỗ trống. Dùng dạng từ phù hợp.

**Hộp từ:**
> *acknowledge / adequate / facilitate / decline / considerable / indicate / sustainable / contribute / evaluate / exclude*

1. Recent data _________________ that literacy rates have improved significantly across the region over the past decade.

2. Critics argue that current minimum wage levels are not _________________ to cover basic living costs in major cities.

3. The study found that a _________________ proportion of participants reported experiencing workplace stress.

4. It is vital to _________________ the long-term outcomes of any policy before committing public resources.

5. The government must _________________ that systematic inequalities exist within the current healthcare system.

6. Investment in digital infrastructure is intended to _________________ broader participation in the knowledge economy.

7. A sharp _________________ in biodiversity has been observed across all major tropical ecosystems.

8. Several vulnerable groups risk being _________________ from the benefits of technological innovation.

9. Renewable energy, if properly managed, can _________________ to both environmental and economic goals simultaneously.

10. Development that meets current needs without compromising future generations is described as _________________.

**Đáp án:**
1. indicate | 2. adequate | 3. considerable | 4. evaluate | 5. acknowledge
6. facilitate | 7. decline | 8. excluded | 9. contribute | 10. sustainable
