## BÀI TẬP TỪ VỰNG

### Bài tập 1: Chọn từ đúng ngữ cảnh

Chọn từ phù hợp nhất để hoàn thành các câu chẻ:

1. It is ______________ (spurious / speculative / pivotal) correlations that most undermine the credibility of poorly designed studies.

2. What the evidence ______________ (corroborates / circumvents / precipitates) is that early intervention is the most cost-effective strategy.

3. It is at the ______________ (nexus / archetype / edifice) of economic policy and social welfare where the most contentious debates occur.

4. What most populist movements ______________ (refute / elicit / engender) is a profound sense of distrust in mainstream institutions.

5. It is the ______________ (reciprocal / extrinsic / binary) nature of the relationship between education and economic development that makes disinvestment so costly.

**Đáp án:** 1. spurious  2. corroborates  3. nexus  4. engender  5. reciprocal

---

### Bài tập 2: Tạo câu chẻ với từ vựng

Dùng các từ sau để tạo IT-cleft hoặc WH-cleft:

1. *catalyst / the 2008 financial crisis / widespread regulatory reform*
→ IT-cleft: It was _______________________________________________

2. *perpetuate / outdated educational systems / intellectual stagnation*
→ WH-cleft: What outdated educational systems _______________________________________________

3. *intrinsic / the value of biodiversity / conservation efforts*
→ Reversed WH-cleft: The intrinsic value _______________________________________________

4. *impede / excessive bureaucracy / entrepreneurial innovation*
→ IT-cleft: It is _______________________________________________

5. *ramification / policymakers consistently underestimate / long-term*
→ WH-cleft: What policymakers consistently underestimate _______________________________________________

**Gợi ý đáp án:**
1. It was the 2008 financial crisis that served as the catalyst for widespread regulatory reform.
2. What outdated educational systems perpetuate is intellectual stagnation and resistance to change.
3. The intrinsic value of biodiversity is what conservation efforts seek to protect.
4. It is excessive bureaucracy that most impedes entrepreneurial innovation.
5. What policymakers consistently underestimate are the long-term ramifications of short-term decisions.

### Bài tập 3: Phân tích câu chẻ trong đoạn văn

Đọc đoạn văn sau và xác định: (a) loại câu chẻ, (b) phần được nhấn mạnh, (c) mục đích tu từ.

> *"What the twenty-first century demands of its citizens is not merely technical competence but adaptability. It is not the accumulation of knowledge per se but the capacity to critically evaluate information that modern education must prioritise. What many educational systems currently produce is graduates who are well-informed yet poorly equipped for the ambiguity of real-world decision-making. A fundamental reimagining of pedagogical purpose is what the evidence consistently supports."*

**Phân tích:**
1. Câu 1: _____________ loại, nhấn mạnh _____________
2. Câu 2: _____________ loại, nhấn mạnh _____________
3. Câu 3: _____________ loại, nhấn mạnh _____________
4. Câu 4: _____________ loại, nhấn mạnh _____________

**Đáp án:**
1. WH-cleft, nhấn mạnh: "not merely technical competence but adaptability"
2. IT-cleft với NOT...BUT, nhấn mạnh: "the capacity to critically evaluate information"
3. WH-cleft, nhấn mạnh: "graduates who are well-informed yet poorly equipped"
4. Reversed WH-cleft, nhấn mạnh: "A fundamental reimagining of pedagogical purpose"
