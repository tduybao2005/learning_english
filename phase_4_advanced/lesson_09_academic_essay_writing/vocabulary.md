## PHẦN 1: BẢNG 80 CỤM TỪ HỌC THUẬT THEO CHỨC NĂNG

### Nhóm 1: HOOK OPENERS — Câu mở đầu gây ấn tượng

| # | Cụm từ | Chức năng | Register | Ví dụ trong ngữ cảnh |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | *In recent years, there has been a marked increase in...* | Mở đầu bằng xu hướng thay đổi | Formal | *In recent years, there has been a marked increase in the adoption of remote working arrangements globally.* |
| 2 | *The rapid proliferation of... has fundamentally altered...* | Nhấn mạnh tốc độ thay đổi | High formal | *The rapid proliferation of smartphones has fundamentally altered patterns of social interaction.* |
| 3 | *Few issues in contemporary society provoke more debate than...* | Mở đầu bằng sự tranh cãi | Formal | *Few issues in contemporary society provoke more debate than the ethics of artificial intelligence.* |
| 4 | *[Statistic]. This figure underscores the urgency of...* | Dùng số liệu làm hook | Formal | *Over 800 million people worldwide live in extreme poverty. This figure underscores the urgency of equitable economic development.* |
| 5 | *It has become increasingly apparent that...* | Mở đầu bằng xu hướng nhận thức | Formal | *It has become increasingly apparent that traditional educational models are ill-equipped for the demands of the 21st century.* |
| 6 | *The question of whether... has long been a subject of scholarly debate.* | Mở đầu học thuật | High formal | *The question of whether capital punishment serves as an effective deterrent has long been a subject of scholarly debate.* |
| 7 | *At a time when... the issue of... has never been more pressing.* | Liên kết thời cuộc với vấn đề | Formal | *At a time when climate change threatens global ecosystems, the issue of carbon taxation has never been more pressing.* |
| 8 | *[Topic] lies at the heart of one of the most pressing challenges facing...* | Nêu tầm quan trọng của chủ đề | Formal | *Education lies at the heart of one of the most pressing challenges facing developing nations today.* |
| 9 | *Despite widespread consensus that..., the reality is far more complex.* | Đặt vấn đề nghịch lý | High formal | *Despite widespread consensus that technology improves productivity, the reality is far more complex and context-dependent.* |
| 10 | *The relationship between... and... is one that merits careful examination.* | Giới thiệu mối quan hệ cần phân tích | Formal | *The relationship between economic growth and environmental degradation is one that merits careful examination.* |

### Nhóm 2: THESIS STATEMENT LANGUAGE — Ngôn ngữ cho luận điểm chính

| # | Cụm từ | Chức năng | Register | Ví dụ trong ngữ cảnh |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 11 | *This essay contends that...* | Đưa ra lập trường trực tiếp | Formal | *This essay contends that renewable energy subsidies represent a necessary investment rather than an unnecessary expense.* |
| 12 | *It is argued herein that...* | Lập trường học thuật, trang trọng | High formal | *It is argued herein that universal basic income would reduce inequality without diminishing workforce motivation.* |
| 13 | *While... has clear merit, ... is ultimately more compelling because...* | Thừa nhận + lập trường | Formal | *While economic growth has clear merit, sustainable development is ultimately more compelling because it ensures long-term viability.* |
| 14 | *Although [concession], [position] because [reason].* | Cấu trúc nhượng bộ cổ điển | Formal | *Although automation displaces certain jobs, it ultimately creates greater economic opportunity because it stimulates innovation and productivity.* |
| 15 | *The central argument of this essay is that...* | Rõ ràng, trực tiếp | Formal | *The central argument of this essay is that stricter internet regulation is essential to protect democratic institutions.* |
| 16 | *This essay will demonstrate that... by examining...* | Preview thesis + structure | Formal | *This essay will demonstrate that urban farming is a viable solution to food insecurity by examining economic, environmental and social factors.* |
| 17 | *The position adopted here is that...* | Lập trường cá nhân nhưng học thuật | Formal | *The position adopted here is that the benefits of globalisation are unevenly distributed and demand policy intervention.* |
| 18 | *It is the contention of this essay that...* | Biến thể trang trọng của thesis | High formal | *It is the contention of this essay that social media, despite its communicative value, exacerbates societal polarisation.* |
| 19 | *[Topic], it will be argued, fundamentally [claim].* | Kiểu thesis đặt giữa câu | Formal | *Digital technology, it will be argued, fundamentally reshapes how knowledge is produced and consumed.* |
| 20 | *On balance, [your position] appears to be the more defensible view.* | Kết luận sau khi cân nhắc | Formal | *On balance, stricter gun control legislation appears to be the more defensible view given current evidence.* |

### Nhóm 3: ADDING POINTS IN BODY — Thêm ý trong thân bài

| # | Cụm từ | Chức năng | Register | Ví dụ trong ngữ cảnh |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 21 | *Furthermore, it is worth noting that...* | Thêm điểm quan trọng | Formal | *Furthermore, it is worth noting that the long-term economic costs of climate inaction far exceed those of preventive measures.* |
| 22 | *An equally compelling consideration is...* | Thêm điểm ngang tầm | Formal | *An equally compelling consideration is the psychological impact of unemployment on individuals and communities.* |
| 23 | *Beyond this, there is substantial evidence to suggest that...* | Mở rộng luận điểm | Formal | *Beyond this, there is substantial evidence to suggest that early childhood education reduces crime rates in adulthood.* |
| 24 | *This is further corroborated by...* | Bổ sung bằng chứng | Formal | *This is further corroborated by a meta-analysis of 47 studies conducted by Oxford University.* |
| 25 | *A second, arguably more significant, factor is...* | Thêm điểm có sức nặng hơn | Formal | *A second, arguably more significant, factor is the role of parental involvement in educational attainment.* |
| 26 | *Compounding this issue is the fact that...* | Thêm vấn đề làm phức tạp thêm | Formal | *Compounding this issue is the fact that healthcare resources are disproportionately concentrated in urban areas.* |
| 27 | *Of equal, if not greater, significance is...* | So sánh tầm quan trọng | High formal | *Of equal, if not greater, significance is the impact of air pollution on childhood cognitive development.* |
| 28 | *It is also important to acknowledge that...* | Nhận nhận điểm bổ sung | Formal | *It is also important to acknowledge that migration, while presenting challenges, contributes substantially to economic growth.* |
| 29 | *Moreover, one cannot overlook the role of...* | Nhấn mạnh điểm bị bỏ qua | Formal | *Moreover, one cannot overlook the role of media literacy in combating the spread of misinformation.* |
| 30 | *Adding to this complexity is...* | Thêm yếu tố phức tạp | Formal | *Adding to this complexity is the widespread variation in cultural attitudes toward gender equality.* |

|---|---|---|---|---|

| 32 | *This is perhaps best illustrated by...* | Ví dụ minh hoạ tốt nhất | Formal | *This is perhaps best illustrated by the case of Finland, where educational investment consistently yields world-leading outcomes.* |
| 33 | *To illustrate this point, consider...* | Mời đọc xem xét ví dụ | Formal | *To illustrate this point, consider the experience of Germany, which successfully integrated dual vocational training into its national curriculum.* |
| 34 | *Evidence of this can be observed in...* | Dẫn bằng chứng quan sát được | Formal | *Evidence of this can be observed in the declining literacy rates among children who lack access to early educational support.* |
| 35 | *The case of [country/company/person] provides a compelling illustration of...* | Ví dụ case study | Formal | *The case of Norway provides a compelling illustration of how resource wealth can be managed sustainably for future generations.* |
| 36 | *[Statistic] offers a clear indication of...* | Dùng số liệu làm ví dụ | Formal | *A 40% reduction in carbon emissions over two decades offers a clear indication of what determined policy can achieve.* |
| 37 | *This phenomenon is exemplified by...* | Hiện tượng được thể hiện qua | Formal | *This phenomenon is exemplified by the rapid urbanisation of Southeast Asian cities over the past three decades.* |

| 40 | *A comparative analysis of [X] and [Y] reveals that...* | So sánh hai ví dụ | Formal | *A comparative analysis of urban and rural healthcare access reveals that disparities remain significant despite policy efforts.* |

### Nhóm 5: CONTRASTING / CONCEDING — Tương phản và nhượng bộ

| # | Cụm từ | Chức năng | Register | Ví dụ trong ngữ cảnh |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 41 | *While it is true that..., this does not necessarily mean...* | Nhượng bộ rồi phản bác | Formal | *While it is true that nuclear energy produces minimal carbon emissions, this does not necessarily mean it represents an entirely safe option.* |
| 42 | *Admittedly,...; however, this overlooks the fact that...* | Thừa nhận rồi chỉ ra điều bị bỏ qua | Formal | *Admittedly, immigration places pressure on public services; however, this overlooks the significant fiscal contributions that migrants make.* |
| 43 | *Proponents of [X] argue that...; yet this position fails to account for...* | Nêu quan điểm đối lập rồi phản bác | High formal | *Proponents of free-market economics argue that deregulation stimulates growth; yet this position fails to account for the resulting increase in inequality.* |
| 44 | *It would be misleading to suggest that...* | Bác bỏ quan điểm quá đơn giản | Formal | *It would be misleading to suggest that access to the internet alone is sufficient to bridge the digital divide.* |
| 45 | *Despite the apparent merits of..., a closer examination reveals...* | Nhượng bộ rồi phân tích sâu hơn | Formal | *Despite the apparent merits of standardised testing, a closer examination reveals its disproportionate impact on disadvantaged students.* |
| 46 | *Although [X] presents certain advantages, these are outweighed by...* | So sánh lợi và hại | Formal | *Although flexible working presents certain advantages, these are outweighed by the challenge of maintaining team cohesion.* |
| 47 | *It is tempting to conclude that...; however, the evidence suggests otherwise.* | Lật ngược kết luận vội vã | Formal | *It is tempting to conclude that economic growth inevitably reduces poverty; however, the evidence suggests otherwise in highly unequal societies.* |
| 48 | *Whilst acknowledging [X], it must be stressed that [Y] remains paramount.* | Thừa nhận nhưng nhấn mạnh điều quan trọng hơn | High formal | *Whilst acknowledging the value of tradition, it must be stressed that institutional reform remains paramount for social progress.* |
| 49 | *The counterargument that... carries some weight; nevertheless,...* | Thừa nhận sức nặng của phản bác | Formal | *The counterargument that social media fosters community carries some weight; nevertheless, its role in spreading misinformation cannot be understated.* |
| 50 | *Critics may contend that...; this objection, however, does not withstand scrutiny because...* | Bác bỏ phê bình | High formal | *Critics may contend that renewable energy is economically unfeasible; this objection, however, does not withstand scrutiny given recent cost reductions.* |

### Nhóm 6: CAUSE AND EFFECT — Nguyên nhân và kết quả

| # | Cụm từ | Chức năng | Register | Ví dụ trong ngữ cảnh |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 51 | *As a direct consequence of..., [result].* | Hệ quả trực tiếp | Formal | *As a direct consequence of rapid industrialisation, air quality in many Asian cities has deteriorated markedly.* |
| 52 | *This has led to a significant increase/decrease in...* | Kết quả là gia tăng/giảm | Formal | *This has led to a significant decrease in the affordability of housing in major urban centres.* |
| 53 | *A contributing factor to [issue] is...* | Yếu tố góp phần vào | Formal | *A contributing factor to youth unemployment is the mismatch between educational curricula and labour market demands.* |
| 54 | *The [effect] can be attributed largely to...* | Nguyên nhân chính của kết quả | Formal | *The rising incidence of lifestyle diseases can be attributed largely to increasingly sedentary work patterns.* |
| 55 | *[X] has given rise to...* | X đã tạo ra | Formal | *The widespread adoption of e-commerce has given rise to a fundamental restructuring of retail employment.* |
| 56 | *The ramifications of [X] extend beyond...* | Hệ quả lan rộng hơn | Formal | *The ramifications of inadequate early education extend beyond individual outcomes to affect national economic productivity.* |
| 57 | *This, in turn, triggers/generates...* | Chuỗi nhân quả | Formal | *Poverty reduces access to education, which, in turn, generates cycles of economic disadvantage across generations.* |
| 58 | *The knock-on effect of [X] is that...* | Hiệu ứng domino | Semi-formal | *The knock-on effect of poor nutrition in early childhood is that cognitive development is compromised in later years.* |

| 60 | *The cumulative effect of [X] and [Y] has been...* | Hiệu ứng tích lũy | Formal | *The cumulative effect of deforestation and industrial emissions has been an acceleration of global warming beyond predicted rates.* |

| 66 | *[X] tends to/appears to...* | Có xu hướng / có vẻ | Formal | *Students who receive personalised instruction tend to demonstrate greater academic progress than those in traditional settings.* |

## PHẦN 2: BÀI TẬP THỰC HÀNH

### Exercise 1: Xác định chức năng của cụm từ (10 items)

**Hướng dẫn:** Đọc mỗi câu và xác định chức năng của cụm từ in đậm. Chọn từ: *Hook Opener / Thesis Statement / Adding Point / Giving Example / Contrasting / Cause-Effect / Hedging / Conclusion*

1. "**A striking example of this can be seen in** the Norwegian pension fund model, which has successfully diversified the country's resource wealth."
2. "**This essay contends that** stricter data privacy legislation is essential to protect individual rights in the digital age."
3. "**Evidence suggests that** prolonged exposure to social media may correlate with increased levels of anxiety in adolescents."
4. "**The rapid proliferation of** ride-sharing applications **has fundamentally altered** urban transportation patterns across the globe."
5. "**While it is true that** globalisation has lifted millions out of poverty, **this does not necessarily mean** its benefits have been equitably distributed."
6. "**Furthermore, it is worth noting that** the economic cost of preventable diseases places considerable strain on national healthcare systems."
7. "**As a direct consequence of** inadequate government investment in public transport, car ownership has increased dramatically in developing cities."
8. "**In light of the evidence presented above, it can be concluded that** a mixed economy offers the most viable path to sustainable development."
9. "**Few issues in contemporary society provoke more debate than** the ethical implications of genetic engineering."
10. "**This may be attributed to** the lack of culturally responsive pedagogical approaches in mainstream education systems."

**Đáp án:**
1. Giving Example | 2. Thesis Statement | 3. Hedging | 4. Hook Opener
5. Contrasting/Conceding | 6. Adding Point | 7. Cause-Effect | 8. Conclusion
9. Hook Opener | 10. Hedging

---

### Exercise 2: Hoàn thành câu với cụm từ học thuật phù hợp (8 items)

**Hướng dẫn:** Điền cụm từ học thuật phù hợp vào chỗ trống. Có nhiều đáp án chấp nhận được.

1. ________________________ that the current education system is failing to prepare students for the demands of the knowledge economy. *(Hint: Thesis statement opener)*

2. ________________________ the long-term economic benefits of investment in renewable energy. *(Hint: Add a point)*

3. ________________________ Denmark's universal childcare model, which has achieved some of the highest rates of female workforce participation in the world. *(Hint: Give an example)*

4. ________________________ urban living reduces social isolation, ________________________ the evidence suggests higher rates of loneliness in cities than in rural communities. *(Hint: Contrast/Concede)*

5. ________________________ mass tourism has devastated many natural environments and traditional cultures. *(Hint: Cause-effect)*

6. ________________________ the introduction of a four-day working week would improve employee wellbeing ________________________ productivity. *(Hint: Hedging)*

7. ________________________, adequate investment in early childhood education is perhaps the single most cost-effective intervention available to governments. *(Hint: Conclusion)*

8. ________________________ increasing urbanisation, the demand for affordable housing has outpaced supply in virtually every major global city. *(Hint: Cause-effect)*

**Đáp án mẫu:**
1. *It is widely argued / This essay contends*
2. *An equally compelling consideration is / Furthermore, it is worth noting*
3. *A striking example of this can be seen in / This is perhaps best illustrated by*
4. *While it is true that..., / Admittedly...; however,*
5. *The rapid growth of / [X] has given rise to the destruction of*
6. *It seems plausible that / Evidence suggests that...and boost*
7. *In light of the foregoing analysis / Ultimately, the weight of evidence suggests*
8. *As a direct consequence of*

### Exercise 3: Cải thiện 5 câu văn không chính thức thành văn học thuật

**Hướng dẫn:** Viết lại các câu dưới đây theo văn phong học thuật. Sử dụng cụm từ từ bảng vocabulary ở trên.

**1. Câu gốc (informal):**
> "Lots of people are worried about climate change these days."

**Câu học thuật của bạn:** _______________

**Mẫu đáp án:**
> *There is growing public concern regarding climate change, with recent surveys indicating that environmental issues now rank among the foremost anxieties of citizens across the developed world.*

---

**2. Câu gốc (informal):**
> "I think technology is making kids less smart because they don't have to think for themselves."

**Câu học thuật của bạn:** _______________

**Mẫu đáp án:**
> *It has been argued that the pervasive use of digital technology may undermine the development of critical thinking skills in young people, as immediate access to information reduces the necessity for independent reasoning.*

---

**3. Câu gốc (informal):**
> "Countries should do more to help the environment."

**Câu học thuật của bạn:** _______________

**Mẫu đáp án:**
> *It is therefore imperative that governments implement more ambitious environmental legislation, including binding emissions targets and substantial investment in renewable energy infrastructure.*

---

**4. Câu gốc (informal):**
> "One good thing about living in cities is that you can get to hospitals easily."

**Câu học thuật của bạn:** _______________

**Mẫu đáp án:**
> *A notable advantage of urban residence is the proximity to healthcare facilities, which evidence suggests is associated with improved health outcomes and reduced emergency response times.*

---

**5. Câu gốc (informal):**
> "Social media causes depression in teenagers."

**Câu học thuật của bạn:** _______________

**Mẫu đáp án:**
> *Evidence suggests that prolonged exposure to social media platforms may contribute to elevated rates of depression and anxiety among adolescents, though the precise causal mechanisms remain subject to ongoing scholarly debate.*

---

> **Lưu ý cuối:** Trong IELTS, không cần dùng tất cả các cụm trên. Hãy nắm vững **15-20 cụm** và dùng chính xác thay vì dùng nhiều nhưng sai.
