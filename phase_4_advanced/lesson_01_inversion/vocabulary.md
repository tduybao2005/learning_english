## BÀI TẬP TỪ VỰNG

### Bài tập 1: Điền từ thích hợp vào chỗ trống

Chọn từ phù hợp từ danh sách: *unprecedented, exacerbate, mitigate, tenuous, polarised, formidable*

1. The ______________ nature of the connection between social media use and depression has been repeatedly challenged by neuroscientists.
2. Never before has society been so ______________ on political issues, making compromise increasingly difficult.
3. The ______________ scale of the environmental crisis demands nothing less than a complete restructuring of global energy systems.
4. Under no circumstances should policymakers implement measures that ______________ existing inequalities.
5. Only through sustained international cooperation can nations ______________ the worst effects of climate change.
6. Rarely have governments faced such ______________ opposition to proposed educational reforms.

**Đáp án:** 1. tenuous  2. polarised  3. unprecedented  4. exacerbate  5. mitigate  6. formidable

### Bài tập 3: Dùng từ vựng trong câu đảo ngữ

Viết lại các câu sau dùng đảo ngữ + từ vựng trong bảng:

1. Such measures rarely succeed in eliminating entrenched disparities.
   → **Rarely** ____________________________________________

2. The exacerbation of the crisis has never been more apparent.
   → **Never** ____________________________________________

3. Authorities should not under any conditions curtail freedom of expression.
   → **Under no circumstances** ____________________________________________

4. The government had hardly implemented the policy when opposition groups began to coerce members.
   → **Hardly** ____________________________________________

5. The problem is by no means tenuous — it is profound and pervasive.
   → **By no means** ____________________________________________

**Gợi ý đáp án:**
1. Rarely do such measures succeed in eliminating entrenched disparities.
2. Never has the exacerbation of the crisis been more apparent.
3. Under no circumstances should authorities curtail freedom of expression.
4. Hardly had the government implemented the policy when opposition groups began to coerce members.
5. By no means is the problem tenuous — it is profound and pervasive.
