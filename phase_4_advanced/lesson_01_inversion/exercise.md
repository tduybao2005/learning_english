**Model answer (Band 7–8):** *Mastering formal inversion structures undeniably enhances the sophistication and persuasive force of academic writing. Never should such structures be dismissed as mere stylistic ornamentation; they signal precision, control, and a nuanced command of register. Not only do inverted constructions allow writers to foreground key information more emphatically, but they also demonstrate the kind of lexicogrammatical range that IELTS examiners actively reward at Band 7 and above. Were students to practise these structures systematically from an early stage, their written output would be considerably more compelling and academically credible.*

## PHẦN B

**B1.** Lỗi: *he has* (chủ ngữ đứng trước trợ động từ sau Never)
→ **Never has he witnessed** such an egregious violation of human rights.

**B2.** Lỗi: *than* (Hardly...when, NOT than)
→ Hardly had the negotiations concluded **when** a new dispute emerged.

**B3.** Lỗi: *rising* (sau does → bare infinitive)
→ Not only does unemployment **rise**, but social unrest also intensifies.

**B4.** Lỗi: *the government should* (sau Under no circumstances → đảo ngữ)
→ Under no circumstances **should the government compromise** on child safety standards.

**B5.** Lỗi: *were she apply* (Were...to + verb)
→ Were she **to apply** for the position, she would undoubtedly be shortlisted.

**B6.** Lỗi: *when* (No sooner...than, NOT when)
→ No sooner had the CEO announced the redundancies **than** employees staged a walkout.

**B7.** Lỗi: *the consequences were* (So + adj → adj + verb + S)
→ **So serious were the consequences that** the board voted to dissolve the partnership.

**B8.** Lỗi: *governments have* (Seldom → trợ động từ trước chủ ngữ)
→ Seldom **have governments acknowledged** the scale of the housing crisis.

**B9.** Lỗi: *that* (Not until + time → did + S + V, không dùng "that")
→ Not until the independent inquiry published its findings **did** the truth emerge.

**B10.** Lỗi: *knew* (sau did → bare infinitive)
→ Little did she **know** how profoundly her research would alter medical practice.

## PHẦN F — Bài viết mẫu (Band 8)

*"Never has the claim that technology alone can solve environmental problems been more dangerously naive. While innovation undeniably plays a critical role, by no means does it constitute a sufficient response to the scale and complexity of the ecological crisis we face. Not only does climate change require technological solutions such as renewable energy and carbon capture, but it also demands fundamental shifts in political will, consumer behaviour, and international governance frameworks. Only through the convergence of these multiple forces can meaningful progress be achieved. Were humanity to rely solely on technological innovation without addressing systemic overconsumption and policy failures, even the most sophisticated green technologies would prove insufficient. The evidence from decades of environmental negotiation is unambiguous: technology, in isolation, has never reversed a major ecological trend. The solutions must be as multidimensional as the problems they seek to address."*

**Phân tích đảo ngữ trong bài mẫu:**
1. Never has the claim... (Never + has + S) ✓
2. By no means does it... (By no means + does + S) ✓
3. Not only does climate change require..., but it also demands... (Not only + does) ✓
4. Only through the convergence... can meaningful progress... (Only through + can) ✓
5. Were humanity to rely... (Were + S + to) ✓ **(bonus — 5 cấu trúc)**
