# BÀI 3: MỆNH ĐỀ PHÂN TỪ (PARTICIPLE CLAUSES)

**Trình độ:** C1 | **Mục tiêu IELTS:** 7.0 – 8.0

---

## 1. TẠI SAO CẦN HỌC MỆNH ĐỀ PHÂN TỪ?

Mệnh đề phân từ (participle clauses) là một trong những đặc trưng văn phong của học thuật và văn viết trình độ cao. Chúng cho phép bạn:

- **Nén thông tin:** Thay một mệnh đề phụ dài bằng một cụm từ ngắn gọn.
- **Tạo văn phong học thuật:** Examiners IELTS chấm Writing band 7–8 cần thấy "a range of complex structures used flexibly and accurately" — participle clauses là một trong những cấu trúc đó.
- **Tránh lặp chủ ngữ:** Giúp câu mạch lạc, tránh wordiness.
- **Diễn đạt nhiều mối quan hệ:** Nguyên nhân, thời gian, kết quả, điều kiện — tất cả không cần từ nối khi dùng participle clause.

**So sánh:**
- Thường (low band): *Because the government lacked sufficient funding, it was unable to implement the new policy.*
- Học thuật (high band): *Lacking sufficient funding, the government was unable to implement the new policy.*

---

## 2. PRESENT PARTICIPLE CLAUSE (V-ING)

Present participle clause dùng dạng **V-ing** của động từ. Nó có thể diễn đạt nhiều mối quan hệ tuỳ vào ngữ cảnh.

---

### 2.1 Simultaneous Action (Hành động đồng thời)

Diễn tả hai hành động xảy ra cùng lúc với cùng một chủ ngữ.

**Cấu trúc:** V-ing + main clause | main clause + V-ing

| Mệnh đề đầy đủ | Participle clause |
|---|---|
| As she walked to work, she thought about the meeting. | **Walking to work**, she thought about the meeting. |
| While he was waiting for the results, he reviewed his notes. | **Waiting for the results**, he reviewed his notes. |
| She sat at her desk and listened to the lecture. | She sat at her desk, **listening to the lecture**. |

**Phân tích:** Trong câu cuối, "listening" ở cuối câu — vị trí này thường diễn tả hành động xảy ra đồng thời hoặc là kết quả tự nhiên của hành động chính.

---

### 2.2 Reason / Cause (Nguyên nhân — thay "because/since/as")

**Cấu trúc:** V-ing (thường đứng đầu câu) + main clause

| Mệnh đề đầy đủ | Participle clause |
|---|---|
| Because he didn't know what to say, he remained silent. | **Not knowing what to say**, he remained silent. |
| Since she had lived abroad for years, she spoke French fluently. | **Having lived abroad for years**, she spoke French fluently. *(→ perfect participle — xem Mục 4)* |
| As they lacked reliable data, the researchers could not draw firm conclusions. | **Lacking reliable data**, the researchers could not draw firm conclusions. |

**Lưu ý âm điệu:** V-ing đứng đầu câu thường mang nghĩa nguyên nhân trong văn học thuật.

---

### 2.3 Result (Kết quả — thay "which caused / resulting in")

**Cấu trúc:** main clause + V-ing (đứng cuối câu — diễn tả kết quả)

| Mệnh đề đầy đủ | Participle clause |
|---|---|
| The government raised taxes, which caused widespread protests. | The government raised taxes, **causing widespread protests**. |
| The factory released toxic chemicals into the river, which contaminated the water supply. | The factory released toxic chemicals into the river, **contaminating the water supply**. |
| Deforestation destroys natural habitats, which forces wildlife to migrate. | Deforestation destroys natural habitats, **forcing wildlife to migrate**. |

**Dấu phẩy quan trọng:** Luôn có dấu phẩy trước V-ing khi diễn tả kết quả ở cuối câu.

---

### 2.4 Condition (Điều kiện — ít gặp, trang trọng)

**Cấu trúc:** V-ing + main clause (nghĩa tương đương "If...")

| Mệnh đề đầy đủ | Participle clause |
|---|---|
| If you work consistently, you will improve. | **Working consistently**, you will improve. |
| If you follow the recommended guidelines, you should see results within weeks. | **Following the recommended guidelines**, you should see results within weeks. |

---

## 3. PAST PARTICIPLE CLAUSE (V3 / ED-FORM)

Past participle clause dùng dạng **V3** (quá khứ phân từ). Nó thường có nghĩa **bị động** hoặc **thu gọn mệnh đề quan hệ bị động**.

---

### 3.1 Reduced Relative Clause — Passive (Thu gọn mệnh đề quan hệ bị động)

Thay thế **which is/was/are/were + V3** bằng chỉ V3.

| Mệnh đề đầy đủ | Participle clause |
|---|---|
| The book, which was written in simple language, is accessible to all. | The book, **written in simple language**, is accessible to all. |
| The policy, which was designed to reduce inequality, had the opposite effect. | The policy, **designed to reduce inequality**, had the opposite effect. |
| Documents that are submitted after the deadline will not be considered. | Documents **submitted after the deadline** will not be considered. |
| Students who are selected for the programme will receive full funding. | Students **selected for the programme** will receive full funding. |

---

### 3.2 After When/While/Once/Until (Sau liên từ thời gian)

Khi chủ ngữ của mệnh đề phụ và mệnh đề chính giống nhau, bỏ chủ ngữ và "be":

| Mệnh đề đầy đủ | Participle clause |
|---|---|
| Once the report is completed, it will be submitted for review. | **Once completed**, the report will be submitted for review. |
| When it was challenged by critics, the theory proved remarkably resilient. | **When challenged by critics**, the theory proved remarkably resilient. |
| After the data had been analysed, the team presented their findings. | **After being analysed**, the data was presented to the team. *(→ "being" thêm vào để rõ bị động)* |

---

### 3.3 Describing a State Caused by a Past Event

Past participle clause đứng đầu câu để mô tả trạng thái do quá khứ gây ra:

- **Surprised by the announcement**, she was unable to respond immediately.
- **Overwhelmed by the volume of applications**, the committee postponed their decision.
- **Exhausted after months of negotiation**, both parties agreed to a temporary ceasefire.

---

## 4. PERFECT PARTICIPLE CLAUSE (HAVING + V3)

Perfect participle clause dùng khi muốn chỉ rõ rằng hành động của participle clause xảy ra **trước** hành động của mệnh đề chính.

**Cấu trúc:** Having + V3 + main clause

---

### 4.1 Dạng chủ động

| Mệnh đề đầy đủ | Perfect participle clause |
|---|---|
| After she had finished the report, she submitted it for review. | **Having finished the report**, she submitted it for review. |
| Because he had studied abroad for three years, he spoke the language fluently. | **Having studied abroad for three years**, he spoke the language fluently. |
| After the scientists had conducted extensive trials, they published their findings. | **Having conducted extensive trials**, the scientists published their findings. |

---

### 4.2 Dạng phủ định

**Cấu trúc:** Not having + V3

- **Not having eaten for several hours**, he felt light-headed during the meeting.
- **Not having read the full report**, the journalist misrepresented the findings.
- **Not having been informed of the change**, the delegates arrived expecting a different agenda.

---

### 4.3 Dạng bị động (Having been + V3)

**Cấu trúc:** Having been + V3

- **Having been awarded a scholarship**, she was able to complete her doctorate without financial pressure.
- **Having been rejected twice by the committee**, the proposal was substantially revised.
- **Having been informed of the delay**, the passengers made alternative travel arrangements.

---

## 5. WITH "BEING"

Dùng **being** khi muốn nhấn mạnh trạng thái hoặc bị động đang diễn ra:

---

### 5.1 Being + adjective / noun (= Because/As + be)

- **Being an internationally recognised expert**, she was invited to address the United Nations. *(= Because she is an expert)*
- **Being the only viable solution**, the proposal was eventually adopted. *(= As it was the only viable solution)*
- **Being highly toxic**, the chemical must be handled with extreme caution.

---

### 5.2 Being + V3 (Passive ongoing)

- **Being discussed at the highest levels of government**, the issue is expected to receive renewed attention.
- **Being reviewed by an independent panel**, the decision cannot be disclosed at this stage.

---

## 6. QUY TẮC VÀNG: CHỦ NGỮ PHẢI KHỚP

**Đây là quy tắc quan trọng nhất và hay bị vi phạm nhất.**

Chủ ngữ ẩn của participle clause **PHẢI** là chủ ngữ của mệnh đề chính.

| WRONG ✗ | RIGHT ✓ |
|---|---|
| Walking to school, the rain started. | Walking to school, **I** got caught in the rain. |
| Having read the report, the solution became clear. | Having read the report, **she** understood the solution. |
| Arriving at the station, the train had already left. | Arriving at the station, **we** found that the train had already left. |
| Written in 1984, **George Orwell** published the novel... | Written in 1984, the novel was published by George Orwell... |

---

## 7. DANGLING PARTICIPLE (MỆnh ĐỀ PHÂN TỪ LƠ LỬNG)

**Dangling participle** xảy ra khi participle clause không có chủ ngữ phù hợp với mệnh đề chính. Đây là lỗi ngữ pháp nghiêm trọng trong văn viết học thuật.

**Cách nhận biết:** Hỏi "Ai/Cái gì đang thực hiện hành động của participle?"

- **WRONG:** *Examining the data, several anomalies were discovered.* → Ai examining? "Anomalies" không thể tự examine.
- **RIGHT:** *Examining the data, the researchers discovered several anomalies.*

- **WRONG:** *Having reviewed all applications, the decision was announced.* → Ai reviewed? "The decision" không review được.
- **RIGHT:** *Having reviewed all applications, the committee announced their decision.*

- **WRONG:** *Considering the evidence, this conclusion seems premature.* → "This conclusion" không consider evidence.
- **RIGHT:** *Considering the evidence, one might argue that this conclusion is premature.* *(Dùng "one" — impersonal)*

## 8. THU GỌN MỆNH ĐỀ TRẠNG NGỮ (REDUCING ADVERB CLAUSES)

Bảng tổng hợp cách chuyển đổi:

| Loại | Mệnh đề đầy đủ | Participle clause |
|---|---|---|
| Time (When) | When she arrived home, she cooked dinner. | **Arriving home**, she cooked dinner. |
| Time (After) | After he had submitted his thesis, he felt relieved. | **Having submitted his thesis**, he felt relieved. |
| Time (While) | While the manager was addressing the team, she took notes. | The manager addressed the team, she **taking notes**. *(ít dùng)* |
| Reason (Because) | Because he was exhausted, he went to bed early. | **Being exhausted**, he went to bed early. *(OR: Exhausted, he went to bed early.)* |
| Reason (As) | As the company had lost its major contracts, it was forced to downsize. | **Having lost its major contracts**, the company was forced to downsize. |
| Result | The storm intensified, and it destroyed several coastal villages. | The storm intensified, **destroying several coastal villages**. |
| Condition | If used correctly, the technology can dramatically improve efficiency. | **Used correctly**, the technology can dramatically improve efficiency. |
| Contrast | Although tired, he continued working. | **Tired though he was**, he continued working. *(inversion — C1)* |

## 9. ỨNG DỤNG TRONG IELTS WRITING

### 9.1 Thay mệnh đề quan hệ

| Bình thường (band 6) | Học thuật (band 7–8) |
|---|---|
| Policies which are designed to tackle inequality often fail. | Policies **designed to tackle inequality** often fail. |
| Countries that are affected by climate change require immediate support. | Countries **affected by climate change** require immediate support. |

### 9.2 Thay mệnh đề nguyên nhân

| Bình thường (band 6) | Học thuật (band 7–8) |
|---|---|
| Because they lacked resources, the researchers abandoned the project. | **Lacking resources**, the researchers abandoned the project. |
| Since the government had failed to act, the crisis deepened. | **Having failed to act**, the government watched the crisis deepen. |

### 9.3 Diễn đạt kết quả

| Bình thường (band 6) | Học thuật (band 7–8) |
|---|---|
| Population growth is accelerating, and this puts pressure on food supplies. | Population growth is accelerating, **putting unprecedented pressure on global food supplies**. |

### 9.4 Tạo câu mở đầu đoạn văn ấn tượng

- *Driven by technological innovation, modern economies are undergoing a structural transformation that challenges traditional models of employment.*
- *Having observed the failures of previous reform attempts, policymakers are now adopting a more evidence-based approach.*
- *Constrained by limited budgets and competing political priorities, governments struggle to deliver comprehensive healthcare reform.*

## 10. BẪY THƯỜNG GẶP

| Lỗi | Ví dụ sai | Sửa |
|---|---|---|
| Dangling participle | *Submitting the form, a confirmation email was sent.* | *Submitting the form, **you** will receive a confirmation email.* |
| Sai thứ tự thời gian | *Finishing the project, he started it.* (vô nghĩa) | *Having finished the project, he submitted it.* |
| Dùng V-ing thay V3 với bị động | *Building in 1920, the bridge is still in use.* | *Built in 1920, the bridge is still in use.* |
| Không có dấu phẩy khi cần | *The researchers published their findings causing a major controversy.* | *The researchers published their findings**,** causing a major controversy.* |
| Perfect participle không cần thiết | *Having walked to school, she arrived.* (hai hành động kế tiếp bình thường) | *Walking to school, she arrived on time.* |

---

## TÓM TẮT CẤU TRÚC

| Loại | Hình thức | Nghĩa chính | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|---|
| Present Participle | V-ing | Đồng thời / Nguyên nhân / Kết quả | *Lacking evidence, the case was dismissed.* |
| Past Participle | V3 | Bị động / Thu gọn relative clause | *Written clearly, the report was easy to follow.* |
| Perfect Participle | Having + V3 | Trước mệnh đề chính (chủ động) | *Having reviewed the data, she revised her conclusions.* |
| Perfect Passive | Having been + V3 | Trước mệnh đề chính (bị động) | *Having been thoroughly tested, the drug was approved.* |
| Being | Being + adj/noun/V3 | Trạng thái / Bị động đang diễn ra | *Being a specialist, he was widely consulted.* |

---

## LỖI THƯỜNG GẶP

| Sai | Đúng | Giải thích |
|---|---|---|
| Walking to school, the rain started. | Walking to school, I got caught in the rain. | Dangling participle: chủ ngữ ẩn của participle phải là chủ ngữ của mệnh đề chính. |
| Building in 1920, the bridge is still in use. | Built in 1920, the bridge is still in use. | Cầu được xây (bị động) → dùng V3 (built), không dùng V-ing (building). |
| The researchers published their findings causing a major controversy. | The researchers published their findings, causing a major controversy. | Bắt buộc có dấu phẩy trước V-ing khi diễn tả kết quả ở cuối câu. |
| Finishing the report, he started writing it. | Having finished the report, he submitted it for review. | Hành động xảy ra trước → phải dùng perfect participle (Having + V3). |
| Having walked to school, she arrived on time. | Walking to school, she arrived on time. | Hai hành động kế tiếp bình thường không cần perfect participle. |
| Having awarded a scholarship, she studied freely. | Having been awarded a scholarship, she studied freely. | Cô ấy được nhận học bổng (bị động) → "Having been awarded", không phải "Having awarded". |
| Submitting the form, a confirmation email was sent. | Submitting the form, you will receive a confirmation email. | "A confirmation email" không thể tự submit form → đây là dangling participle. |
| The policy, designing to reduce inequality, failed. | The policy, designed to reduce inequality, failed. | Thu gọn relative clause bị động → V3 (designed), không phải V-ing (designing). |

---

## TÓM TẮT NHANH

- **Present Participle (V-ing):** Diễn tả đồng thời, nguyên nhân, hoặc kết quả — e.g., "Lacking resources, the researchers abandoned the project."
- **Past Participle (V3):** Mang nghĩa bị động, thu gọn relative clause bị động — e.g., "Policies designed to tackle inequality often fail."
- **Perfect Participle (Having + V3):** Hành động xảy ra TRƯỚC mệnh đề chính (chủ động) — e.g., "Having reviewed the data, she revised her conclusions."
- **Perfect Passive Participle (Having been + V3):** Hành động xảy ra trước và ở thể bị động — e.g., "Having been thoroughly tested, the drug was approved."
- **Quy tắc vàng — Dangling Participle:** Chủ ngữ ẩn của participle clause phải trùng với chủ ngữ của mệnh đề chính — vi phạm tạo câu sai nghiêm trọng.
- **Dấu phẩy kết quả:** Bắt buộc có dấu phẩy trước V-ing khi diễn tả kết quả ở cuối câu — e.g., "The government raised taxes, causing widespread protests."
