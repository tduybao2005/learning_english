### FORMAL vs INFORMAL (12 từ)
| Informal | Formal/Academic | Nghĩa |
|---|---|---|
| use | utilise | sử dụng |
| show | demonstrate | cho thấy |
| think | consider / argue | xem xét, lập luận |
| find out | establish / reveal | khám phá, xác định |
| need | require / necessitate | cần |
| big | significant / substantial | đáng kể, lớn |
| bad | detrimental / adverse | có hại |
| good | beneficial / advantageous | có lợi |
| get | obtain / acquire | có được |
| look at | examine / analyse | phân tích |
| help | facilitate / assist | hỗ trợ |
| make | generate / produce | tạo ra |

## Exercise 1: Choose the word with the RIGHT connotation (10 items)
Choose the better word for each context:

1. The journalist described him as _______ for refusing to change his opinion despite all evidence.
   a) determined  b) obstinate

2. She was _______ when she heard the news about winning the prize.
   a) happy  b) ecstatic

3. His _______ personality made him very effective in negotiations.
   a) aggressive  b) assertive

4. The professor's _______ notes covered every possible detail.
   a) finicky  b) meticulous

5. "I was never involved!" he _______ angrily.
   a) declared  b) snapped

6. The evidence is _______ — we cannot ignore it.
   a) significant  b) big

7. He _______ all the way home after the match. [very tired, slow walking]
   a) strolled  b) trudged

8. She _______ along the beach in the evening sunlight. [relaxed, leisurely]
   a) trudged  b) strolled

9. The company's decision was _______ to its reputation.
   a) bad  b) detrimental

10. The children were _______ with excitement when they arrived at the theme park.
    a) happy  b) ecstatic

**Answers:** 1.b 2.b 3.b 4.b 5.b 6.a 7.b 8.b 9.b 10.b

## Exercise 3: Formal register — replace informal word (8 items)
1. The study tried to **find out** the main causes. → ___
2. This is **bad** for public health. → ___
3. We need to **look at** the data. → ___
4. The policy will **help** economic recovery. → ___
5. Researchers **showed** that the drug works. → ___
6. This method **uses** cutting-edge technology. → ___
7. The results were **good**. → ___
8. They **got** funding from the government. → ___

**Answers:** 1.establish/identify 2.detrimental/harmful/adverse 3.examine/analyse 4.facilitate/support 5.demonstrated/showed 6.utilises/employs 7.favourable/significant 8.obtained/secured/received
