## Exercise 1: Identify Subjunctive (10 items)
Write "SUBJ" if the sentence uses subjunctive mood, or "NORMAL" if it doesn't:

1. It is essential that the report be submitted before noon.
2. It is important that you should arrive on time.
3. She suggested that he takes a different approach.
4. The committee proposed that the vote be postponed.
5. It is vital that every participant be informed.
6. The doctor recommended that she rest for a week.
7. He insisted that the contract is reviewed.
8. It is necessary that the data be verified first.
9. We urge that funding be increased significantly.
10. She spoke as if she was the CEO.

**Answers:** 1.SUBJ 2.NORMAL(incorrect subj) 3.NORMAL(incorrect) 4.SUBJ 5.SUBJ 6.SUBJ 7.NORMAL(incorrect) 8.SUBJ 9.SUBJ 10.NORMAL(spoken/informal)

## Exercise 2: Choose correct Subjunctive form (10 items)
1. It is essential that every member _______ (attend/attends) the meeting.
2. The regulations require that all documents _______ (be/are) signed.
3. She suggested that he _______ (apply/applies) for the scholarship.
4. It is vital that the patient _______ (rest/rests) for 24 hours.
5. The judge ordered that the accused _______ (be/is) released immediately.
6. The committee proposes that the project _______ (be/is) extended by six months.
7. If I _______ (was/were) you, I would reconsider.
8. He insisted that she _______ (receive/receives) equal treatment.
9. It is imperative that action _______ (be/is) taken at once.
10. She spoke as though she _______ (was/were) in charge of the whole operation.

**Answers:** 1.attend 2.be 3.apply 4.rest 5.be 6.be 7.were 8.receive 9.be 10.were
