# EXERCISE — Bài 4: Giả Định Cách (Subjunctive Mood)

## SECTION A: Write sentences using Subjunctive (10 items)
Complete each sentence using the subjunctive form of the verb given:

1. It is essential that every applicant _______ (submit) their portfolio by Friday.
2. The contract stipulates that payment _______ (make) within 30 days.
3. She recommended that all students _______ (read) this book before the exam.
4. The committee demanded that the CEO _______ (be) present at the hearing.
5. It is vital that the results _______ (verify) by an independent team.
6. The judge ordered that the evidence _______ (examine) more carefully.
7. They proposed that a new law _______ (pass) to address this issue.
8. If I _______ (be) in your position, I would negotiate the terms.
9. It is crucial that every mistake _______ (correct) before publication.
10. The policy requires that all staff _______ (attend) the mandatory training.

## SECTION C: Transform to Subjunctive (6 items)
Rewrite each sentence using the cue given in brackets:

1. "You must arrive before 9am." (It is essential that...)
   → _______________

2. "Please submit your work electronically." (The university requires that...)
   → _______________

3. "Everyone should be notified about the changes." (It is important that...)
   → _______________

4. "He ought to step down from his position." (She recommended that...)
   → _______________

5. "All findings need to be reviewed." (It is vital that...)
   → _______________

6. "The meeting should be postponed." (I propose that...)
   → _______________

## SECTION D: Formal writing — Subjunctive in context (5 items)
Each of the following is an informal statement. Rewrite it using formal language with the subjunctive:

1. "We think everyone should get a vote."
   → It is _______ that every eligible citizen _______ given the right to vote.

2. "The boss told everyone to be there at 8am."
   → The director insisted that all staff _______ present no later than 8am.

3. "You should rest after the operation."
   → The physician recommended that the patient _______ for at least 48 hours following the procedure.

4. "They said he has to sign the contract."
   → The legal team required that he _______ the contract before any work _______ commenced.

5. "We need to check everything again."
   → It is imperative that all data _______ re-examined before the findings _______ published.

## SECTION E: IELTS Writing — Formal recommendation paragraph
Write a formal paragraph (80-100 words) making 3 recommendations about how to improve university education. Use at least 3 subjunctive structures.

Starter: "In order to improve the quality of higher education, several measures are recommended. First, it is essential that..."
