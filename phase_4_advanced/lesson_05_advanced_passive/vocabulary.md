## Exercise 1: Transform to Impersonal Passive Type A (5 items)
1. Scientists believe that the universe is 13.8 billion years old.
   → It is believed that _______________

2. Experts report that the economy is recovering faster than expected.
   → It has been reported that _______________

3. People generally accept that early education shapes future outcomes.
   → It is generally accepted that _______________

4. Researchers have found that regular exercise reduces the risk of depression.
   → It has been found that _______________

5. The media claims that the company has been involved in corruption.
   → It is claimed that _______________

## Exercise 2: Transform to Impersonal Passive Type B (5 items)
1. It is believed that he is hiding in the mountains.
   → He is believed _______________

2. It is expected that the prime minister will resign next week.
   → The prime minister is expected _______________

3. It is thought that the painting was stolen in 1943.
   → The painting is thought _______________

4. It is reported that she has left the country.
   → She is reported _______________

5. It is said that the building dates from the 12th century.
   → The building is said _______________
