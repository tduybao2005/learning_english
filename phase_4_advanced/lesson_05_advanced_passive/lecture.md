# BÀI 5: THỂ BỊ ĐỘNG NÂNG CAO (ADVANCED PASSIVE VOICE)

## 1. Ôn lại Passive cơ bản

Cấu trúc cơ bản: **Subject + be (chia thì) + Past Participle**

| Thì | Active | Passive |
|---|---|---|
| Present Simple | They build houses. | Houses are built. |
| Present Continuous | They are building a bridge. | A bridge is being built. |
| Past Simple | They built the wall. | The wall was built. |
| Past Continuous | They were repairing the road. | The road was being repaired. |
| Present Perfect | They have discovered a cure. | A cure has been discovered. |
| Past Perfect | They had finished the project. | The project had been finished. |
| Future Simple | They will announce results. | Results will be announced. |
| Modal + V | They should review the data. | The data should be reviewed. |

---

## 2. IMPERSONAL PASSIVE — Cấu trúc Vô nhân xưng

Đây là cấu trúc rất phổ biến trong văn học thuật và báo chí.

### Loại A: **It + passive + that clause**
- *It is believed that climate change is accelerating.* (người ta tin rằng)
- *It has been reported that unemployment has fallen.* (được báo cáo rằng)
- *It is generally accepted that exercise improves mood.* (được chấp nhận rộng rãi)
- *It was found that the experiment yielded unexpected results.*
- *It is expected that the population will double by 2050.*
- *It has been argued that social media increases anxiety.*
- *It is claimed that the new drug has no side effects.*
- *It is estimated that 8 million people suffer from this condition.*
- *It is widely known that vitamin C boosts immunity.*
- *It has been suggested that schools adopt a shorter day.*

**Động từ thường dùng trong loại này:**
believe / report / find / expect / suggest / argue / claim / estimate / know / say / think / assume / acknowledge / consider / observe / note

### Loại B: **Subject + passive + to infinitive**
*(Biến thể của Loại A — cùng nghĩa, cấu trúc khác)*

| It + passive + that... | Subject + passive + to... |
|---|---|
| It is believed that he stole the money. | **He is believed to have stolen** the money. |
| It is expected that they will arrive soon. | **They are expected to arrive** soon. |
| It is reported that the suspect fled the country. | **The suspect is reported to have fled** the country. |
| It was thought that the disease was incurable. | **The disease was thought to be** incurable. |

**Quy tắc thì:**
- Cùng thời điểm: *He is believed **to be** guilty.* (present)
- Trước đó: *He is believed **to have been** guilty.* (perfect infinitive)

## 3. PASSIVE với GET

Trong văn nói và văn thông thường, "get" thường thay thế "be":
- *She **got promoted** last week.* (được thăng chức)
- *He **got arrested** for speeding.* (bị bắt)
- *They **got caught** in traffic.* (bị kẹt)
- *My wallet **got stolen** on the metro.* (bị mất cắp)

GET passive thường mang ý nghĩa:
- Sự kiện xảy ra ngoài ý muốn (negative events)
- Hành động chủ thể đã đạt được sau cố gắng (positive achievement)

---

## 4. HAVE SOMETHING DONE (Causative)

Cấu trúc: **Subject + have + object + past participle**
Nghĩa: Nhờ ai đó làm gì cho mình

- *She **had her hair cut** yesterday.* (Cô ấy đi cắt tóc — người khác cắt)
- *We **had our house painted** last summer.*
- *He **had his car repaired** at the garage.*
- *I need to **have my eyes tested**.*

So sánh:
- *She cut her hair.* (Cô ấy tự cắt)
- *She had her hair cut.* (Có người cắt cho cô ấy)
- *She had her bag stolen.* (Túi bị ai đó lấy mất — ngoài ý muốn)

GET + have something done (informal):
- *I need to **get my laptop fixed**.*
- *She **got her nails done** before the wedding.*

## 5. PASSIVE INFINITIVES và GERUNDS

### Passive Infinitive: **to be + past participle**
- *She wants **to be promoted** before the end of the year.*
- *He expected **to be told** the results immediately.*
- *Nobody likes **to be criticized**.*
- *The report needs **to be submitted** by Friday.*

### Passive Gerund: **being + past participle**
- *She hates **being interrupted** when she is working.*
- *He avoided **being seen** at the party.*
- *She's afraid of **being misunderstood**.*
- *After **being treated**, patients should rest for 48 hours.*

### Perfect Passive Infinitive: **to have been + past participle**
- *He is believed **to have been given** false information.*
- *The documents appear **to have been altered**.*

---

## 6. PASSIVE trong văn học thuật

Trong IELTS Writing và văn học thuật, passive voice được dùng để:

**a) Duy trì giọng khách quan:**
- ✓ *The data was collected over a three-month period.*
- ✗ *We collected the data over three months.* (quá informal)

**b) Khi tác nhân không quan trọng hoặc không biết:**
- *Thousands of people are affected by this condition annually.*
- *The monument was built in the 12th century.* (ai xây không quan trọng)

**c) Dùng impersonal passive cho lập luận:**
- *It has been widely argued that globalisation has widened inequality.*
- *It is generally acknowledged that early intervention is most effective.*

**d) Mô tả quy trình (Process descriptions):**
- *First, the raw material is heated to 200°C. Then, it is shaped into the desired form. Finally, it is cooled and inspected.*

## 7. Lỗi thường gặp của người Việt

❌ *The book was writing by Shakespeare.* → ✓ *was written*
❌ *It is believed that he stoles money.* → ✓ *It is believed that he stole / He is believed to have stolen*
❌ *I had my hair cutting.* → ✓ *I had my hair cut.*
❌ *She is being promoted next month.* → ✓ *She will be promoted* (future event, not being-in-progress)
❌ *The report needs submitting.* → ambiguous; better: *The report needs to be submitted.*

## LỖI THƯỜNG GẶP

| Sai | Đúng | Giải thích |
|---|---|---|
| The book was writing by Shakespeare. | The book was written by Shakespeare. | Passive voice dùng V3 (past participle), không dùng V-ing. |
| She wants to promote before year end. | She wants to be promoted before year end. | "Muốn được thăng chức" = passive infinitive "to be promoted", không phải active. |
| She is being promoted next month. | She will be promoted next month. | Sự kiện tương lai không thể dùng "is being + V3" (chỉ dùng cho hành động đang diễn ra). |
| I had my hair cutting yesterday. | I had my hair cut yesterday. | Causative "have something done" dùng V3, không dùng V-ing. |
| He avoided be seen at the party. | He avoided being seen at the party. | Sau preposition hoặc "avoid", dùng passive gerund "being + V3". |
| It is believe that he stole money. | It is believed that he stole the money. | Impersonal passive: "It is believed/reported/suggested that..." — động từ ở dạng V3. |
| He is believed to stolen the money. | He is believed to have stolen the money. | Subject + passive + perfect infinitive: "to have + V3", không bỏ "have". |
| It has been suggested that he should resigns. | It has been suggested that he should resign. | Sau "should", dùng bare infinitive, không thêm "-s" hay "-ed". |

---

## TÓM TẮT NHANH

- **Impersonal passive:** "It is believed/reported/expected/suggested that..." — duy trì giọng khách quan học thuật trong IELTS Writing.
- **Subject + passive + to infinitive:** "He is believed to have stolen the money." — cùng nghĩa với impersonal passive nhưng đặt trọng tâm vào chủ thể.
- **Causative "Have something done":** S + have + O + V3 — nhờ người khác làm cho mình — e.g., "She had her hair cut."
- **Passive gerund (being + V3):** Dùng sau preposition hoặc động từ cần gerund — e.g., "She hates being interrupted."
- **Perfect passive infinitive:** "to have been + V3" — e.g., "The documents appear to have been altered."
- **GET passive (informal):** "She got promoted." — dùng trong Speaking, tuyệt đối KHÔNG dùng trong IELTS Academic Writing.
