# EXERCISE — Bài 5: Advanced Passive Voice

## SECTION A: Impersonal Passive Type A (10 items)
Transform the active sentences to impersonal passive with "It is/was/has been + past participle + that":

1. Scientists believe that the ocean floor is still largely unexplored.
2. Researchers have found that meditation reduces cortisol levels significantly.
3. People widely know that a healthy diet prevents many diseases.
4. Experts estimate that climate change will cost $23 trillion by 2100.
5. The report claimed that government funds had been misused.
6. Many people think that the exam system needs to be reformed.
7. Scholars have argued that Shakespeare may not have written all his plays alone.
8. People generally accept that violent video games do not directly cause violence.
9. The media has reported that a major earthquake struck the region.
10. Scientists suggest that the planet may have once had liquid water.

## SECTION B: Impersonal Passive Type B (8 items)
Transform each "It + passive + that" sentence to the "Subject + passive + to-infinitive" form:

1. It is believed that she is the most qualified candidate.
2. It has been reported that he was arrested at the airport.
3. It is widely expected that the company will merge with its rival.
4. It was thought that the document had been forged.
5. It is said that this wine is the finest in the country.
6. It has been suggested that the policy should be reversed.
7. It is understood that negotiations are still ongoing.
8. It was alleged that he had accepted bribes.

## SECTION C: Have something done — Causative (8 items)
Rewrite using have/get + object + past participle:

1. A surgeon removed his appendix last week.
   → He _______________

2. The cleaners are cleaning their office right now.
   → They _______________

3. A mechanic will service her car next week.
   → She _______________

4. A tailor made this suit for him.
   → He _______________

5. The dentist has filled two of her teeth.
   → She _______________

6. A professional is painting their house.
   → They _______________

7. The technician tested all the equipment.
   → They _______________

8. A photographer is taking her portrait.
   → She _______________

## SECTION D: Passive Infinitives and Gerunds (8 items)
Choose the correct passive form:

1. She wants _______ for her contributions. (to recognise/to be recognised)
2. He hates _______ when he's speaking. (to be interrupted/interrupting)
3. The document needs _______ before the deadline. (to be submitted/submitting)
4. He's afraid of _______ the wrong thing. (saying/being said to have said)
5. Nobody likes _______ unfairly. (treating/to be treated)
6. She expected _______ the award. (to receive/to be receiving)
7. After _______ in hospital, she needed months of rest. (treating/being treated)
8. The package appears _______ during transit. (to have been damaged/to be damaged)

## SECTION E: Academic Writing Practice (6 items)
Rewrite each sentence in a more academic style using impersonal passive:

1. People think globalisation increases inequality.
   → _______________

2. Scientists have discovered that coffee can improve short-term memory.
   → _______________

3. Researchers believe that the new treatment reduces symptoms by 50%.
   → _______________

4. People widely argue that social media negatively affects mental health.
   → _______________

5. Experts estimate that the world population will reach 10 billion by 2057.
   → _______________

6. Many people consider this to be the most significant scientific discovery of the decade.
   → _______________
