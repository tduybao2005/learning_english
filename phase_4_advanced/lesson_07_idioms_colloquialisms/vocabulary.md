## BÀI TẬP 3: HOÀN THÀNH CÂU TRẢ LỜI IELTS SPEAKING PART 3

**Hướng dẫn:** Đọc câu hỏi IELTS Speaking Part 3. Dùng một idiom phù hợp từ danh sách đã học để hoàn chỉnh câu trả lời mẫu. Giải thích tại sao bạn chọn idiom đó.

---

**Câu 1:** "Do you think young people today are under more pressure than previous generations?"

*"Absolutely — _______ no. The academic and social pressures have intensified dramatically. With social media, young people are constantly comparing themselves to others, which only _______ anxiety and competition. The mental health statistics we see are merely _______ — the real scale of distress among young people is far greater."*

*(Gợi ý: without a shadow of a doubt / adds fuel to the fire / the tip of the iceberg)*

---

**Câu 2:** "What are the advantages of living in a big city?"

*"Well, there are obvious advantages — career opportunities, cultural diversity, and entertainment. That said, these benefits don't come _______ — the cost of living in major cities has _______ in recent decades, making it extremely difficult for many young workers to _______ on entry-level salaries."*

*(Gợi ý: at a price / gone through the roof / make ends meet)*

---

**Câu 3:** "How important is creativity in education?"

*"Enormously important, in my view. _______, schools need to produce graduates who can _______ and adapt to rapidly changing environments. Merely memorising facts is _______ — employers need flexible thinkers, not human encyclopedias."*

*(Gợi ý: At the end of the day / think outside the box / behind the times)*

---

**Câu 4:** "Do you think environmental problems will be solved in your lifetime?"

*"I'm cautiously optimistic. Renewable energy, for instance, is _______ — adoption rates are accelerating every year. However, I think governments need to _______ and acknowledge that current policies are inadequate rather than _______ the issue. Without bold action, we're simply _______ our planet's future."*

*(Gợi ý: on the up and up / stop sitting on the fence / turning a blind eye to / gambling with)*

---

**Câu 5:** "Is it better to be self-employed or work for a company?"

*"Both have merit, and I suppose I'm _______ on this question — it really depends on personality. Someone who wears their heart on their sleeve and thrives on independence would likely flourish being self-employed. However, _______, the financial security of employment is hard to ignore, especially in uncertain economic times. The key is knowing yourself well enough to make the right choice."*

*(Gợi ý: sitting on the fence / at the end of the day)*
