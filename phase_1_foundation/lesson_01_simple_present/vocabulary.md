### Bài tập 2: Điền vào chỗ trống (Fill in the Blank with Word Bank)

**Word Bank:** brush, commutes, washes, always, reads, exercises, attend, check, goes, prepares

Điền từ thích hợp vào chỗ trống:

1. She __________ her teeth twice a day — in the morning and before bed.
2. My father __________ to work by bus because he doesn't have a car.
3. I __________ my emails first thing every morning.
4. He __________ at the gym on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays.
5. My mother __________ lunch boxes for my brother and me every day.
6. Our team __________ the weekly meeting every Monday at 9 am.
7. She __________ the dishes right after dinner — she hates leaving them.
8. My grandfather __________ the newspaper for one hour every morning.
9. I __________ shopping at the local market on Saturday mornings.
10. He is __________ on time — he has never been late in 5 years.

**Đáp án:**
1. brushes | 2. commutes | 3. check | 4. exercises | 5. prepares | 6. attend | 7. washes | 8. reads | 9. go | 10. always

### Bài tập 3: Viết câu (Sentence Writing)

Dùng các từ gợi ý để viết câu hoàn chỉnh ở Thì Hiện Tại Đơn:

1. My sister / usually / wake up / 5:30 am / every morning
   → ____________________________________________

2. We / never / eat / fast food / on weekdays
   → ____________________________________________

3. The supermarket / open / at 8 am / and / close / at 10 pm
   → ____________________________________________

4. He / take / the bus / to work / twice a day
   → ____________________________________________

5. How often / she / exercise / at the gym?
   → ____________________________________________

6. My parents / always / have / dinner / together / at 6:30 pm
   → ____________________________________________

7. The students / rarely / be / late / for class
   → ____________________________________________

8. I / spend / two hours / read / books / every evening
   → ____________________________________________

**Đáp án gợi ý:**
1. My sister usually wakes up at 5:30 am every morning.
2. We never eat fast food on weekdays.
3. The supermarket opens at 8 am and closes at 10 pm.
4. He takes the bus to work twice a day.
5. How often does she exercise at the gym?
6. My parents always have dinner together at 6:30 pm.
7. The students are rarely late for class.
8. I spend two hours reading books every evening.
