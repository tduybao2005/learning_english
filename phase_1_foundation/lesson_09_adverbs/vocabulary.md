## BÀI TẬP TỪ VỰNG (Vocabulary Exercises)

### Exercise 1: Form the Adverb
*Chuyển tính từ thành trạng từ*

1. angry → ____________
2. happy → ____________
3. terrible → ____________
4. automatic → ____________
5. good → ____________
6. easy → ____________
7. basic → ____________
8. gentle → ____________
9. dramatic → ____________
10. hard → ____________ (careful! same form)

---

### Exercise 2: Choose the correct form (ADJ or ADV)
*Chọn dạng tính từ hay trạng từ*

1. She is a (careful / carefully) driver. She always drives (careful / carefully).
2. The singer has a (beautiful / beautifully) voice. She sings (beautiful / beautifully).
3. He is (extreme / extremely) (good / well) at math. He scores (perfect / perfectly) every time.
4. The dog looks (angry / angrily). It is barking (angry / angrily) at strangers.
5. After the long hike, we felt (tired / tiredly). We walked so (slow / slowly).

---

### Exercise 3: Put the frequency adverb in the correct position
*Đặt trạng từ tần suất đúng vị trí*

1. She is late to work. (never) → ___________________________________
2. He eats breakfast at home. (usually) → ___________________________________
3. They have been to that restaurant. (often) → ___________________________________
4. I drink alcohol. (hardly ever) → ___________________________________
5. The bus is on time. (not always) → ___________________________________

---

### Đáp án:

**Exercise 1:**
1. angrily 2. happily 3. terribly 4. automatically 5. well 6. easily 7. basically 8. gently 9. dramatically 10. hard (same)

**Exercise 2:**
1. careful / carefully
2. beautiful / beautifully
3. extremely / good / perfectly
4. angry / angrily
5. tired / slowly

**Exercise 3:**
1. She is **never** late to work.
2. He **usually** eats breakfast at home.
3. They have **often** been to that restaurant.
4. I **hardly ever** drink alcohol.
5. The bus is **not always** on time.
