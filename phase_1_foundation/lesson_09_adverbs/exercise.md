## SECTION A: FILL IN THE BLANK
*12 câu — Điền trạng từ đúng dạng*

**A1.** She is a careful driver. She always drives ________. (careful → adverb)

**A2.** He is very angry about the situation. He shouted ________ at his team. (angry → adverb)

**A3.** The teacher speaks ________ so all the students can understand. (clear → adverb)

**A4.** She is a fluent English speaker. She can speak English ________. (fluent → adverb)

**A5.** I ________ go to bed before midnight. I am a night owl. (choose: always / never / rarely)

**A6.** He has ________ finished his report. He sent it to his boss an hour ago. (choose: already / yet / still)

**A7.** Have you done your homework ________? (choose: already / yet / just)

**A8.** She ________ studies English — at least 3 hours every day. That's why she's improving. (choose: hard / hardly)

**A9.** The weather is ________ cold today — about 8 degrees! (choose: extremely / absolutely — pick the more common one for gradable adjective)

**A10.** He ________ eats fast food. Maybe once a year. It's bad for his health. (choose: rarely / usually)

**A11.** The children played ________ in the garden all afternoon. (choose: happy → adverb form)

**A12.** I ________ remember where I put my keys! I am so forgetful. (choose: never / hardly ever)

## PHẦN H: SPEAKING / DISCUSSION PROMPT

*Trả lời miệng hoặc viết câu trả lời, dùng cấu trúc/từ vựng của bài.*

1. Describe how you typically work or study. Do you work quickly and efficiently, or do you prefer to go slowly and carefully?
2. How do you think people should behave in public places such as libraries, cinemas, or hospitals?
3. Describe a person you know who does their job particularly well. How do they perform their duties?
4. In your opinion, how important is it to speak English fluently? What can you do to improve quickly?

**Model answer (Band 7–8):** "I believe I work most effectively in the early morning when the environment is quiet and I can think clearly. I tend to work quite methodically — I carefully plan my tasks at the beginning of each day and then systematically work through them one by one. When I am under pressure, I sometimes rush slightly and make minor errors, so I always try to review my work thoroughly before submitting it. I genuinely enjoy working as part of a team because colleagues often handle things more efficiently together than individually. Communicating clearly and listening actively are skills I am constantly trying to improve."

### Section B:
B1. b) sounded beautiful (sound = linking verb → adjective)
B2. b) still works as (frequency adverb before main verb)
B3. b) hard (adverb = hard, not "hardly" which means "almost not")
B4. c) She is always on time. (after "be")
B5. a) a bit (gradable degree; "absolutely" for absolutes only)
B6. a) beautifully / last (manner then time)
B7. b) too (too + adj = so much that... can't do something)
B8. b) has just (just comes after first auxiliary verb)
B9. d) unusually warm (adverb modifying adjective "warm")
B10. b) hardly ("hardly speak" = almost cannot speak)

### Section C:
C1. She works slowly.
C2. He plays guitar well.
C3. She travels frequently.
C4. The students solved the problem easily.
C5. She always attended every class.
C6. He spoke loudly at the meeting.
C7. She is improving remarkably.
C8. The accident happened suddenly / unexpectedly.

### Section D:
D1. "beautiful" → **beautifully** (action verb "sings" needs adverb)
→ *She sings very beautifully.*
D2. "hardly works" → **works hard** (hardly = almost not; hard = with effort)
→ *He works hard 8 hours a day...*
D3. "usually am" → **am usually** (after "be": be + frequency adverb)
→ *I am usually late on Mondays...*
D4. "wonderfully" → **wonderful** (smell = linking verb → adjective)
→ *The soup smells wonderful.*
D5. "finished quickly the report" → **quickly finished the report** OR **finished the report quickly**
→ *She quickly finished the report...* OR *She finished the report quickly...*

## PHẦN H — GỢI Ý

*Câu trả lời tốt nên bao gồm:*
- Trạng từ chỉ cách thức dùng đúng vị trí: sau động từ (work efficiently, listen carefully) hoặc trước tính từ (quite methodical, slightly nervous)
- Trạng từ tần suất: always, often, sometimes, constantly đặt trước động từ chính hoặc sau to be
- Liên kết diễn ngôn: When I am under pressure, so, because, together than individually
