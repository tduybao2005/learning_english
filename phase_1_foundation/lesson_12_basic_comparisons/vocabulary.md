### Exercise 2: Choose the Correct Comparison Word (10 items)

*Chọn từ đúng trong ngoặc:*

1. She is ________ (taller / more tall) than her mother by five centimetres.
2. This is ________ (the most expensive / the expensivest) restaurant in the city.
3. He speaks English ________ (more fluently / fluenter) than his classmates.
4. Today is ________ (much hot / much hotter) than yesterday.
5. She is ________ (as clever as / as cleverer as) her twin sister.
6. The new model is ________ (more better / much better) than the old one.
7. He has ________ (fewer / less) books than she has.
8. My grandfather is the ________ (older / oldest) person in our village.
9. This solution is ________ (more effective / effectiver) than the previous approach.
10. There are ________ (less / fewer) tourists this year because of the economic situation.

**Đáp án Exercise 2:**
1. taller | 2. the most expensive | 3. more fluently | 4. much hotter | 5. as clever as | 6. much better | 7. fewer | 8. oldest | 9. more effective | 10. fewer
