### Bài tập 2: Điền vào chỗ trống (Fill in the Blank)

**Word Bank:** is crying, are travelling, is designing, am waiting, is always complaining, are watching, is hiking, is shouting, are arguing, is resting

Điền từ/cụm từ đúng vào chỗ trống. Dùng Present Continuous.

1. The baby __________________ because she is hungry.
2. My boss __________________ at the staff because of a mistake. He is very angry.
3. We __________________ for the bus. It's 10 minutes late already.
4. My parents __________________ about money again. I wish they would stop.
5. The family __________________ around Southeast Asia this summer.
6. He __________________ in the mountains near Sapa right now.
7. She __________________ a new website for a client. It looks amazing.
8. The whole family __________________ a movie together on the sofa.
9. He __________________ after running a marathon. He is exhausted.
10. She __________________ about the food at the restaurant. She never likes anything!

**Đáp án:**
1. is crying | 2. is shouting | 3. am waiting | 4. are arguing | 5. are travelling | 6. is hiking | 7. is designing | 8. are watching | 9. is resting | 10. is always complaining

### Bài tập 3: Viết câu và phân loại (Sentence Writing & Classification)

Với mỗi tình huống bên dưới, viết một câu ở Thì Hiện Tại Tiếp Diễn và cho biết đó là cách dùng gì (A: đang xảy ra / B: kế hoạch tương lai / C: xu hướng / D: phàn nàn):

1. You look out the window and see dark clouds forming. → _____________________ (Cách dùng: ___)
2. You have already booked a table at a restaurant for tomorrow night. → _____________________ (Cách dùng: ___)
3. Your roommate leaves dirty dishes in the sink every single day. You are frustrated. → _____________________ (Cách dùng: ___)
4. You notice that electric vehicles are becoming more popular. → _____________________ (Cách dùng: ___)
5. Right now, your friend is on the phone and cannot talk to you. → _____________________ (Cách dùng: ___)

**Đáp án gợi ý:**
1. The sky is getting darker. / It is going to rain. (Cách dùng: A)
2. We are having dinner at an Italian restaurant tomorrow. (Cách dùng: B)
3. My roommate is always leaving dirty dishes in the sink! (Cách dùng: D)
4. Electric vehicles are becoming more popular these days. (Cách dùng: C)
5. She is talking on the phone right now. (Cách dùng: A)
