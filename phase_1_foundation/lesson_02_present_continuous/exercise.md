# EXERCISE - BÀI 2: THÌ HIỆN TẠI TIẾP DIỄN (PRESENT CONTINUOUS)
## Bài tập tổng hợp

**Tổng số câu: 40 | Thời gian làm bài: 45-60 phút**

---

## SECTION A: FILL IN THE BLANK (Điền vào chỗ trống)
*Chia động từ trong ngoặc ở dạng đúng của Thì Hiện Tại Tiếp Diễn.*

**A1 — Câu khẳng định**

1. Look out the window! It ____________ (rain) heavily.
2. I can't talk right now — I ____________ (have) an important meeting.
3. She ____________ (study) for her final exams this week.
4. The workers ____________ (build) a new bridge across the river.
5. Listen! Someone ____________ (sing) upstairs. What a beautiful voice!
6. He ____________ (run) in the park — I can see him from here.
7. More and more people ____________ (use) public transport these days.
8. My sister ____________ (plan) her wedding for next spring.

**A2 — Câu phủ định**

9. She ____________ (not / watch) TV. She's reading a book.
10. We ____________ (not / go) to the beach next weekend after all — the weather looks bad.
11. He ____________ (not / pay) attention in class right now.
12. They ____________ (not / argue). They're just having a loud conversation.

**A3 — Câu hỏi**

13. ____________ (be) she ____________ (come) to the party tonight?
14. What ____________ you ____________ (cook)? It smells delicious!
15. Why ____________ he ____________ (laugh)? Did something funny happen?

## SECTION B: MULTIPLE CHOICE (Trắc nghiệm)
*Chọn đáp án đúng (A, B, C, hoặc D).*

16. "Where's Tom?" — "He ______ a shower. He'll be out in a minute."
    - A) has
    - B) is having
    - C) have
    - D) having

17. She ______ to music when I called her.
    - A) is listening
    - B) listens
    - C) was listening *(Chú ý: câu này nên dùng Past Continuous — nhận biết và chọn đáp án đúng nhất trong ngữ cảnh hiện tại)*
    - D) listened

18. I ______ you ______ better every day. Keep it up!
    - A) see / getting
    - B) can see / getting
    - C) am seeing / get
    - D) see / are getting

19. He ______ always ______ his socks on the floor! It's so annoying!
    - A) is / leaving
    - B) does / leave
    - C) has / left
    - D) — / leaving

20. "Can I speak to Mr. Brown?" — "I'm afraid he ______ a client right now."
    - A) meets
    - B) is meeting
    - C) meet
    - D) meeting

21. Electric cars ______ more popular in Vietnam these days.
    - A) become
    - B) became
    - C) are becoming
    - D) is becoming

22. Which sentence uses Present Continuous CORRECTLY?
    - A) She is knowing the answer.
    - B) I am wanting a coffee.
    - C) They are playing football in the garden.
    - D) He is having a sister.

23. "What ______ you ______ this weekend?" "I ______ my grandparents."
    - A) do / do / visit
    - B) are / doing / am visiting
    - C) do / doing / am visiting
    - D) are / doing / visit

24. Look at the sky! Those clouds ______ very dark. It ______ soon.
    - A) get / rains
    - B) are getting / is going to rain
    - C) get / is going to rain
    - D) are getting / rains

25. She ______ in France for a year as part of her university course.
    - A) lives
    - B) is living
    - C) live
    - D) was living

### Section D:
34. "is know" → **knows** (She knows all the answers... — stative verb)
35. "wait" → **waiting** (Are they still waiting for the bus?)
36. "runing" → **running** (He is running very fast... — double 'n')
37. "am not understanding" → **don't understand** (I don't understand... — stative verb)
38. "is" → **are** (They are having lunch... — plural subject)
39. "always is" → **is always** (She is always complaining...)
40. "What he is doing" → **What is he doing** (invert subject & auxiliary)
41. "lieing" → **lying** (I am lying on the beach... — ie → y + ing)
