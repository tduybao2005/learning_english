## BÀI TẬP TỪ VỰNG (Vocabulary Exercises)

### Exercise 1: Fill in the correct pronoun
*Điền đại từ đúng (subject/object/possessive adj/possessive pronoun)*

Complete the sentences with the correct pronoun from the box:
**[ him / she / their / mine / herself / they / our / it / us / his ]**

1. Tom is a great teacher. ________ explains everything clearly.
2. Maria cooked dinner ________. Nobody helped her.
3. That red bicycle is not yours — it's ________.
4. The children lost ________ way home. ________ were scared.
5. My parents live far away. I miss ________ very much.
6. This is our house. The blue one is ________.
7. He hurt ________ while playing football yesterday.
8. Anna doesn't talk to ________. She avoids him completely.
9. ________ dog is very intelligent. ________ can open doors!
10. We invited ________ neighbors to our party.

---

### Exercise 2: Choose the correct word
*Chọn từ đúng trong ngoặc*

1. Is this (your / yours) umbrella? No, it's not (my / mine).
2. She introduced (her / herself) to the class.
3. (They / Them) are (my / mine) best friends.
4. Nobody told (I / me) about the problem.
5. (Everyone / Every one) was quiet when (she / her) spoke.
6. The dog wagged (its / it's) tail when (it / its) saw (us / we).
7. (Him and I / He and I) went to the cinema together.
8. Something (is / are) wrong with this phone.
9. Can (anyone / someone) help me, please? [asking a group of people a question]
10. (Nobody / No body) knows (where / everywhere) she went.

2. This is your → **This is yours**
