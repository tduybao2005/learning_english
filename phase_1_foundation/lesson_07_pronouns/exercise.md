## SECTION B: MULTIPLE CHOICE (Chọn đáp án đúng)
*10 câu*

**B1.** My sister loves ________ new apartment. She says ________ is beautiful.
- a) her / it
- b) hers / its
- c) her / its
- d) hers / it

**B2.** Could ________ help ________ with this heavy box, please?
- a) you / I
- b) you / me
- c) your / mine
- d) yours / me

**B3.** The cat is cleaning ________ paws. ________ does this after every meal.
- a) it's / It
- b) its / It
- c) its / Its
- d) it's / Its

**B4.** ________ is at the door? — ________ is the delivery man.
- a) Who / Him
- b) Who / He
- c) Whom / He
- d) Who / His

**B5.** She lives alone. She takes care of ________ very well.
- a) her
- b) herself
- c) hers
- d) she

**B6.** ________ in the class got 100% on the test. The teacher was very pleased.
- a) Anybody
- b) Nobody
- c) Everybody
- d) Somebody

**B7.** —Is this ________ notebook or ________?
- a) your / my
- b) your / mine
- c) yours / my
- d) yours / mine

**B8.** My parents gave my brother and ________ the same amount of pocket money.
- a) I
- b) myself
- c) me
- d) mine

**B9.** ________ should take responsibility for ________ own mistakes.
- a) Everyone / their
- b) Everyone / his
- c) Everyone / they
- d) Every one / theirs

**B10.** Would you like ________ to drink? We have tea and coffee.
- a) nothing
- b) anything
- c) something
- d) everything

### Section D:
D1. He → **She** (mother = feminine)
D2. me → **I** (subject pronoun needed: "She and I")
D3. it's → **its** (possessive, no apostrophe) AND "its so fluffy" → "**it's** so fluffy" (it is)

D4. are → **is** (Everybody = singular)
D5. hisself → **himself**
D6. isn't nobody → **is nobody** (no double negative) OR change to "isn't anybody"
