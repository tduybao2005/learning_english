# EXERCISE - BÀI 4: THÌ TƯƠNG LAI (WILL / GOING TO / PRESENT CONTINUOUS)
## Bài tập tổng hợp

**Tổng số câu: 38 | Thời gian làm bài: 45-55 phút**

---

## SECTION A: FILL IN THE BLANK (Điền vào chỗ trống)
*Dùng will, going to, hoặc Present Continuous — chọn dạng phù hợp nhất với ngữ cảnh.*

1. "The phone is ringing." — "I ____________ (get) it!" *(quyết định tức thì)*
2. She ____________ (study) in Australia next year — she already accepted the offer. *(kế hoạch)*
3. Look at that child! He ____________ (fall) off the swing! *(bằng chứng trước mắt)*
4. I think the government ____________ (invest) more in education in the future. *(dự đoán)*
5. They ____________ (have) dinner at the Italian restaurant on Friday night — the table is booked. *(đã đặt)*
6. "We've run out of coffee." — "Don't worry. I ____________ (go) to the shop right now." *(tức thì)*
7. She ____________ (not / accept) the job offer. She already told them. *(kế hoạch đã quyết định)*
8. ____________ you ____________ (do) anything on Sunday afternoon? *(hỏi về kế hoạch)*
9. The weather forecast says it ____________ (be) very cold this weekend. *(dự báo)*
10. I promise I ____________ (not / tell) anyone your secret. *(lời hứa — will)*
11. He ____________ (retire) at 65 — it's already decided with the company. *(kế hoạch)*
12. They ____________ (probably / build) a new school in our area next year. *(dự đoán)*

## SECTION B: MULTIPLE CHOICE (Trắc nghiệm)
*Chọn đáp án đúng nhất (A, B, C, hoặc D).*

13. "There's someone at the door." — "I ______ it."
    - A) am going to answer
    - B) will answer
    - C) answer
    - D) am answering

14. Look at the sky. Those black clouds — it ______ soon.
    - A) will rain
    - B) is going to rain
    - C) rains
    - D) is raining

15. She's been saving up for a year. She ______ a new motorbike next month.
    - A) will buy
    - B) is going to buy
    - C) buys
    - D) bought

16. I think electric vehicles ______ very common in 10 years.
    - A) are going to be
    - B) will be
    - C) are being
    - D) have been

17. "I can't carry all these bags." — "Don't worry, I ______ you."
    - A) am going to help
    - B) help
    - C) will help
    - D) am helping

18. They ______ married next spring — the invitations have already been sent.
    - A) will get
    - B) get
    - C) are getting
    - D) are going to get

19. "______ she ______ to the meeting tomorrow?"
    - A) Will / come
    - B) Is / coming
    - C) Does / come
    - D) A or B are both possible here

20. In the future, people probably ______ live on Mars.
    - A) are going to
    - B) will
    - C) are
    - D) going to

21. The president ______ make an important announcement at 8 pm tonight.
    - A) is about to
    - B) will about to
    - C) is going about to
    - D) about to

22. ______ you please close the window? It's cold in here.
    - A) Would / are
    - B) Will
    - C) Are going to
    - D) Shall

## ANSWER KEY (ĐÁP ÁN)

### Section A:
1. will get ('ll get)
2. is going to study
3. is going to fall
4. will invest
5. are having
6. will go ('ll go)
7. is not going to accept
8. Are / going to do (OR: Are you doing — both OK)
9. is going to be / will be (both acceptable with "forecast")
10. won't tell / will not tell
11. is going to retire
12. will probably build

### Section B:
13. B (will answer — spontaneous decision)
14. B (is going to rain — evidence: black clouds)
15. B (is going to buy — pre-planned, saving for a year)
16. B (will be — general prediction/opinion)
17. C (will help — spontaneous offer)
18. C (are getting — arranged, invitations sent)
19. D (both A and B are possible for future arrangements)
20. B (will — general prediction about future)
21. A (is about to — happening very soon)
22. B (Will — polite request)

### Section C:
23. She is not going to visit Japan next summer.
24. Will he call you tomorrow?
25. When are they having the meeting?
26. I don't think she will pass the exam.
27. What is he going to buy?
28. The show will start at 7 pm, won't it?
29. The weather will maybe/perhaps improve by the weekend. (OR: The weather might improve...)
30. Shall I help you with your bags? / Would you like me to help you with your bags?

### Section D:
31. "will going" → **will go** (I will go to the cinema... — no "-ing" after "will")
32. "going to to" → **going to** (She is going to study... — only one "to")
33. "will collapse" → **is going to collapse** (visible evidence — it's already cracking)
34. "goes to be" → **is going to be** (I think she is going to be...)
35. "comes" → **come** (Will he come... — base form after "will")
36. "I am meeting... I just decided right now" → **I will meet / I'll meet** (spontaneous decision)
37. "becomes" → **become** (will definitely become — base form after "will")

### Section E (Sample Answers):
38. Look at those clouds! It is going to rain heavily. We should go home right away / immediately.
39. I will never forget your kindness. Thank you so much.
40. She is planning to / She is going to study abroad in the UK after she graduates next year.
41. The phone is ringing. — I'll get it! It's probably him calling.
42. What are you going to do tonight? / What are you planning to do tonight? — I'm going to stay home and study. I have a test tomorrow.
43. In the future, artificial intelligence will change many industries / professions.
44. I have already booked a table at the restaurant — we are eating / having dinner there on Friday evening.
45. It seems like he is going to get promoted — the director just called him into the office.
