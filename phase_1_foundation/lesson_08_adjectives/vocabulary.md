## BÀI TẬP TỪ VỰNG (Vocabulary Exercises)

### Exercise 1: Choose -ED or -ING
*Chọn dạng đúng của tính từ*

1. The documentary about sharks was really ________ (fascinating / fascinated). I was ________ (fascinating / fascinated) by it.

2. She felt very ________ (embarrassing / embarrassed) when she tripped in front of everyone. It was an ________ (embarrassing / embarrassed) moment.

3. The students were ________ (boring / bored) because the lesson was so ________ (boring / bored).

4. I was ________ (surprising / surprised) by the ________ (surprising / surprised) result of the match.

5. The hike up the mountain was ________ (exhausting / exhausted), but the view was ________ (breathtaking / breathtaken).

---

### Exercise 2: Put the adjectives in the correct OSASCOMP order
*Sắp xếp các tính từ theo thứ tự đúng*

1. a / car / red / big / old / Italian → ___________________________________
2. a / woman / young / beautiful / Vietnamese → ___________________________________
3. some / running / comfortable / new / shoes / white → ___________________________________
4. a / tiny / wooden / lovely / old / cottage → ___________________________________
5. a / cold / glass / tall / wonderful / of / lemonade → ___________________________________

---

### Exercise 3: Fill in the blank with the correct adjective
*Chọn tính từ phù hợp từ danh sách: [ reliable / stubborn / spacious / ancient / crowded / generous / impatient ]*

1. My friend ________ his lunch with everyone. He is so ________.
2. The ________ temple was built over 500 years ago. It looks very ________.
3. The market was so ________ that we could barely move.
4. She is ________ and never listens to advice. You cannot change her mind.
5. He gets ________ when he has to wait in a long queue.
6. The new office is ________ and modern, with lots of natural light.
7. She always comes on time and finishes her work. She is very ________.

---

### Đáp án:

**Exercise 1:**
1. fascinating / fascinated
2. embarrassed / embarrassing
3. bored / boring
4. surprised / surprising
5. exhausting / breathtaking

**Exercise 2:**
1. a big old red Italian car (S-A-C-Or)
2. a beautiful young Vietnamese woman (O-S-Or)
3. some comfortable new white running shoes (O-A-C-P)
4. a lovely tiny old wooden cottage (O-S-A-M)
5. a wonderful tall glass of cold lemonade (O-S-M / the last group describes the drink)

**Exercise 3:**
1. shared / generous
2. ancient / old (hoặc ancient)
3. crowded
4. stubborn
5. impatient
6. spacious
7. reliable
