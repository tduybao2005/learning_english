## PHẦN 1: BẢNG TỪ VỰNG (65 Words)

### Nhóm A: Liên từ kết hợp — FANBOYS (Coordinating Conjunctions)

| Word | /IPA/ | Nghĩa / Chức năng | Ví dụ câu |
|------|-------|-------------------|-----------|
| and | /ænd/ | và (bổ sung) | She is kind **and** intelligent. |
| but | /bʌt/ | nhưng (tương phản) | I wanted to go, **but** I was too tired. |
| so | /səʊ/ | vì vậy (kết quả) | It rained, **so** we cancelled the trip. |
| or | /ɔːr/ | hoặc (lựa chọn) | You can call **or** send an email. |
| for | /fɔːr/ | bởi vì (lý do — văn viết) | She was quiet, **for** she had nothing to say. |
| yet | /jet/ | nhưng (bất ngờ) | He is old, **yet** he is very energetic. |
| nor | /nɔːr/ | cũng không | She didn't write, **nor** did she call. |

|------|-------|-------|-----------|
| when | /wen/ | khi | Call me **when** you arrive. |
| while | /waɪl/ | trong khi | He reads **while** she cooks. |
| before | /bɪˈfɔːr/ | trước khi | Brush your teeth **before** you go to bed. |
| after | /ˈɑːftər/ | sau khi | **After** the meeting ended, we had lunch. |
| since | /sɪns/ | kể từ khi | She has worked here **since** she graduated. |
| until | /ənˈtɪl/ | cho đến khi | Wait here **until** I get back. |
| as soon as | /æz suːn æz/ | ngay khi | I'll call you **as soon as** I land. |
| by the time | /baɪ ðə taɪm/ | vào lúc mà | **By the time** he arrived, we had left. |

### Nhóm C: Liên từ phụ thuộc — Lý do, Tương phản, Điều kiện

| Word | /IPA/ | Nghĩa / Chức năng | Ví dụ câu |
|------|-------|-------------------|-----------|
| because | /bɪˈkɒz/ | bởi vì (lý do chính) | She left early **because** she was tired. |

### Nhóm D: Đại từ quan hệ (Relative Pronouns)

| Word | /IPA/ | Dùng cho | Ví dụ câu |
|------|-------|----------|-----------|
| who | /huː/ | người (S hoặc O) | The woman **who** called is my doctor. |
| whom | /huːm/ | người (O — văn viết) | The man **whom** she met was famous. |
| which | /wɪtʃ/ | vật, động vật | The book **which** I read was excellent. |
| that | /ðæt/ | người hoặc vật (mệnh đề xác định) | The car **that** she bought is red. |
| whose | /huːz/ | sở hữu (của ai) | The girl **whose** bag was stolen cried. |
| where | /weər/ | nơi chốn | The café **where** we met is now closed. |
| when | /wen/ | thời gian | I remember the day **when** we first met. |

### Nhóm E: Từ nối câu / Adverbial Connectors (Linking Expressions)

| Word/Phrase | /IPA/ | Nghĩa / Chức năng | Ví dụ |
|-------------|-------|-------------------|-------|
| however | /haʊˈevər/ | tuy nhiên (tương phản) | She tried; **however**, she failed. |
| therefore | /ˈðeərfɔːr/ | vì vậy (kết quả-logic) | He worked hard; **therefore**, he succeeded. |
| furthermore | /ˈfɜːðəmɔːr/ | hơn nữa (bổ sung) | The hotel was expensive; **furthermore**, it was dirty. |
| moreover | /mɔːrˈəʊvər/ | thêm vào đó (mạnh hơn) | She is talented; **moreover**, she is hardworking. |
| in addition | /ɪn əˈdɪʃn/ | thêm vào đó | **In addition**, the price includes breakfast. |
| nevertheless | /ˌnevərðəˈles/ | tuy nhiên (mặc dù vậy) | It was raining; **nevertheless**, we went out. |
| on the other hand | — | mặt khác | City life is exciting. **On the other hand**, it can be stressful. |
| as a result | /æz ə rɪˈzʌlt/ | do đó, vì vậy | She missed the bus. **As a result**, she was late. |
| for example | /fər ɪɡˈzɑːmpl/ | ví dụ như | I love sports, **for example**, swimming and tennis. |
| in conclusion | /ɪn kənˈkluːʒn/ | tóm lại | **In conclusion**, we should protect the environment. |

### Nhóm F: Từ vựng về cấu trúc câu (Sentence Structure Vocabulary)

| Word | /IPA/ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ câu |
|------|-------|-------|-----------|
| clause | /klɔːz/ | mệnh đề | A **clause** has both a subject and a verb. |
| phrase | /freɪz/ | cụm từ (không có S+V đầy đủ) | "in the morning" is a **phrase**. |
| subject | /ˈsʌbdʒɪkt/ | chủ ngữ | The **subject** of the sentence is "she". |
| predicate | /ˈpredɪkət/ | vị ngữ | The **predicate** contains the verb. |
| independent clause | — | mệnh đề độc lập | "She sings" is an **independent clause**. |
| dependent clause | — | mệnh đề phụ thuộc | "because she loves it" is a **dependent clause**. |
| conjunction | /kənˈdʒʌŋkʃn/ | liên từ | "And" and "but" are **conjunctions**. |
| sentence | /ˈsentəns/ | câu | A complete **sentence** needs S + V. |
| fragment | /ˈfræɡmənt/ | mảnh câu, câu chưa hoàn chỉnh | "Running in the park." is a **fragment**. |
| run-on | /ˈrʌn ɒn/ | câu dài không có dấu câu đúng | "She smiled he laughed" is a **run-on**. |

### Exercise 2: Join Two Sentences Using the Given Conjunction

*Nối hai câu lại thành một câu bằng liên từ cho sẵn. Chú ý dấu phẩy.*

1. She was ill. She came to school. (although)
   → _______________________________________________

2. I missed the first bus. I was late for work. (so)
   → _______________________________________________

3. He saved money for a year. He bought a motorbike. (and)
   → _______________________________________________

4. You can take the train. You can take the bus. (or)
   → _______________________________________________

5. She didn't study. She didn't pass the exam. (because / result)
   → _______________________________________________

6. He is a student. He works part-time at a restaurant. (but)
   → _______________________________________________

7. I'll call you. I arrive at the airport. (as soon as)
   → _______________________________________________

8. She turned off the lights. She left the room. (before)
   → _______________________________________________

9. He was nervous. He gave an excellent presentation. (although)
   → _______________________________________________

10. She didn't have enough money. She couldn't buy the dress. (so)
    → _______________________________________________

**Đáp án Exercise 2:**
1. Although she was ill, she came to school.
2. I missed the first bus, so I was late for work.
3. He saved money for a year and bought a motorbike.
4. You can take the train or the bus.
5. She didn't pass the exam because she didn't study.
6. He is a student, but he works part-time at a restaurant.
7. I'll call you as soon as I arrive at the airport.
8. She turned off the lights before she left the room.
9. Although he was nervous, he gave an excellent presentation.
10. She didn't have enough money, so she couldn't buy the dress.

### Exercise 3: Fix Run-on Sentences and Fragments

*Sửa các câu lỗi sau đây:*

**Run-on sentences:**
1. She is a very talented singer she performs at weddings and events every weekend.
   Sửa: _______________________________________________

2. I love reading books I go to the library every Saturday I always borrow three or four books.
   Sửa: _______________________________________________

**Sentence fragments:**
3. Because she was nervous about the exam.
   Sửa: _______________________________________________

4. The young woman standing by the window in a red dress.
   Sửa: _______________________________________________

5. Walking home alone at midnight through the empty streets.
   Sửa: _______________________________________________

**Comma splices:**
6. It was his birthday, we baked him a cake.
   Sửa: _______________________________________________

7. The weather was beautiful, we decided to go to the beach.
   Sửa: _______________________________________________

**Đáp án Exercise 3:**
1. She is a very talented singer. She performs at weddings and events every weekend. *(hoặc: ...singer, and she performs...)*
2. I love reading books. I go to the library every Saturday and always borrow three or four books.
3. She was nervous about the exam. *(hoặc: Because she was nervous about the exam, she couldn't sleep.)*
4. The young woman standing by the window in a red dress **is** my cousin.
5. **She was** walking home alone at midnight through the empty streets.
6. It was his birthday, **so** we baked him a cake.
7. The weather was beautiful, **so** we decided to go to the beach.
