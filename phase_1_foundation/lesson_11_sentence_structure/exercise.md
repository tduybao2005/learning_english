## ANSWER KEY (Đáp án)

### Section A:
1. My sister(S) works(V) at a hospital in the city(A).
2. The children(S) seem(V) very happy(C) today(A).
3. He(S) gave(V) his teacher(IO) a beautiful bouquet of flowers(DO).
4. They(S) live(V) in a small apartment near the university(A).
5. The old man(S) smiled(V) warmly(A) at the young students(A).
6. She(S) sent(V) her parents(IO) an email(DO) from London(A).
7. The soup(S) tastes(V) delicious(C) with a little salt(A).
8. My father(S) drives(V) to work(A) every morning(A).
9. The team(S) won(V) the championship(O) last year(A).
10. She(S) became(V) a professional pianist(C) at the age of 20(A).

### Section B:
11. Simple | 1 independent clause
12. Compound | 2 independent clauses (she was tired + she went to bed early)
13. Complex | 1 dependent (Although...) + 1 independent
14. Compound | 2 independent clauses (I like reading + she likes drawing)
15. Complex | 1 independent + 1 dependent relative clause (who called you)
16. Compound-Complex | 1 dependent (Even though...) + 2 independent clauses joined by "and"
17. Simple | 1 subject, 3 verbs (compound predicate)
18. Complex | 1 independent + 1 dependent (when I get home)
19. Compound | 2 independent clauses joined by semicolon
20. Compound-Complex | 1 dependent (Because...) + 2 independent (she was able to travel + she visited five countries)

### Section C (Gợi ý đáp án):
21. She wanted to visit her parents, but she didn't have enough time.
22. Because he didn't study for the test, he failed, so he had to retake it.
23. The restaurant was full, so we decided to eat somewhere else.
24. She is a great cook, and she has opened her own restaurant. *(hoặc: She is such a great cook that she opened her own restaurant.)*
25. I wake up at 6 am, then exercise for 30 minutes, and then have breakfast.
26. He had never visited the south of Vietnam until he moved to Ho Chi Minh City last year.
27. Although the film got bad reviews, we watched it, and we enjoyed it a lot.
28. After she studied medicine for 6 years, she became a doctor and worked in a rural hospital.

### Section D:
29. Run-on sentence → He is a good student. He studies every night and never misses class. *(hoặc: He is a good student; he studies every night...)*
30. Sentence fragment (dependent clause only) → Because she loves animals, she volunteers at a shelter. *(thêm mệnh đề chính)*
31. Comma splice → She is very talented; she won three awards last year. *(hoặc: ...talented, and she won...)*
32. Sentence fragment (no verb) → The tall building on the corner of the street next to the park **is** a new hotel.
33. Run-on sentence → He went to the store, bought milk, bread, and vegetables, then came home and cooked dinner.
34. Sentence fragment (dependent clause only) → After the concert ended, we went out for dinner.
35. Comma splice → The book was interesting, so I read it in one day. *(hoặc: ...interesting; I read it...)*
36. Sentence fragment (no subject/main verb) → **She runs** every morning before work for two hours.

### Section E (Gợi ý đáp án):
37. Although it rained heavily, we still decided to go on the picnic, and we had a very enjoyable morning.
38. If you practise every day, your English will improve quickly.
39. The teacher explained the lesson so clearly that all the students understood.
40. He is not only intelligent but also very hardworking, so he always tops the class.
41. The woman standing at the school gate is my friend's mother.
42. She saved money for two years so that she could study abroad.
