## ANSWER KEY (Đáp án)

### Section A:
1. in / in (year → in; place → in) | 2. at / on (specific time → at; day of week → on) | 3. on (day of week) | 4. at / at (specific time → at; airport/station → at) | 5. in / in (large areas → in) | 6. at / on (specific time → at; weekend → on) | 7. at / in / on (place → at; city centre → in; period → on) | 8. on (surface: wall) | 9. in / on (park → in; corner → on) | 10. at / on (workplace → at; floor → on) | 11. at / at (specific place → at; exact time → at) | 12. In / at (season → in; part of day → at) | 13. on (surface: table) | 14. in / on / at (city → in; day → on; time → at) | 15. in / on (month → in; specific period → on)

### Section B:
16. opposite | 17. under | 18. beside / next to | 19. below | 20. near | 21. between | 22. in front of | 23. behind | 24. above | 25. next to / beside

### Section C:
26. across | 27. out of | 28. along | 29. through | 30. from / to | 31. into | 32. past | 33. to / from (*hoặc:* from ... to)

### Section D:
34. "at the evening" → **in** the evening *(in the morning/afternoon/evening)*
35. "in 14th March" → **on** 14th March *(ngày cụ thể → on)*
36. "on midnight" → **at** midnight *(thời điểm chính xác → at)*
37. "in Monday" → **on** Monday *(ngày trong tuần → on)*
38. "in last night" → không cần giới từ hoặc "last night" (không dùng giới từ trước last/next + time)
39. "in Christmas" → **at** Christmas *(kỳ nghỉ/lễ → at: at Christmas, at Easter, at Tet)*
