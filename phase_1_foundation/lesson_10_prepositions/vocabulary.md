## BÀI TẬP TỪ VỰNG (Vocabulary Exercises)

### Exercise 1: Fill in IN, ON, or AT
*Điền IN, ON, hoặc AT phù hợp*

1. She was born _____ 1998 _____ a cold day _____ January.
2. The meeting is _____ Monday _____ 10 o'clock _____ the morning.
3. He lives _____ a small apartment _____ the third floor _____ a building _____ District 3.
4. I'll see you _____ the airport _____ next Tuesday.
5. She is studying _____ the library. She usually studies there _____ the evenings.
6. The children played _____ the park _____ the afternoon _____ Saturday.
7. He was born _____ Vietnam _____ the 1990s. He now lives _____ France.
8. The café is _____ the corner _____ Le Thanh Ton Street.

---

### Exercise 2: Match the collocation
*Nối giới từ phù hợp*

**Left column (Phrase):**
1. She did it ___ (not accidentally, she meant to do it)
2. He called me ___ (wrong number)
3. The old building was ___ (burning)
4. She arrived ___ (not late, exactly when scheduled)
5. We got there ___ (before it was too late)

**Right column (Choose from):**
- by mistake / on purpose / on fire / on time / in time

---

### Exercise 3: Give directions
*Điền giới từ chỉ hướng*

You are at the school. To get to the hospital:
- Walk __________ the road (along) for about 200 meters.
- Turn left __________ the traffic lights.
- The hospital is __________ the right, __________ the bank and the post office.
- If you pass the park, you have gone too far. Come __________ and try again.

---
