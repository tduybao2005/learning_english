## PHẦN C — ĐỌC HIỂU (Đáp án mẫu)
**Đoạn văn mẫu (giám thị đọc):**
"Maria is a teacher who lives in Hanoi. Every morning she wakes up at 6am and prepares breakfast for her family. She usually travels to school by motorbike. Last weekend, she visited her parents in the countryside. She has already planned her summer holiday — she is going to visit Da Nang next month."

1. TRUE (She wakes up at 6am every morning — explicitly stated)
2. FALSE (She travels by motorbike, not by bus)
3. NOT GIVEN (Nothing about her students' performance)
4. She visited her parents in the countryside.
5. She is going to visit Da Nang.
