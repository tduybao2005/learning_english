**HƯỚNG DẪN:**

## PHẦN A — NGỮ PHÁP (50 điểm — mỗi câu 2 điểm)

*25 câu hỏi bao phủ tất cả 12 bài học của Phase 1.*

### A1 — Bài 1–2: Thì Hiện Tại Đơn & Hiện Tại Tiếp Diễn (Câu 1–5)

**Câu 1.** She ________ (study) English for two hours every evening without fail.
   - A) study
   - B) studies
   - C) is studying
   - D) studys

**Câu 2.** — What ________ you ________ right now?
   — I ________ for my sister. She's late.
   - A) do / do / wait
   - B) are / doing / am waiting
   - C) do / doing / waiting
   - D) are / do / am wait

**Câu 3.** Water ________ at 100 degrees Celsius. That's a scientific fact.
   - A) boils
   - B) is boiling
   - C) boil
   - D) will boils

**Câu 4.** My parents ________ in Hanoi at the moment. They'll be back next week.
   - A) travel
   - B) travels
   - C) are travelling
   - D) have travelled

**Câu 5.** He ________ meat because he became a vegetarian last year.
   - A) isn't eating
   - B) doesn't eat
   - C) don't eat
   - D) not eat

## PHẦN C — ĐỌC HIỂU (15 điểm)

*Đọc đoạn văn sau và trả lời câu hỏi bên dưới.*

---

**My name is Minh, and I am a 22-year-old student from Hue City. I am currently studying Information Technology at a university in Hanoi. My daily routine is quite busy but I enjoy it.**

**Every morning, I wake up at 6:30 am and go for a short jog around the lake near my dormitory. After that, I come back, shower, and have a simple breakfast — usually a bowl of pho or some bread with eggs. My first class starts at 8 am, and I usually attend three or four classes per day.**

**In the evenings, I spend about two hours studying and doing assignments. I also study English for 45 minutes every night because I want to take the IELTS exam next year. So far, I have improved a lot since I started using this study plan.**

**Last weekend was especially enjoyable. On Saturday, my friends and I went to a traditional market near the old quarter. We ate street food and took many photos. In the afternoon, we visited a small art exhibition that had opened recently. I had never been to an art exhibition before, so it was a wonderful experience. On Sunday, I stayed home and video-called my family in Hue. My mother cooked my favourite dish — nem cuon — and showed it to the camera, which made me feel both happy and a little homesick.**

**I am working hard this year because I have a clear goal: to graduate with a high GPA and find a good job in technology. Life in Hanoi is more exciting than in my hometown, but it is also more expensive and sometimes lonelier.**
