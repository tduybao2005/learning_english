### Nhóm C: Trạng từ thời gian dùng với Present Perfect

| Word | Pronunciation /IPA/ | Nghĩa tiếng Việt | Vị trí & Ví dụ |
|------|---------------------|-----------------|----------------|
| already | /ɔːlˈredi/ | rồi, đã xong | Trước V3: I **have already** submitted my essay. |
| yet | /jet/ | chưa / chưa à? | Cuối câu: Have you packed **yet**? / I haven't eaten **yet**. |
| just | /dʒʌst/ | vừa mới | Trước V3: She **has just** arrived. |
| ever | /ˈevər/ | đã từng (câu hỏi) | Sau have/has: Have you **ever** tried paragliding? |
| never | /ˈnevər/ | chưa bao giờ | Sau have/has: I **have never** seen snow. |
| recently | /ˈriːsntli/ | gần đây | Cuối câu/Đầu câu: I **have** seen her **recently**. |
| lately | /ˈleɪtli/ | dạo này, gần đây | Cuối câu: Have you been sleeping well **lately**? |
| so far | /səʊ fɑːr/ | cho đến nay | Cuối câu: I **have finished** 3 chapters **so far**. |
| since | /sɪns/ | từ khi, từ lúc | + mốc thời gian: **since** 2020, **since** Monday |
| for | /fɔːr/ | trong (khoảng thời gian) | + khoảng t.gian: **for** 5 years, **for** a week |
| before | /bɪˈfɔːr/ | trước đây, trước kia | Cuối câu: I **have** done this **before**. |

### Exercise 2: Choose the Correct Word

*Chọn từ đúng trong ngoặc để điền vào chỗ trống:*

1. Have you ________ visited Phong Nha Cave? (ever / never)
2. I ________ seen that film. Let's talk about something else. (already / yet) + have
3. She ________ arrived home ________. She left 30 minutes ago. (just / hasn't + yet)
4. Have they finished the project ________? (already / yet)
5. I have lived in this neighbourhood ________ 12 years. (for / since)
6. She has worked at the hospital ________ January. (for / since)
7. He has ________ been to Africa. He wants to go someday. (ever / never)
8. I have ________ eaten — I'm not hungry. (just / yet)
9. We haven't received the package ________. (already / yet)
10. She has known him ________ they were in primary school. (for / since)
11. Have you ________ climbed a mountain? (ever / just)
12. I've been studying ________ 3 hours. (for / since)
13. He has ________ left — you just missed him. (just / yet)
14. They haven't decided ________. Give them time. (yet / already)
15. I have ________ heard this story before. (never / ever)

**Đáp án Exercise 2:**
1. ever | 2. already | 3. just / yet | 4. yet | 5. for | 6. since | 7. never | 8. just | 9. yet | 10. since | 11. ever | 12. for | 13. just | 14. yet | 15. never
